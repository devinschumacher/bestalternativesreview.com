### What is Low Ph Shampoo?

Low pH shampoo is designed to replicate the natural acidity of the scalp, typically between 4.5 to 5.5 on the pH scale. This mild formulation helps maintain the scalp's natural balance and can be gentler on hair and scalp compared to traditional shampoos with higher pH levels. 

These shampoos are particularly beneficial for individuals with sensitive scalps, dandruff, or scalp conditions, as they reduce irritation and inflammation. The lower pH helps prevent scalp infections and maintains healthy hair growth by preserving the scalp's natural microbiome. 


### What pH Level Should Shampoo Have?

Most commercial shampoos have a pH between 6 and 9, which is higher than the natural scalp pH. This can disrupt the scalp's acid mantle, leading to dryness, irritation, and increased production of sebum (oil). 

Low pH shampoos, with a pH between 4.5 and 5.5, help maintain scalp health by restoring this natural balance. This pH range is optimal for removing dirt and product buildup while preserving the scalp's protective barrier.
