Adjustable risers offer the convenience of fine-tuning keyboard height, while fixed-height models provide a simpler, sometimes more stable solution. ## FAQ

![Keyboard-Riser-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Keyboard-Riser-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What is a Keyboard Riser?

A keyboard riser is a height-adjustable stand designed to elevate computer keyboards and mouse platforms. These stands improve ergonomics by raising the work surface to a more optimal position, reducing strain and improving typing and mouse control. 

Common materials include plastic, metal, and wood, with designs ranging from simple, single-stage options to multi-level adjustable stands. The base typically includes non-slip pads to prevent movement during use. 


### Why Use a Keyboard Riser?

Elevating your keyboard and mouse to a more natural height can reduce shoulder, neck, and wrist strain, especially during long work sessions. Proper wrist positioning helps prevent repetitive stress injuries and improves typing accuracy and speed. 

Keyboard risers also create space under the work surface, allowing for better cable management and access to frequently used items. The stable stance prevents accidental spills and protects your work surface from heat-generating components like laptops. 

![Keyboard-Riser-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Keyboard-Riser-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How Do Keyboard Risers Improve Ergonomics?

By raising the keyboard to a comfortable height, risers help align your wrists in a straight line during typing, reducing the risk of wrist flexion and pronation. This positioning decreases pressure on the wrists and improves blood circulation to the hands and arms. 

The elevated position also allows for a more natural arm and shoulder posture, reducing forward-leaning and hunched postures that can lead to chronic discomfort and injury. 


### What Are the Key Features to Consider?

Materials: Look for durable, stable materials that resist tipping and warping. Plastic and metal risers are common, with wooden options providing aesthetic appeal and natural ergonomics. 

Height Adjustability: Multi-stage height adjustments allow precise positioning, while simple stands offer fixed elevation. The maximum height should accommodate your specific needs and desk space. 

![Keyboard-Riser-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Keyboard-Riser-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Are Keyboard Risers Compatible with All Computer Setups?

Most keyboard risers are compatible with standard keyboards and mice, but verify dimensions if you have a unique or extended layout. Ensure the stand's height won't interfere with keyboard connector ports or mouse buttons. 

Consider the distance between the keyboard and mouse platforms. Proper spacing helps maintain natural arm positioning and prevents awkward reaching. 


### Can Keyboard Risers Be Used with Laptops?

Yes, keyboard risers can improve laptop ergonomics by creating a more natural typing angle. Ensure the stand's height allows for proper wrist positioning without stretching, and use a laptop stand to maintain a safe viewing angle. 

Some risers include holder attachments specifically for laptops, while others may require additional support to prevent tipping. Always prioritize stability and proper posture when using a keyboard riser with a laptop.
