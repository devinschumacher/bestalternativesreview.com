## FAQ


### What flavor options are available in 20 oz Red Bull cans?

Red Bull offers several flavor variants in their 20 oz cans, including original Red Bull, as well as limited-time flavors like Raspberry Lemonade and Cherry Cola. Some regional variations include Passion Fruit and Blackcurrant flavors. 

Most regular 20 oz cans contain only the original Red Bull flavor, though availability of flavors may vary by region and country. 


### How much caffeine is in a 20 oz Red Bull can?

Each 20 oz Red Bull can provides 240 mg of caffeine, which is equivalent to about 6 standard cups of drip coffee by volume. This high caffeine content distinguishes Red Bull from many other energy drink brands. 

The caffeine comes from a blend of guarana and caffeine anhydrous, which together create a noticeable energy boost without the bitterness found in some competing energy drinks. 


### Can you purchase 20 oz Red Bull cans individually?

Red Bull cans are typically sold in six-packs or mixed packs containing multiple flavors. Individual 20 oz cans are not commonly available in retail stores, though some online retailers may offer them. 

For single servings, purchasing six-packs and storing them allows easy access to individual cans as needed. 


### Are 20 oz Red Bull cans recyclable?

All 20 oz Red Bull cans are made from aluminum and are fully recyclable. Recycling aluminum cans helps conserve natural resources and significantly reduces environmental impact compared to producing new cans from raw materials. 

The recycling process for aluminum cans is energy-efficient, using 95% less energy than making new cans from raw materials. 


### How long does a 20 oz Red Bull can stay good after opening?

Once opened, a 20 oz Red Bull can should be consumed within 24 hours for optimal taste and freshness. The drink typically stays safe to consume for up to 72 hours after opening, but the carbonation and flavor may degrade over time. 

For best results, store opened cans refrigerated and consume as soon as possible after opening. 


### What ingredients should I avoid if I have specific dietary restrictions?

If you're sensitive to artificial sweeteners, avoid Red Bull drinks containing aspartame, which is used in some flavor variants. People with sulfite sensitivities should check the label for additional sulfites beyond those naturally occurring in natural flavorings. 

For those avoiding caffeine, look for Red Bull products that clearly state "Caffeine-Free" on the label, though these are less common than the standard caffeinated versions.
