## FAQ


### What is an Aerolatte milk frother?

The Aerolatte milk frother is a handheld appliance designed to froth and steam milk for coffee and tea drinks. It uses a whisk-like mechanism to create microfoam, which combines tiny air bubbles with milk, resulting in a smooth, velvety texture. 

Unlike traditional espresso machines, the Aerolatte requires a steaming pitcher or a specially designed Aerolatte bottle that fits onto the whisk base. This makes it a convenient option for home coffee enthusiasts who want to achieve professional-quality frothed milk without the complexity of a full espresso machine. 


### What types of milk can I use?

The Aerolatte works best with cold milk, such as whole milk, half-and-half, or heavy cream. It can handle up to 16 ounces of milk at once. For better results, use cold milk as warm milk may create too much foam. 

Some users have reported success with non-dairy milk alternatives like almond, soy, or coconut milk, though the frothing results may vary. For the best texture, try the milk type that matches your intended drink recipe. 


### How do I clean and maintain the frother?

Aerosol nozzles should be removed after each use and cleaned with warm water. The whisk base can be washed with a damp cloth. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or brushes that might damage the delicate internal parts. For best results, store the nozzles and whisk in a dry place when not in use. 

Regular maintenance includes checking the nozzle for blockages and ensuring the whisk moves freely. If you notice any unusual sounds or reduced performance, check for proper cleaning or potential component wear. 


### Can I use the Aerolatte without a pitcher?

The Aerolatte is specifically designed to work with its included steaming pitcher or compatible bottle. The whisk mechanism needs the enclosed space to create proper microfoam, so using it directly in a regular glass or mug will not produce the same results. 

If you want to froth milk in different containers, consider purchasing additional compatible bottles that you can use with the Aerolatte. This ensures you always have a suitable container for your frothing needs. 


### What are the electrical requirements?

The Aerolatte operates using a rechargeable lithium-ion battery that charges via USB. The charger requires a standard USB wall adapter or a compatible computer USB port. 

The battery typically lasts for 60-90 seconds of continuous use. After each use, allow the battery to cool completely before recharging. The battery should be fully charged between uses to maintain optimal performance. 


### How should I store the Aerolatte?

When not in use, store the Aerolatte in its charging cradle to keep the battery charged and ready for the next use. Always remove the battery before storing the unit for extended periods. 

Keep the nozzles and whisk base in a dry place, protected from dust and moisture. Store them in their included protective caps or in a clean, dry container to prevent damage or contamination.
