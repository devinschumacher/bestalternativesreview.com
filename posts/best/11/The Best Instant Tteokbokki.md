### What is instant tteokbokki?

Instant tteokbokki is a popular Korean street food that has been adapted for quick, easy preparation at home. It consists of chewy rice cakes in a spicy, sweet sauce, often served with vegetables and protein options like chicken or shrimp. 

The convenience of instant tteokbokki comes from pre-cooked rice cakes and sauce packets that require minimal preparation. Simply reheat the ingredients, mix them together, and cook for a few minutes to heat through before serving. 


### How does the flavor compare to traditional tteokbokki?

While the instant version captures the basic flavors of tteokbokki, it may not fully replicate the complex taste profile of traditional street food versions. Homemade or restaurant-quality tteokbokki can offer more nuanced flavors and textures. 

Instant tteokbokki typically delivers the signature spicy-sweet taste but may have a slightly less robust flavor due to the convenience ingredients used.
