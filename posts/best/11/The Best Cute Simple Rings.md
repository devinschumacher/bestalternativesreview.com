### How do I choose the right size?

To find your ring size, measure the base of your finger using a piece of string or a flexible measuring tape. Mark the measurement, then compare it to a ring size chart. 

Leave about 1/8 inch of extra space at the mark, as fingers can expand during the day. If in doubt between sizes, err on the larger side for more comfort during wear. 


### How should I care for simple rings?

Clean your simple ring regularly with mild soap and warm water, then buff it dry with a soft cloth. Store it in a soft-lined box or pouch to prevent scratches. 

Avoid harsh chemicals like bleach or strong perfumes near your ring, as they can damage the metal or stone settings. Have the ring professionally cleaned and checked for sizing every 2-3 years.
