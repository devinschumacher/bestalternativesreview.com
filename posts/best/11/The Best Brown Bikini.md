### What makes a good brown bikini?

A quality brown bikini typically features durable, quick-drying fabric like chlorine-resistant polyester or spandex blends. The color should resist fading from sunlight and chlorinated water. Look for well-placed cups, strong straps, and sturdy clasp closures. 

Consider the fit - a properly fitted bikini should sit smoothly without riding up or causing pressure points. The coverage level is personal preference, but be aware that thicker material tends to cling more in chlorine than thinner options. 


### How do I care for my brown bikini?

Rinse your bikini immediately after use to remove chlorine and saltwater. Machine wash separately in cold water with mild detergent, avoiding fabric softeners which can damage the material. Hang dry away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and shrinkage. 

Avoid using the dryer, bleach, or harsh chemicals. For swim caps or accessories that don't fit in the washing machine, spot clean with a damp cloth and mild soap. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use.
