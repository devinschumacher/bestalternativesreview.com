## FAQ

![Bed-Bumpers-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Bed-Bumpers-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are bed bumpers made of?

Bed bumpers are typically made from foam encased in washable fabric. The foam provides padding and protection against bed edges, while the fabric cover keeps the product clean and comfortable. 

Some premium options incorporate gel-infused foam for better pressure distribution, or feature multiple layers of foam for increased durability. The fabric covers can be made from various materials like microfiber, cotton, or plush fabrics depending on the brand. 


### How do bed bumpers prevent injuries?

Bed bumpers work by creating a protective barrier between a child's body and the hard edges of the mattress, preventing accidental bumps and injuries. They're particularly useful around the head of the bed and at the foot where children may roll off during nighttime wakings. 

By softening the impact of contact with the bed frame, bumpers help prevent bruising, scrapes, and other minor injuries that can occur when a young child rolls or falls against the hard mattress edges. 

![Bed-Bumpers-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Bed-Bumpers-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Are bed bumpers safe for infants?

Bed bumpers are generally considered safe for infants over 6 months old when installed properly and with clear instructions followed. Younger babies should use a mobile bed guard or a side crib with a mattress that fits snugly to prevent entrapment risks. 

Always check the manufacturer's guidelines for recommended age range and installation instructions specific to your child's development stage. 


### How long do bed bumpers last?

With regular use and proper care, bed bumpers typically last 1-2 years before needing replacement due to matting of the foam or wear on the fabric cover. 

Signs indicating it's time to replace your bed bumper include noticeable compression in the foam, worn-out fabric, or separation between the foam and cover material. 

![Bed-Bumpers-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Bed-Bumpers-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Can bed bumpers go in the washer?

Most bed bumpers can be machine washed in a mesh bag using mild detergent and cold water. Check the care label first for specific instructions regarding washing and drying. 

Air drying is recommended to prevent mildew and maintain the shape of the foam. Avoid using high heat settings in the dryer, as they can damage the foam and cause shrinkage. 


### Do bed bumpers need frequent replacement?

With regular care and proper installation, bed bumpers usually last 1-2 years. Frequent replacement may be needed if your child is particularly active or if the bumper shows signs of wear before the one-year mark. 

Monitor the foam compression and fabric condition monthly to determine if the bumper needs replacement. The safety of your child should be the primary consideration in determining when to update your bed protection accessories.
