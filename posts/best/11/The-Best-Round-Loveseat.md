### How should I measure for a round loveseat?

When measuring for a round loveseat, consider both the diameter (width) and the height of the seat. The diameter should be at least 6 inches wider than your entrance door for easy passage. 

Allow 24-30 inches of floor space between the loveseat and any walls or furniture for comfortable movement. Consider the total height, including any bases or legs, to ensure proper clearance. 


### What maintenance requirements do round loveseats have?

Regular maintenance for a round loveseat includes vacuuming the upholstery weekly and spot cleaning any spills immediately to prevent stains. Most fabrics require only mild soap and water for cleaning. 

Check the manufacturer's care instructions for specific recommendations, as some materials may require professional cleaning or specific maintenance products.
