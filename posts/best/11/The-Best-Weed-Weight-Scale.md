### Are Digital Scales More Accurate Than Mechanical Ones?

Modern digital scales use load-cell technology that provides consistent readings down to 0.01 gram increments. The digital display eliminates parallax errors and provides immediate readings. 

Mechanical scales, while less common in professional settings, can still be accurate for home use when properly calibrated and maintained. They're particularly useful in humid environments where electronic components might malfunction.

### How Do I Calibrate a Cannabis Scale?

Calibration is crucial for maintaining scale accuracy. Place the scale on a stable, level surface and align the digital display to zero using the tare function before each measurement. 

Use a known weight standard, such as a 100-gram calibration weight, to verify readings. If the scale consistently over- or under-reads, adjust the calibration setting until it matches the standard weight.
