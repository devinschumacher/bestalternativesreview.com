### What is a froyo machine?

A froyo machine, short for yogurt machine, is a specialized refrigerator designed for commercial use. It maintains a consistent temperature between 35-38°F (1.6-3.3°C) to create frozen yogurt-like products by combining milk or yogurt with food-grade mixers. 

These machines feature dual compartments: one for the frozen base and another for various mix-in options. The process involves churning the mixture to achieve the desired consistency before serving. 


### What are the main types of froyo machines?

The primary types include countertop models for small businesses and bulk machines for larger establishments. Countertop units are ideal for cafes and food trucks, while bulk machines are suitable for supermarkets and convenience stores. 

Both types offer customization options for mix-in stations, allowing customers to create personalized servings by adding toppings and flavors. 

![Froyo-Machine-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Froyo-Machine-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do froyo machines work?

These machines maintain a precise temperature using thermostatic controls. When customers select their desired mix-ins, the machine combines the selected ingredients with the frozen base. The mixture is then churning to create a consistent texture before being dispensed. 

The process typically takes about 2-3 minutes per serving, with the option for customers to choose their serving size. 


### What are the typical operating costs?

Electricity usage varies based on usage frequency and machine type. A basic countertop unit consumes around 1,000-1,500 kWh per year, while larger bulk machines use 3,000-5,000 kWh. 

The cost of maintaining the refrigeration unit must be considered, along with the expenses for ice cream mix ingredients and sanitation supplies.

### How often do froyo machines need maintenance?

Regular maintenance is crucial for optimal performance. Machines should be serviced quarterly by a professional, including cleaning internal components and checking thermostats. 

Customers should perform daily checks on door seals, ice cream flow, and mix-in mechanisms. Annual servicing is recommended to ensure proper operation and extend the machine's lifespan.

The top brands in the froyo machine market include Cold Stone Crystal, MIO, and Helado. These manufacturers offer a range of features, from basic dispensing functionality to advanced mix-in stations and customizable branding options. 

Many brands also provide additional accessories like reusable cups, specialized toppings, and marketing materials to enhance the customer experience.
