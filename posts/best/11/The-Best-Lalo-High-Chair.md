### Q: Are Lalo high chairs suitable for outdoor use?

### Q: How do I properly install the 5-point harness?

Installing the 5-point harness securely is crucial for your child's safety during mealtime. Begin by adjusting the harness straps to fit snugly between the shoulders and hips, ensuring there's no slack when your child is seated. Cross the chest strap at the collarbone level and secure it to the corresponding buckle on the seat base. 

Attach the leg straps at the knees, making sure they fit tightly but not uncomfortably. Ensure all straps are properly locked into place and check the harness regularly to confirm it remains secure and correctly adjusted as your child grows. 


### Q: Can I use a Lalo high chair with my existing stroller?

Some Lalo high chairs feature a detachable seat that can be used as a standalone high chair or attached to certain strollers via an adapter. It's important to check the manufacturer's specifications for compatible strollers. 

If your stroller is not listed as a compatible model, consider purchasing an adapter or carrier specifically designed to attach the Lalo high chair. Always verify the weight capacity of both the high chair and stroller to ensure safe and proper attachment.
