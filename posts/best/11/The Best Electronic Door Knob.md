## FAQ


### What is an electronic door knob?

An electronic door knob combines traditional door knob functionality with electronic access control features. It typically incorporates a digital keypad or touchscreen interface for password entry and can be programmed to grant access to specific users at certain times. 

These door knobs often include advanced security features such as motion detection, activity recording, and remote access control. Some models also offer convenience features like USB charging ports and built-in lighting. 


### How does an electronic door knob differ from a smart lock?

A smart lock usually requires a standalone door body replacement, while an electronic door knob can retrofit onto existing door hardware. Smart locks provide more comprehensive security features, including wireless connectivity and mobile app access. 

Electronic door knobs offer a simpler upgrade path, requiring fewer installation steps and potentially lower costs for replacement parts. They provide basic access control while maintaining the traditional door knob design. 


### What are the compatibility requirements for electronic door knobs?

Most electronic door knobs work with standard door frames, requiring 2-inch strike plates and matching rosettes. They typically need a power source such as batteries or USB charging, and may require additional wiring for certain features. 

Check the manufacturer's specifications to ensure compatibility with your door type, thickness, and existing hardware. Installation guidelines should detail any specific prerequisites for your door model. 


### How do electronic door knobs handle power outages?

Power outage response varies by model. Some electronic door knobs have backup power through rechargeable batteries or integrated USB ports, allowing immediate access during outages. 

Others may lock out completely during power failures. Always check the specific model's features to understand its behavior during electrical interruptions. 


### What maintenance is required for electronic door knobs?

Regular maintenance includes checking battery levels, cleaning the exterior surface, and ensuring the strike plate remains properly seated. Some models require periodic firmware updates. 

Monitor the electronic components for signs of wear, such as erratic keypad responses or reduced lighting brightness. Address any issues promptly to maintain reliable operation. 


### How do electronic door knobs affect energy consumption?

Basic models consume minimal power, similar to traditional door knobs when in use. Features like lighting and advanced security monitoring can increase energy consumption. 

Select models with power management settings to minimize energy use. Compare different brands' energy consumption specifications to choose the most efficient option.
