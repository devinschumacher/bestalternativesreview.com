## FAQ


### What is a 12x17 frame?

A 12x17 frame is a specific size in standard printing and framing dimensions. It refers to a frame that measures 12 inches in width and 17 inches in height, providing a precise canvas for artwork, photographs, or printed materials. 

These frames are commonly used in professional photography, fine art reproduction, and architectural documentation, where consistent sizing across multiple prints and displays is crucial. 


### What types of materials work best in 12x17 frames?

Sophisticated 12x17 frames accommodate a variety of media, including high-quality prints, photographic paper, and fine art canvases. Professional-grade paper with proper acid-free backing and UV protection ensures longevity and optimal display quality. 

For architectural and technical drawings, 12x17 frames support vector graphics, CAD prints, and other professional documents with precise measurement requirements. 


### How do I choose the right 12x17 frame?

Select a frame with adequate strength and durability for your specific use case. Consider materials like aluminum for maximum rigidity and wood for traditional aesthetic appeal. The frame should complement your artwork while providing proper support and protection. 

Look for features like adjustable hanging mechanisms and UV protection to enhance the display quality and longevity of your artwork or print. 


### What are the mounting and display options for 12x17 frames?

Standard 12x17 frames include backing boards, acid-free matboard, and UV-filtering glass or acrylic to protect the display item. Many frames feature reversible mounting corners or clips for easy display without tape residue. 

Hanging options include sawtooth hangers for smaller frames and D-rings for heavier artwork. Some frames incorporate built-in brackets for display in frames or photo albums. 


### How should I maintain and care for my 12x17 frame?

Regularly clean the surface and glass with a soft cloth and mild detergent. Check the backing and matting for integrity, replacing any damaged components to prevent damage to the artwork. Store the frame vertically in a dry environment to maintain its shape and protect the contents. 

Refrain from using harsh chemicals, abrasive materials, or excessive moisture, which can damage the frame's components and the artwork inside. 


### What is the warranty coverage for 12x17 frames?

Most reputable frame manufacturers offer warranties lasting 10-20 years on their products. The warranty typically covers defects in materials and workmanship, but may not include damage from improper mounting or environmental factors. Check the warranty terms for specific coverage details and limitations. 

Extended warranties and protection plans are available for an additional fee, providing broader coverage for potential damage or wear over time.
