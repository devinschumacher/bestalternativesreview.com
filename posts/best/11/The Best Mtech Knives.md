### Ergonomic Design

Mtech knives prioritize user comfort through thoughtful handle geometry and grip patterns. Consider features like textured scales, finger grooves, or choil (the downturned back portion of the handle) for enhanced control during precision tasks. 


### Rigid Construction

These knives emphasize robust, no-flex construction to maintain edge integrity during impact. Look for reinforced pivot points, precision ball bearings, and secure lock mechanisms (like liner locks or framelocks) that prevent blade play.
