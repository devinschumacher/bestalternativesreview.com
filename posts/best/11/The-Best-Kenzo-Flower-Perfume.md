### How should I care for my Kenzo Flower fragrance?

To maintain the longevity and quality of your Kenzo Flower perfume, store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep the bottle upright and avoid exposing it to extreme temperatures, which can degrade the fragrance's quality over time.
