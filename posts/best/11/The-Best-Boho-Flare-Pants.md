## FAQ


### What makes boho flare pants unique?

Boho flare pants combine flowing, oversized silhouettes with bohemian-inspired details like fringing, embroidery, and flowing fabrics. The flare at the hem creates a dynamic, movement-enhancing design that's both stylish and practical for casual wear. 

The loose, flowing cut of these pants makes them ideal for layering with tunics or sweaters, while the often lightweight fabrics allow for airy summer wear or added warmth in cooler months. 


### What fabrics are commonly used in boho flare pants?

The most common fabrics for boho flare pants are cotton, linen, and rayon. These natural fibers allow for breathability and flow, while providing enough structure to maintain the pants' shape. 

Some styles incorporate heavier fabrics like velvet or brocade, which add texture and richness to the design but may not be as suitable for every season or occasion. 


### How should boho flare pants fit?

Boho flare pants typically have a loose fit through the hips and thighs, with the flare beginning mid-calf. They should feel comfortable and roomy while allowing for natural movement. 

The pants should sit just above or at the natural waistline, with no constriction across the hips or waistband. The flare should gradually increase as it extends down the leg to create a cohesive silhouette. 


### What styles of tops pair well with boho flare pants?

Romantic, flowy tops with intricate detailing like lace, embroidery, or fringe are a natural match for boho flare pants. Think flowy blouses, peasant tops, or sheer peasant tunics. 

For a more casual look, pair boho flare pants with simple t-shirts, graphic tees, or tank tops that allow the pants' details to shine through. 


### How do you care for boho flare pants?

The care requirements vary by fabric, but most boho flare pants should be machine washable with mild detergent. It's best to wash them in cold water and avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals. 

Air drying is recommended to maintain the pants' shape and prevent shrinkage. If ironing is needed, use a cool setting and hang the pants from a hanger to prevent stretching. 


### What occasions are boho flare pants appropriate for?

Boho flare pants suit casual outings, beach days, and casual dinners in relaxed settings. They're ideal for weekend getaways, festivals, or casual occasions where a laid-back look is preferred. 

While they work well for daytime wear, additional layers or more structured tops might be needed for evening events or formal occasions.
