Easy care is essential for maintaining the plush's quality. Look for materials that resist pilling and fading, as these properties ensure the toy keeps its appearance longer. The care label should specify if the plush is machine washable, preferable in cold water to prevent shrinkage. Check if the material can withstand frequent washing and drying without losing its shape or softness. 


### Safety and Regulatory Standards

Ensure the plush meets safety standards for children's toys, especially if intended for younger kids. This includes testing for toxic materials and ensuring the plush passes mouth-safety checks. Look for certifications from reputable organizations that verify the toy's safety and durability. Avoid products with small, detachable parts that could pose choking hazards to young children.
