## Buyer's Guide

DKNY towels combine style and functionality, offering a blend of comfort and design that can enhance any bathroom. Understanding the key aspects of DKNY towels will help you select a set that matches your needs and preferences. 


### Material and Quality

The primary material determines a towel's absorbency and longevity. Natural cotton towels, including premium options like Egyptian cotton, offer superior softness and durability. Considering the towel's weight (measured in grams per square meter) helps gauge its quality and thickness - heavier towels generally provide more absorbency and a plush feel. 

Look for towels made from 300 GSM or higher for enhanced absorbency and durability. Microfiber towels, while not as absorbent as cotton, dry faster and pack more towels into the same space. Additionally, consider towels with reinforced stitching and quality construction for longer-lasting use. 

![DKNY-Towels-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/DKNY-Towels-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Breathability and Fast Drying

For daily use, towels need to dry quickly to maintain hygiene and prevent odors. Microfiber and jersey-knit towels dry about 50% faster than traditional cotton towels, making them ideal for quick-draw situations like morning showers. 

Special treatments like moisture-wicking and antimicrobial finishes can enhance drying time and hygiene. However, these treatments may wash out over time, so consider their durability and impact on softness. 


### Size and Quantity

Towel size varies by type and intended use. Bath towels typically measure 28x48 inches, while hand towels are usually 18x28 inches and washcloths are smaller, around 12x12 inches. A good starter set includes three bath towels, two hand towels, and two washcloths per person. 

For multiple users or frequent guests, consider a larger set or additional towels. The number of towels needed depends on usage frequency - households with daily bathers may require more frequent washing and replacement than those with occasional users.

### Style and Durability

DKNY offers a range of designs, from solid colors to patterns. Solid colors and simple patterns are most versatile and fade-resistant, while bold patterns may require more careful consideration for lasting quality. 

Choose towels with design in a scale that suits your bathroom's size and style. Repeat patterns on towels within a set ensure consistency, while mixed patterns across different items can create visual interest. 

Investing in higher-quality towels with good construction and fade-resistant dyes ensures that your purchase matches both your aesthetic preferences and practical needs for years to come.
