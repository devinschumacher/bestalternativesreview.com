Modern handcuffs typically feature a lever-controlled locking mechanism that allows rapid deployment and secure cuffing. The ring design should fit snugly around wrists without being too tight. Personal use handcuffs may be lighter and more comfortable for regular carry. Law enforcement models often prioritize strength and security over weight. Consider carrying capacity if you're selecting handcuffs for transporting multiple subjects.
