## Buyer's Guide

Electric scooters have become a popular mode of transportation, offering a convenient blend of speed and portability. The unagi electric scooter stands out with its sleek design and powerful performance. This guide will help you understand the key features to consider when selecting your perfect electric scooter. 


### Performance Specifications

Start by checking the maximum speed rating, typically ranging from 15 to 20 mph. Capacity for weight and payload is crucial, with most scooters handling up to 220-250 pounds. The motor type affects acceleration and top speed, with brushless motors offering superior efficiency and power. 


### Range and Battery Life

Look for scooters with adequate battery capacity for your needs. Range typically varies from 10 to 25 miles per charge, depending on motor efficiency and rider weight. Higher amp-hour (Ah) battery capacity usually means longer runtime but check the total watt-hours (Wh) for accurate range estimates. 


### Size and Ergonomics

Measure the scooter's dimensions to ensure it fits your space and storage requirements. The handlebar height and seat comfort are vital for a safe and enjoyable ride. Consider the standing height when folded versus fully extended to assess its practicality for your daily use. 


### Safety Features

Essential safety features include reliable brakes, often with both hydraulic and mechanical options. Tires should have good grip and be compatible with the motor type. The frame should be made from durable materials like aluminum alloy, paired with solid construction quality.
