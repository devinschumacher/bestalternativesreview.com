### What is Masterpiece Paint by Numbers?

Masterpiece Paint by Numbers combines pre-painted canvas sections with easy-to-follow numbered instructions to help artists create detailed paintings. This popular method allows beginners and experienced artists alike to achieve professional results with less technical skill. 

The kits typically include everything needed for the project, from the pre-painted canvas sections to the paint tubes and brushes. Some kits also provide additional materials like varnish for finishing the artwork.

### What skill level is suitable for Masterpiece Paint by Numbers?

This style of painting is suitable for all skill levels, from complete beginners to experienced artists who want to create detailed works. The numbered system helps guide you through the painting process, making it accessible to those new to painting. 

While the technique simplifies the painting process, basic art skills such as color mixing and basic brush control can enhance your experience and results.
