### Materials and Construction

Mesh fabrics enhance ventilation by allowing air to flow through the garment. Common mesh materials include polyester, nylon, and blends with spandex for stretch. The mesh pattern can vary from fine to open-weave, affecting breathability and visibility. 


### Fit and Sizing

Mens mesh shirts typically range from slim fit to relaxed, with different sizes optimizing the mesh's performance. Consider your body type and intended use - slim cuts enhance athletic appearance, while relaxed fits provide more freedom of movement. 


### Performance Features

Look for moisture-wicking properties to pull sweat away from the skin, and quick-dry capabilities for faster evaporation. Some shirts include UV protection, while others feature moisture-resistant zippers and reinforced stitching in stress points. 


### Care and Maintenance

Most mens mesh shirts require minimal care, washing well at 30-40°C with mild detergent. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners which can damage the mesh structure. The mesh should be hung to dry rather than machine dried to prevent shrinkage and maintain shape.
