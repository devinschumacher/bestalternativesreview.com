Yes, the cotton sateen and percale sheets are suitable for year-round use. The slightly heavier sateen weight provides additional warmth during cooler seasons, while the breathable percale offers excellent summer comfort. 

The bamboo and Egyptian cotton alternatives also maintain their temperature-regulating properties throughout the year, making them versatile choices for all seasons. 


### What bedding sets are available from Nicole Miller?

Nicole Miller offers a variety of bedding sets including comforters, duvet covers, pillowcases, and coordinating shams. The company's most popular sets include the Classic, Sateen, and Premium collections.

### How should I care for Nicole Miller bedding?

Most Nicole Miller bedding sets are machine washable. We recommend using a gentle cycle and turning items inside out to protect printed designs. The company recommends using mild detergent and avoiding bleach or fabric softeners. 

Air drying is preferred over using a dryer to prevent shrinkage and maintain the fabric's smooth finish. 


### What sizes are available for Nicole Miller bedding?

Nicole Miller bedding is available in standard sizes including twin, twin XL, full, queen, king, and California king. The company also offers split king sets for custom installations. 

Sizes are clearly marked on each product page, and the bedding fits standard mattress sizes with 1" of overhang on each side. 


### Can I mix and match Nicole Miller bedding sets?

Yes, Nicole Miller bedding sets are designed to mix and match across their various collections. However, we recommend purchasing coordinating sets for the best visual impact. 

Combining different patterns or solids from the same colorway will yield the most harmonious results.
