## FAQ


### What is a Boogie Board Tablet?

A Boogie Board Tablet combines the convenience of an electronic tablet with the eco-friendly nature of a digital ink display. These devices allow users to write and read on a whiteboard-like screen using a stylus, making them an innovative alternative to traditional tablets and paper. 

Developed by Boogie Board, these tablets offer a unique writing experience with no power consumption when not in use. They are particularly popular among students, teachers, and anyone looking for an environmentally conscious digital writing solution. 


### What are the main features of a Boogie Board Tablet?

Boogie Board Tablets feature an electrically erasable display that shows writing only when powered. They include a stylus for writing, a protective case, and come with software for organizing and managing notes. Most models offer basic document creation and editing capabilities. 

The devices typically measure around 10.5 inches diagonally, making them similar in size to many electronic tablets. They weigh just a few ounces and provide a smooth, pressure-sensitive writing surface. 


### How does the writing technology work?

The writing technology in Boogie Board Tablets uses an electrophoretic display that shows writing when powered and becomes blank when turned off. To write, users apply pressure to the screen with the stylus, which sends an electrical signal to display the ink-like characters. 

When not writing, the screen display turns off to save power, making the device effectively power-free during these periods. This technology allows for continuous writing without battery depletion or screen aging issues. 


### What are the primary use cases for Boogie Board Tablets?

These tablets excel in educational settings, allowing teachers to create and distribute notes without printing. They are also popular for quick sketching, journaling, and note-taking in both personal and professional contexts. 

Students can use them for class notes, quick reference guides, and creative projects. Their portability and eco-friendly nature make them suitable for on-the-go learning and brainstorming sessions. 


### How long do Boogie Board Tablets last on a single charge?

While Boogie Board Tablets don't require charging when not in use, a fully charged device can typically power the screen and writing functionality for several hours. The actual usage time depends on the model and specific features being used. 

During active use, the device should provide enough power for multiple writing sessions. Users can extend battery life by reducing screen brightness and minimizing background app activity. 


### Can Boogie Board Tablets be connected to other devices?

Most Boogie Board Tablets include basic connectivity features for syncing and data transfer. Users can connect the device to computers via USB for file management and software updates. Some models also offer Bluetooth capabilities for syncing with smartphones and tablets.
