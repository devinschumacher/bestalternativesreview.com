### What are the weight capacity options for 60-inch console tables?

Most 60-inch console tables support between 100 to 300 pounds. Higher-end models often feature reinforced frameworks that can handle heavier loads of 300 to 400 pounds, suitable for large TVs or heavy furniture.

### What materials are commonly used in 60-inch console tables?

The most common materials for 60-inch console tables are engineered wood, solid wood, and metal. Engineered wood offers cost-effective durability with decent weight capacity, while solid wood provides premium aesthetics and natural grain patterns. 

Metal tables combine strength with modern design, often featuring powder-coated finishes for corrosion resistance. Each material offers unique advantages in terms of style, durability, and maintenance requirements. 


### How should I measure my space for a 60-inch console table?

Measure the available space in both length and width. The table should be placed parallel to the longer room dimension, with at least 30 inches of clear space on either side. Additionally, ensure there's at least 18 inches between the table and any walls. 

Consider the room's traffic flow and doorways, as the table can create navigational challenges if positioned too close to entry points or walls. The measurement should account for both the table's dimensions and its impact on the room's functionality.
