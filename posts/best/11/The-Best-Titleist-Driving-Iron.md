Modern Titleist drivers incorporate several key technologies that enhance both distance and accuracy. A prominent feature is their low-profile design, which lowers the center of gravity for improved stability during impact. The crown design often includes ventilation slots to reduce weight, contributing to a higher moment of inertia and reduced twisting on mis-hits. 


### Material Innovations

Titleist drives utilize advanced materials to achieve optimal weight distribution and flexibility. The face cup construction combines stainless steel and titanium alloys, creating a thin, flexible face that maximizes ball speed. The crown typically uses lightweight aluminum with specific thicknesses in different areas to balance strength and reduction.
