### What is the most flattering cut for a neon green dress?

A neon green dress looks best in fitted, form-hugging cuts that accentuate your curves while minimizing the risk of the color appearing too bulky. A sheath or column silhouette works particularly well for highlighting your figure and maintaining a sleek, modern look.

### How should I style my neon green dress for different occasions?

For formal occasions like weddings or galas, pair your neon green dress with neutral accessories like black or silver jewelry, a simple clutch, and sleek heels. This balanced look maintains the dress's impact without overwhelming the overall outfit. 

On more casual occasions, such as a night out with friends, you can add more colorful accessories to complement the dress. Think layered necklaces, colorful belts, and statement earrings to create a coordinated yet playful ensemble.

### How can I care for my neon green dress?

Hand washing or gentle machine washing on a delicate cycle with mild detergent is recommended to prevent color fading. Use cool water and avoid bleach or harsh chemicals that can damage the fabric. 

Avoid using fabric softeners and opt for air drying instead of using a dryer. If you need to iron the dress, use the lowest heat setting and protect the fabric with a pressing cloth to prevent heat damage.
