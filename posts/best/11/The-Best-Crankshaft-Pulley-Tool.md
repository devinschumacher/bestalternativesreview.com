These tools feature adjustable clamps that grip the pulley while turning mechanisms to twist the pulley off the crankshaft. 

Essential components include locking mechanisms to prevent slippage, adjustable clamping arms to accommodate various pulley sizes, and sometimes built-in torque limiters to prevent over-tightening and damage to engine components. 


### What are the main benefits of using a crankshaft pulley tool?

The primary benefit is safety. Manual removal methods risk damaging the pulley, crankshaft, or other engine components. The tool applies even pressure and controlled torque, reducing the risk of broken parts and costly repairs. 

These tools also save time compared to manual methods and are particularly useful for pulleys that are seized or difficult to remove due to corrosion. The adjustable feature allows quick replacement of different pulleys without needing multiple tools.
