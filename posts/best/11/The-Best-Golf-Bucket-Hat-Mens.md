### Which materials are commonly used in golf bucket hats for men?

Quality golf bucket hats for men typically feature durable, breathable fabrics like polyester, cotton-poly blends, or performance-wicking materials. These materials help keep the wearer cool during play while providing protection from the sun. 

The brim is often made from the same material as the crown, but some hats incorporate special sun-blocking UV protection in the brim fabric. The inner sweatband is usually made from moisture-wicking mesh or breathable cotton. 


### How should I determine the right size golf bucket hat?

Measure your head's circumference just above your ears and just below your hairline. Add 1-2 inches to this measurement to ensure a comfortable fit that's not too tight. 

Most golf bucket hats come in sizes small through extra-large. If measurements fall between size categories, err on the side of a slightly larger hat for comfort and proper ventilation.

### How should I care for my golf bucket hat?

Clean your hat regularly using a soft-bristled brush and mild soap, either by hand or in a delicates bag in the washing machine. Air dry the hat completely before wearing it again.
