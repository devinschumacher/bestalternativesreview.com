### What are the Different Types of Amulet Book Entries?

Amulet Book entries typically fall into three categories: Physical Amulets, Ritual Practices, and Symbolism. Physical Amulets describe specific objects, their creation methods, and their protective properties. Ritual Practices detail ceremonial procedures for enhancing an amulet's effects or invoking its power. 

Symbolism entries explain the meanings behind various symbols and their placement within amulets. Together, these elements create comprehensive guidance for creating and using amulets in magical practice.
