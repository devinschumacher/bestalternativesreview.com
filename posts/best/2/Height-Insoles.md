## FAQ


### What are Height Boosters?

Height Boosters, also known as Height Insoles, are shoe inserts designed to provide an additional inch or two of height to the wearer. These inserts are typically made from materials like memory foam, cork, or latex and can be easily inserted into various types of shoes. They have become popular among individuals who wish to appear taller or those with shorter statures seeking the benefits of a slightly taller figure. 

Height Boosters come in several sizes and shapes to accommodate different shoe types. They are user-friendly and can be worn for extended periods without causing discomfort, making them a suitable choice for both casual and professional occasions. If you're looking to feel more confident or increase your height, these inserts might be the solution. 


### Which materials are used to make Height Boosters?

Height Boosters can be crafted from a variety of materials, including memory foam, cork, and latex. Memory foam is the most popular choice due to its comfortability, shape-conforming ability, and support. Cork and latex are also used because they are natural, hypoallergenic, and comfortable. The materials utilized in the height inserts can affect factors like cushioning, weight, and overall comfort. To get the best results, opt for an insole made from a high-quality material that meets your specific needs.

When selecting the material for an insole, it's crucial to consider your personal preferences, the shoes you use the inserts with, and any health issues you may have. People with allergies to certain materials should always check the label before purchasing an insole.


### How do Height Boosters work?

Height Boosters function by adding a little extra height to the wearer. They are usually inserted into the shoe and sit between the insole and the sole of the shoe. This action raises the overall height of the shoe and, consequently, the wearer. The insole achieves this by slightly elevating the heel, giving the wearer the perception of being taller. 

Height Boosters are designed from materials like memory foam, cork, and latex, which are capable of adapting to the shape of the foot. When worn, the insole acts as a cushion, offering additional support and comfort to the wearer's foot. In addition to increasing the wearer's height, these inserts can also help alleviate foot pain and discomfort. 


### How to clean and care for Height Boosters?

Height Boosters can be cleaned by gently washing them with soap and water. Most inserts can be machine-washed or hand-washed in cold water, but it's crucial to avoid hot water, as it can distort or damage the insole. After washing, air-dry the insert away from direct sunlight and heat sources.

If you'd prefer not to wash the insert, you can clean it using a damp cloth and mild soap. Gently rub the sole of the insert with the damp cloth, focusing on stained or dirty areas. Be sure to rinse the insert thoroughly and allow it to air-dry fully before use. Proper maintenance of the insole ensures longer life and continued comfort.


### Are Height Boosters safe to use?
