Falcon, also known as Sam Wilson, is a character in Marvel Comics. He is best known as a former paramilitary operator and a close ally to Captain America. Sam Wilson first appeared as Falcon in Captain America #117 in 1969. Since then, he has played an important role in various Marvel storylines and has even taken over the mantle of Captain America for a while in the comics and animated series, "Captain America: The Winter Soldier.
