### How do I care for my over the knee platform boots?

To ensure the longevity of your over the knee platform boots, proper care is essential. Start by cleaning the boots regularly to prevent dirt buildup. For leather boots, use a damp cloth to wipe away dirt, then allow them to air dry. Never place wet or damp boots near a direct heat source, as this can cause the leather to crack or warp. 

If your boots have a removable insole, take it out and clean it separately. For suede or other delicate materials, use a suede brush to softly remove dirt and debris. Store your boots in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight or harsh chemicals, which can damage the material and color over time.
