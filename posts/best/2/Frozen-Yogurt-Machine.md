### Which features should I look for in a frozen yogurt maker?

When selecting a frozen yogurt maker, various features merit consideration. Initially, take into account the capacity of the machine to ensure it can accommodate the required amount of frozen yogurt for your household or for entertaining guests. Another significant feature to contemplate is the machine's frozen yogurt dispensing options.
