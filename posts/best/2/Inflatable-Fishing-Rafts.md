### What is the difference between inflatable rafts and rigid rafts?

Inflatable and rigid rafts exhibit notable differences in several aspects. Portable and compact, inflatable rafts can be conveniently stored and transported due to their ability to deflate. Many inflatable rafts can be further condensed by deflating and folding them, perfect for times when they aren't in use. 

Conversely, rigid fishing rafts maintain their fixed shape and cannot be deflated, thereby necessitating additional storage space when not in use. However, they boast superior strength and durability as compared to their inflatable counterparts. Specially designed rigid fishing rafts like Old Town's Predator series boast enhanced stability and load capacity. Ultimately, the choice between an inflatable and a rigid fishing raft rests on the user's requirements, budget, and personal preference.

### What is the best way to maintain and care for an inflatable fishing raft?

Maintaining your inflatable fishing raft will prolong its lifespan and ensure optimal performance. Regularly inspect the raft for signs of wear, damage, or leaks, and address these issues immediately. After each use, clean the raft to remove dirt, debris, or saltwater, which may hasten wear and tear.
