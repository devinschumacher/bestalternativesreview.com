### What is a Mini Street Bike?

A Mini Street Bike, also known as a stunt bike, is a smaller version of a standard motorcycle designed for stunt riding and tricks. These bikes are typically lighter, more maneuverable, and have lower seat heights, making them ideal for beginners or those looking for a more agile ride. They also often come with lower seat heights and more affordable prices. 

Mini Street Bikes typically have smaller engines ranging from 50cc to 250cc, depending on the model. They often have adjustable suspension systems and inverted front forks for improved handling, as well as wider handlebars for better control during stunts. Some even come with built-in wheelsies or wheelies bars to help riders perform wheelstands and other advanced tricks.

### What brands offer Mini Street Bikes?

Several brands offer Mini Street Bikes in a variety of models and configurations. Some of the most popular brands include Kawasaki, Suzuki, Yamaha, Honda, and TTZero/EBR. ### What should I consider when buying a Mini Street Bike?

When shopping for a Mini Street Bike, consider the following factors to ensure you find the best bike for your needs and preferences. First and foremost, consider the size of the engine in relation to your skill level and driving experience.
