Yes, most food thermometers are designed for high levels of accuracy, typically within a few degrees Fahrenheit. Accuracy varies depending on the design and technology of the specific thermometer. ### Can food thermometers be used for sous vide cooking?

Although most food thermometers are suitable for monitoring the internal temperature of various food items during conventional cooking methods, there are some specific models designed to be used in sous-vide cooking. Sous vide-specific food thermometers typically feature a high degree of precision and control, with the ability to accurately measure and regulate both the temperature of the water bath and the core temperature of the food. These models often come equipped with features such as WiFi or Bluetooth connectivity, allowing users to monitor and adjust temperatures from a smartphone or tablet.
