### How does an Ice Cream Maker work?

An ice cream maker works by freezing the mixture as it is being churned. This process incorporates air into the mixture, resulting in a light and fluffy dessert. An ice bath, a combination of ice and salt, surrounds the churn to facilitate the freezing process. The churning action gradually cools and freezes the mixture until it reaches a smooth and creamy consistency.
