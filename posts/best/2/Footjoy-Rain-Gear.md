### What is the Hydroshell technology?

Hydroshell technology is a proprietary membrane developed by Footjoy specifically for their waterproof and breathable golf shoes. The membrane consists of millions of microscopic pores that allow moisture vapor to escape while preventing water from entering the shoe. This ensures that the foot remains dry and comfortable, even in wet weather conditions. 

Hydroshell technology provides excellent breathability, meaning that the shoes can effectively dissipate the heat and moisture trapped inside the footwear. This helps to prevent blisters and discomfort, allowing golfers to play comfortably in wet conditions. Additionally, the technology ensures that the shoes remain lightweight, as excess moisture is not absorbed into the footwear material.
