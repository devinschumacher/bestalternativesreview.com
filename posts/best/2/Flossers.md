### What are flossers and how do they function?

Flossers, also known as flossing picks or dental flossers, are tools designed to simplify and enhance the flossing experience. Typically, these devices consist of a compact handle attached to a built-in floss needle or pick at the other end. Users simply slide the floss or picks between their teeth and move it up and down or side to side, effectively removing plaque and debris.
