With their versatility and array of choices, glass paperweights prove to be an affable and stylish addition for anyone looking to enhance their office or home setup.


### Proper Care for Your Glass Paperweight

To preserve the appearance and longevity of your glass paperweight, proper cleaning and handling are essential. Avoid using abrasive or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the surface. Instead, use a soft, damp cloth or microfiber cleaning pad to gently remove dirt and dust.

For delicate or stained paperweights, a mild, glass-safe cleaning solution diluted with water can be utilized. Always thoroughly rinse the cloth before wiping, and after cleaning, dry the paperweight with a soft, clean cloth or air dry naturally.
