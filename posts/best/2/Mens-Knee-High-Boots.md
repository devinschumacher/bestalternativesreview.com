### What is the difference between a tall boot and a knee-high boot?

A tall boot and a knee-high boot are similar, with the primary difference being their height. Tall boots generally reach above the knee, while knee-high boots typically stop just below the knee. Both styles provide protection, style, and warmth, but the height may impact the boot's functionality in certain situations, like walking or riding on a bike. 

Tall boots might offer more coverage and support, but they could also restrict movement, especially in outdoor or rigorous activities. Knee-high boots provide less coverage but offer greater flexibility and ease of movement. ### How can I keep my knee-high boots waterproof?

To keep your knee-high boots waterproof, you need to pay attention to the materials used and care for them properly. Regularly clean and condition your boots to retain their water resistance. For footwear with a waterproof membrane, follow the manufacturer's instructions. For leather boots, use a leather conditioner and avoid direct sunlight, as it can weaken the material. Suede boots require a specific suede cleaner and must be dried naturally. Rubber boots are typically easier to care for but should be cleaned and kept away from direct sunlight or harsh chemicals.
