### What's the distinction between chlorine and bromine in hot tubs?

Both chlorine and bromine effectively sanitize hot tub water, eliminating bacteria, viruses, and other microorganisms. Chlorine is a robust oxidizer that reduces organic compounds and disinfects the water surface. Bromine, conversely, is weaker in oxidation but provides continuous disinfection with a longer action time. 

The choice between chlorine and bromine depends on your personal preference and the unique needs of your hot tub. Chlorine's rapid disinfection may appeal to some users, while bromine's steady approach may be preferred by others. Both chemicals are effective in keeping hot tub water clean and safe.
