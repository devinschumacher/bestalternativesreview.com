### Should I avoid Ladybug Earrings with a silver or gold coating because of Allergies?

Additionally, for those with sensitive skin, it is essential to avoid products made with low-quality materials or those with poor construction, as these can lead to irritation. Regardless of your choice of coating, ensure that the earrings are solidly made and use materials of good quality to minimize the risk of reactions.

### How do I take care of Ladybug Earrings?

Proper care and maintenance are essential to ensure the longevity of your Ladybug Earrings. To keep them looking new and shiny, you should avoid getting them wet or using harsh cleaning agents. Remove them before showering or bathing, and clean them using a gentle, jewelry-specific soap and a soft-bristled toothbrush, being cautious not to scratch the surface. Dry your earrings with a soft cloth, ensuring no moisture remains. 

If your Ladybug Earrings are made of sterling silver, you may want to apply a silver polish to remove tarnish and maintain their original shine.
