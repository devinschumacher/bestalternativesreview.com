The size and capacity of the laundry hamper should be based on the number of people in your household and the frequency of laundry. ### Material

Laundry hampers are typically made from plastic, metal, or fabric. Plastic hampers are lightweight and easy to move, but they may scratch your floors. Metal hampers are durable and can support heavy loads, but they tend to be more expensive. Fabric hampers are stylish and can be easily coordinated with your home décor, but they may require more frequent cleaning. 


### Comfort and Portability

A comfortable and portable laundry hamper will make laundry day easier. Look for hampers with handles that are easy to grip and are made of a comfortable material. Also, consider the locking mechanism, as it can prevent the hamper from tipping over when being moved around. Additional features can enhance the functionality of your laundry hamper. For example, a hamper with a built-in laundry bag can help keep your clothes separated and prevent stray socks from getting lost. Also, consider a hamper with a removable liner for easy cleaning.
