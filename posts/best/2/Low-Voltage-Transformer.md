### Types of Low Voltage Transformers

Low voltage transformers come in various types and configurations, based on their design, construction, and intended use. Some familiar types encompass isolation transformers, auto-transformers, and capacitor switching transformers. Isolation transformers are designed to prevent voltage leakage or interference between circuits, making them perfect for sensitive electronic equipment.

Auto-transformers are singular-unit transformers sans a secondary coil for voltage isolation. Capacitor switching transformers represent a new generation of electronic devices that switch AC voltage to DC voltage through capacitive coupling. Their popularity stems from high energy conversion efficiency and a smaller size. Some low voltage transformers utilize a hybrid approach - employing a combination of magnetic and capacitive technology for heightened energy efficiency.
