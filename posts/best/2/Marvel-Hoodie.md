### What is the proper method of washing and drying a Marvel-inspired hoodie?

To maintain the quality and appearance of your Marvel-inspired hoodie, it's imperative to follow proper care instructions. Most Marvel hoodies can be washed and dried using a standard laundry cycle, but it's crucial to check the care instructions tag attached to the garment. It's also best to turn the hoodie inside out before washing to protect the printing on the exterior. When drying, remove the hoodie immediately from the dryer while it's still slightly damp to minimize wrinkles and maintain the hoodie's shape. Proper care will help extend the life of your Marvel-inspired hoodie and keep it looking great.
