### What's the diff between honing rods and sharpening stones?

Honing rods and sharpening stones serve disparate roles in the upkeep and refinement of knives and cutting implements. Sharpening stones, however, are applied to reforge a blade and restore its effectiveness when it loses its sharpness or sustains damage. Sharpening stones are offered in assorted grit ranges and materials, including oil stones, water stones, or belt stones, each with individual attributes and benefits.
