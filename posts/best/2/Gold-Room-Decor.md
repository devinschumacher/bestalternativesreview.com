## Buyer's Guide

When it comes to designing a room, gold is a color that instantly adds an air of luxury, elegance, and sophistication. Gold room decor can be used in various styles, from classical to modern, making it a versatile choice. However, before diving into the world of gold room decor, consider the following factors to ensure you're making a good investment. 


### Material

Gold can be used as a finish on various materials, such as metal, wood, and plastic. While the look of gold can be achieved through different materials, the quality and durability of the material may differ. Metal gold accents are generally more durable and will last longer compared to wood or plastic. Consider the material and its durability before making a purchase.
