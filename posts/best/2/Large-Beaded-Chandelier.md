### What are the dimensions of a large beaded chandelier?

Large beaded chandeliers typically have a diameter ranging from 30 inches to 42 inches. The height varies between 24 inches to 42 inches, depending on the design and size of the chandelier. It is always best to consult the product specifications or contact the manufacturer for accurate measurements. 

These measurements will ensure that the chandelier is suitable for the space it is intended for and that it meets the requirements for proper installation and safety standards. Additionally, some chandeliers may have detachable or adjustable components, which can be taken into consideration when determining the final size of the chandelier.

### Do large beaded chandeliers require special maintenance?

Large beaded chandeliers do require some special maintenance to keep them looking their best. Firstly, it is essential to dust the chandelier regularly using a soft feather duster or a microfiber cloth. This will prevent the buildup of dirt and dust, which can damage the finish and tarnish the beads over time. It is also recommended to use a soft brush or a vacuum cleaner with a gentle attachment to remove any debris that may have accumulated in hard-to-reach areas. 

Additionally, it is crucial to keep the chandelier away from direct sunlight or heat sources, as excessive exposure can cause the beads to fade or discolor. The chandelier should also be turned off and unplugged when not in use to reduce the risk of damage or electrical issues.
