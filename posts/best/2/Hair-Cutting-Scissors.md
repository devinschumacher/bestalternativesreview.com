### What do you understand by hair cutting scissors?

Hair cutting scissors, also commonly referred to as barber scissors, are specialized apparatus designed exclusively for trimming and cutting hair. These scissors differ significantly from regular scissors as they comprise a wider, curved blade, specifically engineered to facilitate a precise and clean cut. In some cases, hair cutting scissors come with specialized guards designed to prevent any slips while cutting. This feature makes them a more preferred choice for both professional hairdressers as well as amateurs, who are looking to achieve a neat and accurate haircut. 

When selecting hair cutting scissors, consider several factors such as the material, blade design, and the presence of guard [combs](https://github.com/devinschumacher/bestalternativesreview.com/blob/main/posts/best/1/Combs.md). Furthermore, some models offer a customizable guard system that fits different hair types and cutting techniques, enhancing the user experience. 


### Can hair cutting scissors be used for cutting other types of hair like beards or eyebrows?

Definitely, hair cutting scissors designed for trimming and cutting hair are versatile enough to be used for cutting beards and shaping eyebrows. These scissors are equipped with a suitable blade width for the task and some even feature specialized guards to prevent any slips or discomfort during the cutting process. However, it is advisable to sterilize the scissors and any accompanying guards before and after use to avoid the spread of bacteria and potential skin irritation. 

When trimming beards, focus on the desired length and angle, ensuring proper hand positioning and overall flow of the cut. For eyebrows, proceed with gentle strokes using the pointed blade, starting from the inner corner and moving towards the outer edge, always prioritizing gentle strokes to avoid any injury or discomfort.

### Is it safe to use hair cutting scissors on wet hair?

No, hair cutting scissors should not be used on wet hair. Wet hair is inherently more fragile and susceptible to damage, making it harder to control and cut accurately. Additionally, wet hair is slippery, increasing the risk of accidents such as cuts and nicks. Consequently, it is crucial to dry or towel-dry the hair prior to any trimming or haircutting activities.

If you prefer or require wet hair cuts, consider investing in wet hair scissors, which are designed with a narrower, straighter blade, making them more manageable and less risky during wet trimming. However, it is still vital to adhere to correct technique, hand positioning, and care when working with wet hair. 


### What maintenance does a hair cutting scissor require?

Proper maintenance is vital to ensure the longevity and optimal performance of hair cutting scissors. It involves cleaning the blades after every use with a scissor comb or a soft-bristled brush to remove any hair and debris. This should be followed by rinsing the blades with clear water to remove any hair residue. Subsequently, dry the scissors using a soft cloth to prevent corrosion, rust, or discoloration. If the blades become dull, consider using a reputable sharpening service or a specialized scissor strop to restore their sharpness. 

It is equally important to store hair cutting scissors in a dry, secure location away from direct sunlight to avoid rust, corrosion, and any discoloration of the scissors. Labeling different scissors, if you use various ones, can help avoid confusion or contamination while storing them.
