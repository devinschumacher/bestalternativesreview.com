By simply pricking your skin with a lancet or using a blood sampling site, you can obtain a small blood sample. The meter processes the sample and displays your glucose level on a digital screen.

These meters are vital for individuals managing diabetes as they enable regular monitoring of blood glucose levels and help you make necessary lifestyle adjustments and medication changes accordingly. You can choose from various types of glucose meters, including traditional ones with strips and electronic ones with sensors.


### Why use a glucose meter?

Using a glucose meter has several advantages. It allows you to better understand the effect of diet, exercise, and medication on your blood glucose levels.
