### How can I take care of my Miami Shirt?

To maintain the vibrancy and quality of your Miami Shirt, it's essential to follow proper care instructions. Firstly, always wash your shirt separately, particularly if you're using a colored fabric dye. This will prevent the color from bleeding onto other clothing items and help the shirt retain its vibrancy for longer. 

Additionally, be sure to wash your Miami Shirt in cold water and avoid using fabric softeners, as these can weaken the fabric's durability and diminish the print's vibrancy. Instead, opt for a gentle detergent specifically designed for delicate fabrics. When it comes to drying, air-drying your Miami Shirt is the best option, as it helps to prevent fading and maintains the shirt's overall quality. Finally, avoid exposing your Miami Shirt to excessive heat or sunlight, as this can cause the colors to fade and the fabric to become less breathable over time.
