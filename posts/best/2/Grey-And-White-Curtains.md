### How are these curtains treated for durability?

The durability of grey and white curtains depends on the type of material and the level of care they receive. Cotton and linen curtains are naturally durable and can last for several years with proper care. These materials should be washed on a gentle cycle, dried on low heat, and ironed on the lowest heat setting to prevent shrinkage and damage. 

Polyester and rayon curtains are generally more resistant to fading and wear and tear compared to natural fibers. However, they may require more frequent cleaning due to their synthetic nature. These materials should be washed on a gentle cycle, dried on low heat, and ironed on the lowest heat setting to maintain their longevity. Regular vacuuming or dusting can also help to keep the curtains looking clean and fresh. 


### What are the care instructions for these curtains?
