### What makes a garden hose heavy duty?

A heavy-duty garden hose is specifically designed to withstand rigorous use and harsh environmental conditions. It is typically made of strong, durable materials, such as UV-resistant rubber or brass, to withstand constant exposure to sunlight, chemicals, and water. Moreover, a heavy-duty hose often includes reinforced couplings, making it less prone to ruptures and leaks. 

These hoses usually have higher pressure ratings than standard garden hoses, allowing them to handle increased water flow without bursting. Additionally, heavy-duty hoses tend to have thick, textured outer layers that provide better grip and control during use. Overall, a heavy-duty garden hose is a reliable and long-lasting option for demanding tasks such as watering large gardens, cleaning outdoor spaces, or filling up pools. 


### How many types of heavy-duty garden hoses are available?

Heavy-duty garden hoses come in various types, catering to specific needs and preferences. Some of the most common types include brass hoses, rubber hoses, and fabric hoses. Brass hoses are known for their durability, resistance to bending and kinking, and long-lasting performance. They are suitable for professional use or situations where resistance to corrosive materials is required. Rubber hoses, on the other hand, are an affordable option that is lightweight and easy to handle. They are resistant to chemical damage and UV rays, making them ideal for outdoor use. 

Fabric hoses are a newer type of heavy-duty hose that combines the benefits of rubber and brass hoses. They are made of a reinforced fabric material that is strong and flexible, providing resistance against abrasion and damage.
