There are two primary types of Frappe Machines: manual and automatic. Manual machines, also known as blenders, require the user to push down on the blending pitcher or jar to power the blades. Another distinction between Frappe Machines is their size and capacity. Some models are compact, suitable for home use and small-scale businesses.
