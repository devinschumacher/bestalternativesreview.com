### What are low top sneakers?

Low tops are popular for everyday wear and are ideal for various activities such as skateboarding, walking, and casual events. 

The lower height in low top sneakers provides better ankle movement and reduces weight compared to high tops. However, they may offer less support and protection for sports requiring frequent ankle movements or high impact activities. Overall, low top sneakers are a great choice for those seeking comfort and style without sacrificing performance.

### What are the benefits of wearing low top sneakers?

Low top sneakers offer several benefits, including comfort and flexibility. The open ankle design allows for natural ankle movement, making them ideal for daily use and casual activities. Many low top sneakers are designed with a focus on style, making them a popular choice for fashion-conscious individuals.
