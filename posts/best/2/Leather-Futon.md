### What makes a futon different from traditional beds?

A futon is a versatile, multi-functional bed that can be easily transformed from a comfortable single bed into a spacious seating area. Traditional beds, on the other hand, are designed to stay in their sleeping position and are typically more stationary. Futons offer space-saving options and can be an excellent choice for smaller living spaces. 

Additionally, futons can be made from a variety of materials, such as leather, which provides durability, elegance, and a unique look. The leather material can add style and functionality to any room, making a futon an attractive choice for modern, minimalistic interior designs as well as traditional settings. 


### How do I choose the right size futon for my space?

When selecting a futon, consider the size of your available living space, as well as the size of the surrounding furniture. Standard futon sizes include single, double, and queen, each offering varying levels of comfort and sleeping space.
