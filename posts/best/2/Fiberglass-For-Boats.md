Fiberglass provides a strong and stable structure, which helps boats maintain their shape and withstand various weather conditions. Additionally, fiberglass can be easily molded into different shapes and sizes, making it suitable for various boat designs and applications. Its low-maintenance nature also makes it an attractive option for boat owners.
