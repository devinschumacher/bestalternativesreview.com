The Nespresso Vertuo has a unique Centrifusion technology, which uses centrifugal forces to spin the capsule to extract the coffee, while the OriginalLine machines use a pressure system. The VertuoLine can produce cups up to 12 oz. , while OriginalLine capsules are available in 5 different sizes.
