### How do you care for an industrial bar cabinet?

To maintain the appearance and longevity of your industrial bar cabinet, it is essential to follow a proper cleaning and maintenance routine. Start by dusting the cabinet regularly using a soft, dry cloth to avoid scratching the exposed surfaces. To prevent rust or tarnish on metal components, apply a specialized metal cleaner and polish regularly. Avoid using abrasive or harsh cleaning agents, as they may damage the unpolished finish and exposed metal hardware. It is also crucial to periodically reseal the cabinet if it has a sealant or wax finish to ensure its durability over time.
