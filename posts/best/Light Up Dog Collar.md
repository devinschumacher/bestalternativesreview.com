## Buyer's Guide

When it comes to keeping your furry friend safe and visible during nighttime walks or outdoor activities, a light-up dog collar can be a game-changer. ### Rechargeable Battery

Look for a light-up dog collar with a rechargeable battery to save on replacement costs and reduce environmental impact.
