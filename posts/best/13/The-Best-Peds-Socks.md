### What are Peds Socks?

Peds Socks are specialized undergarments designed for people with lower limb edema, such as those with lymphedema or venous insufficiency. They feature unique compression patterns and materials to improve circulation and reduce swelling. 

These medical-grade socks apply graduated compression, with the greatest pressure at the ankles and decreasing towards the leg. This helps push fluid out of the extremities and improves blood flow. 


### Who Needs Peds Socks?

Peds Socks are recommended for individuals with lymphedema, venous insufficiency, or other medical conditions that cause lower limb swelling. They're also suitable for people recovering from surgeries or injuries affecting the lower extremities. 

The socks are particularly beneficial for those who spend long periods sitting or standing, as they help prevent blood from pooling in the legs.

### What Makes Peds Socks Different?

Unlike regular socks, Peds Socks feature specialized compression panels that target specific areas of the leg. They're made from medical-grade materials that provide consistent pressure without restricting movement. 

The compression range typically varies from 15-20 mmHg at the ankles to 10-15 mmHg at the knees, providing a balanced pressure gradient to improve circulation. 


### How Do I Choose the Right Size?

Measure your calf circumference at the thickest part of your lower leg. Compare this measurement to the sock size chart provided by the manufacturer to determine your correct size. 

It's important to measure before purchasing, as the graduated compression can make the socks feel tighter than regular socks. Allow about 1-2 inches of give for proper fit.

### How Long Do Peds Socks Last?

With regular wear and proper care, Peds Socks typically last 3-6 months before needing replacement. The lifespan depends on the frequency of use, washing habits, and the severity of the underlying condition. 

Look for signs of wear such as thinning material, lost compression strength, or visible damage before replacing your socks. The socks should maintain their shape and compression properties throughout their usable life. 


### Can I Wash Peds Socks?

Yes, Peds Socks are machine washable. Use a gentle cycle and cold water to prevent shrinkage and maintain the compression properties. Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach, as these can damage the elastic material. 

After washing, hang the socks to air dry rather than using a dryer. Proper drying helps maintain the socks' shape and extends their lifespan.
