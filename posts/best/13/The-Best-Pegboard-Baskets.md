## FAQ

Pegboard baskets offer an organized way to store tools, crafting supplies, or household items. These modular storage solutions hang on pegboards, making them space-efficient while keeping your items off the floor. 

![Pegboard-Baskets-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Pegboard-Baskets-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What materials are pegboard baskets typically made from?

Pegboard baskets are most commonly made from high-density polyethylene (HDPE), which is durable and resistant to moisture. Some premium options feature stainless steel construction for even greater durability. 

Their construction usually consists of a woven mesh fabric that's backed with a stiff plastic frame. This design balances flexibility with strength, allowing the basket to maintain its shape while accommodating varying item sizes. 


### How do I measure for a pegboard basket?

![Pegboard-Baskets-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Pegboard-Baskets-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)

To determine the right size basket, measure the available pegboard space where you plan to install it. Consider the depth between pegs and round your measurements to the nearest standard basket size. 

Standard basket sizes typically range from 5 to 10 inches in depth and 8 to 14 inches in diameter. This allows you to find a size that fits both your space and your storage needs. 


### What weight capacity should I look for in a pegboard basket?

The weight capacity of pegboard baskets varies by design, with most capable of supporting between 30 to 60 pounds per basket. Premium baskets may offer capacities up to 100 pounds. 

Most baskets are rated based on their surface area rather than their volume, so even slightly deeper baskets won't necessarily hold more weight than shallower ones of the same diameter. 


### How should I clean pegboard baskets?

Pegboard baskets are generally easy to clean. For everyday maintenance, wipe them down with a damp cloth using mild soap and water. This removes dust, grime, and minor stains effectively. 

For more stubborn dirt or residue, you can use a brush to scrub the interior. Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners that might damage the surface or weaken the material over time. 


### Can I stack items in a pegboard basket?

While pegboard baskets can hold stacked items, it's important to consider their weight capacity when doing so. The baskets are designed to support their contents when the weight is distributed evenly. 

For optimal organization, consider using clear dividers or smaller organizers within the basket to keep items separated and prevent them from shifting when stacked.
