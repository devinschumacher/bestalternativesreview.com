### What makes a double umbrella stroller different from a regular stroller?

A double umbrella stroller is specifically designed to accommodate two children in side-by-side seats. ### How do double umbrella strollers compare to double strollers?

While both types of strollers can accommodate two children, double umbrella strollers typically have a more compact design, making them easier to maneuver in tight spaces and store when not in use. They also tend to be lighter and more portable. 

Double strollers usually have a larger footprint and offer more features like built-in storage, separate cup holders, and sometimes even reclining seats. However, they require more storage space and can be more cumbersome to move through crowded areas.
