### How should I measure myself for tactical work pants?

Measure around your natural waist (the smallest part of your torso), rather than your hips, to ensure proper fit. The pants should allow for about 1-2 inches of breathing room when zipped up. 

Measure your inseam from the crotch to your desired cuff length. Most tactical pants offer multiple cuff options, allowing you to adjust the leg length to your preference. 


### What sizes do tactical work pants come in?

Most tactical work pants run true to size with fewer variations than casual pants. They typically offer straight, regular, and slim fit options across standard waist sizes (28-40).

### How should tactical work pants be maintained?

Machine wash tactical pants using cold water and mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the materials. Dry them flat or hang them to prevent shrinking and maintain the integrity of the pockets. 

Avoid tumble drying, as it can cause shrinkage and damage the reinforced stitching. Store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and maintain their shape.
