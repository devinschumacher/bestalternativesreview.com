### What are Pruvit Ketones?

Pruvit Ketones are products designed to provide ketones, the body's alternative energy source when carbohydrate intake is restricted. These supplements typically contain beta-hydroxybutyrate (BHB), the primary circulating ketone body, and are available in various forms including ketone ester solutions and ketone salts. 

Unlike dietary supplements that provide metabolic precursors to ketone production, Pruvit products supply ketones directly into the bloodstream, potentially offering faster and more consistent ketone elevation. 


### How do Pruvit Ketones work?

The ketone esters in Pruvit products, such as HVMN Ketone and Keto OS, are converted to BHB through hydrolysis in the body. This process bypasses the normal ketogenic mechanism involving fatty acid oxidation and subsequent ketogenesis in the liver. 

The resulting BHB molecules cross the blood-brain barrier, providing an alternative fuel source for brain cells. This rapid ketone elevation can improve cognitive function, enhance athletic performance, and provide metabolic flexibility.

Direct ketone supplementation through Pruvit products can provide faster and more reliable elevation of blood ketone levels compared to dietary ketosis or fasting.
