### What makes a quality leather pleated skirt?

A leather pleated skirt should sit just below the natural waistline, creating an hourglass silhouette. The length should reach mid-calf for a classic look, though midi or shorter lengths are available. 

The skirt should fall smoothly from the waist without bagging or wrinkling. The pleats should maintain their shape when sitting and walking, with the skirt hugging the curves of the hips and thighs.
