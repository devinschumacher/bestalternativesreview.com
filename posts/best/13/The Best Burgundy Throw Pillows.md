## Buyer's Guide

Burgundy throw pillows blend rich color and texture, making them a striking addition to any room. When selecting these plush accessories, consider several key factors to find the perfect pieces for your space. 


### Color and Tone

Burgundy ranges from deep, rich maroon to lighter, warmer red tones. Consider how this color will interact with your existing d√©cor. Deep burgundies work beautifully in formal spaces, while lighter shades can add warmth to neutral rooms. The tone can also affect the mood of a space - warm tones tend to create cozier environments, while cooler shades can make rooms feel more vibrant. 

![Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Material and Texture

The right material can transform a throw pillow from casual to luxurious. Velvet creates a rich, sumptuous texture that takes well to embroidery or pattern embellishments. Chenille offers softness and warmth, while linen brings a natural, textured feel. Consider the level of maintenance required for each material and how it will maintain its shape over time. 


### Size and Shape

Standard throw pillows measure approximately 18 inches square, while smaller options are often 16 inches. Consider the scale of your furniture and room size when choosing dimensions. Mixing different sizes and shapes can add visual interest, but consistency creates a more cohesive look. 

![Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-3](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-3/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Functionality and Durability

While texture, color, and shape are important, the pillow's functionality should not be overlooked. The filling material affects both comfort and maintenance - down alternative blends provide good support without the traditional drawbacks, while foam inserts offer consistent shape. Consider how frequently the pillow will be used and choose materials that match your needs. 


## FAQ

### What sizes are available for burgundy throw pillows?

Burgundy throw pillows typically come in standard sizes: 18 inches by 18 inches and 20 inches by 20 inches. Some specialty options may offer 22-inch or 24-inch dimensions for extra fullness. 

The most popular size is 18 inches by 18 inches, providing a good balance of comfort and visual impact for most couches and chairs. 


### What materials are used in burgundy throw pillows?

Burgundy throw pillows are commonly made from quality textiles like cotton, linen, and microfiber. Premium options may feature velvet or genuine leather covers for an elegant finish. 

The fill material usually consists of high-resilient foam or down-alternative blends for lasting shape and comfort. 

![Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Burgundy-Throw-Pillows-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How should I care for my burgundy throw pillows?

Most burgundy throw pillows require standard care, including spot cleaning for minor stains and regular rotation of the pillow covers. Machine washable options can be cleaned in a mesh bag on a gentle cycle. 

Air drying is recommended to prevent moisture damage and maintain the shape of the pillow. Avoid using a dryer unless the care instructions specifically allow it. 


### What styles pair well with burgundy throw pillows?
