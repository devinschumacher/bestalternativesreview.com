### Durable Construction

Investing in a coffee maker with robust construction ensures longevity and consistent performance. Look for models with stainless steel or durable plastic components. The brewing chamber and carafe should be easy to remove and clean, with dishwasher-safe options available for convenience. 


### Coffee Strength and Flavor Control

Adjustable settings for coffee strength allow you to customize your brew according to preference. Some models feature pre-infusion technology, where water sits in the grounds before brewing begins. This helps extract maximum flavor without over-extraction, which can occur with weaker coffee makers.
