Proper protection comes from both lens technology and frame design. Additional protection features might include sweat-resistant nose pads and an adjustable chin strap to keep the glasses in place during intense activities.

### Size and Fit

A proper fit ensures maximum protection and comfort. Measure your face width to find frames that fit snugly but aren't too tight. The glasses should sit level on your nose with the top of the frame aligned with your eyebrows. The width of the arms should match your ear size, and the nose bridge should rest comfortably between your eyes. Consider trying on multiple pairs to find the most comfortable and protective fit for your face shape and activity level.
