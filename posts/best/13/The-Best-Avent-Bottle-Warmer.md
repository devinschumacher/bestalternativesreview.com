### What is an Avent bottle warmer?

An Avent bottle warmer is a device specifically designed to safely heat baby bottles to the ideal temperature for feeding. It features precise temperature control and can heat either expressed breast milk or formula milk without the risk of burning. 

The Avent warmer uses advanced thermal technology that heats milk from the inside out, ensuring that the temperature is consistent throughout the bottle while maintaining its nutritional properties and the delicate components of breast milk. 


### How does it compare to microwave heating?

Microwaving baby bottles can heat the milk unevenly, causing hot spots that can burn a baby's mouth. The Avent warmer prevents this by heating from the center of the bottle, ensuring the entire contents reach the correct temperature simultaneously. 

Additionally, microwaving can alter milk proteins and destroy certain nutrients. The Avent warmer's thermal technology preserves the milk's composition and nutritional value, making it a safer choice for infant feeding.
