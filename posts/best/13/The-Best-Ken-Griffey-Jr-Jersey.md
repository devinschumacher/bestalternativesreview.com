### How should I care for my Ken Griffey Jr. jersey?

Clean the jersey by turning it inside out and washing it in a mesh bag with mild detergent in cold water. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the material.

The primary design elements of a home jersey are replicated in a more understated fashion for road jerseys, keeping the core team identity while allowing for a slightly different appearance.
