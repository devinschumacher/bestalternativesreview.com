## Buyer's Guide

With its signature tropical prints and comfortable design, the Life Is Good beach chair has become a popular choice for summer gatherings. Understanding key features will help you select the right chair for your needs, whether you're spending long days at the beach or enjoying a sunny backyard barbecue. 


### Build Quality and Durability

Choose a chair with powder-coated aluminum frames for lasting strength. The seat should feature heavy-duty slats or mesh material that prevents sagging while providing good airflow. Check that all joints have secure locking mechanisms to prevent accidents during transport. 


### Compact Design and Storage

Most beach chairs collapse to a small size for easy transport, but consider models with reinforced hinges for smoother folding and unfolding. Some chairs include carrying bags or straps for securing in vehicles. If storage space is limited, look for models designed to stack when not in use. 


### Comfort Features

The seat should be at least 18 inches wide with additional padding for long-term comfort. Look for chairs with adjustable backrests that can recline to your desired position. Some models offer cup holders, built-in coolers, or storage pockets for essentials like sunscreen and snacks. 


### Print Quality and Style Options

While the cheerful prints are the brand's signature feature, inspect multiple samples to ensure color quality doesn't fade after exposure to sun and water. The manufacturer often releases new patterns each season, so consider whether you want a classic design or something more current.
