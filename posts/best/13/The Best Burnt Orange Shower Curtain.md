### What materials are used to make these shower curtains?

Burnt orange shower curtains are typically made from polyester, poly-cotton blends, or PVC (vinyl). These materials offer different levels of durability, water resistance, and price point, so your choice will depend on your specific needs. 

PVC curtains are waterproof and easy to clean but may not be as breathable as polyester or poly-cotton options, which can be more suitable for damp environments. 


### What if my shower curtain has color bleeding?

If you notice color bleeding in your burnt orange shower curtain, it's likely due to either the washing process or contact with certain materials. Always check the care label and avoid mixing with dark colors during washing. 

Store your curtain separately from other items, especially light-colored towels and linens, to prevent color transfer. If the bleeding continues, you may need to consider a different color or material for your shower curtain.
