### What makes Precious Moments dolls unique?

Precious Moments dolls are known for their detailed sculpting and lifelike expressions, inspired by the delicate artwork of artist Don Wood. Each doll features hand-painted clothing and accessories, creating a unique piece of collectible art. 

Unlike mass-produced dolls, Precious Moments dolls are individually crafted, with each one slightly different from the next. This attention to detail makes them highly collectible and valuable. 


### What sizes are available?

Precious Moments offers dolls in three primary sizes: tea cup size (10-12 inches), regular size (18-20 inches), and oversized size (28-30 inches). Each size includes its own display stand, showcasing the doll to its best advantage. 

Some special edition dolls may vary in size, so it's important to check the product description for specific details.

### What materials are used?

These dolls are made from high-quality polyresin, which provides durability while maintaining a smooth, sculpted appearance. The clothing and accessories use textured fabrics and detailed trims to match the doll's themed outfit. 

Each element, from the dolls to the stands, is designed to create a cohesive display that's both visually appealing and long-lasting. 


### What themes and occasions do they represent?

Precious Moments dolls come in countless themes, from traditional holiday scenes to modern, contemporary designs. Common themes include birthdays, weddings, and everyday moments. 

Every doll represents a specific occasion or feeling, making them ideal for personal gifts or collections. Each features a unique story or sentiment that captures a special moment in time.

### How much do they typically cost?

The cost of Precious Moments dolls varies significantly depending on the size, rarity, and condition. Regular-sized dolls typically range from $150 to $300, while larger, more unique pieces can reach several thousand dollars. 

Rare or limited edition dolls can be significantly more expensive, with some selling for tens of thousands of dollars in the collector's market. 


### How should they be cared for?

To maintain their condition, Precious Moments dolls should be kept away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. The display stands are specifically designed to support the weight and balance of the dolls. 

If the doll's clothing becomes soiled, it can be carefully removed and spot-cleaned using a soft cloth and mild soap solution. However, it's important not to immerse the doll in water or use harsh chemicals, as this can damage the polyresin material.
