You'll need to use the included USB-C to Lightning cable to connect the keyboard to your iPad Air. The cable converts the USB-C port's power output into a format compatible with the iPad's Lightning connection. 


### How do I set up the Magic Keyboard with my iPad Air?

After connecting the keyboard, your iPad will detect the new input device. No additional software installation is required on the iPad side. The keyboard automatically pairs and starts working once connected. 

If you're using the keyboard for the first time, charge it fully before use. The keyboard should maintain a charge sufficient for daily use without additional charging needed. 


### Is the Magic Keyboard compatible with all iPad Air models?

The Magic Keyboard requires an iPad Air with USB-C port, specifically models from 2019 and later. Previous iPad Air models with Lightning ports are not compatible. 

Ensure your iPad Air's operating system is up to date to avoid compatibility issues when using the Magic Keyboard. 


### What's the battery life of the Magic Keyboard?

The battery provides up to 12 hours of typing on a single charge, based on regular use. The keyboard charges via USB-C and takes approximately 3 hours to go from 0% to 100%. 

The keyboard includes a status indicator light that shows battery level: solid blue (80%+), flashing blue (20-80%), and no light (0-20%). 


### Can I customize the Magic Keyboard's features?
