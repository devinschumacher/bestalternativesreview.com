## FAQ


### What is a full XL mattress?

A full XL mattress combines the standard full mattress size with extended length options. It typically measures 60 inches wide by 84-96 inches long, offering more surface area than a standard full-size mattress. 

This size is ideal for couples or individuals who need extra space while sleeping. The longer dimensions help prevent the partner effect, where one person's movement affects their sleeping partner. 


### What's the main difference between a full XL and a standard full mattress?

The primary distinction is in length. While both sizes are 60 inches wide, full XL mattresses range from 84 to 96 inches long, compared to standard full-size mattresses that are typically 75 inches long. 

The extra length in full XL mattresses reduces the likelihood of sleep disturbances caused by partner movement and provides more generous legroom. 


### Which mattress foundation works best with a full XL mattress?

A full XL mattress requires a specific foundation designed to support its unique dimensions. Opt for a platform bed or adjustable base that matches the mattress's length to ensure proper support and alignment. 

Investing in a premium foundation extends the mattress's lifespan and enhances overall comfort by preventing sagging and maintaining proper sleep surface alignment. 


### Can you fit a full XL mattress in standard bed frames?

Standard full-size bed frames (75 inches long) are too short to support a full XL mattress safely. Attempting to fit one may compromise structural integrity and reduce the mattress's lifespan. 

Always measure your available space and verify compatibility with your intended bed frame before purchasing a full XL mattress. 


### What are the most critical features to consider when choosing a full XL mattress?

Key factors include pressure-relieving materials, edge support, and temperature regulation. Full XL mattresses often require specialized construction techniques to maintain consistent comfort across their extended length. 

Look for models with reinforced borders, progressive support layers, and breathable fabrics to optimize sleep comfort for larger sleepers or couples. 


### How should I measure my room for a full XL mattress?

When measuring your room, prioritize available floor space rather than existing furniture. Full XL mattresses are 96 inches long, so ensure you have at least a 10-foot clearance for safe movement and bed positioning. 

Factor in doorways, windowsills, and other obstructions to confirm total room dimensions can accommodate the mattress and its foundation.
