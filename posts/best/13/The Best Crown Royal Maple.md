### How is Crown Royal Maple different from classic Crown Royal?

### What makes Canadian whisky, like Crown Royal, distinct?

Canadian whisky is distinct from American bourbon or Scotch whisky due to its unique aging process. It's typically aged in charred oak barrels but must be diluted to 40% alcohol by volume before bottling. 

This practice gives Canadian whisky its distinctive smoothness while maintaining clarity and brightness in its flavor profile. 


### How long should I let the bottle age before drinking?
