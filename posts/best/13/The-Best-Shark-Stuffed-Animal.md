### Material Quality and Construction

Premium plush materials and durable stitching indicate higher quality. Look for stuffed animals made from washable fabrics that can withstand regular cuddling and cleaning. The seams should be reinforced, especially around the mouth and belly areas, to prevent unraveling. 

Some high-end models incorporate additional elements like weighted bases, articulated fins, or musical functions. These features can enhance play value but may affect the overall weight and balance of the toy. 


### Accuracy and Artistry

Shark stuffed animals vary in their anatomical accuracy. For educational value, choose a model that closely represents real shark characteristics. Detailed sculpting of fins, tails, and body patterns adds to the authenticity. 

Artistic interpretation often results in more expressive and engaging designs. Consider personal preference between scientifically accurate representations and more stylized, cartoonish versions.
