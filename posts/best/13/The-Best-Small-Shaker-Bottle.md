## Buyer's Guide

Small shaker bottles are versatile kitchen tools designed for mixing and storing ingredients. Whether you're preparing cocktails, homemade dressings, or specialty sauces, these bottles offer convenience and organization. Understanding their key features will help you choose the right one for your culinary needs. 


### Capacity and Size
