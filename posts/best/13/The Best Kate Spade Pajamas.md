## Buyer's Guide

Kate Spade's pajama collection combines fashionable design with comfortable sleepwear. Our guide highlights essential features to help you choose the perfect pair. 


### Quality Fabrics

Opt for pajamas made from soft, breathable materials like cotton or bamboo. The fabric should be dense enough to avoid see-through but gentle on your skin. 


### Comfort Features
