### How do you maintain a fencing staple gun?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the firing mechanism after each use to remove accumulated debris and checking the nail feed system for proper alignment. The staple gun's frame and trigger should be lubricated periodically with a lightweight grease to reduce wear and ensure smooth operation.
