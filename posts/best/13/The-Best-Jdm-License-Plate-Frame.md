## FAQ


### What are JDM license plate frames?

JDM stands for Japan Domestic Market, which refers to automotive parts specifically designed for the Japanese market. JDM license plate frames are custom-designed additions that sit around the license plate, offering aesthetic enhancements and additional features. 

These frames often incorporate elements like LED lighting, sleek black designs, or protective coatings to make the license plate more visible and stylish in various weather conditions. 


### What makes JDM frames different from regular license plate frames?

Unlike standard frames, JDM designs typically feature premium materials like high-quality plastic or metal construction. They also often include distinctive Japanese-inspired styling elements, such as illuminated logos or intricate patterns. 

These frames frequently come with additional functionality, like integrated license plate lock systems or anti-theft mechanisms that are common in Japanese automotive accessories. 


### Are JDM license plate frames legal to install?

The legality of JDM license plate frames varies by jurisdiction. While many states in the U. S. allow them as long as they don't obscure the license plate information, it's important to check local regulations before installation. 

Some frames may require specific mounting patterns or have height restrictions. Always verify compliance with local traffic laws to ensure safe and legal installation. 


### What maintenance requirements do JDM frames have?

Most JDM license plate frames are made from durable materials that require minimal maintenance. Regular cleaning with water and mild soap is usually sufficient to keep them looking new. 

If your frame includes LED features, ensure the batteries are properly sealed and replace them according to the manufacturer's instructions to maintain proper functionality. 


### How do I clean and maintain my JDM frame?

For daily cleaning, use a soft cloth or brush to remove dirt and debris. Avoid abrasive materials that might scratch the surface of your frame. 

If the frame has specific care instructions, such as LED cleaning, follow the manufacturer's guidelines to prevent damage and ensure optimal performance. 


### What warranties are typically offered with JDM frames?

Most reputable JDM frame manufacturers provide warranties ranging from 1 year to lifetime guarantees, particularly for their premium models. Check the warranty terms before purchasing to understand coverage details. 

Warranties typically cover manufacturing defects and include replacements for damaged or non-functioning parts. Some manufacturers also offer extended warranties for an additional fee.
