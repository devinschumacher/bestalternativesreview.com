### What is a Honeywell Warm Mist Humidifier?

A Honeywell warm mist humidifier adds moisture to the air through vaporization, creating warm mist that helps relieve dryness and improve respiratory comfort. The humidifier features specialized heating elements that safely warm the water before it's dispersed as mist. 

These units typically include automatic shut-off and humidity indicators to prevent overuse and ensure optimal performance. The warm mist also carries beneficial properties like natural room fresheners and relief from minor cold symptoms. 


### How does a warm mist humidifier differ from other types?

Unlike cool mist humidifiers that create water vapor at room temperature, warm mist humidifiers use heating elements to boil water before producing steam. This process eliminates bacteria and minerals and prevents the spread of microorganisms through the air. 

The warm mist can help with cold symptoms and soothe irritated airways, while cold mist humidifiers may need regular cleaning to prevent mold and bacteria growth.
