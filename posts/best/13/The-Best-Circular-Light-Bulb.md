### Evaluating Base Compatibility

Circular bulbs come in various base types, including E26 (standard screw base) and PAR38 (candelabra screw base). Measure your fixture's base diameter and compare it to common bulb sizes. Consider the room's purpose; warm whites create cozy atmospheres, while cool whites enhance clarity and focus.

### What is a circular light bulb?

### What bulb shapes are considered circular?

Common circular bulb shapes include E26/E27, which are the most widely used standard screw bases in North America and Europe. Other circular shapes include bi-pin and mogul bases, often found in specialized fixtures. 

Decorative circular bulbs can range from simple domes to complex designs incorporating faceted glass or other materials, while retrofit bulbs maintain a circular shape while improving energy efficiency.
