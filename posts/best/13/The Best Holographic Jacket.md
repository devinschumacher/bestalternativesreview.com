### What is a holographic jacket?

A holographic jacket features special materials or technology that create optical illusions, making it appear as though the jacket has depth and imagery that shifts when viewed from different angles. The effect can range from simple patterns to complex scenes. 

Holographic jackets often combine reflective materials with printed patterns, or use 3D printing techniques to create the illusion of depth and movement. The technology can add a unique visual element to the jacket's design. 


### How does a holographic jacket work?

The materials or technology used in holographic jackets create an optical illusion through reflections and refractions. These effects are often achieved by layering reflective materials with specific patterns or using specialized printing processes that manipulate light. 

When light hits the jacket at different angles, the reflective surfaces and patterns interact with the viewer's perspective to create the illusion of depth and movement. This creates the distinctive holographic effect that changes as you move around the jacket.
