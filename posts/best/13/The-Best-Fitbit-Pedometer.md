## Buyer's Guide

A Fitbit pedometer is a wearable device that tracks your steps, helping you monitor your daily activity level. These devices use advanced sensors to count your steps accurately and often come with additional health and fitness features. Here's what to consider when choosing the right Fitbit pedometer for you.
