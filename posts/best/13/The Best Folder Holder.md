### What materials are folder holders made from?

Folder holders are typically made from durable materials like plastic, metal, or wood. Plastic holders are lightweight and come in a wide range of colors and designs. Metal holders, often constructed from aluminum or steel, provide a sturdy base and can support heavier loads. Wooden holders offer a classic, professional appearance and natural feel.

### What sizes are available for folder holders?

Folder holders come in various sizes, from compact 6-inch square bases to larger 12-inch square or rectangular designs. The height can range from 3 inches to over 12 inches depending on the style. 

Small holders are ideal for personal desks or clipped to notebooks, while larger ones can support multiple documents and even items like binders or notepads. The size selected depends on the specific organizational needs and available space. 


### How much weight can folder holders support?

The weight capacity of folder holders varies based on their material and design. Plastic and aluminum holders typically have a 2-5 pound capacity, while steel and heavy-duty plastic models can support up to 10-15 pounds. 

When selecting a folder holder, consider the weight of the documents you'll be storing. For lighter papers and single documents, basic holders suffice. For organizing thicker stacks or multiple documents, opt for models with higher weight capacity.
