## FAQ


### What sets lavender Crocs apart from regular Crocs?

Lavender Crocs incorporate a natural lavender scent into their footwear, combining the comfort and durability of Crocs' signature foam with a relaxing aroma. The lavender-infused material helps create a more pleasant and calming wearing experience. 

While the basic structure and Crocs' famous footbed remain the same, the addition of lavender enhances the overall sensory experience, making these Crocs particularly appealing for relaxation and sleepwear. 


### How do lavender Crocs compare to other scented footwear options?

Compared to traditional scented footwear, lavender Crocs offer a more subtle and natural odor, with the fragrance derived from actual lavender rather than artificial chemicals. This gives them a distinct advantage in terms of authenticity and lasting scent quality. 

The scent retention in lavender Crocs is generally longer-lasting than many synthetic-scented options, as the lavender extract is more effectively encapsulated in the foam material. 


### Can lavender Crocs be machine washed?

Yes, lavender Crocs are machine washable. To maintain the scent and integrity of the material, it's recommended to wash them either alone or with similar scented items to prevent the fragrance from bleeding onto other clothes. 

When washing, use a mild detergent and avoid bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the foam material and affect the longevity of the scent. 


### How long does the lavender scent last in Crocs?

The fragrance typically lasts for several washes, gradually diminishing over time. Proper care, including washing with similar scented items and using a mild detergent, can help extend the scent's duration. 

For best results, air drying the Crocs after washing can prevent water damage to the foam and maintain the material's integrity. 


### Are lavender Crocs suitable for all seasons?

The lightweight and breathable nature of Crocs makes them suitable for year-round wear. However, their primary appeal is during warmer months or when seeking a cooling footbed sensation. 

During colder seasons, they work well in indoor environments like homes and offices, but may be less appropriate for outdoor activities where warmth is needed. 


### How can I refresh the scent in my lavender Crocs?

To refresh the scent, wear the Crocs and allow natural body heat to reactivate the fragrance over time. Placing them in a plastic bag with actual lavender buds can also help restore the scent. 

Regular air drying and occasional baking soda treatments (not recommended for daily use) can help neutralize odor and maintain freshness between wears.
