The Marlin 336 comes with 1/2" scope mounts, compatible with most one-inch rings and bases. The system also features a dovetail mount that accepts Weaver-style bases, allowing for use with a wide variety of scopes including budget-friendly options and premium optics. 

These mounts enable quick scope change capabilities and secure mounting for both tactical and precision shooting applications. They are compatible with 1" scopes, which represents the most common scope size for the Marlin 336 firearm. 


### Can Marlin 336 scopes be mounted directly to the rifle?

The Marlin 336 scope mounts attach to the rifle's dovetail grooves in the receiver, eliminating the need for additional base hardware. This direct mounting option reduces setup time and provides a robust, three-point attachment system. 

While some optics may require additional adapter rings, the included mounts support both 1" and Weaver-style bases, making direct mounting practical for most scope configurations.

The Marlin 336 scope mounts are designed to fully retract when not in use, allowing the rifle to maintain its original iron sight configuration. This retraction feature ensures that the iron sights remain accessible without interference from the scope mounts.
