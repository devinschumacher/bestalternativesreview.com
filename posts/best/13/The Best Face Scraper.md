## Buyer's Guide

A face scraper, also known as a facial instrument or dermascraper, is a tool used for exfoliating the skin and removing surface-level impurities. These tools typically feature multiple blades or edges that can help in achieving a deeper cleanse compared to traditional exfoliation methods. When selecting a face scraper, several factors should be considered to ensure you choose a safe and effective tool for your skincare routine. 


### Blade Design and Material

The blades of a face scraper are crucial for its performance and safety. Look for tools with surgical-grade stainless steel blades, as they maintain their sharpness and resist rust. The blade design should have varying lengths and angles to accommodate different skin types and areas of the face. Each blade should be smooth and not contain sharp edges that could damage the skin. The handle should be secure and comfortable to grip, with an ergonomic design that prevents slippage during use. 


### Handle and Grip Design
