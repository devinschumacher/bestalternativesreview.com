There are several sighting mechanisms available, including flip-up sights, open sights, and aperture sights. Look for sights with multi-coated optics and high light transmission capabilities. The coating quality affects both daytime visibility and the ability to use the sight in low-light conditions. Higher transmission rates allow more ambient light to pass through, improving target visibility.
