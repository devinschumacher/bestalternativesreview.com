### Power and Pulsation Settings

The power and pulsation settings determine the intensity and effectiveness of the massage. Higher power levels can penetrate deeper muscle layers, while adjustable pulsation rates allow targeting specific discomfort levels or areas. Consider a model with at least three power settings and customizable pulsation options. 


### Massage Head Design

Massage heads with interchangeable attachments offer versatility for different massage techniques. Look for designs with smooth surfaces for general relaxation, smaller heads for targeted areas, and textured surfaces for deeper tissue work. The massage head should comfortably fit your palm and apply consistent pressure during use. 


### Battery Life and Power Source

Battery life varies significantly between models, with most lasting between 20-60 minutes per charge. If you plan to use the massager daily, opt for a model with longer battery life or consider one with a portable battery pack. Some models also offer USB charging, while others require standard wall adapters. 


### Additional Features

Common additional features include heat therapy, which enhances relaxation and muscle recovery, and water resistance for use in the shower. Certain models also incorporate Bluetooth connectivity for music playback or guided massage programs. Consider features that align with your specific goals and preferences.
