## Buyer's Guide

Find the perfect Nirvana poster that captures the essence of the band's iconic sound and rebellious spirit. Our guide helps you choose a high-quality print that stands the test of time. 


### Image Quality and Resolution

Choose posters with sharp details and rich color saturation. Look for prints with a resolution of at least 300 DPI to ensure clarity, especially for larger sizes. Check if the image has any visible artifacts or lost details before purchasing. 


### Print Materials and Durability

Invest in posters printed on professional-grade materials like matte or lustre paper for lasting quality. For outdoor displays or high-traffic areas, consider canvas or metal prints which resist fading and scuffing. The backing material should match your intended display location and maintenance requirements. 


### Frame and Mounting Options

While some may prefer unframed posters for wall-mounting, consider the overall presentation. Frames protect the print from dust and UV damage, while adding a professional finish. Ensure the frame material matches your room's decor and offers proper mounting options for your space. 


### Size and Scaling

Measure your space before selecting a poster size. Common frame dimensions range from standard 8x10" to large gallery wrap canvas up to 40x60". Consider how the poster's proportions will look in your room, and whether you prefer portrait or landscape orientation.
