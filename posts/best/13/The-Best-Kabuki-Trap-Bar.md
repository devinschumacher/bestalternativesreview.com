## Buyer's Guide

A kabuki trap bar is a versatile fitness tool that combines elements of a traditional trap bar and a round barbell. This unique design offers several advantages for strength training and functional exercises. Understanding its features and capabilities will help you choose the right bar for your fitness goals. 


### Design Features

The kabuki bar's most distinctive feature is its V-shaped profile, which provides a wider grip than traditional trap bars. This design reduces wrist stress and increases the range of motion for certain exercises. The bar's diameter typically ranges from 1.125 to 1.25 inches, offering a balance between comfort and durability.
