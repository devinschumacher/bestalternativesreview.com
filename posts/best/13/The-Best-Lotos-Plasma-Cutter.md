## FAQ


### What is a plasma cutter?

A plasma cutter is a specialized tool that uses a plasma arc to cut through metal. This high-temperature plasma stream, created by ionizing air or gas, melts and blows away metal, allowing for precise cuts. 

Plasma cutters are highly versatile, capable of cutting through materials ranging from thin sheet metal to thick steel plates. They offer advantages over traditional cutting methods, including cleaner cuts and faster processing times. 


### What are the benefits of using a Lotos plasma cutter?

Lotos plasma cutters offer reliable performance with minimal maintenance requirements. The compact design makes them portable, suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. The devices typically feature durable construction to handle heavy-duty cutting tasks. 

These cutters provide consistent cut quality with minimal heat-affected zones, preserving the mechanical properties of the metal. The integrated safety features help prevent accidents during operation, making them a practical choice for professional and DIY users alike. 


### What types of materials can a Lotos plasma cutter cut?

Lotos plasma cutters excel at cutting through carbon steel, stainless steel, aluminum, and copper. The cut quality and efficiency vary slightly between these materials, with steel typically providing the best results. 

While these cutters can handle most common metals, thicker materials may require additional power or specialized tips for optimal performance. Always consult the manufacturer's specifications for recommended material thickness ranges. 


### How does a plasma cutter work?

A plasma cutter works by accelerating and ionizing a gas (usually compressed air or pure oxygen) through a narrow nozzle to create a plasma arc. The high-temperature plasma stream melts the metal at the cutting point, while an airflow removes the molten metal as a molten pool. 

The cutter controls the arc temperature and gas flow through precise electrical and mechanical systems. The operator guides the cutting nozzle along the material to create the desired cut line. 


### What maintenance is required for a Lotos plasma cutter?

Regular maintenance includes checking and cleaning the cutting nozzle and electrode, inspecting gas flow regulators, and verifying the integrity of the electrical connections. These components typically need replacement every 100-200 hours of use. 

The cutter should also be stored in a dry environment with regular battery maintenance if applicable. Regular servicing by a professional can extend the tool's life and ensure optimal performance. 


### What safety precautions should be taken when using a Lotos plasma cutter?

Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment, including safety glasses with side shields, gloves, and flame-resistant clothing. Ensure the work area is well-ventilated and free from flammable materials. 

Check the gas connections for leaks before operation, and keep the nozzle away from combustible materials during cutting. Follow manufacturer guidelines for proper use and storage to prevent accidents and equipment damage.
