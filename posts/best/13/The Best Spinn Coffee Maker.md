## FAQ


### What is a Spinn coffee maker?

The Spinn coffee maker is an innovative brewing system that uses pressure to extract coffee directly from coffee beans. Unlike traditional drip or pressure-based coffee machines, the Spinn brews coffee similarly to how it's done in professional settings, creating a rich, complex flavor profile. 

The device consists of a brewing chamber where ground coffee is placed, and a pressure vessel that heats the water and forces it through the grounds under high pressure. This extraction process develops a concentrated coffee base that can be used to make various coffee beverages. 


### How does the brewing process work?

The Spinn brews coffee through a process called high-pressure extraction. Ground coffee is placed in the brewing chamber, and water is heated in the pressure vessel. The system then applies pressure to force the hot water through the grounds, extracting flavors and oils. 

The brewing cycle typically takes around 4-6 minutes. The resulting coffee, which is more concentrated than brewed coffee, can be diluted with hot or cold water to create different coffee beverages, including drip coffee, cappuccino, and espresso. 


### What types of coffee can be made with the Spinn?

The Spinn is versatile and can be used to make a wide range of coffee beverages. Users can brew standard drip coffee, adjust settings for espresso-like consistency, or create cold brew by using the appropriate water-to-coffee ratio and brewing time. 

The system works with both whole bean and pre-ground coffee. For the best results, it's recommended to use freshly roasted whole beans and grind them just before brewing to maintain optimal coffee freshness and flavor. 


### What are the brewing settings and options?

The Spinn offers several brewing options to suit different preferences. Users can adjust the brewing time between 4-6 minutes for different coffee strengths. The system allows for manual adjustment of water temperature and pressure to fine-tune the brewing process. 

Additional features include the ability to brew coffee that requires short or extended extraction times, making it suitable for various coffee types and brewing methods. 


### How easy is the Spinn to clean and maintain?

Cleaning the Spinn coffee maker is straightforward. After each use, the brewing chamber and pressure vessel can be disassembled and rinsed with water. The device includes a built-in filter basket that needs regular cleaning to prevent any residue buildup. 

For more thorough maintenance, users should regularly check and clean the heating elements and pressure seals to ensure optimal performance and longevity of the equipment. 


### What is the recommended workflow for brewing?

To brew coffee with the Spinn, start by grinding your coffee beans to the appropriate consistency. Place the grounds in the brewing chamber and secure the pressure vessel. Add cold water to the vessel, which will automatically heat the water during the brewing cycle. 

The system will automatically apply pressure to extract the coffee. Once brewing is complete, release the pressure by opening the safety valve, and then carefully lower the pressure vessel to remove it. The brewed coffee can be used immediately or stored in a thermal carafe provided with the system.
