### What is bleeding tissue paper?

Bleeding tissue paper is a specialized type of toilet paper that contains activated carbon granules or other similar materials. These products are designed to help reduce stains and odors from bodily fluids, making them particularly useful for personal hygiene after certain medical procedures or conditions. 

The activated carbon in these tissues works by adsorbing (attracting and holding) moisture and odors. This process helps to reduce the wetness and unpleasant smells that can result from incontinence or similar issues, providing a more comfortable and hygienic cleaning experience. 


### How does bleeding tissue paper work?

The activated carbon granules or other absorbent materials in bleeding tissue paper work through a process called adsorption. When the tissue comes into contact with bodily fluids, the activated carbon particles draw in and hold the moisture, helping to prevent penetration of the stain into clothing or bedding. 

At the same time, the activated carbon helps to neutralize odors by trapping molecules responsible for unpleasant smells. This dual action of moisture absorption and odor neutralization makes bleeding tissue paper effective at keeping clothing and living spaces fresher and cleaner.
