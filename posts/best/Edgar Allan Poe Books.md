### Who is Edgar Allan Poe?

Edgar Allan Poe was an American literary figure who was born on January 19, 1809, in Boston, Massachusetts. He is considered one of the greatest American writers of short stories and poetry. Poe died on October 7, 1849, in Baltimore, Maryland. Although his life was short, his impact on American literature has been significant and enduring.
