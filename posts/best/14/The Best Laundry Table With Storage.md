### What materials are commonly used for laundry tables with storage?

MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard), particleboard, and engineered wood are popular choices for their affordability and durability. Many manufacturers also offer options with solid wood, melamine, or laminate finishes. 

Stainless steel and aluminum frames provide a modern look and resist moisture damage. Look for quality construction with visible bracing and solid wood or metal corners for longevity. 


### How much storage capacity should I expect?

A standard laundry table with storage typically offers between 15 to 30 cubic feet of storage space. This includes open shelves, closed storage compartments, and extra space for folding clothes. 

Consider units with at least 20% of their total space dedicated to adjustable shelving to accommodate different-sized items. Look for roll-out baskets, dual-door cabinets, and deep shelves for efficient use of vertical space.

Most laundry tables with storage are designed to support up to 150 lbs (68 kg) per shelf or drawer. However, this weight capacity varies by manufacturer and model design.
