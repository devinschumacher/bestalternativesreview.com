## FAQ


### What is scar wax?

Scar wax is a specialized product designed to help soften and reduce the appearance of scars. It typically consists of a mixture of natural ingredients such as beeswax, cocoa butter, and other botanical oils that work together to improve skin texture and promote healing. 

These products are applied directly to the scar area and work through a combination of moisturizing, soothing, and gentle exfoliating properties. Regular use can help reduce scar redness, improve skin flexibility, and make scars less noticeable. 


### What are the benefits of using scar wax?

Regular use of scar wax can lead to several improvements in scar appearance and skin health. It helps soften scar tissue, reduce redness and inflammation, and improve the overall texture of the affected area.
