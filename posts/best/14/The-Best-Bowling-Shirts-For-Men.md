### How should I care for my bowling shirt?

Machine wash your shirt in cold water using a mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the moisture-wicking properties. 

Avoid hanging the shirt to dry, as this can stretch the fabric. Instead, lay it flat or tumble dry on low heat to maintain its shape. 


### What's the difference between casual and competition shirts?

Casual shirts focus on comfort and style with less attention to technical performance. They're suitable for social games and everyday wear. 

Competition shirts prioritize moisture management, breathability, and precise fit. They're designed for extended game sessions and intense gameplay.
