The Optimized Face Technology (OS) creates a thinner face at the crown while maintaining strength in the toe and heel, maximizing ball speed. The club's versatile design incorporates open C-face technology, a wide face with a closed leading edge behind the face to adjust swing patterns. This helps neutralize slices and draw-like tendencies while maintaining the club's neutral moment of inertia (MOI).
