### How Long Do Bob Box Braids Last?

Bob Box Braids typically last between 4-6 weeks with proper care. Regular touch-ups every 1-2 weeks help maintain the style and prevent unraveling.

### What Hair Types Work Best with Bob Box Braids?

Most hair types can accommodate Bob Box Braids, but fine or thinning hair might need additional support. Curlier textures often produce more defined braid patterns. 

Very thick hair can create overly bulky braids, so a consultation with a stylist is essential to determine if this style is suitable.
