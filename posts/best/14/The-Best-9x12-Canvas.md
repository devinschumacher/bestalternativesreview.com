## FAQ


### What is 9x12 canvas?

9x12 canvas refers to a standard canvas size measuring 9 inches by 12 inches. This format is widely used for paintings, prints, and other visual artworks, providing a convenient medium for both professional artists and hobbyists. 

The canvas can be purchased in different thicknesses (measured in millimeters, like 1.5mm, 2.0mm, or 2.5mm), with the thicker options offering more depth and durability for finished pieces. 


### What types of coatings are available for 9x12 canvas?

Common coatings include gloss, matte, and semi-gloss finishes. Gloss provides the highest sheen and is ideal for photographic prints, while matte finishes reduce glare and are better for fine art reproductions. 

Semi-gloss is a popular choice that offers more protection than matte while maintaining some shine. The coating helps preserve colors and protects the canvas from scratches and UV damage. 


### What painting techniques work well on 9x12 canvas?

Small-scale brushwork and detailed work perform well on 9x12 canvas. Watercolor, acrylics, and gouache are popular medium choices due to their quick-drying properties and suitability for small formats. 

For mixed media projects, decoupage, collage, and intricate line work excel on this scale. The canvas provides enough surface area for technique exploration while maintaining manageable proportions. 


### How should 9x12 canvas be mounted?

Lightweight pieces can be framed in standard 9x12 frames. Acrylic protection is recommended for lasting clarity and UV protection, especially when displaying photographs or detailed paintings. 

Bulkier canvas pieces may need stretchers and backing for stability. Consider stretcher clips or hanger bars for easy display without additional framing. 


### What are the dimensions for canvas prints on 9x12 canvas?

Canvas prints maintain the same 9x12 dimensions as the original canvas base. Most printing services will automatically adjust resolution and image size to fit this standard format. 

Sizes may vary slightly depending on the specific print service or whether the print includes a border. Always check the service's specifications for final print dimensions. 


### How should I care for 9x12 canvas artwork?

Regular dusting with a soft brush or clean cloth maintains the artwork's appearance. For deeper cleaning, use a mild soap and water solution, avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage the coating. 

Avoid direct sunlight and extreme temperature changes, which can affect the canvas' integrity and color stability. Store the canvas in a cool, dry place when not on display.
