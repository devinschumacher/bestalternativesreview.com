The standard serving size for Carnivor protein powder is typically 25-30 grams, but dosage varies based on individual goals. For muscle recovery, use 20-30 grams post-workout. For mass gain, consider 1-1.5 grams per pound of body weight daily, adjusting based on progress and digestive tolerance.

Proper storage is crucial for maintaining protein powder quality. Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent degradation. Most powders should be refrigerated after opening to preserve freshness and prevent mold growth. Check expiration dates and avoid using past-their-prime products for optimal nutrition and safety.
