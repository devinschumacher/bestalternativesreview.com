### What are the different types of eucalyptus?

### How do you care for eucalyptus plants?

Eucalyptus plants require minimal care once established. They prefer well-draining soil and full sun, with some species tolerating partial shade. Water regularly during the first year to help them establish a deep root system. 

Mature trees typically need watering only during extended dry spells. Prune only when necessary to remove dead or diseased branches, as eucalyptus trees naturally shed their lower branches as they grow taller. 


### Are eucalyptus plants invasive?

Some eucalyptus species, particularly blue gum (Eucalyptus globulus), can become invasive in certain regions due to their rapid growth and ability to outcompete native vegetation. Plant in containers or in restricted areas to prevent spread. 

If you live in a colder climate, choose more cold-hardy species like the red ironbark (Eucalyptus sideroxylon) or mallee eucalyptus, which are less likely to behave invasively.
