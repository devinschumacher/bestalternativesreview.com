### What materials are used in Givenchy tote bags?

Givenchy's tote bags are typically crafted from high-quality leather or canvas materials, known for their durability and elegant finish. The leather versions come in various grades, from premium grain leather to smoother patent finishes, while canvas bags offer a more casual, textured look.

### How should I care for my Givenchy tote bag?

Maintaining your Givenchy tote bag requires regular care to ensure its longevity. For leather bags, wipe down the exterior with a soft cloth using a specialized leather cleaner, avoiding harsh chemicals that might damage the surface. Canvas bags are more resistant to water and can be cleaned with mild soap and water, allowing them to air dry completely before use. 

Store your Givenchy tote in a dust bag when not in use to protect it from dust and scratches. If the bag develops a noticeable crease, you can use a cotton cloth slightly dampened with water to help it reshape. Always handle the bag by the top or sides to avoid stretching the bottom corners, which can affect the structural integrity.
