### What are polarized sunglasses and why should I care?

Polarized sunglasses reduce glare from reflective surfaces like water, glass, and metal. This can significantly improve visibility and make outdoor activities more comfortable. 

By blocking horizontally polarized light, these sunglasses enhance contrast and definition, making them particularly useful for driving, fishing, and other outdoor pursuits. 


### How do I know if my sunglasses are polarized?

You can test polarized sunglasses by looking through them at a reflection from a shiny surface. If the reflection becomes noticeably dimmer when you tilt the glasses, they are polarized. 

Manufacturers typically label polarized sunglasses with a clear "Polarized" sticker or symbol on the frame or lens.
