Looking for the right high chair for your growing infant? The Oxo Tot Sprout high chair is a popular choice, but how do you know if it's the right fit for your family? This buyer's guide examines key features, safety considerations, and practical aspects to help you make an informed decision. 


### Comfort Features

Infants need a chair that's both comfortable and secure. Look for a chair with a spacious seat and padded backrest that can grow with your child. The seat should be wide enough to prevent tipping and have adjustable features like reclining positions and height adjustments.

### Safety Features

Safety is crucial when selecting a high chair. Ensure the chair has four-legged stability and a safety harness system with adjustable straps. The wheels should lock securely, and the tray should have a lock mechanism to prevent sliding off the seat. Check for BPA-free materials and easy-to-clean surfaces that can handle messy feedings. 


### Ergonomic Design

A well-designed high chair should be easy for parents to use while keeping infants comfortable. Consider chairs with footrests for older babies and space-efficient storage solutions for younger children. The chair's weight capacity should be appropriate for your child's age and size.

### Portability and Storage

When space is limited, a high chair that folds or disassembles is ideal. Look for models with compact storage solutions, such as under-bed bins or space-saving stands. The chair's weight and size when fully assembled should be manageable for daily use while storing.
