### What maintenance does a Goplus treadmill require?

Regular maintenance includes checking and replacing the belt every three to five years, keeping the machine clean, and applying lubrication to moving parts as recommended by the manufacturer. This maintenance schedule helps ensure optimal performance and extends the equipment's lifespan. 

The deck rollers, motor housing, and electronic components should be visually inspected monthly for wear or damage. Belts and parts should be cleaned weekly with a damp cloth to prevent powder buildup and maintain smooth operation. 


### How long does a Goplus treadmill belt last?

Treadmill belts typically last between 3,000 to 10,000 miles (4,800 to 16,100 km) with normal use, depending on factors like daily usage duration and weight capacity. To get the most out of your belt, maintain proper weight limits and regularly check for signs of wear such as cracks or thinning. 

Most manufacturers recommend replacing the belt every three to five years, even if it appears undamaged, to ensure safe and reliable operation. Replacement costs typically range from $70 to $200, depending on the specific model.
