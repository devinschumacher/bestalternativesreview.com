### Material Quality

The upper material determines how well the boot retains its shape and durability over time. The Alpha Force uses a blend of leather and synthetic materials, with waterproofing as a standard feature. Higher-quality boots will have tightly stitched seams and reinforced stitching at stress points like the toes and heel. 


### Outsole Design

The sole dictates how well the boot performs on various surfaces and how long it will last. The Alpha Force features a dual-density EVA midsole for cushioning and comfort. The outsole should provide appropriate traction for your work environment - look for patterns that match the surfaces you'll be working on most frequently.
