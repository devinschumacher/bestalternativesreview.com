## Buyer's Guide

A hurricane spin scrubber is a powerful cleaning tool that combines suction and scrubbing action to remove dirt and grime from floors. Understanding the key features and considerations will help you select the right model for your cleaning needs. 


### Power Source and Operating Requirements

These machines come in corded and cordless models. Corded units offer consistent power but require access to an electrical outlet. Lithium-ion battery-powered models provide freedom from cables but need regular charging. Consider your availability of outlets and required runtime when choosing a power source. 


### Brush Design and Cleaning Power

Standard brush rolls effective for most residential cleaning. Turbo brush rolls increase cleaning power for tough dirt. Some models feature digital controls adjusting brush speed and pressure. Evaluate the brush design based on your floor type and cleaning demands. 


### Suction Power and Filtration

Higher airflow rates improve dirt pickup efficiency. Look for machines with adjustable suction control. Effective filtration prevents re-depositing dust and particles. HEPA filters provide the best allergen removal. 


### Size, Weight, and Maneuverability

Machine size affects reach and maneuverability. Compact models are easier to store and operate in tight spaces. Weight distribution impacts ease of use. Consider your physical capabilities when selecting a model.
