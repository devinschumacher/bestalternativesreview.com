### Formulation Considerations

Formulas impact product absorption and results. Look for lightweight, non-greasy textures that distribute easily through hair without residue. Water-based formulas are generally preferable to oil-based ones, which can leave hair feeling weighed down. Consider whether your hair type requires a leave-in conditioner, mask, or specialized treatment product. 


### Application Techniques

Proper application maximizes product effectiveness. Start with clean, damp hair for better absorption and distribute product evenly from roots to tips. For treatments, leave products on for the recommended time (usually 20-30 minutes) before rinsing thoroughly. Follow up with a deep conditioning mask once a week to maintain results.

Realistic expectations vary based on hair type and severity of damage. Generally, noticeable improvements in texture and manageability occur within 4-6 weeks of consistent use. Regular use of protective styles and avoid heat styling to extend product benefits. Incorporating a balanced diet rich in proteins and essential nutrients supports overall hair health.
