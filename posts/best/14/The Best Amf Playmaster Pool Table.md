Standard Playmaster tables measure 9 feet (2.74 meters) in length, offering ample playing surface for competitive matches. Some models also come in a 7-foot (2.13 meters) version for smaller spaces. Consider the available floor space when selecting your table size. 


### Construction Quality and Materials

These tables feature a robust construction typically built with select hardwood frames. The playing surface uses high-quality slate backed with two layers of marine-grade plywood for durability. The choice of materials significantly impacts the table's longevity and play quality. 


### Game Features and Accessories

Basket options range from traditional wood to metal, with some models offering wire baskets for improved performance. The playing surface often includes white diamond spotting guides for precise shots. Consider additional features like ball set styles and cue storage options when evaluating accessories. 


### Maintenance Requirements

Regular maintenance includes periodic reapplication of slate backing material, cleaning the playing surface with appropriate products, and checking the felt condition. Understanding the basic care requirements ensures your table maintains its playing quality over time.
