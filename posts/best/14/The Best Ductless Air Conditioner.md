## FAQ

### What is a ductless air conditioner?

A ductless air conditioner, also known as a mini-split system, is an efficient cooling solution that doesn't require traditional ductwork. It consists of an outdoor unit and one or more indoor units connected by thin tubing. 

Key advantages include flexible placement, minimal installation, and precise temperature control. 


### How energy-efficient are ductless air conditioners?

Modern ductless systems are highly efficient, typically achieving ENERGY STAR ratings. They use variable-speed compressors that adjust to maintain optimal indoor temperature, reducing energy consumption. 

Installation and maintenance costs are generally lower compared to traditional systems, making them cost-effective in the long term. 

![Ductless-Air-Conditioner-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Ductless-Air-Conditioner-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What maintenance do ductless air conditioners require?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the indoor unit's filter once a month during operation and checking outdoor unit components for debris twice yearly. 

Professional servicing every 1-2 years ensures optimal performance and prolongs system lifespan. 


### How quiet are ductless air conditioners?

Most models operate between 20-30 dB, making them very quiet. The outdoor unit runs slightly louder, typically between 45-60 dB, which is similar to a refrigerator. 

Advancements in technology have significantly reduced noise levels in recent years, with many units featuring near-silent operation in whisper mode. 

![Ductless-Air-Conditioner-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Ductless-Air-Conditioner-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What installation requirements are there for ductless air conditioners?

Indoor units need electrical access and a nearby drain for condensate removal. The outdoor unit requires space for proper ventilation and clear access for maintenance. 

Installation typically requires only 1-2 inches of ceiling space and can be done in newly constructed or existing buildings without extensive ductwork. 


### Are ductless air conditioners suitable for all climates?

While most models can handle outdoor temperatures down to -15° to -20°C, extreme cold may affect performance. Consult the manufacturer's specifications for your local climate. 

For colder regions, consider systems with heating capabilities or supplementary heating solutions.
