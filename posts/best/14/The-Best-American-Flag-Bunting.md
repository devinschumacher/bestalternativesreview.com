## FAQ


### What is American flag bunting?

American flag bunting is a festive patriotic decoration consisting of small flags or flag elements sewn onto a long strip of material. These bunting pieces create elegant drapes or wall hangings that add a touch of American spirit to any celebration or event. 

The flags vary in size, from tiny 1-inch elements to larger 6-inch versions, allowing you to choose the right scale for your space. The material is typically made from durable polyester that holds up well to outdoor conditions. 


### How long are American flag bunting pieces?

Most American flag bunting pieces range from 2 to 3 feet in length. This standard size works well for hanging across doors, creating party archways, or draping over tabletops. 

For longer decorations, you can find bunting pieces that measure 6 to 12 feet, perfect for spanning room corners or creating outdoor canopies. 


### Are American flag bunting pieces washable?

Yes, American flag bunting pieces are machine washable. Most manufacturers recommend washing them in mild detergent with similar colors to prevent bleeding and maintain their vibrant appearance. 

After washing, these bunting pieces air dry quickly, often taking just a few hours to regain their shape. For best results, consider using a clothespin to hang the bunting while drying to maintain its straight lines. 


### How should I care for American flag bunting?

American flag bunting requires minimal care. Regularly dust the pieces with a soft brush or vacuum attachment to remove dirt and debris. Store them in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent moisture damage and color fading. 

If you notice any fraying edges, hand-stitch them to secure the fabric. For deeper cleaning, you can spot treat any stained areas with a mild bleach solution, but be cautious with the flags to avoid discoloration. 


### What are the sizing options for American flag bunting?

Most American flag bunting comes in two standard sizes: 1-inch elements and 6-inch elements. The 1-inch versions are ideal for indoor celebrations or fine-detail displays, while the 6-inch pieces work well for both indoor and outdoor use. 

Specialty sizes include 3-inch elements for closer displays and 12-inch flags for impactful outdoor presentations. The length of the bunting pieces typically ranges from 2 to 12 feet, allowing you to create various visual effects depending on your space. 


### What occasions are American flag bunting pieces appropriate for?

American flag bunting is perfect for July 4th celebrations, Veterans Day events, and other patriotic gatherings. It adds an elegant touch to backyard barbecues, indoor parties, and classroom decorations. 

The bunting works well for both small gatherings and large events, from personal celebrations to public displays. You can use it to decorate living rooms, outdoor patios, and even business entrances during holiday seasons.
