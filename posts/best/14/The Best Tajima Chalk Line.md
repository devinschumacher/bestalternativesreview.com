### How does a Tajima Chalk Line work?

When activated, the trigger releases water through a narrow nozzle. The water flow creates pressure that forces chalk powder from the container to exit as a fine line. As the line dries, it marks the surface with visible chalk. 

Adjusting the water flow through the trigger controls the line's thickness. A slower flow produces a thinner line, while a faster flow creates a wider mark. 


### What surfaces can a Tajima Chalk Line mark?
