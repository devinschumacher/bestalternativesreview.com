## FAQ


### What is a nugget ice maker?

A nugget ice maker creates small, cube-like ice pieces that fit perfectly in drinks while maintaining their integrity. These compact ice cubes melt slower than traditional cubes and are ideal for mixed drinks and cocktails. 

Unlike flake ice makers, which produce snow-like ice, nugget ice makers combine the convenience of automatic ice production with the superior quality of cube ice. 


### How much electricity does a nugget ice maker consume?

On average, a nugget ice maker uses 12-15 kWh of electricity per month when producing ice daily. This consumption varies based on the model's efficiency and local climate conditions. 

These units typically have an energy efficiency rating of around 2.0 kWh per 2 lbs of ice, making them more power-efficient than many traditional ice makers. 


### How much water does a nugget ice maker use?

A standard nugget ice maker requires approximately 1 gallon of water to produce 2 lbs of ice. This equates to 12-15 gallons of water usage per day for a typical ice production cycle. 

Water consumption may vary based on the specific model's settings and ice production frequency. Most units include water recycling systems to minimize overall water usage. 


### What maintenance is required for a nugget ice maker?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the evaporator plate and ice bin monthly, checking the water filter every 3-6 months, and emptying the ice bin daily to prevent mold and bacteria growth. 

Additional maintenance tasks may include descaling the unit every 3-6 months using a mixture of water and white vinegar to remove mineral buildup. 


### What is the typical lifespan of a nugget ice maker?

The average nugget ice maker lasts between 8-12 years with proper maintenance. Key factors affecting longevity include regular cleaning, proper installation, and adherence to manufacturer's guidelines. 

Most models come with a warranty ranging from 1-3 years, covering parts and labor for manufacturing defects. Extended warranties are available for an additional cost. 


### How much space does a nugget ice maker require?

Standard nugget ice makers measure approximately 18 inches wide, 23 inches deep, and 35 inches tall, requiring a dedicated space in the kitchen or bar area. 

The unit needs at least 1.5 inches of clearance on all sides for proper air circulation, and a nearby water source for filling the unit's reservoir.
