### How do I remove a stuck or difficult-to-remove cork?

If a cork is stuck or hard to remove, try using a cork remover tool or a corkscrew with a spiral or thin blade. These tools are specifically designed to grip and extract stubborn corks without causing damage. If the cork still proves challenging to remove, you can immerse the bottle in warm water for a few minutes to loosen the cork or freeze it with an ice cube to shrink it slightly, making it easier to remove.

If all these methods fail, you can use a hammer and a nail to gently tap the cork out of the bottle. Be sure to hold the bottle securely and protect yourself from potential splinters or shards of cork by wearing an apron or gloves. After the cork is removed, use a small brush or damp cloth to clean the mouth of the bottle.
