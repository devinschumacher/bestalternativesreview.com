### Are there any special care instructions for ceramic and porcelain dinner plates?

Ceramic and porcelain dinner plates are generally durable and withstand daily use. However, they require special care to maintain their beauty and ensure longevity. Always hand wash your ceramic and porcelain dinner plates rather than scrubbing them in a dishwasher, as overly rough handling can cause microscopic stress fractures and lead to cracks over time. You can use a soft sponge or cloth with warm, soapy water to gently clean your plates. Avoid soaking your dinnerware at any length, as this can weaken the ceramic glaze and deteriorate the paint or finish on the outer edges. 

Another essential consideration is avoiding sudden temperature changes when using ceramic and porcelain dinnerware. Never subject your plates to direct heat without first testing them for even heating. If you plan to place hot food directly on the plates, it is best to slowly heat them in a low-temperature oven to avoid damaging their surface.
