A smart bulb is an energy-efficient LED light bulb connected to the internet through Wi-Fi or Bluetooth. Smart bulbs can also be integrated with popular home automation systems, like Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant, for a more seamless experience. They offer convenience, energy savings, and enhanced home security features that make them a popular addition to any smart home setup. Some top brands include Philips Hue, Ikea, and Sylvania.

A smart thermostat is a Wi-Fi enabled programmable thermostat that allows users to remotely control their home's heating and cooling system via a smartphone app or through integration with a smart home assistant like Amazon Alexa or Google Assistant. Smart thermostats deliver multiple benefits, ranging from energy savings to added convenience. They adapt to your family's schedule and preferences, optimizing energy use, and reducing energy bills.
