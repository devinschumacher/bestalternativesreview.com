### What is Adidas Boost?

Adidas Boost is a revolutionary cushioning technology that provides excellent energy return and comfort. It is made up of millions of tiny beads that are compressed when you walk, run, or jump, and then bounce back, returning energy to your foot and reducing fatigue. The Boost technology has been incorporated into various Adidas shoes, including running, training, and lifestyle models.
