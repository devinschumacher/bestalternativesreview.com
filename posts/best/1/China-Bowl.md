### How do I clean a China bowl properly?

To clean a China bowl properly, you should avoid using abrasive cleaning materials or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the delicate glazes and decoration on the bowl. Instead, fill the bowl with warm water and a mild dish soap, then gently agitate it to release the food residue. You can use a soft sponge or cloth to gently scrub the bowl if needed. Afterward, rinse the bowl thoroughly with clean water and let it air dry to prevent water stains and spots. 

If your China bowl has intricate designs or embellishments, it is best to consult the manufacturer's guidelines or a professional for cleaning and maintenance. It is also advisable to avoid stacking or stacking too many bowls on top of each other, as this can lead to scratches and damage to the delicate surface.
