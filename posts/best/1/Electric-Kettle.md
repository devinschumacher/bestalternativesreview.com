### What is boil-dry protection, and why is it beneficial?

Boil-dry protection is a safety feature found in many electric kettles. When a kettle is turned on, it starts heating the water within it. If the water level drops due to evaporation or other factors, the kettle may encounter dryness. Boil-dry protection ensures that the kettle automatically shuts off when it detects low water levels to avoid overheating or damage to the kettle. 

This benefit becomes particularly important in cases where you forget to refill the kettle, leaving the water to evaporate completely. Without boil-dry protection, the kettle could become damaged or pose a fire risk, as the excess heat would have no liquid to dissipate into. By including this feature, manufacturers aim to enhance the safety and longevity of their electric kettles.
