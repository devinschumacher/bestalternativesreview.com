## Buyer's Guide

If you're in the market for an EOS wallet, there are several factors to consider before making your purchase. This buyer's guide will provide you with essential information to help you make an informed decision. 


### What is an EOS Wallet?

An EOS wallet is a digital wallet specifically designed to store, manage, and transfer EOS tokens. EOS, also known as EOS. IO, is a blockchain-based platform that supports decentralized applications (dApps). In order to interact with dApps and manage your EOS holdings, you'll need a suitable wallet. There are several types of EOS wallets available, including desktop, mobile, web, and hardware wallets.
