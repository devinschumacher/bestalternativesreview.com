### What are 90s Mom Jeans?

90s Mom Jeans refer to the high-waisted, fitted, and often denim pants that were popular among women in the 1990s. They typically feature a button-fly, pleated front, and a boot-cut or mid-calf length. These jeans have since made a comeback in popular fashion trends, offering a classic and versatile option for contemporary style. 

The 90s Mom Jeans trend was popularized by many celebrities and fashion influencers, including Britney Spears and Jennifer Lopez. Today, they continue to be a style staple for those seeking a vintage-inspired look or a throwback to the iconic era of the '90s. As a result, various brands have replicated or recreated this fashion statement, offering a diverse range of styles, washes, and fabrics for consumers to choose from.
