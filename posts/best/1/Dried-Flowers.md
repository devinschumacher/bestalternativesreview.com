## FAQ


### What are dried flowers?

Dried flowers are flowers that have been dried, often by natural means like sun-drying or with assistance of a dehydrator. They can be used in various home decorations, crafts, and as a natural alternative to synthetic fragrances in aromatherapy. Dried flowers are available in a wide range of colors, shapes, and sizes, making them versatile for different purposes. 

They can be purchased pre-dried or bought fresh and then dried by the user. Dried flowers are an eco-friendly option, as they do not require any water or electricity for maintenance after drying, unlike live flowers. This makes them a popular choice for people who desire a long-lasting, low-maintenance alternative to fresh flowers. 


### What are some common uses for dried flowers?

Dried flowers have numerous applications, both for their beauty and fragrance. Some common uses include: home decorations like wreaths, arrangements, garlands, and table centerpieces; as a dried bouquet for personal enjoyment or as a thoughtful gift; and as craft materials for projects like making stationary, jewelry, or even clothes. Additionally, dried flowers can be incorporated into aromatherapy for their calming scents and potential therapeutic benefits. 

Some dried flowers are also edible, such as rose petals and calendula, which are used to add natural flavors or color to culinary dishes, teas, and beverages. These edible dried flowers can be found in specialty stores or online, often packaged in a way that preserves their freshness and maintains their attractive appearance. 


### What is the difference between fresh and dried flowers?

Fresh flowers require regular watering, care, and maintenance to maintain their appearance and fragrance. On the other hand, dried flowers have already undergone the process of drying, which means they no longer need water and can last for an extended period with proper storage. This makes dried flowers a lower-maintenance option than fresh flowers. 

There is also a difference in appearance between fresh and dried flowers. Fresh flowers tend to have a soft, supple texture and vibrant colors. When dried, flowers lose some of their delicate texture and become more rigid. Colors may also fade slightly over time. However, dried flowers can maintain their colors and appearance for months or even years, depending on the storage method. 


### How should I store dried flowers to maintain their quality?

To ensure that dried flowers maintain their quality, they should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. This will help prevent color fading and maintain their fresh appearance. If possible, use airtight containers or zipper bags to protect them from dust and moisture, which can also affect their quality. 

Avoid storing dried flowers in areas with strong odors, as they can absorb these scents. It is also essential to keep them away from heat sources, as excessive heat can cause them to discolor or lose fragrance. With proper storage, dried flowers can last for a long time, providing enjoyment and natural beauty to your home decorations and craft projects. 


### What are some popular varieties of dried flowers?

There are numerous varieties of dried flowers available, each with its unique color, shape, and fragrance. Some popular dried flowers include: roses, which come in various colors and are often used for their romantic fragrance; lavender, known for its soothing scent and calming properties; marigolds, available in various shades of yellow, orange, and red, and often used for their vibrant colors in home decorations; and chamomile, a fragrant and delicate dried flower that is often used in teas for its potential therapeutic benefits. 

Other popular dried flowers include petunias, cornflowers, daisies, and burgundy-leaved eucalyptus, all of which offer a wide range of colors and fragrances for different purposes. When purchasing dried flowers, consider your preferences for color, scent, and desired usage to find the perfect variety for your needs.
