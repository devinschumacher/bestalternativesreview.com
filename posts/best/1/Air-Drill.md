### What distinguishes a pneumatic air drill from a hammer drill?

A pneumatic air drill, commonly known as a pneumatic drill, is engineered for precise drilling tasks across various materials. It combines the force of a drill with the percussion action of a hammer, resulting in expedited and smoother drilling in demanding applications. Nevertheless, hammer drills are generally heavier and more robust than pneumatic air drills, which can make them less desirable for less strenuous tasks.
