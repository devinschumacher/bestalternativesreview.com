### How does the Cool Air Fan work?

The Cool Air Fan utilizes a portable evaporative cooling system that effectively cools down the nearby air. It works by drawing in humid warm air, passing it through a damp fabric pad, and then releasing the now cooled, moist air back into the surrounding environment. This process is known as evaporative cooling, which is an energy-efficient method of air conditioning. 

As the warm air passes through the moist fabric pad, the water molecules from it evaporate and absorb heat, creating a cooling effect. Simultaneously, the fan circulates the cooled air around the user, providing instant relief from stuffy atmospheres or hot and humid environments.
