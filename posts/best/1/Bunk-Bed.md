The most common configuration is two single beds on separate frames, with the top bunk typically being shorter to accommodate the height difference. However, there are also bunk beds with a loft bed on top and a bed below or even bunk beds designed for three people with one bed on the bottom and two on either side. 


### What materials are bunk beds made of?

Bunk beds are typically made from materials that are sturdy enough to support two beds without collapsing. The most common materials used include wood, metal, and plastic.
