### What is a DLP 3D printer?

A DLP (Digital Light Processing) 3D printer uses digital light to create physical objects. It works by projecting patterns of light onto a build platform, curing the resin layer by layer to create a three-dimensional object. DLP 3D printers are known for their high-quality prints, fast print speeds, and large build volume. 

This technology is ideal for creating detailed models, prototypes, and even functional parts. It is often used in industries such as automotive, medical, and aerospace, where precision and high-quality finished products are essential. DLP 3D printers are generally more expensive than other 3D printing technologies, but they offer superior results in terms of resolution and surface finish.
