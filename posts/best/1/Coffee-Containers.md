### What is the advantage of vacuum-sealed coffee containers?

Vacuum-sealed coffee containers are designed to keep coffee fresh for a longer period by removing air from the container. This process prevents oxygen from coming into contact with the coffee, which can cause it to go stale. Vacuum-sealed containers are particularly useful for whole bean coffee, which has a higher oil content and is more susceptible to oxidation than ground coffee. 

Another advantage of vacuum-sealed containers is that they are airtight, which means that moisture, odors, and other air particles cannot get in. This feature is particularly important if you live in a humid area or if you want to store your coffee for an extended period. Additionally, vacuum-sealed containers are relatively lightweight, which makes them easy to carry and store.
