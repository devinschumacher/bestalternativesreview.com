### What is the difference between glass and acrylic mirrors?

Glass and acrylic mirrors are both popular options for bathroom mirrors. The primary difference between the two is their durability and weight. Glass mirrors are thicker and heavier than acrylic mirrors and generally offer better durability and resistance to cracking or shattering. They can be heavier and more expensive to install but are more suitable for larger wall mirrors. 

Acrylic mirrors, on the other hand, are lighter in weight and less expensive. They are more flexible than glass mirrors, making them a better choice for smaller or more intricately shaped mirrors. Acrylic mirrors are also more resistant to fogging and are often more shatter-resistant than glass mirrors, although their durability may be more prone to fading or discoloration over time.
