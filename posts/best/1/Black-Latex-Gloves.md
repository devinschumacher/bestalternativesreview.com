### Size and Comfort

Latex gloves come in various sizes to accommodate different hand measurements. To ensure a proper fit, measure the circumference of your dominant hand, placing the index and middle fingers side by side. Use the glove manufacturer's size chart or consult an expert for recommendations. Opt for gloves with adjustable wrists or cuffs, which allow for a more customized fit. Comfort is essential, as ill-fitting gloves can lead to irritation, discomfort, or even injury. 


### Purpose and Application

Black Latex Gloves serve various purposes, from everyday wear to specialized tasks. Consider the intended use when making your purchase. Gloves for general use may have a smoother finish, making them suitable for tasks like gardening or household chores.
