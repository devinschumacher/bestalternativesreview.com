### How do I remove tie dye stains from my skin and clothing?

To remove tie dye stains from your skin, simply wash your hands with soap and water. For clothing, it's best to treat the stains immediately before they have a chance to set. Use cold water and a mild detergent to wash the stained area, and avoid using hot water or bleach, as this can set the dye and make it more difficult to remove. 

If the stain persists, you can try using a stain remover specifically designed for tie dye, or consult the manufacturer's instructions for the best course of action. In some cases, the stain may not be completely removed, but it should fade over time with repeated washing. To prevent future tie dye stains, always wear old, disposable clothing and work in a well-ventilated area with gloves on.
