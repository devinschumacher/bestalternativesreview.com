## Buyer's Guide

Cabinet backplates are essential additions to your cabinet doors, as they provide an aesthetic and functional improvement. The right backplate will enhance the overall look of your kitchen or bathroom while offering protection to the back of the cabinet doors. In this buyer's guide section, we will discuss the essential features, considerations, and general advice for cabinet backplates to help you make an informed purchase. 


### Important Features

1. Material: Cabinet backplates are typically made of metal, plastic, or a combination of both. Metal backplates, such as stainless steel or brushed nickel, provide a durable and long-lasting option.
