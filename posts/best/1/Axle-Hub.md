### What are the different types of Axle Hubs?

There are two primary types of Axle Hubs: solid and independent suspension. Solid Axle Hubs are commonly found in front-wheel drive vehicles with rear suspension, while independent suspension systems are more prevalent in rear-wheel-drive vehicles and those with four-wheel suspension. Additionally, there are different configurations within these types, depending on the application and design. 

Solid Axle Hubs are typically more robust and straightforward, with no independent suspension components. They consist of a single shaft that rotates within the wheel bearings to allow the wheel to turn. Independent suspension Axle Hubs, on the other hand, enable each wheel to move independently, providing better handling and performance. They have several moving parts, reducing the risk of damage due to impacts or other forces.
