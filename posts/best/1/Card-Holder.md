### What is RFID blocking?

RFID blocking refers to the ability of a card holder or wallet to hinder unauthorized access to your sensitive information stored on Radio Frequency Identification (RFID) chips. RFID chips are commonly incorporated in credit cards, debit cards, and other forms of identification, allowing for wireless communication between the chip and a nearby RFID reader. This technology offers convenience in various situations, such as facilitating payment processes or granting access to secure areas. However, it can also be vulnerable to theft and hacking if it is not adequately secured. 

RFID-blocking card holders and wallets are designed to prevent unauthorized access to your RFID chips by obstructing or absorbing the radio signals sent between your card and the reader. This is typically achieved using specialized materials such as aluminum, copper, or a specific type of metal alloy. By utilizing an RFID-blocking card holder, you can safeguard your personal information and verify that only authorized parties have access to your sensitive data.
