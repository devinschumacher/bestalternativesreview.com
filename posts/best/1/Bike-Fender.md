## Buyer's Guide

Bike fenders are an essential accessory for any cyclist. They help keep you dry during rainy rides, protect your clothing from mud splashes, and even extend the life of your bike by preventing rust from water damage. When shopping for bike fenders, consider the following important features, considerations, and advice to make an informed decision. 


### Compatibility with Your Bike
