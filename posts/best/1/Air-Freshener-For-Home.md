### How do air fresheners function?

Air fresheners operate by releasing scents into the ambient air. The active ingredients in the air freshener, such as essential oils or synthetic fragrances, are encapsulated within a carrier substance. When activated, the carrier material evaporates, releasing the scents which subsequently circulate in the air. 

Air fresheners work in diverse ways. Some sprays can be sprayed directly into the air, while gel air fresheners are designed to be placed on a surface, gradually releasing fragrances over an extended period. Plug-in air fresheners disperse fragrances into the air by heating the carrier material, generating a vapor that transports the essence. In all instances, the objective is to distribute fragrance to improve air quality and eliminate unpleasant odors.
