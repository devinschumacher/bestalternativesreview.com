### What is a boning knife, and what is it used for?

A boning knife is a specialized kitchen tool designed for cutting and trimming meat and fish around bones and connective tissues. Its thin, flexible, and sharp blade enables easy and precise cutting, essential for any serious chef or home cook preparing poultry or seafood dishes. 

The distinctive features of a boning knife are its narrow, flexible blade and a small, offset handle. This design allows the blade to slice through fat and connective tissue, facilitating trimming and filleting tasks. The offset handle provides excellent grip and control, particularly when dealing with delicate tasks like fish filleting or deboning chicken.
