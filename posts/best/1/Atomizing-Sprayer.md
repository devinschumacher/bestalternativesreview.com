### What is the difference between atomizing sprays and spray bottles?

These sprays typically have adjustable nozzles for precision and offer enhanced coverage and convenience compared to traditional spray bottles. 

Spray bottles, on the other hand, are simple containers with a plastic or metal nozzle that relies on the user's manual actuation to release the liquid. They are often less precise and can cause inconsistent discharge, leading to uneven coverage or waste. Although spray bottles are more commonplace, atomizing sprays provide a more efficient and controlled solution for various applications, such as cleaning, insect repellent, aromatherapy, and personal care.
