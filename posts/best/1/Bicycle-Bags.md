### What are the various types of bicycle bags available?

The market offers several types of bicycle bags, including handlebar bags, frame bags, saddle bags, and backpacks. Handlebar bags and frame bags are typically utilized to transport frequently used items, such as maps, snacks, or small tools. Saddle bags are designed to carry heavier items, like clothing or larger items, and are usually attached to the seat post of the bike. 

Backpacks, on the other hand, are worn on the cyclist's back and can carry a wide range of items.
