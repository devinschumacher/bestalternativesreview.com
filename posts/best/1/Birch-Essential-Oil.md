### The Art of Extracting Birch Essential Oil

Each spring, the birch tree produces a resinous liquid known as 'birch sap', primarily extracted during the months of March and April. This precious sap is collected and then subjected to steam distillation, a process that yields the coveted essential oil. With an abundance of birch sap demanded, the output is a superior-quality oil with a delightful aroma-packed punch, valued for its therapeutic properties.
