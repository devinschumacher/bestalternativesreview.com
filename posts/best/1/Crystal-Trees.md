### What are Crystal Trees?

Crystal Trees are a unique and popular type of crystal formation that is characterized by the growth of crystal branches on a tree-like structure. These crystals are often found in rock salt mines and are typically composed of minerals such as gypsum, calcite, and aragonite. Crystal Trees are prized for their beauty, rarity, and the symbolism they represent. 

The growth of these crystals on a tree-like structure is believed to be the result of mineral-rich water seeping into the mine and filling the voids left behind by the tree. As the water evaporates, the minerals gradually crystallize, forming the intricate and delicate crystal structures that make Crystal Trees so captivating.
