### What is the distinction between acid-free and non-acid-free art and craft papers?

Art and craft papers come in two primary forms: acid-free and non-acid-free. Acid-free paper is free of chemical agents containing lignin, a compound that can lead to yellowing or deterioration in the paper over time, thereby making it more resistant to fading and discoloration. This paper is perfect for long-term storage or display. 

On the other hand, non-acid-free paper can deteriorate faster than acid-free paper, particularly when exposed to high levels of humidity or direct sunlight. However, non-acid-free paper is typically more affordable and may be more suitable for short-term projects or disposable items that don't require long-term preservation.
