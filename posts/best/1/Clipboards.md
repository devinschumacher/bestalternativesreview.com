## Buyer's Guide

When it comes to choosing the right clipboard for your needs, there are several factors to consider. In this buyer's guide, we will discuss some important features, considerations, and general advice to help you make the best decision for your clipboard. 


### Material

Clipboards are typically made from materials such as plastic, metal, or wood. Each material has its own advantages and disadvantages. Plastic clipboards are lightweight and inexpensive, but they may not be as durable as other materials. Metal clipboards are sturdy and durable, but they can be heavier and more expensive. Wooden clipboards are also durable and can be aesthetic, but they are typically more expensive than plastic or metal options.

### Size and Portability

The size of the clipboard is an important consideration, especially if you need to take it with you on the go. Smaller clipboards are generally more portable, but they may have limited space for holding papers. Larger clipboards provide more room for papers but may be less convenient to carry around.
