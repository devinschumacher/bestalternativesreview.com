## Buyer's Guide

Black light fixtures add a unique and captivating touch to any space. ### Purpose and Application

Before diving into the features and considerations, it's essential to understand the purpose and application of black light fixtures. Black light fixtures emit ultraviolet (UV) light, which reveals hidden patterns and designs when used with fluorescent materials such as ultraviolet-reactive paint, fabric, or posters.
