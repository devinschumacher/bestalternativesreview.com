### Which types of lighting are best for creating a warm and cozy atmosphere?

To create a warm and cozy atmosphere, it's best to choose lighting with a warm color temperature. Warm lighting, typically between 2700K and 3000K, is well-suited for living rooms, dining areas, and bedrooms. Soft glow bulbs or warm-toned LED bulbs are great options for creating a warm ambiance in these spaces. 

Consider using dimmable lighting controls to further adjust the lighting levels in your room. ### What is the difference between warm and cool lighting?

Warm lighting has a color temperature of around 2700K to 3000K, while cool lighting has a color temperature of around 4000K or higher. Warm lighting typically has a yellow or orange hue and creates a cozy, inviting atmosphere. Cool lighting, on the other hand, has a blue-white hue and can make a space feel more modern and energetic. 

When selecting lighting for your home, consider the desired mood and ambiance for each space.
