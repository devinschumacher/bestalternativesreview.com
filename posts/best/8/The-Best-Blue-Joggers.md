### Fit and Sizing Considerations

Standard sizing options include regular, relaxed, and athletic fits. The regular fit provides a classic look, while relaxed fit offers extra room through the legs. Athletic fits are snug and form-hugging, ideal for intense workouts. Consider your preferred movement range and intended use when selecting a fit.

### MATERIAL AND DURABILITY

Polyester and cotton blends offer excellent value for money, combining moisture-wicking properties with durability. Higher-quality options feature reinforced stitching at stress points and thicker knitting for long-lasting structure. Pay attention to the construction quality, particularly in areas like the waistband and ankles. 


### DESIGN ELEMENTS

Look for practical design details such as multiple pockets for storage, reinforced stitching, and adjustable waistbands. Pippers or elastic at the ankles improve fit and prevent bunching. Consider the length and style of pockets based on your intended use - zippered pockets offer better security for valuables.
