### What is Tylan powder?

Tylan powder is an antibiotic medication used to treat bacterial infections in animals. It combines two active ingredients: tylosin tartrate and phenylpropanolamine hydrochloride. 

The product is available in both powder and liquid formulations and is commonly administered through drinking water or mixed with feed. 


### How is Tylan powder administered?

For poultry, Tylan powder is typically mixed with drinking water at a specific concentration recommended by the manufacturer. 

In cattle, it's often administered through feed at a specified dose per pound of body weight.

### What conditions does Tylan powder treat?

The antibiotic is used to treat respiratory infections, including bacterial pneumonia and bronchitis. 

It's also effective against enteric infections, such as salmonellosis and clostridial enteritis.

In cattle, the recommended duration is 10-14 days for systemic infections.
