### How should mens flare jeans be styled?

### How should mens flare jeans be washed and maintained?

Treat flare jeans like any other pair of jeans, washing them regularly with like colors to prevent fading. Avoid mixing them with items that produce lint or fibers, which can cling to the wider leg openings.
