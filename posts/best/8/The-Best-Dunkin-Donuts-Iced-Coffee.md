### What types of iced coffee does Dunkin' offer?

Dunkin' offers several types of iced coffee, including regular iced coffee, iced coffee with flavored syrup, and premium blends like its signature Iced Coffee with Half-and-Half. They also provide iced coffee varieties blended with milk or soy milk. 

Customers can choose from multiple flavors, including classic roast, french vanilla, and mocha, each available in regular or decaf versions to suit their preference. 


### Can I customize my Dunkin' iced coffee?

Dunkin' allows customization options for its iced coffee drinks. You can choose between regular and decaf, select flavors, and opt for various cream and sweetness levels. 

The coffee can be served with or without steamed milk or soy milk, and customers can request adjustments in sweetness through the addition of simple syrup or by asking for it unsweetened.

### What sizes are available for Dunkin' iced coffee?

Dunkin' traditionally offers 12oz, 16oz, and 20oz sizes for its iced coffee. Some locations may offer additional sizes like 24oz for select drinks. 

Many customers prefer the 20oz size as it provides a generous amount while maintaining a reasonable price point for a coffee shop chain. 


### How do I order a cold brew at Dunkin'?

To order a cold brew, simply tell your server you would like a cold brew coffee. It can be served black or with your choice of syrup and milk alternatives. 

If you prefer a larger size, you can ask for a double or triple shot of cold brew coffee. The drink is made with cold water, resulting in a smoother, less acidic coffee experience.

### What temperature options are available?

Dunkin' iced coffee comes served cold, but they also offer warm versions for those who prefer their coffee hot. If you're ordering iced and want it served warm, make sure to specify this when placing your order. 

On particularly hot days, some locations may offer a "warm cold brew" option where your coffee is served steaming hot but with enough ice for the drink to remain cold over time. 


### Are Dunkin' Iced Coffee options dietary-friendly?

Dietary options abound with Dunkin' iced coffee. They offer both regular and decaf versions, and you can customize with various syrups and milk alternatives like almond milk, soy milk, or oat milk. 

For those avoiding dairy, all syrup and milk alternative options can be used to create a suitable drink. They also offer a dedicated plant-based milk option for customers with specific dietary requirements.
