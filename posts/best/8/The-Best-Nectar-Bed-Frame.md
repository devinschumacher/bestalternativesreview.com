## Buyer's Guide

A nectar bed frame combines durability with a supportive foundation for your mattress, enhancing both sleep quality and longevity. Understanding key features helps you select a frame that matches your needs and preferences. 


### Construction Quality

The frame's durability depends on its construction. Common materials include steel for affordability and longevity, or hardwood for superior stability. Check if the frame uses powder coating or slats for extra support and prevent sagging over time. 


### Size Compatibility

Ensure the frame matches your mattress size exactly. Incorrectly sized frames can wobble or allow the mattress to sit unevenly, reducing stability and support. Consider a frame slightly larger than your mattress if you're using specific pillows or need extra space for movement. 


### Storage and Design Features

Bed frames with storage typically feature slatted bases or drawers, ideal for organizing pillows, blankets, or spare mattresses. Consider frames with adjustable legs for uneven floors or those with hidden casters for easy mobility. 


### Paint and Finish Options

Most frames offer basic white or neutral color options, while others provide customizable finishes. The frame's paint quality affects its durability and appearance, so look for frames with smooth, even finishes that resist chalking or peeling.
