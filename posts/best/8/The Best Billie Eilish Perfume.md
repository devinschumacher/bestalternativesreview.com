## FAQ


### Billie Eilish's Most Iconic Perfumes

The young artist has crafted two signature scents that have become instant classics in the perfume world. The first, available since 2021, combines top notes of bergamot and rosemary with heart notes of vanilla and patchouli. The second, launched in 2022, features a fresh blend of mandarin and blackcurrant, followed by middle notes of jasmine and vetiver. 

Both perfumes come in sleek, minimalist bottles that perfectly match Billie's distinct style. The 100ml size retails for around $145, while the 10ml travel-size bottle costs approximately $40. 


### How Do These Perfumes Compare to Others in the Market?

Billie's perfumes stand out for their unique combination of fresh, youthful notes with more sophisticated undertones. While many teen-oriented fragrances use artificial ingredients, these use natural essences to create their distinctive scents. 

Compared to similarly priced colognes and perfumes from established brands, Billie's offerings maintain their quality while staying true to her artistic vision. 


### Can You Recommend a Billie Eilish Perfume?

Both of Billie's perfumes have their merits. The 2021 release captures her signature blend of clean, modern scents, while the 2022 fragrance brings a bit more complexity with its jasmine and vetiver notes. The travel-size bottle is ideal for testing before committing to a full 100ml bottle. 

Ultimately, the choice comes down to personal preference. If you enjoy fresh, modern scents, the 2021 release will likely suit you. For something a bit more complex, the 2022 fragrance might be the best choice. 


### Are These Perfumes Available in Other Sizes?

The primary sizes available are 100ml for the full-size bottles and 10ml for the travel-size version. However, some retailers may offer additional sizes, so it's worth checking with your preferred seller. 

If you prefer to try a scent before committing to a larger purchase, consider getting one of the travel-size bottles. These are suitable for personal use or as gifts. 


### Can These Perfumes Be Purchased Directly from Billie Eilish?

Billie Eilish's official website offers her perfumes for purchase, though supplies tend to sell out quickly. The website also provides detailed information about each fragrance and its ingredients. 

For those purchasing directly from the artist, the process is straightforward. The website accepts major credit cards and offers free shipping within the US. Delivery times can vary, so it's recommended to place your order well in advance of any special occasions. 


### Are These Perfumes Suitable for All Skin Types?

The natural ingredients in Billie's perfumes make them generally suitable for all skin types. However, if you have particularly sensitive skin, you might want to perform a patch test before applying the fragrance widely. 

Both fragrances contain alcohol, which can cause irritation for some individuals. If you've experienced sensitivity to otherperfumes containing alcohol, it's best to use these products with caution.
