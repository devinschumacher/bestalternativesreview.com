The consistent stick shape makes them ideal for sharing and prevents mess, while the convenient size allows for portion control. These portable treats are suitable for both home and on-the-go consumption. 


### What flavors are available?

Classic Nutella Sticks feature the beloved hazelnut-chocolate spread. Some manufacturers offer variations like white chocolate or peanut butter versions, while others include seasonal flavors such as white chocolate macadamia or almond. Always check the packaging for specific flavor options. 

Most Nutella Sticks maintain the traditional hazelnut-chocolate flavor, but adventurous eaters can explore regional flavors or seasonal releases for a taste of something new. 


### How should they be stored?

Once opened, Nutella Sticks can be stored in the refrigerator to maintain their freshness. The unopened plastic wrapper provides airtight protection, but opening the stick requires refrigeration to prevent spoilage. Consumed Nutella should be used within 5-7 days for optimal quality. 

Avoid leaving opened sticks at room temperature, as they may become soft and difficult to use. For extended storage, transfer the remaining spread to an airtight container and keep it refrigerated. 


### Are Nutella Sticks suitable for different dietary needs?
