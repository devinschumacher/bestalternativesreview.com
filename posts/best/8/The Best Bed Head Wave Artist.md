## FAQ


### What is a Bed Head Wave Artist?

A Bed Head Wave Artist is a portable hair styling device that creates a wind tunnel effect to style and shape hair while you sleep. It features a motorized cylinder that blows air through flexible tubes, allowing you to adjust the styling position and strength of the airflow to achieve different hairstyles. 

The device consists of a main unit that connects to three flexible tubes, each with three adjustable nozzles. You place these tubes in your hair and turn on the device before bed to have your hair styled while you sleep. 


### How does a Bed Head Wave Artist work?

A Bed Head Wave Artist uses a motorized cylinder that generates airflow through flexible tubes connected to the main unit. The airflow is directed through three adjustable nozzles at the end of each tube, which can be positioned to target specific areas of your hair. 

The device operates at different power levels, allowing you to create various hairstyles including waves, curls, and volume. You can adjust the airflow strength and the positioning of the nozzles to achieve your desired style while sleeping. 


### What hair types are suitable for using a Bed Head Wave Artist?

Bed Head Wave Artists work well with all hair types, including fine, medium, and thick hair. The adjustable airflow allows you to create volume and texture suitable for different hair thicknesses. 

For finer hair, use the lowest airflow setting to prevent over-styling. Thicker hair may require the medium or high settings and proper positioning of the nozzles to achieve the desired results. 


### How long does it take to style hair with a Bed Head Wave Artist?

It typically takes between 30 minutes to 2 hours for a Bed Head Wave Artist to style your hair, depending on the desired style and hair length. For basic styling, such as adding volume or texture, you may only need 30-60 minutes. 

For more extensive styling, like creating large curls or waves, you may need to set the device overnight to achieve the desired results. The styling time can vary based on hair type and the style you're aiming to create. 


### Can a Bed Head Wave Artist damage my hair?

When used properly, a Bed Head Wave Artist should not damage your hair. The device uses cool air with adjustable settings to prevent overheating and potential damage. 

However, using the device with incorrect settings (such as too high of an airflow or leaving it on for too long) can potentially cause damage. Always follow the manufacturer's guidelines and adjust the settings based on your hair type and desired style. 


### How do I maintain and clean my Bed Head Wave Artist?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the main unit and tubes with a damp cloth and ensuring all connections are secure. The nozzles should be cleaned after each use to remove any hair or debris buildup. 

Before each use, check that all connections are tight and the tubes are properly positioned. After use, store the device in its carrying case to protect it from dust and damage.
