### What are the key factors to consider when selecting an elegant wedding guest dress?

When choosing an elegant wedding guest dress, consider the wedding's theme, your body type, and the event's formality level. The length (floor-length, cocktail-length, or short), silhouette (A-line, mermaid, sheath), and color should complement both the occasion and your personal style.

### Which dress lengths are most appropriate for different occasions?

Cocktail-length dresses (to mid-thigh) are ideal for casual weddings or events where a full-length dress would be too formal. Floor-length gowns are appropriate for formal events, outdoor weddings, or larger venues where fullness can enhance your appearance. 

Short dresses (above the knee) work well for intimate gatherings, destination weddings, or events with a casual dress code. The length you choose should balance your personal comfort with the event's expectations.

### How do I determine my dress size?

Take accurate measurements of your bust, waist, and hips before trying on dresses. Compare your measurements to the dress manufacturer's size chart, as sizing can vary between brands. Consider your body type and the dress silhouette when determining your size, as some styles will require different measurements.

### How do I care for my wedding guest dress?

Follow the dress manufacturer's care instructions for the best results. Most formal wedding guest dresses require dry cleaning, while some lighter fabrics might be machine washable. Store the dress in a breathable garment bag when not in use to prevent damage from moth larvae or exposure to harsh light. 

For home storage, use padded hangers to maintain the dress's shape. Avoid plastic hangers, which can stretch and damage the fabric. If the dress includes embellishments, store it in a cool, dry place to prevent changes in color or deterioration of the design elements.
