Opt for a generous fit that allows for layering and ease of movement. ### Cargo Features

Pockets are the primary selling point of cargo pants, but their effectiveness varies. Look for multiple pockets of different sizes, including those for small items like keys and larger ones for water bottles or books. The organization should allow you to access items quickly while keeping everything secure during activity. 


### Materials and Construction

High-quality cotton or a cotton-blend fabric provides comfort and durability while maintaining breathability. Some pants incorporate moisture-wicking materials in the crotch area for extended use in warm conditions. The construction quality affects longevity - reinforced stress points on zippers and around pockets indicate a more durable design.
