## Buyer's Guide

A 22 375 x 34 poster frame, approximately 88.75 inches by 13.4 inches, provides generous space for displaying large artwork, advertisements, or professional prints. When selecting this size frame, several factors will influence your choice, including the type of mounting hardware required, wall mount compatibility, and the visual impact you want to achieve in your space. 


### Mounting Hardware

Standard mounting hardware typically includes D-rings or sawtooth hangers, which are appropriate for lightweight to medium-heavy posters weighing up to 10 pounds. For heavier prints or professional-grade artwork, consider frames equipped with wire clips or metal brackets that distribute weight more effectively across the mounting points. 


### Frame Construction

The frame's core construction affects both its stability and longevity. Look for aluminum or stainless steel frames for indoor use, as these materials resist warping and corrosion. If the frame will be exposed to extreme temperatures or frequent handling, select a construction type that maintains its shape and finish over time. 


### Matting and Backing

Proper matting protects the artwork while enhancing the display. Acid-free mat boards prevent yellowing and damage to the print, while UV protective backers reduce fading from sunlight exposure. For framed artwork intended for display in offices or commercial spaces, backing options like acid-free foam board or plexiglass provide additional protection against scratches and damage. 


### Customization Options

Frame finishing options vary from sleek black to warm wood tones, allowing you to match the frame style with your existing decor. Consider gold or silver frames for contemporary settings, while bronze or dark wood finishes suit more traditional interiors. Custom sizes or special corners may also be available for frames where standard cutting and cornering wouldn't provide the desired visual effect.
