### What is the Garrett Ace 300?

The Garrett Ace 300 is a portable metal detector designed for coin hunting and small artifact detection. Built for both beginners and experienced users, it features Garrett's Precision Locate technology and an intuitive display. 

The unit comes with built-in ground balance and depth compensation, eliminating the need for external sensors in many situations. It also includes Garrett's Target X-Trace technology for precise target identification. 


### How sensitive is the Garrett Ace 300?

The Ace 300 offers three sensitivity levels - Standard, High, and Maximum - allowing users to adjust detection capabilities based on ground conditions and target size. 

At maximum sensitivity, the detector operates between 0 and 99, with lower settings for deeper targets or more challenging ground conditions.

### What's the operating range of the Garrett Ace 300?

The detector has a detection depth of up to 10 inches (25 cm) for small targets like coins, and up to 15 inches (38 cm) for large targets. This ranges from roughly 6 to 12 inches (<15-30 cm) at maximum sensitivity. 

The audible signal and visual indicator become active when a target is detected within this range, helping users locate the exact position of the buried object. 


### Can the Garrett Ace 300 work in all weather conditions?

The detector functions effectively in various weather conditions, including rain and humidity, making it suitable for outdoor metal detecting in most environments. 

While it's not designed for completely submerged targets or deep water, the Ace 300 performs well in damp soil and during light rainfall.
