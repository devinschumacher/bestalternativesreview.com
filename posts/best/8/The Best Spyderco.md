### What's the difference between Spyderco folders and fixed blades?

Spyderco folders typically feature more intricate designs with distinct blade shapes, while fixed-blade models often have more straightforward profiles. Folder blades usually incorporate locking mechanisms for secure blade retention, while fixed blades rely on tang recesses in the handle for stability. 

Most Spyderco folders use ball-bearing pivot systems for smooth blade deployment, while many fixed-blade models feature traditional full tang designs for maximum rigidity. Both categories offer similar performance levels, though fixed blades generally provide better precision for fine work. 


### How should I maintain my Spyderco knife?

Regularly clean your Spyderco knife after use with mild soap and water. For deeper cleaning, disassemble the blade and handle if possible, using appropriate solvents for different materials. Regularly lubricate moving parts like pivot points and lock mechanisms to maintain smooth operation. 

Store your Spyderco in a dry environment with proper blade protection. If you store the knife with the blade open, use a blade cover or insert the blade into the handle to prevent tang damage and maintain proper blade alignment.
