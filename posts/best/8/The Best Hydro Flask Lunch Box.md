Look for a vacuum-insulated construction that maintains temperatures for at least 12 hours. For beverages, this means hot liquids stay hot and cold liquids stay cold. The lid's seal is crucial; premium models feature double-sealed designs that prevent leaks completely.

## FAQ


### What is a Hydro Flask lunch box?

A Hydro Flask lunch box is an insulated food container designed to maintain the temperature of your food and drinks. It features double-wall vacuum insulation to keep contents cold for up to 24 hours or hot for up to 12 hours. 

The lunch box typically includes a leak-proof food container with a secure lid and a matching drink sleeve. It's made from BPA-free materials and comes in various sizes and designs to suit different needs. 


### How does the Hydro Flask lunch box keep food cold?

The vacuum insulation technology in the double-walled construction prevents heat transfer, keeping your food and drinks at the desired temperature. The insulated walls minimize contact between the contents and external temperatures. 

The vacuum-sealed lid and watertight closure system prevent cold air from escaping and warm air from entering, maintaining consistent temperatures for extended periods. 


### What materials are used in Hydro Flask lunch boxes?

Hydro Flask lunch boxes are constructed from 18/8 stainless steel with a brushed matte finish. The food container features BPA-free, dishwasher-safe materials lined with 304-grade stainless steel. 

The drink sleeve is made from BPA-free, food-grade plastic with a double-walled vacuum design that enhances thermal insulation while maintaining easy grip. 


### How do you clean a Hydro Flask lunch box?

Most Hydro Flask lunch boxes are dishwasher-safe, making cleaning quick and easy. For manual cleaning, hand wash the food container with warm, soapy water and rinse thoroughly. 

Always clean the drink sleeve before storing it, as it can trap moisture and affect the insulation. Wipe down the exterior with a damp cloth and air dry completely before use. 


### What sizes and designs are available?

Hydro Flask offers several sizes, including small (10 oz), medium (14 oz), and large (20 oz) options for food containers. Drink sleeves range from 9.5 oz to 14 oz. 

The company provides various designs, from basic color options to patterns and prints. Size compatibility allows mixing and matching containers with compatible drink sleeves. 


### How long does a Hydro Flask lunch box last?

With proper care, a Hydro Flask lunch box can last for years. The double-wall vacuum insulation and durable stainless steel construction resist corrosion and wear. 

Regular maintenance, including thorough cleaning and avoiding extreme temperature exposure, extends the product's lifespan and maintains its performance.
