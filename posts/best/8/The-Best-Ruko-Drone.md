### How do I fly the drone safely?

To fly safely, maintain a clear view of the drone and keep it within visual line of sight during operation. Follow all local laws and regulations regarding drone flight, and always fly in open areas to avoid obstacles and people. 

Before each flight, perform a safety check of the drone and its components, including the propellers, batteries, and landing gear. Keep the drone away from overhead power lines and congested areas to prevent accidents.

### Can I fly indoors with a Ruko drone?

Yes, many Ruko drone models can be flown indoors, but this depends on the specific model and space available. Indoor flights should be conducted in well-ventilated areas with high ceilings to prevent collisions with walls and fixtures.
