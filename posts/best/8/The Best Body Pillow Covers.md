### What is a body pillow cover?

### Why should I use a body pillow cover?

Body pillow covers protect your investment by preventing damage from liquids, oils, and bacteria. They also make it easier to maintain hygiene, as you can simply remove and wash the cover when needed. 

These covers are particularly useful for shared pillows or situations where multiple people use the same pillow, ensuring each user's sleep environment remains clean and fresh.

### How do I clean a body pillow cover?

Most body pillow covers are machine washable, typically requiring a gentle cycle with cold water. You may need to remove the cover's attachments before washing and check the care label for specific instructions. 

After washing, make sure to dry the cover thoroughly before reattaching any attachments. Most covers can be air-dried quickly, although some may require a low-heat setting on your dryer. 


### What maintenance requirements are there for body pillow covers?

Regularly inspect your cover for signs of wear and tear, such as stitching damage or fabric deterioration. Replace the cover if you notice any significant damage that exposes the pillow's core. 

Store your clean cover in a dry, cool place when not in use. Avoid folding or stacking the cover directly on metal objects that could damage the material.
