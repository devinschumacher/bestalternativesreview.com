### What temperature range is safe for using Blue Devil Gasket Sealer?

Our sealers can be applied between 40°F to 120°F (4°C to 49°C). Below 40°F (4°C), the sealant may not cure properly, while temperatures above 120°F (49°C) can cause accelerated curing or poor adhesion. 

For optimal results, perform the application at room temperature between 60°F to 75°F (15°C to 24°C). This temperature range allows for proper curing while minimizing viscosity changes in the sealant. 


### Can Blue Devil Gasket Sealer be applied in cold weather?

While our sealers can be applied between 40°F to 120°F (4°C to 49°C), we recommend avoiding extremely cold conditions. Temperatures below 40°F (4°C) can prevent proper curing, resulting in a weaker seal. 

If you need to seal in cold weather, consider bringing the parts indoors to warm them slightly before application. This helps improve the sealant's workability and ensures better adhesion to the colder surfaces.
