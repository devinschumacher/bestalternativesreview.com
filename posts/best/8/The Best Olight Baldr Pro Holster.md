## Buyer's Guide

The Olight Baldr Pro holster is designed for attaching the Baldr Pro flashlight to clothing or gear, providing hands-free operation. When selecting a holster for your Baldr Pro, consider the following key factors to ensure optimal functionality and durability. 


### Attachment Options

Standard attachments include clip and belt clips, allowing the holster to secure to clothing or equipment. Look for clips that offer adjustable positions and secure locking mechanisms to prevent accidental detachment during use.

### Flashlight Orientation

Check if the holster keeps the flashlight in the same orientation as when held manually. Proper orientation ensures easy access to controls and optimal grip, whether the flashlight is mounted horizontally or vertically. 


### Maintenance Requirements

High-quality holsters use materials that resist wear from repeated deployment and environmental factors. The holster's construction should include protective elements for the flashlight's body and lens while remaining lightweight and unobtrusive.
