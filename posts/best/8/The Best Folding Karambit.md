## Buyer's Guide

A folding karambit combines tactical functionality with brutal effectiveness. Understanding its key features will help you select the right blade for your needs. 


### Blade Design

Opt for a recurve design with a slightly curved tip for improved cutting grip. The blade should measure between 2.5 to 3 inches, legal in most jurisdictions. Choose a material like 1095 carbon steel for edge retention.
