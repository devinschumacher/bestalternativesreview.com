### Size and Fit Considerations

Proper fit is crucial for effectiveness and comfort. Key measurements include natural waist circumference, hip width, and desired cup size for padding. The product should provide 2-3 inches of lift while maintaining natural mobility. Consider trying on multiple sizes and styles to find the best balance of support and comfort. 


### Wearing and Care Recommendations

Most butt lifter shapewear requires hand washing in cold water with mild detergent to maintain shape and condition. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners, which can damage the elastic. The items should be hung to dry rather than folded, and worn only for short periods, typically 8-12 hours per day. For optimal results, consider wearing them with loose-fitting tops to prevent visible underlay.

### How does it work?

Butt lift shapewear employs several techniques to create its lifting effect. ### How long does it take to notice an effect?
