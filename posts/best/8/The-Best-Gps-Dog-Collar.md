### What is a GPS dog collar?

A GPS dog collar combines a traditional pet collar with global positioning system technology. It uses satellite signals to track your dog's location in real-time and transmit that information to your phone or dedicated app. 

These collars help pet owners monitor their dog's movements, set boundaries for safe roaming, and locate lost pets quickly. 


### How accurate are GPS dog collar locations?

Modern GPS dog collars typically provide location accuracy within 10-15 meters. In ideal conditions, this can improve to around 5 meters. 

The actual accuracy may vary based on environmental factors like tall buildings, dense foliage, or urban canyons that block satellite signals.

Key features include clear location tracking, app compatibility, battery life, water resistance, and adjustable settings for roaming zones.

### How do I set up a GPS dog collar?

First, charge the collar's battery and program it with your pet's ID number and collar size. 

Then, download the companion app, create an account, and follow the on-screen instructions to pair the collar with your phone.
