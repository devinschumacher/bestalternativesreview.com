Proper sizing is crucial for comfortable Fenoglio boots. Italian footwear sizing tends to run smaller than US standards, so measuring your feet before purchasing is essential. When trying on boots, ensure they feel snug in the heel but allow about a thumb's width between your longest toe and the toe box. The boots should be comfortable enough to walk in immediately, as breaking them in can be challenging with these styles. 


### Care and Maintenance

Regular care helps maintain the appearance and longevity of your Fenoglio boots. Leather boots require protection from water and sunlight, so applying a quality conditioning cream and avoiding direct sun exposure are essential. Most boots come with care instructions, which typically include recommendations for cleaning (using a soft cloth and mild soap) and storage (keeping in a cool, dry place). 

Consider purchasing a boot brush and polish kit to keep your boots looking their best. Professional shoe care services can also extend the life of your footwear and give it a professional shine.
