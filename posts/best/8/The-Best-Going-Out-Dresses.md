### How do I choose the right size?

Accurate measurements are crucial for finding your ideal fit. Measure around your chest, waist, and hips while wearing undergarments you would typically wear with the dress. Refer to the specific dress's sizing chart for any additional guidance on length requirements. 

Consider ordering a size smaller if you prefer a tight fit or a size larger if you like a looser silhouette. If you're between sizes, opting for the smaller size usually provides better structure and prevents the dress from riding up during movement. 


### What care instructions should I follow?

Most dresses can be machine washed on a gentle cycle with cold water and mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the fabric and cause color bleeding. Delicate elements like lace or embroidery may require hand washing to maintain their integrity. 

Air drying is recommended to preserve the dress's shape, and avoid using a dryer unless the care label specifies it. Store the dress loosely hung or folded in a dry, cool place to prevent creasing and maintain its quality.
