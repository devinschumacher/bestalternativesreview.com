### How do I know which size hat to order?

Minor league hats typically come in standard sizes ranging from 7 to 8 inches in circumference. The sizing chart provided by the manufacturer should specify which size corresponds to each circumference measurement. 

If you're purchasing for someone else or ordering based on measurements, ensure you measure the head's circumference just above the ears to determine the appropriate size. 


### What are the key features to look for in a minor league baseball hat?

Quality materials, such as durable cotton twill, are standard in minor league hats. Look for well-finished seams, precise embroidery, and adjustable back panels that secure the hat firmly without pressing down on the ears. 

Many hats include moisture-wicking fabrics in the sweatband and ventilation holes to keep the wearer comfortable during games and practices. 


### Are minor league hats suitable for everyday wear?

While minor league hats share many construction qualities with everyday baseball caps, their smaller logos and more subdued branding make them less suitable for casual wear in non-baseball contexts.

### Can I wear a minor league hat in games or practices?

These hats are specifically designed for game operations and player use, so wearing them during official games or practices is appropriate. They typically meet the same standards for visibility and appearance as major league hats.
