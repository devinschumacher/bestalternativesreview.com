## Buyer's Guide

When selecting a frame, it's important to consider several key aspects that will affect both the functionality and aesthetic appeal of your purchase. The 24x30 frame size is a popular choice for photographers and decorators, offering a generous canvas for displaying photos, artwork, or documents. 


### Frame Construction and Quality

The frame's construction should be robust enough to support the weight of the artwork while minimizing warping or bending. Common materials include wood, metal, and composite framing systems.
