### What materials are commonly used for black placemats?

Most black placemats can be wiped clean with a damp cloth to remove food residue and dust. For tougher stains, use mild soap and water, ensuring complete drying before reuse.

### What sizes are available for black placemats?

Standard sizes typically range from 12 to 18 inches in diameter, suitable for most dinner plates. Rectangular placemats often measure between 18 and 36 inches in length.
