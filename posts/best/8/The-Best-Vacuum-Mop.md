## FAQ

### What is a vacuum mop?

A vacuum mop combines the cleaning action of a traditional mop with vacuum suction to remove dirt, dust, and debris from your floors. It works by capturing the dirt as it's dislodged from the surface, making it an efficient alternative to conventional mopping methods. 

Most vacuum mops come with multiple attachments, including brushes for carpeted areas and crevice tools for tight spaces, making them versatile for different cleaning needs. 


### What kind of floor surfaces can a vacuum mop clean?

Vacuum mops are designed to work on hard floors such as tile, vinyl, and hardwood. Some models include specialized brushes that make them suitable for certain types of carpet, but they may not perform as effectively on deep pile carpets as they do on smooth surfaces. 

Always refer to your specific model's cleaning guidelines to determine what types of flooring it's appropriate for, as different vacuum mops have varying levels of carpet cleaning capability. 

![Vacuum-Mop-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Vacuum-Mop-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What's the difference between corded and cordless vacuum mops?

Corded vacuum mops connect directly to an electric outlet, providing constant power and typically longer operating time between charges. They're suitable for regular cleaning sessions and areas with good electrical access. 

Cordless vacuum mops run on rechargeable batteries and offer greater mobility, allowing you to clean multiple rooms without recharging. However, their runtime is limited by battery capacity. 


### How much suction power do different vacuum mops have?

The suction power of a vacuum mop can vary significantly based on the model. Higher suction power is ideal for removing stubborn dirt and debris, especially on carpeted surfaces. 

Most vacuum mops come with different attachments that affect suction strength. For example, the brush attachments typically increase suction for carpet cleaning, while the floor brush reduces it for hard surfaces. 

![Vacuum-Mop-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Vacuum-Mop-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do you maintain a vacuum mop?

Maintenance requirements vary by model, but most vacuum mops need regular filter cleaning or replacement and attachment maintenance. Always check your user manual for specific care instructions. 

When storing your vacuum mop, clean the brush or floor attachment and ensure the tank is empty to prevent water damage. Keep the unit in a dry place and run an empty cleaning cycle periodically to maintain suction power. 


### What accessories come with a vacuum mop?

Standard accessories often include a floor brush for hard surfaces, a dusting brush for cleanable floors, and a crevice tool for tight spaces. Some models offer additional attachments like stairs scrubbers or pet hair brushes. 

Check if your specific model includes the accessories you need or if you'll need to purchase extra attachments separately. The included accessories can significantly affect the vacuum mop's versatility and cleaning capacity.
