Integrated graphics are standard in most Toshiba laptops, suitable for basic tasks and web browsing. ### Storage Options

Storage capacity and type impact how you use your laptop. Toshiba offers laptops with SSDs (Solid State Drives) for faster boot times and application loading, while HDDs (Hard Disk Drives) provide more storage space for documents and media. Look for laptops with at least 6-8 hours of battery life for daily use, with 10-12 hours ideal for extended trips.
