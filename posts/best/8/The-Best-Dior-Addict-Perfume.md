## FAQ


### What are the main notes in Dior Addict Perfume?

The fragrance opens with fresh notes of mandarin and blackcurrant leaf. The heart notes include lily of the valley, jasmine, and iris. The base notes feature vanilla, amber, and woody accords. 

These scents combine to create a signature floral-gourmand profile with underlying vanilla warmth and iris depth. 


### How does Dior Addict compare to other versions in the Addict family?

Each Addict scent builds on the original formula while adding distinctive elements. For example, Addict Legend adds spicy notes, while Addict Sensual incorporates more woody tones. Each version maintains the signature floral-gourmand character while evolving the fragrance experience. 

Addict Perfume centers the composition around lily of the valley and iris, with a particularly prominent vanilla note that sets it apart from the other versions. 


### What is the recommended spritzing technique?

To get the best diffusion, apply the perfume to pulse points like the wrists, neck, and behind the ears. Allow the skin's natural warmth to activate the scents. For lasting power, apply a light touch initially and allow it to develop rather than heavily layering the fragrance. 

When spraying, hold the bottle 8-10 inches away from your skin and sweep the spray across the pulse points in an outward motion. This technique helps distribute the fragrance more evenly and prevents alcohol burn. 


### How long does the fragrance last?

When applied properly, Dior Addict lasts approximately 6-8 hours on the skin. The initial impact is strong due to the prominent vanilla note, but the longevity comes from the well-structured heart and base notes that develop through the wear. 

The scent projects moderately well, meaning it maintains its character up to 2-3 feet away from the wearer. The signature iris and lily of the valley base notes provide good tenacity, allowing the fragrance to resurface after activities. 


### Should I try the eau de toilette or eau de parfum?

The eau de parfum (EDP) version, which this article focuses on, delivers a more intense and long-lasting fragrance experience. It contains a higher concentration of aromatic compounds than the eau de toilette (EDT) version, which makes it ideal for daily wear and special occasions. 

If you prefer a lighter, fresher impression that's still recognizable as Dior Addict, the eau de toilette is a great choice. It offers good performance without the intense presence of the EDP. 


### What are the bottle variations?

The fragrance comes in several bottle designs, each representing different versions of Addict. The classic clear bottle houses the original Addict formula, while limited-edition versions feature special finishes or different color schemes. 

When choosing a bottle, consider whether you prefer the sleek modern design of the original or if you'd like to collect a different version. The bottle design can significantly impact the visual appeal of your fragrance collection.
