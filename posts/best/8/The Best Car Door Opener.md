### What is a car door opener?

A car door opener is a device that helps individuals open their car doors when they're unable to do so manually. This can be particularly useful for people with limited mobility, arthritis, or if the door handle is damaged. 

These devices work by applying pressure or using a mechanical arm to push the door open from the inside. Some advanced models can also be used to unlock the door before opening it. 


### How does a car door opener work?

Most car door openers work by mounting a lever or extending arm near the door handle. When the user pulls on the lever or arm, it applies pressure to the door's frame, effectively opening the door from the inside. 

Some models include additional features like a built-in pull cord that activates when the door handle is pulled, making it easier to operate for people with arthritis or limited hand strength.

Car door openers provide independence for individuals who struggle to open doors manually, such as those with arthritis, limited mobility, or physical disabilities.
