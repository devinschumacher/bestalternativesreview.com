## FAQ

![Cricut-Pens-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Cricut-Pens-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are Cricut pens?

Cricut pens are specialized pens designed for the Cricut Maker and Cricut Explore Air 2 machines. These pens feature interchangeable tips that allow users to select between drawing, writing, or creating textures on various surfaces. 

Each Cricut pen includes a holder that mounts to the machine, allowing the pen to be raised and lowered for precise control. The pens are sold separately from the machine and require compatible Cricut cartridges to function. 


### What surfaces can I use Cricut pens on?

Cricut pens work best on smooth, non-porous surfaces such as paper, cardstock, vinyl, and some plastics. The pens perform poorly on rough, textured, or highly absorbent materials like fabric, wood, or uncoated cardboard. 

For best results, avoid using Cricut pens on surfaces with intricate patterns, raised elements, or surfaces that might trap ink. Always test pens on a small, inconspicuous area before covering larger surfaces. 

![Cricut-Pens-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Cricut-Pens-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What's the difference between the Cricut Pen and Cricut Fine Point Pen?

The standard Cricut Pen features a wider tip (0.31mm) that makes it ideal for covering large areas quickly. It's best for creating blocky letters and basic line art. 

The Cricut Fine Point Pen offers a narrower (0.02mm) tip that allows for detailed writing and fine details. This pen is essential for handwriting, calligraphy, and adding small textures to designs. 


### Can I use regular pens with my Cricut machine?

Regular pens cannot be used directly with the Cricut machine. The pen holders are specifically designed for Cricut cartridges and cannot accommodate standard pen mechanisms. 

However, you can purchase specialized pen holders from third-party manufacturers that allow regular pens to be used with Cricut machines, though these may affect the quality and consistency of your output. 

![Cricut-Pens-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Cricut-Pens-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How long do Cricut pens last?

With normal use, Cricut pens typically last between 6-12 months before needing replacement, depending on usage frequency and the quality of the materials you're using. 

To extend the life of your pens, avoid pressing too firmly when drawing and keep the pen and machine clean to prevent clogs and ink buildup. 


### How do I clean Cricut pens?

After each use, unplug the pen, remove the felt tip, and wipe the exposed metal portion with a clean, slightly damp cloth to remove any residual ink. 

If the pen starts to clog or the tip becomes dry, you can run it through the machine's cleaning sequence in the software, which helps to clear out any stubborn ink deposits.
