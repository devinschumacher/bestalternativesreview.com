## Buyer's Guide

Aurora dresses combine natural stone patterns with ethereal qualities, making them a striking choice for both indoor and outdoor events. These shimmering garments demand careful consideration of several key elements to ensure you select the perfect piece. 


### Stone Pattern Selection

The aurora stone patterns emerge from subtle gradations of blue and green, mimicking the natural glow of Northern Lights. When selecting your pattern, consider whether you prefer a consistent color scheme or enjoy the complexity of varying intensity and hue throughout the dress. 


### Shimmer Quality

Aurora dresses generate their distinctive effect through carefully engineered sequins or specialized fabrics that reflect light. The quality of shimmer can range from soft, subtle sparkles to bold, facet-like reflections. Consider whether you want to showcase the dress's pattern through gentle highlights or through more dramatic, eye-catching glitter. 


### Fit and Tailoring

While aurora dresses typically offer a flowing, billowing silhouette that enhances the stone patterns, the fit remains crucial. Look for dresses that allow for gentle movement while maintaining shape, with attention to details like waist definition and hem length. 


### Event Considerations

The lighting conditions of your event significantly impact how the dress's aurora effect appears. Indoor events with warm, ambient lighting can beautifully highlight the stone patterns, while outdoor settings might require more natural, diffused light to showcase the dress's full effect.
