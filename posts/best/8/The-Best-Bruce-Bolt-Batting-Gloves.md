### Enhanced Grip

Improved grip is achieved through textured palms, finger wrap designs, and tacky rubberized areas. Pore-free construction helps prevent moisture buildup, keeping the glove surface dry and grippy during games. ### Fit and Construction

Look for stretchy synthetic or natural leather materials that conform to your hand shape while providing flexibility. Clarified measurements for cuff height, web spacing, and overall circumference help ensure proper fit.

### Durability and Maintenance

Higher-quality leather gloves with reinforced stitching and protective padding tend to last longer. Avoid gloves with thin, brittle leather that shows signs of wear after minimal use.
