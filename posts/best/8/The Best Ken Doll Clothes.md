Most Ken doll clothing can be cared for like regular washable fabrics. Machine wash in cold water with mild detergent, and avoid using bleach or fabric softeners. Tumble dry on low heat or air dry to maintain the clothes' shape and quality.

Ken doll clothing is designed to be mostly washable and wrinkle-resistant, making it less suitable for extensive customization. However, some basic alterations may be possible, such as fixing minor tears or adjusting buttons.
