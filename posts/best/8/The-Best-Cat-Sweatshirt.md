## FAQ


### Can I wash my cat's sweatshirt?

Cat sweatshirts can typically be machine washed, but it's important to check the specific care instructions on the garment first. Use a gentle cycle and a mild detergent to avoid damaging the fabric or fading the color. 

Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can irritate your cat's skin. After washing, air dry the sweatshirt rather than putting it in the dryer to prevent shrinkage and maintain its shape. 


### What is the best material for cat sweatshirts?

The softest and most breathable option is typically a cotton blend. Look for sweatshirts made from 100% cotton or a cotton-polyester mix, as these materials wick away moisture and provide thermal insulation without being too heavy for your cat. 

Avoid sweatshirts made from synthetic materials like polyester alone, as they can trap heat and cause discomfort in warm weather. The fabric should also be free from loose threads or tags that could hurt your cat. 


### How do I size a cat sweatshirt?

Most cat sweatshirts come in small, medium, and large sizes, matching common pet sizes. To ensure the best fit, measure your cat from the base of their neck around their torso, just behind the front legs. Choose a size that allows for about one finger width between the sweatshirt and your cat's body. 

Neck measurement is also crucial, as cats can easily get their heads stuck in too-tight sweatshirts. The sweatshirt should fit snugly around the neck but allow for easy movement. Always check the product's size chart and note any specific length measurements provided. 


### Can I wash my cat while wearing a sweatshirt?

It's generally safe to wash your cat while wearing a sweatshirt, but make sure the garment fits properly and isn't too cumbersome. The material should allow some movement and breathing room, especially around the head and body. 

If your cat tends to struggle when wearing clothes, consider removing the sweatshirt before bathing. This prevents discomfort and reduces the risk of the sweatshirt coming off during the wash cycle. 


### How do I put a cat sweatshirt on?

To put a cat sweatshirt on, start by gently lifting your cat's front legs and sliding the sweatshirt over their head. Ensure the sweatshirt fits snugly but isn't too tight, allowing for about one finger width between the fabric and your cat's body. 

If your cat resists, try making the process more comfortable by wearing the sweatshirt yourself and using treats to distract and reward your cat during the putting-on process. 


### Are cat sweatshirts safe for all cats?

Most cats can safely wear sweatshirts, but kittens and cats with short hair may not need or tolerate them. Check with your veterinarian if your cat has any medical conditions that could be affected by wearing clothing, such as respiratory issues or severe allergies. 

Some cats may not adapt well to wearing clothing, while others might enjoy the warmth and security it provides. Monitor your cat's behavior and comfort level when wearing a sweatshirt, and remove it if your cat shows signs of discomfort or difficulty breathing.
