These dresses typically run true to size, but check the manufacturer's sizing chart to ensure the right fit. Consider whether you prefer a slim, athletic cut or a more relaxed, dressy style. Choose established NBA licensed retailers to ensure authentic jerseys. Verify the brand's authenticity through official NBA licensing logos or certification. Be cautious of extremely discounted prices, as counterfeit options may pose health risks or violate NBA trademarks.
