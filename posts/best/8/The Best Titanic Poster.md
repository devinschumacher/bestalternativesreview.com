## Buyer's Guide

Titanic posters can range from simple prints to detailed, limited-edition reproductions. When selecting a poster, consider factors like print quality, dimensions, and edition size to ensure you're getting an accurate representation of the artwork. 


### Print Quality and Resolution

High-quality posters useGiclée printing with archival inks to preserve the original artwork's detail and color accuracy. Look for posters printed at 300 dpi or higher to maintain sharpness, especially in close inspection areas like the ship's details or crowd scenes. 


### Dimensions and Display Options

Standard poster sizes include 24x36 inches and 30x40 inches. Consider whether you want a portrait or landscape orientation, and measure your intended display space to ensure proper fit. Some posters offer different frame or mounting options, so choose based on your display style. 


### Edition and Authenticity

Limited-edition posters typically feature a lower print run and may include serial numbers or signatures from the artist. These can be more valuable and authentic, while unnumbered posters may be mass-produced and less valuable. Verify edition details from the seller to ensure authenticity. 


### Additional Features and Value Add-ons

Consider posters with extra features like UV protection coating, premium backing, or enhanced border designs. These can protect the print and improve its physical presentation. Higher-priced options might include artist proofs or alternative color versions for collectors.

Titanic posters draw from meticulous research, including blueprints, photographs, and eyewitness accounts. Every element, from the ship's architecture to the events that unfolded, is cross-referenced with primary sources. 

Historical accuracy is particularly noted in the poster's depiction of the ship's interior, which closely matches contemporary accounts and architectural plans.

### What are the printing qualities like?

Premium Titanic posters use high-resolution printing to capture every detail of the design. These prints employ techniques like Giclée printing to ensure color accuracy and image fidelity.
