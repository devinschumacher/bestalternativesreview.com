### How do skunk traps work?

Skunk traps operate on the principle of offering the trapped animal an escape route that can only be accessed once the door is closed. The trap typically has a baited entry point and a secure latch mechanism that opens when the skunk enters. 

Once inside, the skunk triggers a mechanism that closes the door, trapping it securely. The door then locks to prevent the skunk from re-entering or escaping through the baited entrance.
