A stable mount is crucial for tracking celestial objects. Equatorial mounts offer better tracking capabilities at higher prices, while altazimuth mounts are simpler to use and generally less expensive.

A finder scope helps locate objects in the night sky. Consider models with adjustable power and illuminated reticles for easier aiming. Additional useful accessories include a camera adapter for astrophotography and solar filters for safe viewing of the sun.
