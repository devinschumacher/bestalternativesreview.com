### What makes Wera screwdrivers different from other brands?

Their ergonomic handles use a unique shape with a 6-sided profile that contours to the user's hand. This design reduces hand fatigue and provides better control during precise tasks. 


### Which Wera driver bit sizes are available?

Wera offers a wide range of sizes, from 1.0mm to 6.5mm for slotted bits, and 1.5mm to 7.0mm for Phillips. Their modular system allows users to purchase individual bits or complete sets with all sizes included. 

For specialty sizes, they also offer fractional-inch bits that cover a wide range from 1/32" to 5/16". The system includes both double-ended and straight-shaft versions to meet various toolboxes' needs. 


### How do Wera's magnetic tip attachments work?

The magnetic tips have a spherical design that adapts to the screw head, maximizing magnetic surface contact while minimizing pressure on the bit. This allows for easy removal and installation of screws without stripping heads. 

The tip automatically detaches from the bit shaft when the driver is removed from the screw, preventing accidental dropping and protecting both the bit and the tool. 


### Are Wera drivers compatible with other brands' bits?

Wera's StarTecPlus system is proprietary, meaning their bits are only compatible with Wera drivers and compatible tools from other manufacturers. This design choice ensures optimal alignment and torque application. 

Users who need cross-compatibility can choose universal 60-degree bits that work with most standard driver systems, though they may experience slightly reduced precision compared to Wera's specialized bits. 


### What maintenance is required for Wera screwdrivers?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the driver mechanism and checking the magnetic tips for proper function. The driver head and handle should be wiped down after use to remove debris and moisture. 

When not in use, store the tools in a dry place and apply a light coat of lubricant to the mechanism once a year to maintain smooth operation and prevent rust.
