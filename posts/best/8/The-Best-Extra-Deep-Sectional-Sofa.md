## FAQ


### What defines an 'extra deep' sectional sofa?

An extra deep sectional sofa features seats with a width of 36" or more, as opposed to standard-depth sofas that typically measure between 24" and 30". This increased width provides generous legroom and can accommodate larger pillows and throw blankets. 

The depth of a sofa influences its comfort level and space requirements. Extra deep sections enhance relaxation but may need more wall space and turning radius for optimal use. 


### How do extra deep sofas affect seating capacity?

Extra deep sections increase overall seating capacity by allowing more passengers with legroom. When selecting an extra deep sofa, consider the number of people who will use the space regularly to ensure adequate comfort for all. 

These larger sections work well in family rooms where multiple people need to sit simultaneously, or in spaces where visual depth is desired without compromising on width. 


### What style considerations should accompany an extra deep sofa?

The architectural details of an extra deep sofa should complement the room's scale and style. These larger pieces work well with contemporary, farmhouse, or modern designs where clean lines and open spaces are valued. 

Choose a sofa with subtle architectural elements, clean edges, and neutral colors to maintain visual balance in rooms with standard ceiling heights. Textured fabrics and rich colors can add warmth without overwhelming smaller spaces. 


### How much space is required for an extra deep sectional?

Extra deep sections typically require a minimum room width of 20 feet for easy maneuvering, plus additional space for foot traffic. Measure your room carefully before selecting a model to ensure it fits comfortably within your space. 

These larger pieces often need their own dedicated space with clear pathways to the entrance. Consider the room's layout and traffic patterns when planning the best location for your extra deep sectional. 


### Are extra deep sofas more challenging to move?

Extra deep sections are more difficult to move through doorways and hallways due to their wide profile. Measure doorways and hallways carefully to ensure the sofa will fit, and consider the turning radius needed for 180-degree rotations. 

Plan the sofa's placement during the moving process, especially if furniture needs to be rearranged. Many dealers offer moving assistance or can guide you through proper placement techniques. 


### What maintenance requirements do extra deep sofas have?

Care requirements for extra deep sectionals are similar to standard models, focusing on regular vacuuming and occasional deep cleaning. The increased surface area can collect more dust and debris, so maintain a consistent cleaning schedule. 

Invest in quality foam cores and high-density cushions to maintain shape and support over time. Regularly check springs and wooden frames for integrity, as larger pieces may stress these components more significantly.
