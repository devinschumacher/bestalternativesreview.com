### What are snake chaps and what do they do?

Snake chaps are protective leg coverings designed to shield horse riders from snake bites. They typically consist of two long leg protectors that zip up the length of the leg, offering comprehensive coverage from ankle to knee. 

These protective garments utilize thick, snake-resistant materials like kevlar or cordura, with reinforced stitching and padding for durability. The internal mesh lining provides ventilation while the external material prevents bites. 


### How do snake chaps fit and what sizes are available?

Snake chaps typically come in standard sizing, with measurements taken from the top of the leg to just below the knee. Most brands offer sizes from small to extra-large, with specific measurements provided for each size. 

To ensure proper fit, measure your horse's leg from the top of the knee to the pastern. This measurement should correspond to the chaps' leg length, with additional length allowed for adjusting the chaps to their proper position. 

![Snake-Chaps-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Snake-Chaps-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Can snake chaps be worn with regular riding pants?

Yes, snake chaps are designed to fit over regular riding pants or jeans. They typically have a 2-3 inch overlap at the top to secure the chaps in place while riding. 

The chaps' design allows for both comfort and ease of movement during riding, with non-slip edges that prevent the pants from sliding down during the ride. 


### Are snake chaps comfortable to wear?

Many riders find snake chaps comfortable to wear, especially during snake season or in areas with high snake populations. The padded design and breathable mesh lining reduce pressure points and maintain air circulation. 

The thick material may feel slightly cumbersome at first, but proper break-in and regular use help riders become accustomed to the added padding and protection. 

![Snake-Chaps-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Snake-Chaps-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I clean and maintain my snake chaps?

Snake chaps should be washed after each ride using mild soap and warm water. Due to their protective materials, they typically don't require harsh cleaning products. Air drying is recommended to maintain the chaps' shape and integrity. 

Store the chaps in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent mold and mildew growth. Regularly inspect the seams and padding for wear, replacing the chaps when signs of damage or wear are visible. 


### What are the drawbacks of wearing snake chaps?

The primary drawback is the added bulk that affects airflow and movement compared to standard riding leg protection. Some riders may experience reduced flexibility, particularly in tight turns or when jumping. 

The cost of high-quality snake chaps can be significant, and they may not be suitable for riders who spend most of their time in low-snake-risk areas. The weight and thickness may also make them less practical for tropical or humid climates.
