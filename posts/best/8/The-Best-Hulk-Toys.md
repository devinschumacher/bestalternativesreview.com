### What age ranges are Hulk toys suitable for?

Hulk toys are typically designed for children ages 3 and up, with some larger figures and collectibles suitable for ages 6 and up. Always check the packaging for specific age recommendations. 

Smaller detailed toys may have choking hazards, so they're not recommended for children under 3 years old. Parents should supervise younger children playing with smaller Hulk toys. 


### What materials are Hulk toys made from?

Most Hulk toys are made from PVC plastic, which provides durability and resistance to fading from sunlight. Some premium figures use silicone for certain parts like eyes and mouths. 

Costume playsets and accessories may include fabrics, which are often polyester blends for durability. Always avoid toys with loose threads or small parts that could be choking hazards.

### How should I care for Hulk toys?

Regular dusting with a dry cloth keeps Hulk toys clean and looking their best. Store them in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading and warping. 

For cleaning, use mild soap and water on soft parts like fabric costumes. Always allow figures to completely dry before storing, as moisture can cause mold and mildew growth. 


### What colors are available for Hulk toys?

Most Hulk toys feature his iconic green color scheme, with variations in shade and intensity. Some collectible figures include alternate color schemes like red and black variants. 

Costume playsets often include a range of colors to match different Hulk looks, from his standard green to military-inspired camouflage patterns.
