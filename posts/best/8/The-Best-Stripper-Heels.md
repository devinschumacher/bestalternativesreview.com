These specialized shoes range from 6 to 12 inches in height, with most popular styles being 7-9 inches. The heel itself typically measures between 1.5 to 2 inches for stability during performance. 


### How do you wear stripper heels?

Strpper heels require some practice to master. The metal blade extends from the bottom of the heel and must be properly aligned with the pole before each performance. The blade allows the dancer to grip the pole while performing spins, climbs, and other choreographed moves. 

When not in use, the blade can be completely removed and stored safely. Always ensure the blade is securely attached before performance and that it's clean and free of debris to prevent slipping. 


### How do you clean stripper heels?

Regular maintenance keeps your stripper heels in top condition. After each use, wipe down the blade and heel with a soft cloth and mild soap. Rinse thoroughly and let dry completely before storing. 

To remove heavier grime or sweat residue, you can use a mixture of baking soda and water as a natural cleaning solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that might damage the metal blade or finish of the shoe. 


### Can you wear stripper heels in regular shoes?

These specialized shoes are not meant for everyday wear due to their height and design. The blades and metal components make them unsuitable for street wear or casual occasions. 

Some performers choose to wear compatible ballet shoes or platform heels underneath when not using the blade, but the stripper heel itself should only be used during performances. 


### What are the sizing considerations for stripper heels?

Most stripper heels come in standard women's US sizing from 5 to 11, with half sizes available. Always measure your foot carefully before purchasing, as stripper heels tend to run slightly smaller than regular footwear. 

It's crucial to get proper fitting when buying stripper heels. Ill-fitting shoes can cause discomfort during performance and may reduce the longevity of your footwear. 


### How long do stripper heels last?

With regular use and proper care, stripper heels can last 2-3 years. Factors affecting longevity include the frequency of use, quality of the metal blade, and maintenance practices.
