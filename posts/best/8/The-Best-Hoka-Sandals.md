### Can Hoka sandals be worn in wet conditions?

For extended wet-weather use or water-based activities, Hoka recommends pairing the sandals with socks or using them in environments where complete water protection is not necessary. The shoes perform best in light rain or damp conditions while staying dry at the feet.
