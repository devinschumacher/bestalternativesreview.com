## Buyer's Guide

Large 95-inch curtains make a dramatic statement while providing substantial window coverage. Understanding the key aspects of these curtains helps you select a style that matches your space and needs. 


### Measurement and Installation

These curtains measure 95 inches wide, offering generous coverage for most windows. Consider the height of your window when selecting length options, typically available in 84, 96, or 108 inches. Proper installation requires measuring from the window frame rather than the floor, ensuring the curtains hang smoothly and align with window treatments on adjacent walls.

### Maintenance and Cleaning

95-inch curtains often require regular cleaning to maintain their appearance. Look for curtains with durable, easy-to-clean fabrics such as cotton, polyester blends, or washable silks. Consider the practicality of cleaning large panels, especially if you have pets or frequent spills. Proper care instructions should be clearly stated on the product label. 


### Style and Compatibility

Select curtains that complement your room's style while considering the window's architectural details. The length, hem style, and rod placement affect the curtains' visual impact. Coordinate with existing window treatments, considering color, pattern, and texture to create a cohesive look.

### Practical Considerations

Think about how the curtains will affect your room's temperature and light levels. Heavy curtains provide better insulation but may feel too warm in certain climates. Consider the seasonality of your window coverings, whether for year-round use or as seasonal decor. The hardware and mounting system should support the curtain's weight while allowing easy adjustment and maintenance.
