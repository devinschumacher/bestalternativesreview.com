### What is an athletic supporter?

An athletic supporter, also known as a jockstrap or athletic undergarment, is designed to provide support and protection for male reproductive organs and the surrounding area during physical activities. It typically consists of a cup pouch connected to a pair of legs that hold the garment in place. 

These undergarments are commonly worn by athletes, particularly in contact sports, to prevent injuries and provide confidence during performance. 


### What materials are athletic supporters made from?

Most athletic supporters are made from a blend of moisture-wicking fabric and elastic materials. The cup pouch is usually constructed from a soft, breathable mesh material with padding for comfort, while the leg straps are made from stretchy spandex or similar elastic fabrics. 

Some premium models incorporate additional moisture management features, such as antimicrobial treatments and faster-drying materials, to enhance comfort during extended periods of use. 

![Athletic-Supporter-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Athletic-Supporter-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I choose the right size?

Mens' athletic supporters typically come in a range of sizes, usually labeled as small, medium, and large. The most important measurement is the circumference of your natural waist, which should correspond to the size chart provided by the manufacturer. 

For proper fit, ensure there is minimal movement when the supporter is worn. The waistband should sit comfortably but securely, while the leg straps should provide the necessary support without being too tight. 


### What activities are athletic supporters suitable for?

Athletic supporters are ideal for everyday activities, sports, and physical exercise where additional protection is beneficial. They are particularly recommended for contact sports like football, wrestling, and rugby, as well as activities involving jumping, running, or repeated impacts. 

These undergarments are not suitable for activities requiring extremely low-profile protection, such as competitive swimming, where specialized swimwear is recommended instead. 

![Athletic-Supporter-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Athletic-Supporter-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I care for my athletic supporter?

Most athletic supporters can be machine washed in a mesh bag or with similar undergarments to prevent tangling. Use a mild detergent and avoid bleach or fabric softeners, which can damage the elastic components. 

Allow the supporter to air dry completely before wearing again. Avoid using a dryer, as high heat can shrink the elastic materials and affect the fit. 


### Can athletic supporters affect fertility?

When worn properly and for short periods, athletic supporters do not affect fertility. The cup design allows for optimal heat dissipation and protection, which is essential for male reproductive health.
