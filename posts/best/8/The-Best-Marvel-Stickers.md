### Size and Placement Options

Marvel offers stickers in various sizes, from small detail pieces to large wall decals. Consider whether you need small stickers for specific details or larger ones for prominent displays. Look for stickers that adhere well to both smooth and textured surfaces without leaving residue. Quality stickers can be removed and re-applied multiple times without damage. Avoid products that contain harsh chemicals in the adhesive, as these can damage surfaces or irritate sensitive skin. 


### Set Content and Theme Coherence

Complete sets often provide more coherent designs and better value than single pieces. Check if the sticker set includes diverse elements like characters, vehicles, or iconic locations. Ensure the themes and designs match your existing collection or intended display space.
