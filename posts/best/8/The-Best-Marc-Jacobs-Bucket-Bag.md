### Color and Design

Marc Jacobs offers an extensive range of colors and patterns, from neutral tones to bold statements. Consider how the bag's design elements complement your personal style and wardrobe. Popular options include metallic finishes, contrasting stitching, and the brand's signature double J logo.

The bucket bag's versatility lies in its multiple carrying options. Look for styles that offer both top handles and shoulder straps to adapt to your needs throughout the day.
