### What makes a one-piece swimsuit strapless?

A strapless one-piece swimsuit features a design that eliminates shoulder straps, with the top of the suit cupping directly under the bust. The suit's cups and shaping work together to support the breasts without the need for straps. 

The technique often involves molded cups that conform to the breast shape and a supportive underband around the torso. Some designs incorporate a racerback style at the back to maintain fit and prevent sagging. 


### How does the fit differ between competitive and casual strapless one-piece swimsuits?

Competitive swim suits prioritize maximum performance with features like seam-free construction, high elasticity, and body-hugging designs. They're typically made from specialized polyurethane or spandex blends. 

Casual strapless one-pieces focus on comfort and style, often featuring softer fabrics like chlorine-resistant polyester with built-in compression. These suits may have more relaxed fits and decorative elements.

### What maintenance requirements do strapless one-piece swimsuits have?

Machine wash cold with mild detergent, using a gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners as they can damage the suit's elasticity. Always turn the suit inside out before washing and reshape while damp to maintain its form. 

Avoid direct sun exposure and use a UV protection spray to extend the suit's lifespan. Store in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent tangling and maintain shape. 


### Can I wear a bra underneath the suit for added support?

While some people choose to wear a wireless bra or sports bra underneath a strapless one-piece, this can affect the suit's fit and shape. Most modern swimsuits provide adequate support without innerwear. 

If you prefer an added layer of support, opt for a bra that's built for swimming and doesn't have heavy straps or underwire. The bra should be slightly smaller than your usual size to prevent showing through the suit.

### What sizes and styles are typically available in strapless one-piece swimsuits?

Most manufacturers offer sizes ranging from small to plus sizes, though plus sizes may be less common. Styles vary from competition to casual, with options including modesty panels, athletic designs, and mermaid styles. 

Check the specific brand's sizing chart as sizing can vary between manufacturers. Consider ordering a size up if you have fuller breasts or prefer a more relaxed fit. 


### How long does a strapless one-piece swimsuit typically last?

Regular maintenance like washing after each use, avoiding harsh chemicals, and storing in a cool place can significantly extend the suit's lifespan. Replace your swimsuit if you notice significant deterioration in fit or color.
