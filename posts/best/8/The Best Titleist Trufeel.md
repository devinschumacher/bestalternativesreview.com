## Buyer's Guide

The Titleist Trufeel golf ball has earned a reputation for combining forgiveness with a solid feel. These golf balls feature a dual-core design that helps absorb impact energy, leading to reduced spin and increased distance for mis-hit shots. The urethane mantle provides a soft feel off the clubface, while the ionomer mantle helps maintain ball speed through impact. 


### Core Design and Materials

The dual-core structure consists of a soft inner core and a firmer outer core. This design helps to soften the initial impact and then transitions to a more traditional feel during the later stages of compression. The inner core often contains a higher percentage of liquid or flexible materials to enhance forgiveness, while the outer core provides the necessary stiffness for consistent ball speeds. 

![Titleist-Trufeel-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Titleist-Trufeel-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Urethane and Ionomer Mantles

The urethane mantle creates a balanced combination of feedback and feel. It's thin enough to provide immediate feedback from greenside rough while still maintaining a comfortable hit. The ionomer mantle surrounding the urethane helps to maintain ball speed by protecting the urethane layer from the full force of impact. Together, these layers create the distinctive feel that Titleist associates with their premium golf balls. 


### Spin and Trajectory Control

Developed with distance optimization in mind, these golf balls typically feature lower spin rates compared to their contemporaries. The dual-layer core and mantle combination helps reduce both backspin and sidespin, which can be particularly beneficial for players who struggle with consistent ball flight. This design choice allows the ball to maintain a higher initial velocity and fly straighter, even with off-center hits. 

![Titleist-Trufeel-3](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Titleist-Trufeel-3/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Roll and Durability

The urethane construction provides excellent roll characteristics, maintaining speed through the green and delivering consistent distances on shorter approach shots. The ionomer mantle adds durability by protecting the more sensitive urethane layer from damage during repeated impacts. This combination helps the ball maintain its dimensional integrity over time, which is crucial for consistent performance.
