### Construction Details

Authentic white leather Vans employ the brand's signature vulcanized rubber midsole bonded directly to the upper without adhesives. This construction method creates a lightweight, responsive ride. The leather should cover both the upper and sides of the midsole, with crisp lines and precise seams. The canvas lining within the leather should feel smooth and well-sewn. 


### Care Requirements

Regular white leather maintenance helps preserve the material's appearance and longevity. Use specially formulated leather cleaners and conditioners designed for white leather footwear. White leather is more susceptible to fading and marks compared to black, so protect them from direct sunlight and harsh chemicals. Consider the time and effort you're willing to invest in maintenance when selecting your pair.
