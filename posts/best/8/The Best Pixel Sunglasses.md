### Display Technology

Pixel sunglasses employ various display technologies, including OLED and LCD panels, to project information directly into the wearer's field of vision. OLED displays offer superior contrast and color accuracy but consume more power, while LCD panels provide better brightness and visibility under direct sunlight.
