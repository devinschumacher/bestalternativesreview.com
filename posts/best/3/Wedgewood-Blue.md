### What is Wedgwood Blue?

Wedgwood Blue refers to a specific blue shade that gained its name from the prominent English pottery manufacturer, Josiah Wedgwood. Introduced in 1775 as a tribute to the color found in sapphires, this rich, deep blue with a hint of green is highly prized for its allure. Visuals of a Wedgwood Blue item often evoke the feeling of the interior of a sapphire.
