### What are swivel barrel chairs?

Swivel barrel chairs are a type of seating furniture that combines a round, barrel-shaped backrest with a 360-degree rotation mechanism. This unique design allows the users to swivel and pivot easily, providing them with added comfort and freedom of movement. Swivel barrel chairs are available in a variety of styles, materials, and colors, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor settings.

### How do swivel barrel chairs work?

Swivel barrel chairs work through the use of a rotating mechanism, typically located at the base of the chair. This mechanism is usually made of durable, heavy-duty components that can withstand constant use. When the user wants to swivel, they simply apply pressure with their body weight to the chair's base and turn it in the desired direction. The chair will then slowly rotate, allowing the user to easily change the direction they are facing. 

Some models of swivel barrel chairs have a locking mechanism, which can be used to secure the chair in place when desired. This is especially useful in windy outdoor settings, where the chair might otherwise rotate on its own. The locking mechanism is usually very simple to operate—often involving a switch or turn of a knob located on the base of the chair.
