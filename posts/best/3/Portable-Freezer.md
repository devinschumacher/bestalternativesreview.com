### How does a portable freezer work?

A portable freezer works by using a cooling unit consisting of a compressor and an evaporator coil, which helps to remove heat from the air. This process is known as vapor compression refrigeration. The compressor raises the temperature and pressure of the refrigerant (a substance that absorbs and releases heat) vapor. The vapor then passes through an expansion valve, causing it to cool down and become a liquid. The cold liquid refrigerant now travels through the evaporator coil, absorbing heat from the air inside the freezer, which causes the air's temperature to drop. 

As the temperature inside the freezer lowers, it can maintain the required temperature for storing food items. The process continues as the cold refrigerant liquid absorbs more heat from external sources and returns to the compressor to repeat the cycle. This ensures that the freezer maintains its stable temperature for prolonged periods.
