### How should I measure my feet to ensure the right fit of platform wedge sandals?

To guarantee a proper fit, ensure you measure your feet while standing. Position a ruler or measuring tape on the ground and stand on it with your heel against the edge. Record the distance between the tip of your longest toe and the back of your heel. This measurement will give you the length of your foot in inches or centimeters, which you can refer to when selecting the ideal size platform wedge sandals.

Don't forget to assess the width of your foot when measuring. Utilize a measuring tape to determine your foot's width in inches or centimeters at its widest point. This information will help you find the proper width for the sandals' upper part or straps. Most brands provide size charts with measurements for both length and width, making it easier to pinpoint the perfect pair.
