### Are the tote bags machine washable?

Yes, tie-dye tote bags are machine washable, but we recommend hand washing them to preserve the intricate patterns and ensure vibrant colors last longer. To hand wash, simply soak the bag in cool to warm water with mild detergent and gently rub the stains or discoloration areas with your hands. Avoid rubbing too hard or using abrasive sponges, as this might wear out the patterns and damage the fabric. After washing, rinse the bag in cool water and let it air dry. 

If you prefer to use a washing machine, select a gentle cycle, cold water, and a mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they may damage the dye and affect the color retention. Always remove the tote bag from the washing machine as soon as the cycle is complete to prevent any color bleeding onto other items.

### What is the best way to dry the tie-dye tote bag?

We recommend air drying your tie-dye tote bag to preserve the vibrant colors and intricate patterns. Hang the bag on a clothesline or hangar in a well-ventilated area, away from direct sunlight, as excessive heat or exposure to the sun may cause some colors to fade or change. When air drying, the tote bag should be allowed to dry completely before folding or storing it. This helps maintain the shape and prevents any stress on the fabric. In case of rain or moisture, ensure the bag is completely dry before using it again to avoid any mold or mildew growth. 

If you need to partially or fully dry your tote bag, you can use a clothes dryer, but be cautious! Tie-dye fabrics can be prone to scorching and fading due to high heat. Select a low to medium heat setting and avoid using a tumble dryer, as this may damage the delicate patterns and colors. Always check the bag for any discoloration or damage after drying.
