### How do I clip in and out of Shimano clipless pedals?

To clip in, first position your foot over the pedal with the cleat recessed into the pedal's body. Then, apply pressure to the pedal while rotating your foot to engage the release mechanism within the pedal. Once clicked into place, your foot should feel securely attached to the pedal. To release, twist your body so that your leg moves slightly backward while pushing on the pedal with one of your feet. This will initiate the pedal's release mechanism, allowing your foot to be easily unclipped from the pedal. 

It is important to practice clipping in and out in a safe environment, such as an empty parking lot or grass field, and to gradually increase intensity to build your confidence and muscle memory. Practicing regularly will help you become more proficient and adapt to different situations on the trail or road.
