## Buyer's Guide

Welcome to our buyer's guide for a White Coat Rack. This guide will help you understand the key features, considerations, and general advice when purchasing a white coat rack for your professional or home use. 


### Important Features

When selecting a white coat rack, consider the following features to ensure it meets your needs and preferences: 
1) Material: Look for a high-quality and durable material, such as metal or plastic, that can withstand the weight of the white coat and other accessories. 2) Style: Choose a design that complements your home or office décor. There are many styles available, including traditional, modern, and minimalistic. 3) Height Adjustability: If possible, opt for a rack that has adjustable height settings so you can customize it to fit various spaces and white coat heights. 4) Storage Space: Some racks come with built-in storage compartments for additional organization of white coat accessories, such as stethoscopes, pens, or pocket protectors. 5) Finish: Ensure a smooth and non-slippery surface, preferably with a white epoxy coating to prevent dirt build-up and maintain a professional appearance.
