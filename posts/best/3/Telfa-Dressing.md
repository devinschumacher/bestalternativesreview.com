## FAQ


### What is Telfa Dressing?

Telfa Dressing is a type of sterile, non-adhesive wound dressing that is commonly used by medical professionals to treat various types of wounds. Telfa dressings are made of a polyurethane-polyisobutylene foam material that is designed to provide protection against infection while also promoting the healing process. 

These dressings come in different sizes and shapes, making them suitable for use on a wide range of wound types, including cuts, abrasions, and surgical incisions. The non-stick material ensures that the dressing does not adhere to the wound, making it easy to remove without causing further injury to the affected area. 


### How does Telfa Dressing work?

Telfa Dressing works by creating a moist wound environment, which is beneficial for the healing process.
