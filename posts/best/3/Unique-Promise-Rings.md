### What are promise rings?

A promise ring is an engagement ring, but not quite. Unlike traditional engagement rings that usually represent a commitment to marriage, these rings simply represent a commitment of love and friendship. They are usually exchanged to symbolize a milestone in a relationship or show a promise to remain loyal and faithful to each other. 

For example, a couple might exchange promise rings to demonstrate their commitment to each other before they're ready to get married. These rings can be exchanged as a symbol of future commitment, to remind each other of a special moment, or simply as tokens of their love and dedication to one another. 


### How are promise rings different from engagement rings?

The main difference between a promise ring and an engagement ring is the purpose they serve in a relationship. While an engagement ring is typically given to signify a commitment to marriage, a promise ring symbolizes a commitment to love and loyalty in a relationship, without necessarily indicating an intent to marry. 

 Promise rings often come with more flexible meanings and are typically less expensive than engagement rings. They can be given at any stage of a relationship - be it the early stage or a more mature one. Moreover, promise rings come in a wide variety of designs, metals, and stones, offering customers more options to choose from.

### Can promise rings be exchanged for engagement rings?

Yes, in some cases, promise rings can become engagement rings. While traditional engagement rings often come with a more significant price tag, promise rings are typically less expensive. They can be seen as a stepping stone towards an engagement ring. Couples could initially exchange promise rings, and later upgrade to an engagement ring when they decide to get married. 

For instance, a couple might start by exchanging simple promise rings - like a silver band, or a simple gemstone ring - and then later they can upgrade to a more expensive and elaborate engagement ring that symbolizes their commitment to marriage. This allows couples to first establish trust and commitment before they take the next step of marriage. 


### How can I wear my promise ring?

Wearing a promise ring is a deeply personal choice, reflecting individual style and taste. It depends on the nature of the relationship, the meaning of the ring for the giver and the recipient, and the wearer's unique sense of style. The ring can be worn on various fingers, depending on what feels most comfortable and looks best to you.
