### How do I maintain my patio storage bench?

To ensure your patio storage bench remains in good condition, follow these maintenance tips. For wooden benches, apply a weather-resistant sealant or stain yearly to protect the wood from water damage, fading, and rot. If the bench comes with a lift-up seat, make sure to close it gently to avoid damages over time. Additionally, you may need to oil the hinges and other moving parts to ensure smooth operation. 

For metal benches, clean the surface regularly using a mild soap and water solution. Avoid using abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the finish. Dry the bench thoroughly after cleaning, and oil the moving parts to prevent rusting. Protect metal storage compartments with a clear weather-resistant sealant to prevent them from fading or deteriorating over time
