### What is the difference between freestanding and semi-freestanding tents?

Freestanding tents have detachable poles that do not connect directly to the tent body via sleeves or grommets. Semi-freestanding tents are designed with poles that are partially or fully connected to the tent body, offering both the convenience of a freestanding tent and the weather-resistance of a full-coverage tent. These tents can be staked down for improved stability and are suitable for various camping environments, including cold weather conditions.
