### What is the difference between a platform bed and a box spring bed?

A platform bed and a box spring bed are two different types of beds that have some key differences. A platform bed is a type of bed frame that has a flat, solid platform that supports the mattress. This platform is usually made of a sturdy material like wood or metal and provides a stable and supportive surface for the mattress. 

A box spring bed, on the other hand, is a type of bed that comes with a separate box spring that provides support for the mattress. The box spring is usually made of wood or metal slats that are covered with fabric or upholstery. This type of bed is often more expensive than a platform bed, but it can provide better support and comfort for the mattress.
