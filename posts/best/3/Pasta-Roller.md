### What are common mistakes to avoid when using a pasta maker?

Overworking the dough or excessive flouring can make the dough tough and elastic. To prevent these issues, knead the dough just enough to create a smooth and cohesive consistency. Make sure not to over-flour the dough, as this can cause uneven rolling and gummy noodles.

Incorrect blade alignment or inaccurately adjusted thickness settings can lead to irregular or misshapen noodles. Ensure you follow the manufacturer's instructions and maintain your pasta maker regularly to achieve optimal performance and results.
