## Buyer's Guide

Welcome to our buyer's guide for steam cleaners specifically designed for couches. In this section, we will discuss the essential features, considerations, and general advice that you should take into account when purchasing a steam cleaner for your couch. Let's dive in! 


### Types of Steam Cleaners

There are two primary types of steam cleaners available in the market: upright and portable handheld models. Upright steam cleaners are designed to clean large areas quickly and efficiently. They have a powerful motor, which enables them to generate high-pressure steam, making them suitable for removing stubborn stains and deep-seated dirt from your couch. Portable handheld steam cleaners, on the other hand, are more versatile and lightweight. They are perfect for reaching tight spaces and corners and are ideal for cleaning upholstery.

### Steam Output and Power

The steam output and power of a steam cleaner play a crucial role in determining its cleaning efficiency. Higher steam output and power enable the cleaner to generate more steam, which in turn helps to penetrate deep into the fibers of your couch fabric and remove dirt and stains more effectively. Look for a steam cleaner with a steam output of at least 1000 watts for optimal results. 


### Cleaning Pads and Attachments

The cleaning pads and attachments are another essential aspect to consider when purchasing a steam cleaner for your couch. Ensure that the cleaner comes with a variety of pads and attachments, including upholstery-specific pads, to clean your couch efficiently. This will help to protect the fabric from heat damage and ensure that no water is absorbed into the cushions.

### Water Tank Capacity

The water tank capacity of a steam cleaner is also an important factor to consider. A larger water tank will allow you to clean for longer without needing to refill it. Look for a steam cleaner with a water tank capacity of at least 500 milliliters for the best cleaning experience.
