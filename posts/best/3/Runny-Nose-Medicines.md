### What are the different types of runny nose medicines available in the market?

There are several types of runny nose medicines available in the market, including anti-histamine (diphenhydramine), corticosteroid (budesonide), and decongestant (phenylephrine). Anti-histamines help reduce swelling of the nasal passages, while corticosteroids help decrease inflammation. Decongestants help relieve nasal congestion by narrowing blood vessels. 

Each type of medicine has its own set of benefits and possible side effects. It's essential to choose the right medication based on your symptoms, overall health, and any underlying medical conditions. Consulting a healthcare professional can be helpful in determining the best option for you or your child. 


### When should I use a runny nose medicine?
