### Base Material

Rainbow paper can be made from various materials such as cardstock, felt, or even metallic substances. Each material offers its unique benefits, and choosing the right one depends on your specific use case. Cardstock is generally the most affordable option and suitable for everyday use. Felt paper, on the other hand, provides a softer touch and is ideal for more delicate projects. Lastly, metallic rainbow paper can add a touch of luxury and elegance to your creations, but it may be more expensive and have a different texture. 


### Size and Weight

The size of rainbow paper you choose will depend on the projects you plan to use it for. Standard sizes include 8.5 x 11 inches, 11 x 17 inches, and 12 x 12 inches. If you require larger sheets for banners, posters, or scrapbooking, consider purchasing sheets in the 18 x 24 or 22 x 30 inch range. The weight of the paper can also influence its usability, with lighter paper suitable for projects that require cutting and folding, while thicker paper is ideal for more durable projects like gift tags and greeting cards.

### What is Rainbow Paper?

Rainbow Paper is a type of paper that is designed with an assortment of colorful patterns, often in a gradient or alternating hues of various colors. It is popularly used for different creative projects such as scrapbooking, greeting cards, invitations, and wrapping paper, among others. The vibrant colors make it a great choice for adding a pop of color to any project.
