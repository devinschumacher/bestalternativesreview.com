### How do I care for yellow strappy heels?

Effective care for yellow strappy heels involves regular cleaning and maintenance to ensure their longevity and appearance. Start by gently brushing the heels to remove any loose dirt or debris. A soft-bristle brush or an old toothbrush can be used for this purpose. Always brush in a light, even manner to avoid scratches or damage.

For deeper cleaning, use a mixture of mild dish soap and warm water on a damp cloth to clean the shoes. Make sure the shoes are not soaked in water as that can damage the materials. Dry them completely before storing them away. Regularly applying leather or shoe conditioner to the straps is also recommended to maintain their elasticity and extend the life of the shoes.
