### How does a programmable coffee maker work?

A programmable coffee maker works by using an electric heating element to heat water to the desired brewing temperature. When the brewing process is complete, the machine signals that the coffee is ready, usually with a beep or a light on the control panel. 

Some programmable coffee makers have a feature that allows users to set a specific temperature for the brewing process. This is handy for those who prefer their coffee at a particular temperature.
