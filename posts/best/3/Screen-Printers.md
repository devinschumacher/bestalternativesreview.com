### What is a screen printer?

A screen printer is a device used for transferring ink or other print materials onto various surfaces, such as fabric, paper, and other materials. The screen printing process involves using a mesh screen with an emulsion coating to block out areas that are not intended to be printed. The ink is then applied and passed through the screen onto the desired surface. 

This technique is often used for producing high-quality, long-lasting prints, especially when it comes to mass production or printing on durable materials like clothing or accessories. Screen printers are an essential piece of equipment for various industries, including textiles, graphic arts, and promotional products.

Automatic printers are designed for high-volume production and may include a variety of advanced features to ensure maximum efficiency and output.
