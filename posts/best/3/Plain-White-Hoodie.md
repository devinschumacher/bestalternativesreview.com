### What are the major characteristics of a basic white hoodie?

A basic white hoodie typically showcases a basic design comprising of a hood, making it an adaptable and simple piece to match with different outfits. It can be used as an everyday garment or worn under jackets during colder months for extra warmth. The fabric is generally made from cotton or a cotton blend, providing both comfort and breathability. Some hoodies might incorporate drawstrings to adjust the fit or hood size. 

The construction of a basic white hoodie typically entails durability, with reinforced seams and strong cuffs to prevent fraying or wear. This ensures that the hoodie can endure regular use without losing its shape or quality. Furthermore, plain white hoodies are accessible in a variety of sizes to accommodate diverse body types. 


### How should I maintain my basic white hoodie?

To maintain your basic white hoodie, adhere to the manufacturer's washing instructions. Typically, most cotton hoodies should be washed from the inside out in cold water to keep their color intact and prevent shrinkage. To achieve the best results, avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they may harm the fabric or cause it to lose its shape over time. 

When drying, avoid high heat or tumble drying, as this may weaken the fabric and cause it to shrink. Instead, air-dry your hoodie, which will help preserve its shape and prevent any stretching or distortion. Ironing is usually unnecessary, but if you must iron the hoodie, use the lowest heat setting and do so only when the garment is damp. This will help lessen the risk of scorching the fabric.
