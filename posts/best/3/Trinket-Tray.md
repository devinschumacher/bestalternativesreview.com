## Buyer's Guide

A trinket tray is a perfect addition to any living space, offering both function and style. These trays provide a convenient and stylish place to store small items, making them easy to find and organize. When considering buying a trinket tray, it's essential to keep several factors in mind. This buyer's guide will cover the key features and considerations to help you make an informed decision when purchasing a trinket tray. 


### Size and Shape

The size and shape of the trinket tray are essential considerations. Trays come in various shapes and sizes, and it's crucial to choose one that fits your space and needs. Shapes include round, square, rectangular, and even irregular shapes.
