### How should I store velvety red wines?

Velvety red wines, like most wines, should be stored at a consistent temperature between 50°F (10°C) and 65°F (18°C). Exposure to direct sunlight, heat, and vibration should be minimized to prevent premature aging and oxidation, which can negatively impact the wine's quality and flavor. Generally, a wine refrigerator or a cool, dark area in your home is ideal for storing wines. 

When storing, ensure that the bottles are upright and not laid on their side. This position allows the sediment to settle to the bottom of the bottle, reducing interference with the wine's flavors. Always keep the bottles horizontal when opening, to avoid any unwanted sediment. Once opened, velvety red wines should be consumed within 3-4 days, as exposure to air will cause the wine to oxidize and lose its qualities.
