### Which materials are commonly used to make petite armoires?

Petite armoires come in various materials depending on style, design, and price. The most common and widely used materials include solid wood, wood veneer, engineered wood, and even metal and glass. Engineered wood is a material made from layers of different types of wood, glued together and finished to resemble solid wood. Metal and glass materials can also be used in the construction of petite armoires, typically in more contemporary styles.
