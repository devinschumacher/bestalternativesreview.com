### What is a screen magnifier?

A screen magnifier is a software or hardware tool that allows users to enlarge the displayed content on a computer or mobile device screen. They are particularly useful for individuals with visual impairments or those who require better visibility while reading text or navigating through menus and controls. 

Screen magnifiers can magnify the screen's content to different degrees of magnification and offer various customization options to suit individual preferences. They may come in the form of standalone applications, browser extensions, or built-in accessibility features of operating systems and devices. 


### What are the benefits of using a screen magnifier?
