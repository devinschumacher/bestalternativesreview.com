### How do I care for small plants?

The care for small plants may vary depending on the species. However, most small plants require similar levels of care, which include: proper drainage, appropriate lighting, and consistent watering. Remember that overwatering is one of the most common causes of plant death, so ensure your pot has proper drainage to prevent water from pooling at the bottom. 

For most small plants, indirect sunlight is best. Avoid direct exposure to the sun, which can cause the plant to dry out too quickly. When it comes to watering, it's always best to use a watering can or a watering spout, as this allows for better control over the amount of water you're providing. Most small plants prefer the soil to be moist but not soggy. If you're unsure of how much water your plant needs, it's usually best to under-water rather than over-water.
