### Tips for Maintaining Your Tufted Wingback Chair

To ensure your Tufted Wingback Chair looks as good as new for as long as possible, follow these simple maintenance tips:

* Use a soft-bristled brush or vacuum cleaner on a gentle setting to remove dust and dirt. Avoid using water or abrasive cleaners, as they can damage the fabric or stain the wood.

* For a more thorough clean, use a mild, upholstery-safe detergent diluted with water. Apply it with a soft cloth, working in small sections. Remember to rinse thoroughly with a damp cloth, and let the chair air dry to prevent mold or mildew formation.

* To prevent wear and tear, consider using slipcovers or chair pads for added protection. These can be removed for cleaning or washing as needed.
