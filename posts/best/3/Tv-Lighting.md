### How can I avoid glare from TV lighting?

Glare can be minimized by choosing the right type of lighting and its positioning. For TVs, it's generally recommended to position lighting sources below eye level or to the side to avoid direct reflection. Additionally, using softer lighting with frosted or opaque shades can help reduce glare. Opt for lights with a lower-intensity bulb or utilize dimmers to control the light level. Choosing the appropriate lighting color temperature, such as a warm-toned light, can also help reduce glare on your TV screen. 

If possible, experiment with different positions and lighting types to find the optimal setup that minimizes glare while providing the best TV viewing experience. Using glare-reducing software or settings on your TV can also help further reduce the impact of glare from TV lighting on your viewing experience.
