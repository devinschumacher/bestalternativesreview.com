### How do I measure my Travel Trailer for a customized cover?

 To ensure the perfect fit for your travel trailer, accurate measurements are vital. You will need to provide three key measurements: 
1) Length - Measure from the front of the trailer to the rear, including bumpers or platforms, 2) Width - Measure from one side of the trailer to the other, including awnings or other protrusions, and 3) Height - Measure from the ground to the highest point of the roof, including all features, like air conditioners or antennas. Some manufacturers offer online measurement tools or mobile apps for a more streamlined process. Remember to account for the type of trailer, number of slides, and any additional exterior features, like lighting or vent systems, for the most precise and suitable cover.


### How do I install and remove a Travel Trailer Cover?

 Proper installation and removal of a Travel Trailer Cover are essential for maintaining the trailer's protection and longevity of the cover. To do so, follow these steps: 
1) Ensure the trailer is parked on a flat, level surface and that it is stable, 2) Make sure any slide-outs or awnings are retracted, 3) Park the trailer in a position where the cover's door and zipper can be easily accessed, 4) Carefully unfold the cover and align it with the trailer, and 5) Secure the cover using Velcro, bungee cords, or provided straps, and close the zipper or door securely. Removing the cover involves the reverse process.
