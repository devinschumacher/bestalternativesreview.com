### How do I properly maintain my Ninja Foodi Blender?

Maintain your blender by cleaning the container, lid, and blades after each use. Hand wash with warm, soapy water, ensuring no food residue remains. For extra stubborn stains, use a non-abrasive cleaner. 

Store the blender components in a dry place when not in use. The container can be refrigerated if you plan to use it later, but make sure it's completely dry before blending. 


### What types of containers come with the Ninja Foodi Blender?

The standard containers range from 1.5 to 3.5 quarts and are made of durable BPA-free plastic. These containers are microwave-safe for quick reheating and dishwasher-safe for easy cleaning. 

Higher-end models may include additional containers with different capacities and materials, such as stainless steel for premium versions. 


### Can I blend hot liquids in my Ninja Foodi Blender?

Yes, the blender is designed to handle hot liquids, but it's recommended to blend hot liquids in smaller batches to prevent overflow and ensure even blending. Start with a smaller container and gradually increase the amount as needed. 

Allow hot liquids to cool slightly before blending to prevent splattering and damage to the blender's motor and heating elements. 


### What types of blades come with the Ninja Foodi Blender?

The blender typically includes standard blades for everyday blending tasks. Some models offer additional blade attachments, such as a chopping blade or smoothie blade, which are specifically designed for certain types of food preparation.

### How long does the blender take to heat up hot liquids?
