### What is the 1800 Milenio?

The 1800 Milenio is a Spanish luxury sedan that was produced from 2003 to 2008. It's known for its Italian-inspired design and premium materials. 

Developed by Pininfarina, the vehicle combines a sleek sports sedan body with a powerful V8 engine, offering both style and performance. 


### What makes the 1800 Milenio unique?

Unlike other Spanish cars of the time, the 1800 Milenio features a distinct Pininfarina design, setting it apart from domestic competitors. 

The car's most distinctive feature is its unique front grille inspired by classic Ferrari designs, paired with aerodynamic bodywork that hints at its sports sedan roots.

### What are the main technical specifications?

Power comes from a 4.0-liter V8 engine producing 300 hp, mated to a five-speed automatic transmission. Full power is transferred to the rear wheels via a limited-slip differential. 

The car rides on a 105mm lower suspension setup compared to standard 1800 models, with adjustable dampers and sport-tuned suspension settings available as an option.

Entry-level models with basic equipment typically sold for around €40,000 to €45,000 when new. In excellent condition today, you might find one for €20,000 to €25,000.

Like many European cars of the 2000s, the 1800 Milenio can suffer from corrosion issues, particularly around windows and door pillars if not properly maintained.

### How do I maintain a 1800 Milenio?

To maintain optimal performance, change the engine oil every 10,000 km (6,200 miles), and check the transmission fluid monthly for older cars, or every 15,000 km for newer ones. 

The front suspension components should be inspected regularly for wear, and the brakes should be serviced every 20,000 km to ensure proper functioning.
