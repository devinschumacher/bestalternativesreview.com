## FAQ


### What are Melona Bars?

Melona Bars are a popular candy bar featuring an outer layer of candy with an inner chewy center. The classic flavors include Watermelon and Orange, though other varieties like Blue Raspberry and Cherry are available. 

These bars combine the refreshing taste of watermelon or orange with a chewy texture, making them a favorite among young and old candy enthusiasts alike. 


### What's inside a Melona Bar?

Each Melona Bar consists of a candy shell surrounding a chewy center. The candy shell provides the distinctive texture and flavor, while the chewy center contains the fruit base. 

The specific ingredients can vary by flavor, but common elements include sugar, corn syrup, and artificial flavorings that give each variety its unique taste. 


### How do I eat a Melona Bar?

The best way to enjoy a Melona Bar is to break it in half and bite into the candy shell, experiencing the burst of flavor followed by the chewy center. 

Alternatively, you can simply unwrap the bar and bite directly into it, though this may result in a more rapid consumption due to the candy shell melting. 


### Are Melona Bars suitable for diabetics?

Due to their high sugar content, Melona Bars are not recommended for individuals following a diabetic diet. They contain both natural and artificial sweeteners, making them unsuitable for diabetic consumption. 

If you have diabetes or are following a specialized diet, consider alternative candy options that meet your dietary requirements. 


### How should Melona Bars be stored?

For best results, store Melona Bars in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. When properly stored, they can maintain their texture and flavor for several weeks. 

Avoid storing them in humid areas or the refrigerator, as exposure to moisture can affect their texture and accelerate melting. 


### What's the recommended serving size?

The standard serving size for a Melona Bar is the entire bar, which provides a single serving of candy. Each bar contains approximately 140 calories and about 25 grams of sugar. 

While these bars can be enjoyed at any time, consuming them as part of a balanced diet helps maintain overall nutritional balance.
