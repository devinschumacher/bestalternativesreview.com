### Are Gucci perfumes suitable for sensitive skin?

Gucci perfumes contain a blend of natural and synthetic ingredients, which can vary between fragrances. If you have sensitive skin, it's recommended to perform a patch test before full application. Test the perfume on a small area of skin, such as the inner wrist, and wait 24 hours to ensure there's no adverse reaction. 

For individuals with severe allergies or fragrance sensitivities, it's advisable to consult a dermatologist. Gucci offers a diverse range of fragrances, so if you experience any irritation, you can try a different scent within the collection.

### How should I store my Gucci perfume to maintain its quality?

To preserve the quality and longevity of your Gucci perfume, store it in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. The ideal storage temperature is between 60-75°F (15-24°C). 

Avoid placing the perfume near heat sources, such as radiators or ovens, which can degrade the fragrances over time. Keeping the bottle upright and protecting it from impact helps maintain its integrity. 


### What is the typical shelf life of Gucci perfume?

Gucci perfume typically has a shelf life of 3-5 years when properly stored. After this period, the scent may begin to fade and lose its freshness. 

To determine freshness, check the expiration date if it's included. If no date is present, consider the age of your bottle. Keep your perfume away from direct sunlight and temperature fluctuations to maximize its longevity.
