## FAQ


### What is human crochet hair?

Human crochet hair is a form of hair weaving that uses human hair to create braids and patterns. This technique allows for a natural look while providing length and volume. 

Each strand is attached to a base with specialized crochet hooks, creating realistic-looking extensions that blend seamlessly with natural hair. 


### How long does human crochet hair typically last?

With proper care, human crochet hair can last between 6-12 months. Factors like daily care, lifestyle, and maintenance habits affect the longevity of the extensions. 

Regular maintenance including weekly washes and monthly deep conditioning helps extend the life of your human crochet hair. 


### Is human crochet hair suitable for all hair types?

Most hair types can accommodate human crochet hair, but very fine or thin hair might not support the additional weight. Extremely curly or kinky textures may also need extra consideration. 

Always consult a professional stylist to determine if human crochet hair is the right choice for your specific hair type and texture. 


### What maintenance is required for human crochet hair?

Weekly washing with mild shampoo and cool water is recommended. Avoid using harsh chemicals or heat styling tools, as they can damage the hair extensions. 

Deep conditioning treatments every 4-6 weeks help maintain the health and shine of the extensions. 


### Can human crochet hair be colored and styled?

Coloring is possible with specially formulated hair dye products. Some natural color variations may appear with light processing. 

The hair can be styled with heat tools, but excessive use can damage the extensions. Always use appropriate heat protection products when styling. 


### How should human crochet hair be stored when not in use?

Store the hair in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. The base should remain assembled to maintain its shape. 

Regularly brush the hair with a wide-tooth comb to prevent tangles and maintain its condition during storage.
