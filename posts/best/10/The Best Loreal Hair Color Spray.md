### Permanent vs. Temporary Formulas

Permanent hair color sprays undergo a chemical process to alter hair pigmentation, while temporary formulas simply coat the hair shaft. The choice between the two depends on whether you're looking for lasting results or a seasonal color change. 


### Application and Coverage

Factors like hair type, color intensity, and application technique affect coverage. Fine hair may require multiple applications for even results, while coarser hair might need less product for full coverage. 


### Results and Maintenance

Color retention varies based on factors such as hair type and environmental conditions. Regular touch-ups may be necessary to maintain the desired shade. Following the product instructions helps achieve the best possible results and minimize potential issues.
