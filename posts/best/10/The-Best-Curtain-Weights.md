## Buyer's Guide

Curtain weights anchor curtains at the bottom, preventing them from pooling or moving too freely when the window is opened. This guide outlines key characteristics to consider when selecting curtain weights, helping you find the right balance of functionality and style.

### What are curtain weights, and why are they important?

### How do curtain weights differ from other curtain hardware?

Curtain weights are specifically designed to address bottom hem issues, while other hardware like curtain rods, rings, and poles manages the top and middle sections of curtains. Weights focus on the lowermost part of the curtain. 

While similar products like bottom bars or tabs serve related purposes, curtain weights are generally more discreet and easier to install for most curtain styles and lengths.
