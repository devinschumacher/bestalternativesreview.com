## Buyer's Guide

Adding a boho blanket to your home decor can transform your space with warm, natural textures and vibrant colors. These lightweight, multi-purpose throws combine functionality with a free-spirited aesthetic, perfect for both casual living rooms and cozy bedrooms. 


### Material Quality and Durability

Look for boho blankets made from high-quality materials like cotton, linen, or blends that offer softness and longevity. Organic options are ideal for those who prefer natural fibers. Ensure the blanket is well-sewn with reinforced edges to prevent fraying.

### Size and Proportion

Standard boho blanket sizes range from 50" to 70" in length and 40" to 60" in width. Consider the space where you'll use it: floor-length versions need more room to spread out, while smaller options work well on beds or sofas. The blanket should complement your furniture while providing ample coverage.

### What is a boho blanket?

A boho blanket combines natural materials and colorful patterns to create a warmth-meets-bohemian-eclecticism statement piece for your living space. Common elements include soft wool, cotton, and linen blends with intricate hand-knitted designs, tribal patterns, and earthy color palettes. 

The term "boho" stands for bohemian, reflecting the free-spirited, artistic influence behind these blankets - they blend traditional crafting techniques with contemporary design sensibilities. 


### What makes a good boho blanket?

A high-quality boho blanket should feature natural fibers like alpaca wool, cotton, or linen for lasting comfort and durability. The weave quality is important, with dense, tightly-knit fabrics providing better longevity than loosely spun blends. 

Look for unique, hand-woven patterns like macrame, tapestry, or patchwork that showcase traditional craftsmanship. Earthy color combinations of warm neutrals with pops of rich browns, deep greens, and mustard yellows create the authentic boho look. 

![Boho-Blanket-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Boho-Blanket-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How should a boho blanket fit with my existing decor?

Boho blankets work best in environments with warm neutral tones and organic textures. They complement rustic wooden furniture, woven baskets, plush indoor plants, and natural materials like stone or rattan. 

The blanket's size should match the piece it's placed on - throw pillows look great with smaller square throws, while larger living room pieces work well with generously-sized blankets. 


### How should I care for a boho blanket?

Most boho blankets are machine washable in cold water with a gentle cycle and mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals that could damage the natural fibers. 

Air drying or using a low heat setting on your dryer helps maintain the blanket's shape and prevents shrinkage. Store your boho blanket loosely folded to keep the weave from becoming bunched or warped.

### What dimensions do boho blankets typically come in?

Common throw blanket sizes range from 50x60 inches (127x152 cm) to 60x80 inches (152x203 cm), suitable for most standard living room furniture. Larger sizes like 72x90 inches (183x229 cm) work well as statement pieces on beds or as floor coverings. 

Smaller 20x20-inch (51x51 cm) versions are perfect for adding texture to headboards or as decorative pillows. Always measure your furniture before purchasing to ensure a proper fit. 


### How can I incorporate a boho blanket into my space?

Use boho blankets as oversized throw pillows for your sofa or bed, folding them slightly at the corners for a casual, lived-in look. Layer them over existing blankets for added warmth and texture. 

Hang them as wall art, featuring the natural frills on display, or drape them over furniture like a sofa-back throw for an instant aesthetic upgrade.
