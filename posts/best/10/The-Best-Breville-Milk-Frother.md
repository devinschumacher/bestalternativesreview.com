## FAQ


### What is a Breville Milk Frother?

A Breville Milk Frother is small kitchen appliance designed to quickly and efficiently froth milk for coffee and tea. These compact devices heat and froth milk simultaneously, making them ideal for home use and apartment living where counter space is limited. 

These appliances typically feature stainless steel construction and a straightforward controls system. They come in both corded and cordless models, with capacities ranging from 500ml to 600ml to accommodate different brewing needs. 


### What types of milk can I froth?

Standard Breville milk frothers are compatible with all types of cow's milk, including whole milk, skim milk, and lactose-free options. For the best results, use fresh milk with a temperature of around 6-8 degrees Celsius (43-46°F). 

These appliances may also work with plant-based milk alternatives like almond milk, soy milk, or oat milk, though results may vary. For optimal frothing, blend these alternatives with a small amount of water first to prevent excessive foaming. 


### How do I clean my Breville Milk Frother?

After each use, wipe down the exterior of your milk frother with a damp cloth. For the interior components, remove the milk jug and steam wand. Wash these parts thoroughly with soap and water, ensuring all components are completely dry before reassembly. 

Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials, as they can damage the finish. For stubborn stains, a mixture of warm water and mild dish soap may be sufficient for effective cleaning. 


### What is the capacity of a Breville Milk Frother?

The typical capacity of a Breville Milk Frother ranges between 500ml and 600ml. This size is standard for home use and provides enough frothed milk for one to two cups of coffee or tea. 

If you frequently need larger quantities, consider models with adjustable settings that allow you to froth smaller or larger volumes. However, keep in mind that exceeding the maximum capacity may lead to poor frothing results or damage to the appliance. 


### How long does it take to froth milk?

The average Breville Milk Frother can froth milk in approximately 30-45 seconds. The exact timing may vary based on the milk type, starting temperature, and the speed setting you choose. 

For best results, start with a room temperature milk that is between 6-8 degrees Celsius (43-46°F). If your milk is warmer, the frothing process may take slightly longer. 


### What are the power requirements for a Breville Milk Frother?

Most Breville Milk Frothers operate on standard household voltage, with a power rating between 500-900 watts. They require a standard electrical outlet and plug type, making them suitable for both homes and apartments. 

Some models include a universal power adapter that allows use with different voltage standards, including European and Australian outlets. Always check your specific model's specifications for the most accurate requirements.
