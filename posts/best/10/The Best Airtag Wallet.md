## FAQ


### What is an Airtag Wallet?

The Airtag Wallet is a small, discreet tracking device designed to help users keep track of their valuable items, including wallets. It's part of Apple's Airtag ecosystem and works in conjunction with the Find My network to locate lost items. 

Similar to other Airtags, the Wallet model has a built-in U1 chip for precise location tracking, up to 200 feet indoors. It's activated through the Find My app and can be customized with a personal message that appears when nearby. 


### How does the Airtag Wallet work?

Once activated through the Find My app, your Airtag Wallet sends out a signal that can be picked up by other Apple devices in the area. When a participating device detects the signal, it can display your contact information or a custom message you've set up. 

For precise tracking, users can use the Find My app to request detailed location data from nearby Apple devices. This works best in crowded areas where your device might lose signal. 


### What makes the Airtag Wallet different from other tracking devices?

The Airtag Wallet stands out with its small, wallet-friendly design. Unlike larger tracking devices, it's discreet enough to attach to your actual wallet while remaining functional. 

Its built-in speaker allows it to emit a sound when within close proximity to your phone, helping you locate it quickly if you misplace it in your belongings. 


### Can multiple people use the same Airtag Wallet?

Yes, multiple people can use the same Airtag Wallet. When you find the device, you can add it to your Find My network and start tracking it independently of others who might also be using it. 

However, if you give the wallet to someone else, they'll need to remove it from their account in the Find My app before you can track it again. 


### What features does the Airtag Wallet have?

The Airtag Wallet includes a built-in U1 chip for precise location tracking, a built-in speaker for sound alerts, and supports both Bluetooth and Wi-Fi connectivity. It's water-resistant and can be personalized with a custom message through the Find My app. 

The device also works with Apple's Find My network to help locate the wallet, even if it's not connected to any device. It charges via USB-C and can last up to a year on a single charge. 


### How much does an Airtag Wallet cost?

The Airtag Wallet retails for $29 in most regions. It's available in multiple color variants to match your personal style. 

Like other Airtags, the wallet can be purchased directly from the Apple Store or authorized resellers. It's often available with discounts during sales or when purchased alongside other Apple devices.
