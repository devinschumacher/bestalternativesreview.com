## Buyer's Guide

A great poster frame can transform a blank wall into a personalized display area. When choosing a frame for your artwork or photograph, consider the frame's size, style, durability, and protective features. 


### Size Compatibility

Measure the artwork you want to frame and select a frame that matches the dimensions. Popular frame sizes include standard print sizes like 8x10 inches or 16x20 inches. Consider frames with adjustable hardware for precise sizing when purchasing unframed prints.

### Style and Material

Frame styles range from simple matte finishes to ornate wood frames. Common materials include metal, wood, and plastic. Choose a frame that complements your room's decor. For outdoor use, select weather-resistant materials or opt for indoor display. 


### Protection Features

Quality frames include UV protection to prevent fading, glass protection for the artwork, and acid-free matting to prevent damage. Consider frames with adjustable tension for proper artwork fit and protection against shifting.

### Installation and Hanging

Frames with hanging hardware and mounting options increase versatility. Common mounting methods include D-rings, wire hangers, and sawtooth hangers. Check the frame's weight capacity to match your artwork's size and weight.
