## Buyer's Guide

A Smeg coffee grinder combines the brand's signature design elements with high-quality coffee grinding capabilities. Understanding the key features and considerations can help you select the right grinder for your needs. 


### Capacity and Grind Size

Consider how much coffee you plan to grind regularly. Smeg grinders typically offer capacities ranging from 12 to 22 ounces. The grind size options usually include espresso, medium, and coarse, allowing you to adjust the setting based on your brewing method and preference. 


### Convenience Features

Look for grinders with convenient features such as automatic shut-off after grinding, easy-to-clean parts, and simple operation. Some models include LED displays showing the grinding time and progress, enhancing both functionality and user experience. 


### Maintenance and Cleaning

When selecting a Smeg coffee grinder, consider how easy it is to maintain and clean. Look for grinders with dishwasher-safe components and straightforward disassembly instructions. The blade grinding area should be accessible for thorough cleaning to prevent coffee residue buildup. 


### Power and Performance

Smeg grinders are known for their powerful motors. The grinder's voltage rating (usually 120V) and motor wattage determine its performance. For standard coffee brewing, a motor between 200-300 watts is sufficient. Higher wattage can be beneficial for tougher coffee beans or increased grinding volume.
