## Buyer's Guide

Sts blue jeans combine style and durability, making them a popular choice for both casual and active wear. When selecting a pair, consider several key factors to find the right balance of quality, comfort, and fit. 


### Fit and Construction

Sts offers various fits from slim to relaxed, so start by choosing the style that best matches your body type and preference. The construction quality, such as double-needle sewing and reinforced stress points, affects longevity and wear durability. 


### Denim Quality

Higher-quality denim typically means more natural stretch, better dimensional stability, and reduced shrinking. Look for weight options between 13 and 17 ounces for durability while maintaining flexibility. 


### Bleaching and Treatment

Wet-treated jeans have less fading and wrinkling, making them a practical choice for daily wear. Dry-bleached jeans achieve the similar look with less fade resistance, ideal for those who like brighter colors. 


### Care Requirements

While Sts jeans are machine washable, the care label indicates specific settings for optimal results. Gentle cycles with cold water and minimal soak time preserve the fabric's integrity and color.
