### What makes All Clad griddles different from other cookware?

All Clad griddles combine their signature Tri-Ply construction with a ribbed surface that promotes even heat distribution and prevents food from sticking. The aluminum core is sandwiched between stainless steel layers, creating a cookware piece that's both lightweight and responsive to temperature changes. 

The grate-like surface is designed to prevent foods from slipping while cooking, making it ideal for burgers, steaks, and other grilled items. Available in various sizes, All Clad griddles offer versatility for different cooking needs. 


### How do I properly care for an All Clad griddle?

Hand wash the griddle with mild soap and warm water immediately after use to prevent food residue from setting. Avoid using abrasive pads or harsh cleaners that could scratch the cookware's surface. For thorough cleaning, you can soak the griddle in warm, soapy water for 10-15 minutes before scrubbing. 

To maintain the non-stick properties, reseason the griddle annually by wiping it with a thin layer of cooking oil after cleaning and allowing it to dry completely before storage.
