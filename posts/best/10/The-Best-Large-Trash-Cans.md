### What materials are trash cans typically made from?

### How should I maintain a trash can?

Regular maintenance extends the life of your trash can and ensures proper function. Rinse the can with water after emptying to remove residue and prevent odor buildup. For plastic or metal cans, wipe down the exterior with a mild soap solution and dry thoroughly. 

Check for signs of wear, such as cracks, dents, or loose parts, especially around the lid and handle areas. Replace or repair any damaged components promptly to prevent leaks or safety hazards.
