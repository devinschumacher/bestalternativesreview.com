## Buyer's Guide

A carpet steam cleaner combines powerful deep cleaning with the sanitizing properties of hot water extraction. These machines use high-temperature steam to loosen and remove dirt, grime, and bacteria from carpets, offering an effective alternative to chemical cleaners. 


### Steam Temperature and Penetration

The steam temperature is crucial for effective cleaning. Professional-grade machines typically reach 250°F (121°C) or higher, which is hot enough to kill many bacteria and viruses. The steam should penetrate deep into the carpet fibers to reach embedded dirt. 


### Machine Design and Size

Consider the machine's weight and balance, especially if you need to carry it around the house or up stairs. Lightweight models with ergonomic handles are more comfortable to use for extended cleaning sessions. The size of the cleaning head determines the versatility - smaller heads are better for detailed cleaning, while larger ones are more efficient for quick passes over larger areas. 


### Water Reservoir and Accessories

Look for a machine with a generous water reservoir to reduce refilling needs during extended cleaning sessions. Essential accessories include a variety of nozzles and cleaning heads for different carpet types and maintenance needs. A crevice tool and rotating brush head are particularly useful for cleaning baseboards, furniture edges, and pet hair. 


### Steam Output and Operating Time

The steam output affects both cleaning effectiveness and runtime between refills.
