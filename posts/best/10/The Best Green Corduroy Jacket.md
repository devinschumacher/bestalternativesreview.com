### What are the advantages of corduroy material?

Corduroy has excellent breathability and moisture-wicking properties, making it suitable for various weather conditions. The natural texture provides insulation without adding bulk.

### How should I care for a green corduroy jacket?

The best way to maintain your corduroy jacket is to machine wash it in cold water with a mild detergent. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners as they can damage the texture. 

Air dry the jacket flat to prevent stretching, and avoid using a dryer. If needed, reshape the jacket while it's still slightly damp before hanging it to dry.
