### What makes a blazer "summer-friendly"?

Summer blazers prioritize lightweight fabrics like linen, cotton, and breathable blends to keep you cool during warm weather. Look for structured jackets with loose shoulder seams, roomy armholes, and ventilation details like diagonal seams or mesh accents.

### Why should I choose a single-breasted blazer for summer?

Single-breasted blazers are more practical for warmer weather as they require fewer buttons to close, reducing bulk. The design also allows for better air flow compared to double-breasted versions. 

These jackets are versatile enough to transition between casual and formal summer occasions, from business meetings to casual Fridays.
