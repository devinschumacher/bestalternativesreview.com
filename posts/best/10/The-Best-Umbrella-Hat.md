### What is an umbrella hat?

Essentially, an umbrella hat combines the functionality of a casual hat with an integrated umbrella. These hats often feature a canopy that pops up when needed, providing shade on sunny days while protecting your head from rain. 

Most umbrella hats include several key components: a sturdy frame that holds the umbrella when it's not being used, a release mechanism to open and close the canopy, and a comfortable headband or strap system to keep the hat secure. 


### How do umbrella hats work?

To use an umbrella hat, simply push a button or pull a lever to extend the canopy. The hat's design maintains structural integrity so the umbrella remains open, protecting your face and head from sun, rain, or wind. Most models require one hand to operate the mechanism while still keeping the other free for balance or carrying items. 

When not in use, the umbrella typically folds back into a flat headband, which can be worn like a traditional hat. Some advanced models offer multi-position settings for the umbrella canopy, allowing you to adjust the amount of shade based on your surroundings.
