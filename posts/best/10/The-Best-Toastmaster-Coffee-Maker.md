Regular cleaning is essential for optimal performance. Weekly descaling using a vinegar solution prevents mineral buildup, while the brew carafe should be washed by hand after each use. 

The coffee maker's exterior can be wiped with a damp cloth weekly, and the drip tray and warming plate should be cleaned monthly. The brew basket needs descaling every 2-3 months based on usage frequency. 


### Are Toastmaster coffee makers easy to operate?
