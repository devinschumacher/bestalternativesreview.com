### Capacity and Size

Measure your kitchen countertop space to ensure the toaster you choose fits comfortably. Consider whether you prefer a single slice or a four-slice toaster, as well as the size difference between horizontal and vertical models. Horizontal toasters typically take up more counter space but allow for easier removal of toast.

Check that the toaster offers adjustable heating settings to accommodate various bread types and preferences. Look for clear markings indicating the position of the slots and the corresponding crumb tray size. Higher-end models may feature digital controls and preset programs for different types of bread. 


### Brand Compatibility and Authenticity

When selecting a Star Wars toaster, verify the brand's reputation for producing authentic looking products. Authentic toys and kitchen items often feature detailed sculpting, proper color schemes, and accurate vehicle/profession figures. Be cautious of generics that may lack the intricate details collectors seek.

### How should I care for my Star Wars toaster?

Regular care for a Star Wars toaster is the same as for any standard toaster. Always unplug it before cleaning and use a damp cloth to wipe down the exterior. The heating elements should only be cleaned with a soft brush or crumpled paper towel to remove crumbs. 

LED displays and other electronic components should be dusted carefully using compressed air or a soft brush. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive materials on any part of the toaster.
