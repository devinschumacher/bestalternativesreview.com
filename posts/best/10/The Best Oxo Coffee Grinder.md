### Capacity and Performance

Coffee grinders are available in three main capacities: single-serve (12 ounces), mid-range (18 to 24 ounces), and commercial (over 24 ounces). For home use, a mid-range capacity grinds enough coffee for 20-30 cups before needing refills. Performance is measured by grind size consistency and blade efficiency. Look for grinders with stainless steel burrs for longevity and ceramic grinding mechanisms for precise control over grind settings.

### Convenience Features

Modern coffee grinders offer various convenience features to enhance your brewing experience. Tamper-resistant lids prevent spills and simplify cleaning. Dose control mechanisms allow precise measurement of ground coffee. Some models include timer functions for consistent grind sizes, while others feature multi-grind settings for different brewing methods. Consider features that align with your regular coffee routine and preferred grind styles. 


### Cleanability and Maintenance

The ease of cleaning can significantly impact a coffee grinder's longevity and effectiveness. Look for grinders with dismantleable components that wipe clean easily, such as grinding chambers and collection containers. Avoid models with complex parts or those that require disassembly for basic cleaning. Regular maintenance, including regular cleaning and occasional burr lubrication, should be straightforward with your chosen model.

### Which Oxo coffee grinder is right for me?

Oxo offers several coffee grinders, including manual models and electric options. The manual grinders are ideal for occasional use or travel, while electric grinders provide consistent grind quality and are suitable for daily use in households or small offices. 

Consider factors like grind size options, ease of use, and cleaning requirements when choosing between models. The electric grinders typically offer more features and convenience, while manual models require no electricity and are often more affordable. 


### How do I properly maintain my Oxo coffee grinder?

Regular cleaning is essential for maintaining your Oxo coffee grinder. After each use, remove the grind basket and clean it thoroughly with soap and water. For manual grinders, wipe down the exterior with a damp cloth. For electric grinders, also clean the coffee container and any particle collectors. 

Store your grinder in a dry place and consider cleaning the blade mechanism every 3-6 months, depending on usage frequency. This helps prevent clogs and ensures optimal grinding performance.

### Can I grind other ingredients besides coffee?

Oxo coffee grinders are primarily designed for coffee beans, but some models can handle other dry ingredients like spices, nuts, or seeds. Always check your specific model's capabilities before attempting to grind alternative ingredients. 

When grinding other items, use the coarsest grind setting to prevent over-processing. Clean the grinder thoroughly between uses to prevent cross-contamination. 


### What grind settings are available on Oxo coffee grinders?

Oxo coffee grinders typically offer multiple grind settings, ranging from coarse to fine. The number of settings varies by model, but common options include 4, 6, or 8 settings. 

These settings allow you to choose the right grind for different brewing methods. For example, a fine grind is suitable for espresso, while a medium grind works well for drip coffee.
