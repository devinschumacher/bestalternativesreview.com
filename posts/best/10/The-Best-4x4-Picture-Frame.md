## Buyer's Guide

Display your valuable photos and artwork with confidence using a 4x4 picture frame. These sturdy frames protect your photos while adding a touch of class to any room. Understanding the key features and considerations will help you select the perfect frame for your collection. 


### Frame Construction and Materials

4x4 frames are available in various materials, including wood, metal, and plastic. Wood frames provide warmth and character but require more maintenance. Metal frames offer durability and resistance to warping, making them ideal for humid environments. Plastic frames combine affordability with light weight, though they may scratch more easily than other materials. 


### Protection Features

Quality 4x4 frames include UV protection to prevent photo fading and shatter-resistant glass to safeguard against breakage. Some frames feature acid-free backing to prevent damage from environmental factors. Consider frames with adjustable spacers to allow proper air circulation behind the glass, which helps prevent condensation and mold growth. 


### Installation and Hanging

Most 4x4 frames include mounting hardware such as picture wire, D-rings, or sawtooth hangers. Consider frames with built-in levels for precise hanging. For heavier frames, look for models with reinforced backs and multiple hanging points. If hanging multiple frames, choose ones with standardized mounting systems for uniform display. 


### Size and Style Options
