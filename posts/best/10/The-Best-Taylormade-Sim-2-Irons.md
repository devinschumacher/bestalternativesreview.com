### What are the key features of the Taylormade SIM 2 Irons?

The SIM 2 Irons combine a thin, 60/40 face/mass design with a high MOI for maximum forgiveness. The adjustable hosel allows for customization of face angle and loft, while the compact shape provides a feel similar to traditional blade irons. 

Each iron features TaylorMade's Speed Fuse technology, which creates a thinner, more flexible face that flexes during impact to increase ball speed. The sole design incorporates a wide, trailing-edge weighting to improve ball-striking consistency and turf interaction. 


### What are the most significant improvements in the SIM 2 compared to the original SIM Irons?

The SIM 2 Irons feature a redesigned face that's 10% thinner, allowing for more consistent ball striking across the face. The face also incorporates a new dual-material structure combining titanium and 455 stainless steel for increased ball speed and forgiveness. 

Additional improvements include updated weighting and a revised internal structure that reduces weight while maintaining stability. These changes result in a slightly lighter clubhead while preserving or improving the original's forgiving design. 


### What are the loft and length options for the SIM 2 Irons?

The SIM 2 Irons offer a standard 5-9 iron configuration with lofts ranging from 37° (5-iron) to 60° (9-iron). Each iron is available in both standard and stiff flex patterns, with the option to customize lie angles and shaft options. 

Club lengths range from 35.5" for the 5-iron to 35" for the 9-iron, with standard shafts measuring 36". The irons are available in both right-handed and left-handed versions, with left-handed clubs featuring a mirror-imaged face design.
