## Buyer's Guide

Cologne samples offer an opportunity to test different scents before committing to a full-size bottle. These smaller versions, typically 1.7 oz or 50 ml, allow you to explore new fragrances without investment. Understanding how to select and properly use these samples can enhance your fragrance discovery process. 


### Size and Portability

Standard sample sizes range from 1.7 oz to 1.875 oz, with 50 ml being common for international packaging. Consider whether you prefer a larger sample for extended evaluation or a smaller size for travel and daily use. Some brands offer refill options for their sample containers, making them a practical choice for repeated trials of the same scent. 

![Cologne-Samples-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Cologne-Samples-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Scent Characteristics

Pay attention to the top, middle, and base notes of a sample to understand its full character. Top notes dominate immediately after application, while middle and base notes develop over time. Notable characteristics include sillage (how far the scent projects), longevity (how long the fragrance lasts on the skin), and whether it contains any strong, unpleasant chemicals. 


### Application Method
