Clip-in braids attach to the existing hair with clips, allowing for quick application and removal. 

The choice between these options depends on personal preference, lifestyle requirements, and the desired level of maintenance. Hair extension braids offer a more permanent solution, while clip-in braids provide greater flexibility and ease of use.
