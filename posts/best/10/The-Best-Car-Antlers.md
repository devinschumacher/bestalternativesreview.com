## FAQ

![Car-Antlers-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Car-Antlers-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are car antlers?

Car antlers are automotive accessories that attach to the roof of a vehicle to create the appearance of deer antlers. They are typically made from plastic with metal accents, though some high-end models use real deer antler material. 

These accessories come in various sizes and designs, ranging from simple spikes to elaborate branched formations. They're popular for Halloween, themed car shows, or as a conversation piece for everyday use. 


### How do I install car antlers?

Installation typically requires drilling holes in the vehicle's roof and attaching the antlers with included hardware. The process can vary based on vehicle type and antler design, so it's important to follow specific installation instructions provided by the manufacturer. 

Some models come with double-sided tape for quick installation, while others use metal brackets that need to be securely fastened to the roof. Always ensure the vehicle's roof and the antlers themselves are clean before installation to prevent damage and ensure a proper fit. 

![Car-Antlers-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Car-Antlers-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What vehicles can accommodate car antlers?

Car antlers work best on vehicles with a solid roof structure that can support their weight. They're commonly used on hatchbacks, station wagons, and some SUVs with higher rooflines. The vehicle's roof color should match or complement the antlers for the best visual effect. 

While most antlers are designed to fit typical roof heights, some vehicles may have unique roof shapes that affect compatibility. Always measure your vehicle's roof to ensure the antlers will fit properly before purchasing. 


### How do car antlers affect vehicle handling?

Car antlers can change a vehicle's aerodynamics and center of gravity, potentially affecting handling and performance. The impact is usually minimal for small antlers on vehicles with standard roof heights. However, very large antlers on high-roof vehicles could affect stability at higher speeds. 

Most manufacturers recommend removing car antlers when driving in extreme weather conditions or on open roads to maintain optimal vehicle performance and safety.

### Are there legal considerations when using car antlers?

Car antlers are legal in most jurisdictions unless they significantly alter the vehicle's appearance beyond standard modifications. Some areas have specific regulations regarding roof-mounted accessories that could affect vehicle classification or registration. 

Always check local laws and insurance policies before installing car antlers. Some insurance providers may consider them a safety hazard and increase premiums or deny claims if an accident occurs while the antlers are installed. 


### What maintenance is required for car antlers?

Basic maintenance includes regular cleaning to remove road grime and UV protection to prevent fading. Many manufacturers recommend applying a clear coat spray or using UV-resistant paint to protect the antlers' finish. 

Check the hardware for any signs of wear or corrosion, especially if the antlers are metal. Repainting or reapplying hardware may be necessary every 1-2 years to maintain the antlers' appearance and functionality.
