## FAQ


### What materials are Disney water bottles made from?

Disney water bottles are typically made from BPA-free plastic or stainless steel. The plastic versions often feature durable polypropylene construction, while stainless steel options provide both insulation and scratch resistance. 

These materials ensure the bottles are lightweight and secure, designed to keep drinks cold for several hours while remaining safe for repeated use. 


### Are Disney water bottles insulated?

Most Disney water bottles offer some level of insulation, with stainless steel versions providing the best temperature retention. The insulation keeps drinks cold for 12+ hours or hot for 6+ hours, depending on the bottle model. 

Plastic bottles with double-wall construction also offer decent insulation, though they won't maintain temperatures as long as their stainless steel counterparts. 


### What sizes are Disney water bottles available in?

Disney water bottles come in several standard sizes, including 17oz (500ml), 21oz (625ml), and 24oz (710ml) capacities. Most bottles fit standard cup holders and conveniently pack for travel or outdoor activities. 

Some sizes are available in multiple styles, allowing you to choose based on your specific needs and preferences. 


### How do I care for a Disney water bottle?

Care requirements vary by material. For plastic bottles, hand washing with mild soap and water is recommended, while stainless steel bottles should be washed with a soft cloth to prevent scratches. 

Always ensure the bottle is completely dry before closing the lid to prevent condensation and maintain the integrity of the seal.

Water bottles feature a wide array of Disney characters, including princesses, princes, animals, and popular movie characters. Each bottle highlights a different character, offering something for every fan.
