Selecting the appropriate size is crucial for proper fit and leakage prevention. Diapers typically come in newborn, size 1, and size 2+ categories, with manufacturer sizing charts to guide your choice. A well-fitting diaper should feel secure while allowing for easy diaper changes.

For overnight or extended wear, opt for diapers with highly absorbent cores and reinforced leak barriers. Modern diapers use materials like charcoal and bamboo to enhance moisture-wicking and prevent leaks, ensuring your baby stays dry and comfortable.

Diaper rash is common, but choosing hypoallergenic materials can reduce irritation. Look for diapers made from natural fabrics like cotton or those with oatmeal-infused formulas. Regularly checking your baby's diaper area helps identify potential sensitivities and ensures proper skin care.
