Opt for coasters made from durable materials like ceramic, stone, or quality acrylic. These options resist chipping and fading, keeping your humorous designs looking sharp for years. Avoid cheaper plastic alternatives that can scratch easily or warp over time. 


### Consider the Size and Design of Your Collection

### How durable are the coasters?

Made from high-quality materials like BPA-free resin or thick cardboard, these coasters resist moisture damage and scratches. The backing prevents water rings on surfaces and keeps drinks secure during transport. 

While they're sturdy enough for regular use, some designs feature slightly thinner elements for decorative purposes, so they're best suited for indoor use where the coasters won't face extreme conditions. 


### Are the coasters dishwasher safe?

Many coasters feature dishwasher-safe materials that allow for easy cleaning, with simple designs that prevent water accumulation in intricate patterns. This makes them convenient for quick maintenance between gatherings. 

Some specialized designs, like those featuring glued-on elements or textured surfaces, should be hand-washed to prevent damage or dislodging of decorative components. 


### What size options are available?

Standard coasters typically measure 3.5 inches in diameter, providing sufficient space for both text and decorative elements while keeping shipping costs and storage needs manageable. 

Some premium sets offer larger wall-mounted coasters measuring 5-6 inches, featuring detailed designs and enhanced durability for high-traffic areas. 


### How do I display the coasters?

These coasters look great grouped together on a coffee table, mounted in a frame, or displayed individually in guest rooms and offices. The designs work well in both traditional and modern decors. 

Many people use multiple sets to create themed displays, like pairing sports-related coasters with a TV stand or mixing animal designs with botanical prints for a natural aesthetic. 


### What if I want to customize the text?

While most coasters feature pre-printed designs, some premium sets offer customization options through third-party services. You can often choose from multiple font styles and color combinations.
