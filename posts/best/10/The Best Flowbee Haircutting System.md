## FAQ


### What is a Flowbee Haircutting System?

A Flowbee Haircutting System is a home-use grooming appliance that allows users to trim their hair while the motor cuts through the hair with a disposable blade. The system comes with attachments for different hair lengths and styles, providing versatility for various haircut needs. 

The Flowbee system works by drawing hair through its mechanism while the blade cuts, making it a convenient alternative to salon visits for regular hair maintenance. 


### What hair types are suitable for the Flowbee system?

The Flowbee system works well with medium-length hair that is not too thick or curly. Hair that is too short or too thick may not feed properly into the machine, while very curly hair might not cut as neatly with the standard attachments. 

For most hair types, the system can still provide a basic trim, but professional grooming is recommended for significantly shorter or more textured hair styles. 


### How long does the Flowbee battery last?

The Flowbee system has a rechargeable battery that lasts approximately 30 minutes on a full charge. This is sufficient for one standard haircut session. The system includes a charger that can completely charge the battery in about 2.5 hours. 

Power consumption is reduced during the cleaning cycle, so the system can perform several quick touch-ups between full charges as needed. 


### How do I maintain the Flowbee system?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the vacuum filter after each use to remove hair debris and checking that the blade is properly seated in its housing. The system's blade should be replaced when it shows signs of dullness or uneven wear. 

A complete cleaning cycle should be performed monthly to remove any built-up hair and dust from the internal components. This helps maintain the system's performance and ensures hygiene for future use. 


### Can I use different blade lengths for different haircuts?

The Flowbee system comes with multiple blade attachments that allow you to select the appropriate hair length for your trim. The shorter the blade, the closer the finish on your haircut will be, making it suitable for various style preferences. 

Attachments include options for fading, thinning, and regular cutting, providing flexibility for different haircut needs without the need for multiple machines. 


### Is the Flowbee system suitable for all skill levels?

While the Flowbee is designed to be user-friendly with adjustable lengths and attachment options, it requires practice to achieve professional-looking results. Beginners might find it challenging to create precise fades or layering without proper technique. 

For most basic haircuts, the system can provide a satisfactory result, but advanced styling techniques or significantly shorter hair may require more precise grooming tools or professional assistance.
