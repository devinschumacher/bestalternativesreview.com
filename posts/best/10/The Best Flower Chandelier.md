### What are the most durable materials for flower chandeliers?

Materials that resist corrosion, scratches, and wear best serve flower chandeliers. The most durable options include powder-coated metal, solid brass, and crystal. These materials maintain their luster over time and can handle regular cleaning and handling. 

If the chandelier has glass elements, choose lead crystal or tempered glass, as these materials resist shattering and damage from temperature changes. Aluminum finishes quickly show scratches and wear, while cheaper plastic components typically lack the longevity of metal and crystal alternatives. 


### How do I safely install a flower chandelier?
