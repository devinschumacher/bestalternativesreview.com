## Buyer's Guide

Dorai Bath Stone brings natural elegance to bathrooms with its premium quality and realistic marble effect. Understanding the key features and considerations will help you select the right slab for your project. 


### Material Quality and Authenticity

Dorai Bath Stone combines actual marble chips with a resin base, creating a durable yet lightweight alternative to natural stone. The material's resilience makes it suitable for both wet and dry areas of the bathroom. Opt for samples with consistent veining patterns and a smooth surface finish for the best results. 


### Size and Weight Considerations

Standard slabs measure approximately 1200mm by 600mm, with varying thicknesses. Lightweight resin construction reduces installation complexities compared to natural marble. When purchasing, allow for a 2mm tolerance in both length and width for proper grouting. 


### Maintenance Requirements

Regular cleaning with mild soap and water maintains the surface's shine. Avoid abrasive cleaners or harsh chemicals that can damage the protective seal. Applying a high-quality stone sealer upon installation and annually protects against stains and water damage. 


### Installation and Professional Help

While less challenging than natural stone, professional installation ensures optimal results, especially for larger slabs. Consider retaining an experienced installer who understands the material's characteristics. Proper preparation of the subfloor and use of appropriate adhesives are crucial for lasting performance.
