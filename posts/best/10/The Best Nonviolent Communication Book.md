## FAQ


### What is Nonviolent Communication (NVC)?

Nonviolent Communication (NVC) is a powerful language and communication model developed by Marshall Rosenberg. It helps individuals connect deeply with themselves and others by focusing on expressing oneself authentically and understanding others' needs without judgment. 

The core of NVC involves four components: observing what is happening, expressing feelings and needs, making requests, and listening empathetically. This framework helps transform conflicts into opportunities for connection and mutual understanding.
