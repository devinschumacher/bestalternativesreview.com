## Buyer's Guide

Epson laser printers blend robust performance with energy efficiency, making them a practical choice for home offices and small businesses. Understanding key aspects will help you select a printer that matches your needs. 


### Print Quality and Resolution

The print resolution determines how sharp and detailed your documents will be, measured in dots per inch (dpi). For standard office use, a resolution of 1200 x 1200 dpi is sufficient. Graphic-intensive work may benefit from higher resolutions up to 2400 x 2400 dpi.

### Print Speed and Capacity

Print speed in pages per minute (ppm) and first-page-out (FPO) time indicate how quickly you'll receive printed documents. Meanwhile, monthly duty cycle shows the rated page volume, helping you gauge whether the printer handles occasional or frequent use. 


### Connectivity Options

Modern laser printers support both wired and wireless connections. Ethernet and USB ports enable direct computer connectivity, while Wi-Fi allows network printing. For mobile convenience, consider printers with built-in Bluetooth or mobile app support.

### Maintenance and Operating Costs

When comparing printer models, look beyond initial cost. Calculate total operating expenses by considering factors like toner yield, replacement costs, and electricity consumption. Printer duty cycle affects how often you need maintenance, impacting long-term ownership costs.
