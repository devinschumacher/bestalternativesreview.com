### What are Ion Wheels and how do they work?

Ion Wheels are compact devices that generate charged particles to create ionized air. These positively charged ions attach to airborne particles like dust, pollen, and smoke, making them heavier and easier to capture by gravity or filtration systems. The process helps improve indoor air quality by removing these microscopic pollutants. 

The technology works through a series of electrodes that produce ions through corona discharge. When properly installed and maintained, Ion Wheels can effectively reduce indoor particulate matter without the need for traditional mechanical filters. 


### How effective are Ion Wheels in removing airborne contaminants?

Ion Wheels are particularly effective at removing small particles such as dust, pollen, and smoke, which are commonly 0.1 to 10 microns in size. They are less effective against larger particles like pet dander or viruses, which require additional filtration methods. 

The efficiency of Ion Wheels can vary based on several factors including the concentration of contaminants in the air, the size and quality of the device, and the installation environment. Regular maintenance is crucial for optimal performance.
