## FAQ

![Sticky-Tabs-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Sticky-Tabs-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are sticky tabs?

Sticky tabs are adhesive-backed strips designed to securely attach to books, notebooks, or any paper-based material. They feature a strong, pressure-sensitive adhesive on one side and a protective backing sheet that's removed before use. 

The primary advantage of sticky tabs is their ability to remain firmly in place without damage to the attached material. They're particularly useful for organizing pages, bookmarking, or labeling documents. 


### How do sticky tabs work?

Sticky tabs work by applying pressure-sensitive adhesive directly to the paper they adhere to. Once the protective backing is removed, the adhesive bonds immediately to the surface, creating a secure hold. 

The tabs' design allows them to stay in place even with regular handling and flipping through pages, making them ideal for quick reference points or permanent organization solutions. 

![Sticky-Tabs-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Sticky-Tabs-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are the benefits of using sticky tabs?

Sticky tabs offer easy page marking and organization without the need for writing or additional hardware. They're perfect for highlighting important pages, creating customizable bookmarks, or establishing clear document sections. 

Because they're adhesive-based, sticky tabs maintain their position through regular use, making them more reliable than loose bookmarks or paper clips for long-term document management. 


### Which materials work best with sticky tabs?

Most paper-based materials, including books, notebooks, and loose sheets, work well with sticky tabs. The key requirement is that the paper isn't too thick or glossy, as this can affect adhesion quality. 

For best results, avoid using sticky tabs on coated paper, plastic, or non-porous surfaces. These materials may prevent the adhesive from bonding effectively, rendering the tab ineffective. 

![Sticky-Tabs-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Sticky-Tabs-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How long do sticky tabs typically last?

Sticky tabs generally last as long as the paper they're adhered to, though adhesion strength can vary based on environmental factors like temperature and humidity. Under normal conditions, they maintain their position for several months to years. 

Regular handling and exposure to oils from skin can slightly reduce adhesion over time, but proper storage in a cool, dry place can significantly extend their effective lifespan. 


### Can I remove sticky tabs without damaging the paper?

While sticky tabs are designed to stay in place, most can be carefully removed without significant paper damage. To remove them, gently peel the tab from the paper while holding the surrounding page to prevent tearing. 

For stubborn tabs or in cases where the paper appears damaged, consider using tape or a specialized tab removal tool designed for delicate paper surfaces. Proper removal techniques help preserve the paper's integrity and maintain its usability.
