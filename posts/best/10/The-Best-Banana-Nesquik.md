### Can Banana Nesquik be prepared ahead of time?

Banana Nesquik drink mixes can be prepared ahead of time for convenience. Mix the recommended amount of powder with milk in a sealed container and refrigerate it overnight. Shake well before serving to achieve a smooth consistency. Prepared Banana Nesquik typically lasts in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours when stored properly sealed. 

For longer-term storage, consider freezing the prepared drink in ice cube trays, then transferring the frozen cubes to a freezer bag. Thaw individual cubes in the refrigerator as needed, or add them directly to cold milk for a quick, frozen dessert option. 


### What alternatives are available for those who cannot drink milk?

For individuals who cannot consume milk, there are several alternatives available. Nut-based milk alternatives like almond or coconut milk can be used to prepare Banana Nesquik, though results may vary from the traditional milk-based version. Plant-based milk alternatives may affect the texture and consistency. 

Some readers have successfully made milk substitutes by blending banana with milk alternatives and adding Nesquik powder separately for a homemade version. Always check the compatibility with specific milk alternatives before attempting substitutions.
