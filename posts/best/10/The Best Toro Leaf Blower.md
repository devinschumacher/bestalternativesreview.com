The engine size directly impacts how effectively the leaf blower can move leaves. Toro offers both gas and electric options, with horsepower rating a key indicator of performance. The nozzle design affects how forcefully leaves are dislodged and moved. ### Cord Options for Electric Models

For electric blowers, the cord length and type are crucial considerations. The basic rule is that the cord should be long enough to reach from your starting point to the power source, plus some extra length for movement. Toro offers both standard and extended cord options, as well as cord organizers to maintain tangle-free operation.

### Maintenance Requirements

Regular maintenance can extend the life of your leaf blower and keep it running efficiently. Toro blowers typically require oil changes and spark plug replacement for gas models, while electric models have fewer maintenance needs.

### What features differentiate Toro's leaf blowers?

Toro leaf blowers vary in power, mobility, and operating mode. Higher-end models feature adjustable nozzles, variable speeds, and ergonomic handles for comfort during extended use. Some models include built-in vacuums for collecting debris as it's blown.
