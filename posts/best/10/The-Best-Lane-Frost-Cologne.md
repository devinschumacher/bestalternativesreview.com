Lane Frost Cologne features a bold, distinctive fusion of oriental and spicy notes. The top notes combine coriander and ginger, creating an intense, slightly spicy opening. ### How long does Lane Frost Cologne last?

Due to its complex oriental formula, Lane Frost Cologne typically lasts 8-12 hours on the skin. Factors that can affect lasting power include skin type, body temperature, activity level, and environmental conditions. On dry skin or in cooler weather, the scent may last closer to 12 hours. In humid conditions or with vigorous activity, the longevity can drop to 8 hours or less. 

Some wearers extend the scent's life by applying a small amount to pulse points (wrist, behind ear, décolletage) rather than swiping liberally across clothes. A single spray is usually sufficient for most, as the concentration of Lane Frost is strong enough to be noticeable without overwhelming. Light, frequent touches with a perfume roller can refresh the scent throughout the day.

### How should Lane Frost Cologne be stored?

Like all fragrance products, Lane Frost should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Exposure to these elements can degrade the fragrance quality and shorten its shelf life. Fragrances should be kept in their original packaging to protect them from environmental factors. 

When purchasing, choose sealed bottles with original factory caps to maintain freshness. If you have open bottles, store them upside down after use to protect the contents from air exposure. The ideal storage temperature range is between 60-75°F (15-24°C), as extreme heat or cold can compromise the scent's integrity.
