### How do you properly care for a horse-themed necklace?

Proper care extends the life of your horse necklace and maintains its appearance. For metal pieces, avoid harsh chemicals and store them in a dry place. Leather should be conditioned regularly to prevent cracking, while wooden elements need protection from water and extreme temperatures. 

Clean metal parts with a mild cleaner and soft cloth, while leather can be cleaned with a specialized leather cleaner. Avoid using water on metal parts, and do not expose the necklace to direct sunlight or extreme heat.
