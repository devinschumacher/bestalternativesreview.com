## FAQ


### What is the Pie Face Game?

A physical board game where players answer trivia questions to earn pie slices, which they then use to "pie" other players. The game combines knowledge testing with the classic "pie in the face" prank, creating an engaging social experience. 

Each player starts with a custom face card featuring a pie or face design. Correct answers earn you pie slices, while incorrect answers result in being "pie'd" by opponents. The game includes a pie-shaped token that players must strategically place to target others. 


### How many players can it accommodate?

The Pie Face Game supports 2-12 players, making it suitable for small gatherings or larger parties. The multiplayer format encourages interaction and friendly competition among participants. 

To keep the game engaging with fewer players, you can use elimination rounds or team play. For smaller groups, consider playing multiple rounds with different players taking turns as the target. 


### What's included in the game package?

The core game comes with 10 custom face cards, a pie token, 50 pie slices, a pie-shaped stand, and a detailed instruction manual. Additional accessories include different colored markers for team play and expansion packs featuring bonus questions and tokens. 

The game is designed for ages 12 and up due to the slightly competitive nature of the gameplay and the physical interaction element. Each component is made from durable materials to withstand the "pieing" process. 


### How long does a typical game last?

A standard game session lasts approximately 30-45 minutes, depending on the number of players and their familiarity with the game. This time frame allows for several rounds of questioning and "pieing. "

For longer sessions, consider implementing time limits per question or round-robin play to maintain pacing. The game's brief rounds make it ideal for casual gatherings or as a quick entertainment option. 


### What kind of questions are asked?

Trivia questions cover a wide range of topics, including pop culture, general knowledge, and sometimes intentionally silly or obscure facts. The goal is to test players' knowledge while keeping the game light-hearted. 

Each question card presents a multiple-choice format, with four possible answers. Questions range from easy (e. g. , "What is the capital of France? ") to challenging (e. g. , "What is the chemical symbol for gold? "). 


### Can you win without getting pie'd?

While "pieing" opponents is a significant part of the game, it's possible to win without directly getting pie'd. Players can earn pie slices by correctly answering questions and strategically place the pie token to target others. 

Winning typically requires a combination of knowledge, strategy, and a bit of luck. Players with fewer pie slices need to be especially careful, while those with more can take calculated risks.
