## Buyer's Guide

Burberry Blush Perfume combines fresh floral notes with subtle woodsy undertones, creating a delicate scent that's both elegant and approachable. This guide helps you understand the key aspects of the fragrance, from its top notes to its lasting power. 


### Top Notes

Burberry Blush opens with zesty lemon and grapefruit, which give way to soft jasmine and pink pepper. These refreshing elements set the stage for the more refined scents that follow. 


### Heart Notes

The heart of the fragrance consists of Bulgarian rose and patchouli. The rose adds a delicate floral quality, while the patchouli provides a touch of warmth and earthiness, balancing the brighter opening notes. 


### Base Notes

The base introduces vetiver and woods, which soften the rose fragrance and add depth. This combination creates a harmonious blend of floral elegance and natural earthiness that persists throughout the scent's development. 


### Longevity and Sillage

Like most Burberry fragrances, Blush has good longevity, with the top notes fading after about 2-3 hours to reveal the heart and base notes. The sillage, or trail left behind, is moderate, making it suitable for both casual and professional settings. 


## FAQ


### What is Burberry Blush Perfume?

Burberry Blush, created in 2006, is a light, floriental fragrance for women. It features notes of strawberry, cherry blossom, jasmine, and vanilla, designed to be fresh and floral. 

The perfume comes in a pink bottle with a distinctive cap, matching the Burberry color scheme. It's available in eau de toilette (EDT) and eau de parfum (EDP) concentrations. 


### What is the fragrance family of Burberry Blush?

Burberry Blush belongs to the floriental fragrance family, combining floral and fruity notes to create a fresh, feminine scent. 

Top notes include strawberry and cherry, while the heart features jasmine and neroli. The base combines vanilla, sandalwood, and musk to create a subtle, lingering presence. 


### What are the key scent notes in Burberry Blush?

The top notes are fresh and fruity, with hints of strawberry and cherry. These give way to a vibrant heart of jasmine and neroli. 

The base notes incorporate warm vanilla, along with woods and musk, creating a soft, lasting finish. 


### What is the recommended application of Burberry Blush?

Apply Burberry Blush to pulse points such as the wrists, neck, and behind the ears for the most effective scent projection. 

Use a light, dabbing motion to avoid excessive perfume application, which can lead to an artificial or overwhelming fragrance. 


### How long does Burberry Blush last?

As an eau de toilette, Burberry Blush typically lasts 3-6 hours on average skin. Applications in warm climates or after bathing may result in shorter lasting power. 

The fragrance reaches its peak intensity 20-30 minutes after application and begins to fade after 2-3 hours. 


### Is Burberry Blush suitable for all skin types?

Burberry Blush is generally well-tolerated by most skin types. However, individuals with highly sensitive skin or allergies to certain fragrance components may experience irritation. 

If you're uncertain about your skin's reaction, consider applying a small amount to your skin patch test and waiting 24 hours before full application.
