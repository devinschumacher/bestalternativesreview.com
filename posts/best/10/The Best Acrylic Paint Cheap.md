Acrylic paint is a type of fast-drying paint that uses pigment suspended in an acrylic polymer emulsion. It combines the versatility of watercolors with the covering power and texture of oil paints. 

These paints are known for their durability, allowing for easy mixture of colors and application over both smooth and textured surfaces.
