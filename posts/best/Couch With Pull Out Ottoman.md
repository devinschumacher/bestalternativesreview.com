### What is the difference between a pull-out and a reclining ottoman?

The primary difference between a pull-out and a reclining ottoman lies in their functionality and design. A pull-out ottoman serves as an additional seating area that extends from the base of the couch. It can be easily moved into place when needed, providing extra space for guests or a comfortable footrest. 

On the other hand, a reclining ottoman has a separate mechanism that allows it to recline, providing additional comfort and support for your feet and legs. While it offers more functionality, it may take up more space due to its reclining feature. Make sure to consider your personal preferences and requirements when deciding between a pull-out or a reclining ottoman.
