### What's the difference between a crepe pan and a regular skillet?

A crepe pan has specific features that make it ideal for making crepes. It typically has sloped sides that prevent batter from sticking, and the surface is non-stick or has raised patterns to help with browning and flipping. 

While you could use a regular skillet to make crepes, the specialized features of a crepe pan can lead to better results and easier clean-up. 


### How do I properly care for my crepe pan?

Always hand wash with mild soap and warm water. For non-stick pans, avoid steel wool or abrasive cleaners that can damage the coating. Store the pan properly in a dry place to maintain its condition. 

If using a cast iron crepe pan, clean it after each use and season periodically to maintain its non-stick properties. Never fully immerse in water or use harsh chemicals. 

![Crepe-Pan-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Crepe-Pan-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What temperature is best for cooking crepes?

The ideal temperature range for cooking crepes is between 350-425°F (175-220°C). The heat should be consistent throughout the cooking process to achieve a golden-brown color and tender texture. 

If your pan gets too hot, the outside of the crepe will burn before the inside is cooked. If it's too cool, the crepe will be soggy and difficult to flip. 


### Can I use a crepe pan on all cooktops?

Most crepe pans are made of materials that work on electric, gas, and induction cooktops. Check the manufacturer's specifications to ensure compatibility with your particular cooktop. 

Avoid using metal utensils on non-stick crepe pans, as they can scratch the surface. Instead, use silicone or plastic utensils for the best results.

### How do I know when a crepe is ready to flip?

Cook for about 2-3 minutes, until the surface appears dry and slightly firm to the touch, with small bubbles appearing. The edges should start to lift slightly. 

Use a flexible spatula to gently loosen the edges. If the batter begins to pull away from the sides of the pan, it's ready to flip. Cook the second side for the same amount of time. 


### How long can crepes be stored and reheated?

Cooked crepes should be eaten within a few hours of preparation. Store them loosely wrapped in the refrigerator for up to 24 hours. 

To reheat, stack the crepes between sheets of parchment paper and microwave for 30-60 seconds, or cook in a lightly oiled pan over medium heat until warm throughout.
