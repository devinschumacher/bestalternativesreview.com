### What is the difference between ceramic and glass blue mosaic tiles?

Ceramic blue mosaic tiles are more durable and affordable, suitable for high-traffic areas. They come in a wider range of colors and shapes but are more prone to scratches and fading. 

Glass blue mosaic tiles offer superior shine and a wider spectrum of color options. They resist fading and wear better over time but are more fragile and sensitive to temperature changes. 


### What are the installation requirements for blue mosaic tiles?

Both ceramic and glass blue mosaic tiles require a stable substrate, such as concrete or dense masonry. Proper mortar bed and specialized adhesive are necessary for secure installation. 

The surface must be clean, dry, and free from contaminants. Thorough preparation prevents future issues and ensures lasting bond quality.

### How should blue mosaic tiles be maintained?

Clean blue mosaic tiles using mild soap and water, avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage the surface. Regular cleaning helps maintain the tile's luster and prevents accumulation of grime. 

For glass mosaic tiles, avoid abrasive cleaners that can scratch the surface. Check manufacturer recommendations for specific care instructions based on the tile's composition.
