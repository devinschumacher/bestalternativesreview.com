## Buyer's Guide

Uomo cologne represents modern masculinity through its blend of clean, fresh scents with a touch of sophistication. Understanding your preferences and considering specific fragrance notes will help you select the ideal scent. 


### Identification of Scent Profile

Modern uomo colognes often combine top notes of citrus and green elements with heart notes of elemi, cardamom, or lavender. Base notes typically include vetiver, sandalwood, or musk, creating a lasting yet refined aroma. 


### Identification of Strength and Sillage

Eau de cologne typically contains 4-8% concentration, providing a subtle fragrance that lasts 2-3 hours. For a stronger scent with better longevity, opt for eau de toilette (10-15% concentration) or eau de parfum (15-22%). 


### Personal Skin Type Considerations

Prone to sensitivity? Choose hypoallergenic formulas and test on a small patch of skin first. Oily skin might prefer lighter formulations, while dry skin may benefit from more moisturizing colognes. 


### Application and Layering Tips

Apply to pulse points like wrists, neck, and behind ears for maximum diffusion. Consider layering with a matching cologne splash in the evening to intensify your signature scent.
