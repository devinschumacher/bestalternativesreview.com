### How do I measure for farmhouse kitchen curtains?

Measure your window width from side to side, then add 10-15% to account for fullness and rod overlap. For the length, measure from the top of the curtain rod to your desired pooling point - typically 6-12 inches off the floor to create a casual gather. 

Consider installing a simple, classic curtain rod that hides at least 2 inches in the curtain seam. This creates a more cohesive look while keeping the rod's hardware minimal and unobtrusive. 


### What color schemes work best with farmhouse kitchen curtains?

Soft neutrals like white, cream, and warm grays provide an elegant backdrop that complements virtually any kitchen color scheme. These neutral tones help maintain visual balance and open up the space, making it feel larger and more airy.

### How do I care for natural fiber farmhouse curtains?

Care for natural fiber curtains with regular cool water hand washing or gentle machine wash cycle using mild detergent. Reshape while damp and hang to dry naturally to preserve the fabric's texture and shape. 

Store curtains by rolling them rather than folding to prevent creasing. For extended storage, place them in a cotton garment bag to protect from dust and damage. 


### What window treatments complement farmhouse curtains?
