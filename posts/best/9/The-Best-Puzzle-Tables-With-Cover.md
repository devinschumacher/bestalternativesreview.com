### What materials are commonly used for puzzle tables?

Puzzle tables are typically made from durable materials that can withstand regular use and weight. Common materials include MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard), plywood, and engineered wood. Some premium models feature solid wood construction for a more premium appearance. 

The edges are often protected with a laminate or veneer finish to prevent scratches and damage. The storage compartment may be lined with PVC or similar material to prevent moisture damage.

### What sizes are available for puzzle tables?

Puzzle tables come in a variety of sizes to fit different spaces and needs. Standard dimensions range from 18" to 36" in height, with tabletop sizes typically between 36" and 72" in length.
