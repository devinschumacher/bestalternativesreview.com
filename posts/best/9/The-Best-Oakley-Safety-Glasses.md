Oakley safety glasses feature advanced lens materials and construction standards that exceed general sunglasses requirements. They're engineered to protect against impact, UV radiation, and eye injuries, making them suitable for industrial, sports, and specialized applications.

### How do I know if Oakley safety glasses meet ANSI standards?

Oakley safety glasses that meet ANSI Z87.1 standards will have a "Z87+" marking on the frame or lens. This certification indicates they've passed impact tests for particles and objects, as well as chemical splash and UV radiation protection. 

Always verify compliance with local regulatory requirements, as some industries may need additional certifications beyond basic ANSI standards.
