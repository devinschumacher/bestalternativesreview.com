### What should I consider when selecting a black catsuit?

Key considerations include material quality, fit, and functionality. Choose a suit made from high-quality stretch fabric that provides proper compression while allowing full range of motion. The fit should be slim but not tight, with secure closures and comfortable waistbands. 

Think about the intended use of the suit - whether for performance, costume, or competition. Features like padding, moisture management, and specialized closures can vary based on the suit's intended purpose.

### Can I wear a black catsuit under other clothing?

Yes, black catsuits can be worn under other clothing. The stretchy, form-fitting nature of the material makes them ideal for layering under uniforms, dance costumes, or athletic wear. The catsuit will create a sleek, second-skin appearance without bulky under-layering. 

Consider wearing a seamless undershirt or moisture-wicking baselayer to prevent showing through the black catsuit fabric. The combination creates a professional, polished look suitable for various activities. 


### How do I size a black catsuit?

Black catsuits typically size smaller than regular clothing due to their form-fitting nature. Take accurate measurements of your bust, waist, hips, and inseam to determine the correct size. Consider visiting a specialist store for tried-on guidance if possible. 

Keep in mind that different brands may have varying sizing charts, so it's essential to measure before ordering. Allow for compression when selecting sizing, as the catsuit will typically feel slightly smaller when worn than when laid flat.

### How do I maintain a black catsuit?

Machine wash black catsuits using a mild detergent on the gentle cycle. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals that can damage the elastic material. Wash similar colors together to prevent color bleeding. 

After washing, hang the catsuit to dry rather than using a dryer. This helps preserve the shape and elasticity of the material. Store the suit flat or hung on a padded hanger to prevent stretching.
