### What makes an avocado stuffed animal unique?

Unlike traditional stuffed animals made from synthetic materials, avocado stuffed animals are crafted from real avocado seeds. This eco-friendly choice reduces waste while providing a natural, sustainable toy option. 

The irregular shape and distinctive texture of avocado seeds give these stuffed animals a unique character. Each one is a one-of-a-kind keepsake that feels different from conventional plush toys. 


### Are avocado stuffed animals safe for children?

Yes, avocado seeds are non-toxic and safe for children to play with when properly cleaned and maintained. However, they should always be supervised by adults to prevent choking hazards, especially for toddlers. 

Regularly wash the stuffed animal with mild soap and water to maintain hygiene. Store it in a dry place to prevent mold growth, especially during periods of high humidity.

### How long do avocado stuffed animals last?

With proper care, an avocado stuffed animal can last several years. The lifespan depends on regular cleaning, proper storage, and the frequency of play. 

Moderate wear and tear is normal, but check periodically for any loose seeds or unraveling stitching that might need repair before posing a safety hazard. 


### How should I clean an avocado stuffed animal?

For regular cleaning, gently hand wash the stuffed animal with mild soap and warm water. Rinse thoroughly and pat dry with a clean towel. 

Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach, as they can damage the natural material. Allow the stuffed animal to air dry completely before storing to prevent mold growth.

### Can I machine wash an avocado stuffed animal?

While machine washing might seem convenient, it's generally not recommended as it can damage the natural material and cause uneven drying. The tumbling motion and high water pressure may also affect the stuffed animal's shape and structure. 

It's best to handle cleaning by hand to ensure the avocado seed maintains its integrity and texture.
