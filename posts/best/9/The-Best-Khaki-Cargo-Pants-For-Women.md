### What's the difference between regular and tall sizing in khaki cargo pants?

Regular sizing typically runs true to the inseam measurement, while tall sizing adds an additional 2-3 inches to the inseam length. Most brands offer tall options in both standard and relaxed fits. 

The rise measurement remains the same for both regular and tall sizes, meaning both styles maintain the same leg position and pocket configuration.

### What maintenance requirements should I consider for khaki cargo pants?

Most khaki cargo pants can be machine washed with like colors, though some premium or moisture-wicking styles may require hand washing. Most brands recommend air drying to maintain the pants' shape and prevent shrinkage. 

Sizing can vary slightly between manufacturers, so spot-checking fit after the first wash is recommended before committing to a purchase. 


### How do you properly store khaki cargo pants to prevent damage?

Store pants in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent color fading. Consider rolling pants rather than folding them to reduce creases and improve longevity.
