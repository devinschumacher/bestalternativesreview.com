### What exactly is a flameless taper candle?

Unlike traditional wax candles that use an open flame, a flameless taper candle produces light through a battery-powered LED system. The outer shell resembles a real taper candle, complete with the distinctive pointed top, but it contains electronic components that create a soft, warm glow. 

The LED technology behind flameless taper candles allows them to produce light without the risk of fire or open flames, making them a safer alternative for various settings, including homes with children or pets, restaurants, and public spaces. 


### How long do flameless taper candles typically last?

Flameless taper candles last significantly longer than their flame-powered counterparts. With standard batteries, most models provide consistent light for 50-200 hours, depending on battery quality and usage frequency. 

Some newer models incorporate rechargeable batteries, extending the candle's operational life to hundreds of hours of use between charges. The lifespan of the LED light source itself is much longer than that of a regular candle wick, typically lasting around 5,000 hours.
