## Buyer's Guide

A Atlanta Braves snapback cap combines style and functionality, serving as both a fashion accessory and a way to show team spirit. Understanding the key aspects of these caps will help you find one that fits well and performs as expected. 


### Materials and Construction

The primary materials in a Braves snapback include the main body fabric (commonly cotton or polyester), the visor material, and the snap closure mechanism. Higher-quality caps use durable, breathable fabrics that retain their shape and color. The snap closure should sit smoothly in the center of the cap, allowing for precise sizing adjustments. 


### Fit and Sizing

Braves snapbacks typically range from size 6 1/8 to 7 3/8 in standard sizing. The cap's sizing triangle should sit flat against your head when properly fitted. Consider your hair type and thickness when choosing a size, as long hair might require an adjustment. 


### Style Elements

Modern Braves snapbacks incorporate elements like team colors, logos, and numerals. Pay attention to the placement and quality of these elements. Premium caps use screen printing for logos and numerals, while cheaper versions might use heat transfer methods that can peel off over time. 


### Care and Maintenance

Proper care extends the life of your Braves snapback. Machine wash the cap using a mild detergent and cold water cycle. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the fabric and logos. Air drying is recommended to prevent shrinkage and maintain the shape.
