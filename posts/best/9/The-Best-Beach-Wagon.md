### What is a beach wagon?

A beach wagon is a specialized cart designed for transporting beach equipment and supplies. It typically features a spacious, enclosed cargo area with dual wheels for easy maneuvering on both paved and uneven surfaces. 

Most beach wagons are made from lightweight materials like aluminum or plastic, making them portable yet durable enough to handle regular beach use. They usually come with multiple storage options, including side pockets and rear baskets. 


### What materials are beach wagons typically made from?

Beach wagons are commonly constructed from aluminum or durable plastic. These materials offer a balance of weight and strength, making them ideal for frequent use at the beach. 

Some premium models incorporate stainless steel for the wheels and frame, while others feature folding designs that allow for compact storage when not in use.

### How much weight can a beach wagon carry?

Most beach wagons have a weight capacity between 60 and 100 pounds, depending on the model and materials used. This capacity includes both the weight of the wagon itself and the cargo it carries. 

It's important to note that the weight capacity may be reduced if the wagon is not evenly distributed, so it's best to keep heavier items closer to the center of the cargo area. 


### What types of equipment do beach wagons typically carry?

Beach wagons are commonly used to transport umbrellas, chairs, coolers, and other beach essentials. Many models include dedicated storage for these items, such as built-in baskets or hooks for beach chairs. 

Some specialized beach wagons also feature coolers with built-in insulation, while others have additional storage compartments for personal items like towels and sunscreen.

### How do beach wagons compare to traditional wagons?

Beach wagons share similarities with traditional wagons but are designed with specific features for beach use. They typically have dual wheels for better maneuverability on both smooth and uneven surfaces. 

Most beach wagons also include additional storage features like side pockets and rear baskets, while some traditional wagons may lack these specialized storage options. 


### What maintenance does a beach wagon require?

Basic maintenance for a beach wagon includes regular cleaning to remove sand and salt, which can damage the materials over time. The wheels should be checked for proper inflation and the cargo area inspected for wear and tear. 

Some models may require periodic lubrication of moving parts and inspection of the mounting points for the cargo carrier. Regular cleaning and simple maintenance can extend the life of your beach wagon significantly.
