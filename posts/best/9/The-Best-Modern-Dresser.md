## Buyer's Guide

Modern dressers combine clean lines, minimalist design elements, and practical storage solutions to create focal points in any bedroom. Understanding the key features and considerations can help you select a dresser that matches your style preferences while providing functional storage. 


### Size and Dimensions

Selecting the right size dresser depends on available space and storage needs. Common widths range from 30 to 60 inches, with standard depths of 20-22 inches. Consider the room's dimensions, doorways, and existing furniture when choosing a size. The height should complement your bed; typically, dressers are 60-80 inches tall. 


### Storage Solutions

Modern dressers often feature open shelves, hidden storage compartments, and adjustable shelving. Look for dressers with at least 3-4 drawers and some open storage space. Drawer organizers can maximize storage efficiency. Consider the materials used for shelving and drawers - wood provides excellent organization, while glass doors offer easy visibility. 


### Metal vs. Wood Construction
