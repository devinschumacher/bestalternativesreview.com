### Construction Quality and Durability

The durability of an Hermes pillow is largely determined by its construction materials and manufacturing process. Opt for pillows with high-quality stitching that prevents loose feathers or filling material, and look for reinforced corners and edges where the pillow meets the stitching. 


### Filling Material and Feather Quality

Hermes pillows typically use down or feather fillings. Down provides better insulation and resilience, while feather fillings offer excellent comfort. Consider the percentage of down versus feathers in the filling, with at least 80% down being ideal for premium pillows. The material quality, including the absence of clumps and even distribution of fill, directly affects the pillow's comfort and longevity. 


### Size and Shape Options

Standard Hermes pillows come in multiple sizes, including the popular King-sized option. The pillow's shape can also vary, from traditional rectangles to more contemporary styles.
