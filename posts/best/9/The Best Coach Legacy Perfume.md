### What is Coach Legacy?

Coach Legacy is a men's fragrance that combines top notes of bergamot and mandarin with heart notes of cardamom and clove. The base features warm tones of vetiver and amber for lasting depth. 

The fragrance has a moderate intensity, lasting approximately 6-8 hours on the skin with average sillage (body scent projection). 


### When was Coach Legacy released?

Coach Legacy was launched in 2018 as part of the brand's Legacy collection, which focuses on modern interpretations of classic perfumery ingredients. 

The release marked a return to Coach's heritage of fragrances, following its acquisition by a new parent company in 2014.
