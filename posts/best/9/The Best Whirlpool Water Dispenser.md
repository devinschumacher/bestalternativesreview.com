## Buyer's Guide

Whirlpool water dispensers offer a convenient way to access clean, filtered water right from your kitchen countertop. These dispensers typically come with built-in filters to remove impurities and improve taste. Understanding the key features and considerations can help you select a water dispenser that meets your family's needs. 


### Capacity and Water Flow

Capacity ranges from 3 to 7 gallons, depending on your household size and consumption habits. Higher capacity models are suitable for larger families or those who prefer filling up larger containers. Water flow speed is also important, with faster flow rates providing quicker access to water but using more energy. 


### Filter System

Most Whirlpool water dispensers use under-sink filtration systems, which remove chlorine, sediment, and other contaminants. Some models offer additional stages of filtration for further purification. Consider the filter capacity and replacement schedule to ensure consistent water quality. 


### Power Consumption and Features

Energy efficiency is important, especially if the dispenser runs continuously. Models with automatic shut-off features can help save energy. Other useful features include LED indicators, child locks, and refill level indicators. Some advanced models offer premium filtration systems or connected compatibility with smart home devices. 


### Maintenance and Durability

Regular maintenance, such as filter changes and reservoir cleaning, ensures optimal performance and water quality. Look for models with easy-access components and durable construction. The water reservoir material should be BPA-free and resistant to bacterial growth.
