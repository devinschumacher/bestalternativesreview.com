### Airflow and Coverage

Calculate the room's square footage to determine the appropriate purifier size. Clarifion models range from small desktop units for individual rooms to larger models for entire homes. Consider room shape and furniture placement, as they affect airflow. 


### Smart Features

Modern Clarifion purifiers often include smart technologies like Wi-Fi connectivity, smartphone apps, and air quality sensors. These features allow remote control, filter replacement reminders, and real-time monitoring of indoor air quality. 


### Energy Consumption
