## FAQ


### What types of documents can the SG1000 scan?

The SG1000 scans virtually any paper document, including letters, photos, business cards, and receipts. It automatically handles double-sided documents and offers various settings for special media like photos and thick paper. 

You can also create custom document presets for specific types of documents to ensure consistent scanning quality. The machine's automatic image enhancement gives the best results for photos and color documents. 


### How fast is the SG1000 scanning process?

The SG1000 processes standard documents at 10 pages per minute (ppm). For double-sided documents, it scans one side at 20 ppm. Under ideal conditions, it can handle up to 200 pages per minute for single-sided documents. 

The machine's internal 16-page document feeder speeds up multi-page scanning tasks. A one-page scan takes just 4 seconds, making it efficient for both small office and home use. 


### What is the maximum document size supported by the SG1000?

Standard A4 paper is the default document size for the SG1000. For larger documents, it supports A3 paper with manual feeding. This allows you to scan legal-sized documents and bound books without dismantling them. 

With the included document feeder, you can scan documents up to 28.3 x 19.6 inches (719 x 500 mm). This feeder handles up to 16 pages at once, making it suitable for quick batches of multi-page documents. 


### How does the SG1000 handle different types of paper?

Designed for versatility, the SG1000 uses advanced paper handling technology to process various paper types including glossy photos, thick business cards, and multi-part forms. It automatically detects paper type and applies the appropriate settings. 

For special media like photos, the machine uses its precision paper feed system to prevent creasing and ensure perfect image quality. You can also adjust paper settings via the included control panel for optimal results with any paper type. 


### What connectivity options does the SG1000 offer?

The SG1000 includes USB and Wi-Fi connectivity options. USB allows direct connection to computers for simple document scanning, while Wi-Fi enables wireless scanning to computers and mobile devices via the included software. 

You can also connect the scanner to networks for shared access in small offices or home networks. The machine's USB 3.0 port provides faster data transfer compared to previous models. 


### What are the power requirements and operating conditions?

The SG1000 requires a standard 100-240V power supply and draws minimal power during operation. It uses a single USB 3.0 connection for power and data, making it compatible with any computer's USB port. 

The recommended operating temperature range is 14-32°C (57-90°F) with a maximum ambient humidity of 35-85% non-condensing. The scanner includes an automatic power-saving mode that activates when no scanning occurs for 60 minutes.
