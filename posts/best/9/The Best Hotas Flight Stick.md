A Hotas Flight Stick, short for Honeywell Operational Headtrack System, is a specialized flight control device used primarily in military and some civilian aircraft. The Hotas system allows pilots to control various aircraft functions using natural hand and head movements, enhancing situational awareness and reducing the need for direct gaze at instrument panels.
