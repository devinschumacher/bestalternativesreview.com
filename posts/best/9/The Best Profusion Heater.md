## Buyer's Guide

A profusion heater is designed to efficiently increase the temperature of large spaces, making it an essential for both residential and commercial heating needs. Understanding its key features and considerations will help you select the right unit for your space. 


### Heating Capacity

The heating capacity of a profusion heater is crucial for ensuring it can effectively warm your space. Consider the square footage of the area you need to heat and look for heaters with a BTU (British Thermal Unit) rating that matches or exceeds your space's size. A general rule is to choose a heater with 20-30 BTUs per square foot. 


### Efficiency Ratings

Profusion heaters with high efficiency ratings reduce energy consumption and operating costs. Look for models with an Annual Fuel Utilization Efficiency (AFUE) rating of 90% or higher. This indicates that a high percentage of the fuel consumed is actually converted into heat for your space. 


### Installation Requirements

Consider the installation requirements when selecting a profusion heater. Some models, particularly those designed for commercial use, may need professional installation. Ensure you have access to proper ventilation, electrical connections, and maintenance space for your chosen unit. 


### Additional Features

While basic heating functionality is essential, additional features can enhance performance and convenience. Look for heaters with adjustable thermostats, timers, and remote controls. Some advanced models offer features like humidification, air filtration, and smart home compatibility, which can improve both comfort and indoor air quality.
