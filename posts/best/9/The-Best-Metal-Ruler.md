## Buyer's Guide

Metal rulers are essential tools for precise measurements in various settings, from professional woodworking to everyday crafting. Understanding key features and considerations will help you select the right ruler for your needs. 


### Material and Construction

Metal rulers are available in different alloys, with stainless steel offering the best combination of durability and resistance to corrosion.
