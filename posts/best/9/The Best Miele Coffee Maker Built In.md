### What maintenance requirements do Miele coffee makers have?

Miele coffee makers need regular descaling every 2-3 months to maintain optimal performance and prevent mineral buildup. The brew basket, carafe, and drip tray require daily cleaning after each use. 

The coffee maker's water reservoir and drip tray should be emptied when not in use to prevent mold and bacteria growth. Professional cleaning of the coffee maker's heating elements and brew systems is recommended annually. 


### Can Miele coffee makers handle cold water?

Miele coffee makers are designed to work with both cold and room-temperature water. Using warm water can slightly improve brewing time, but it's not necessary for optimal results. 

For best performance, fill the water reservoir with fresh cold water daily. Avoid using hot water directly, as it can affect the coffee's temperature and pressure settings during brewing.

The coffee maker draws approximately 1500W during brewing, while the warming plate uses around 200W to maintain optimal coffee temperature. Always ensure proper electrical connections and use a qualified electrician for installation. 


### How long does it take for Miele coffee makers to brew?

Miele coffee makers brew a full pot of coffee in approximately 4-6 minutes, depending on the selected brewing program and coffee volume. The heating element remains active for 15-30 minutes after brewing to keep the coffee warm. 

Express or single-cup brewing modes complete in just 2-3 minutes, ideal for quick mornings. The brewing time slightly increases when using fresh ground coffee or alternative brewing methods like espresso or cappuccino.
