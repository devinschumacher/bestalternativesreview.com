## FAQ


### What are metallic hair dyes?

Metallic hair dyes create vivid, high-impact colors that range from deep blacks to bright, shimmering shadings. These specialized products contain reflective pigments that catch light, giving your hair a modern, edgy look. 

Unlike traditional hair colors that primarily rely on solid pigments, metallic dyes incorporate fine metal particles or holographic pigments to produce their distinctive effects. This results in a broader spectrum of metallic shades compared to other hair coloring techniques. 


### What types of metallic dyes are available?

Metallic hair dyes come in two main varieties: single-process and multi-process. Single-process dyes can be used alone to achieve metallic effects on natural or previously colored hair, while multi-process dyes require bleach to create deeper, more intense shades. 

These dyes are also available in several formulations: paste, cream, and powder. Paste and cream formulas are applied like traditional hair color, while powder formulations require a developer and mixing with water. 


### How do metallic hair dyes work?

Metallic dyes contain specialized pigments that reflect light at specific wavelengths, creating vibrant metallic effects. These pigments can range from metallic silver and gold to holographic silver and blue. 

The color appearance depends on several factors, including the type of dye, the hair's natural color, and lighting conditions. These dyes often produce more dynamic results when used on hair that has been previously lightened or treated with bleach. 


### What are the benefits of metallic hair dyes?

Metallic hair dyes offer several advantages, including intense color saturation, long-lasting results, and versatile applications. They combine the strength and coverage of traditional hair color with the reflective properties of metallic paints. 

The colors tend to be more saturated and vibrant compared to standard hair color options, making them ideal for bold statements or evening out dramatic haircuts. These dyes also offer better hold and shine retention than temporary or semi-permanent alternatives. 


### Are metallic hair dyes safe to use?

Metallic hair dyes are generally considered safe for use when applied according to the manufacturer's instructions. However, like all hair dye products, they may cause sensitivity reactions in some individuals. 

It's important to perform a patch test before application and use protective gloves to minimize exposure. People with metal sensitivities or existing scalp conditions should exercise additional caution and consider consulting a professional before use. 


### How do metallic dyes compare to other hair color options?

Metallic dyes offer several advantages over other hair color options. They provide more intense color with better light reflection properties compared to regular hair color. Unlike temporary or semi-permanent colors, metallic dyes offer longer-lasting results and more consistent color retention. 

However, they are more expensive than basic hair color products and typically require more specialized application techniques. Multi-process metallic dyes also need bleach, which adds to the cost and complexity of the coloring process.
