### What safety features should I look for in toddler toy trains?

For safety, ensure the train has rounded corners and smooth edges. Avoid small parts that could pose a choking hazard and check that all moving parts are securely fastened. 

The track should have secure clips to prevent parts from coming loose, and the motor should be reliable to prevent sudden movement that could startle children.

### How many toy trains are recommended for a toddler?

Starting with just one or two trains allows toddlers to focus on playing with them, which helps in developing imagination and coordination. As they grow more comfortable, you can add more trains to their collection.

### What type of power source should I choose?

Many toddler toy trains use rechargeable batteries. These are safer than single-use batteries and more convenient for parents to manage. 

Some trains run on tracks equipped with conductive strips, eliminating the need for batteries entirely. This option is ideal if your toddler has sensitive skin or you're concerned about battery toxicity.
