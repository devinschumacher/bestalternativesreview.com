### How does an apple corer work?

Apple corers work by inserting a hollow shaft into the apple, which removes the core through a central tube or compartment. Some models allow for adjustable depths to prevent over-coreing the fruit. 

The design typically includes a cutting mechanism that removes the core while leaving the apple intact, though some modern versions can also cut the apple into slices. 


### What types of apple corers are available?

Common types include single-core devices for simple apple preparation, multiple-core containers that hold several apples simultaneously, and specialized models for varieties with larger cores. 

Handle designs vary from straight to ergonomic, with some featuring adjustable mechanisms for different fruit sizes. Portable options are available for outdoor cooking or picnics. 


### Are apple corers difficult to use?

### Can apple corers damage the fruit?
