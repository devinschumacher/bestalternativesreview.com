### What is a Logitech USB Receiver?

A Logitech USB Receiver is a small device that connects to your computer's USB port and allows you to pair and use various Logitech wireless devices, including keyboards, mice, and gamepads. The receiver acts as a bridge between your wireless devices and your computer. 

Each Logitech wireless device requires a single USB receiver to function, even if you have multiple devices from the same series. For example, a single USB receiver can power both a wireless mouse and keyboard at the same time. 


### What types of devices work with Logitech USB Receivers?

Logitech USB Receivers support a wide range of wireless devices, including but not limited to keyboards, mice, gamepads, and trackballs. This includes both standard and ergonomic variants, as well as specialized gaming models. 

Some devices may require additional software installations for specific features, but basic functionality works out of the box with the USB receiver.

### How do I install a Logitech USB Receiver?

Installation is straightforward. Simply insert the USB receiver into an available USB port on your computer. Windows and Mac systems automatically recognize the receiver and pair it with your wireless devices. 

If pairing issues occur, you can perform a quick reset by unplugging the receiver, waiting 10 seconds, and then replugging it into a different USB port. 


### What's the range of Logitech USB Receiver devices?

Standard Logitech USB receivers offer a wireless range of up to 30 feet (10 meters) in open space, with minor obstacles like books or furniture not significantly affecting performance. 

Gaming-focused receivers often support extended ranges of up to 100 feet (30 meters) in unobstructed environments, making them suitable for larger offices or gaming setups. 

![Logitech-Usb-Receiver-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Logitech-Usb-Receiver-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How long do Logitech USB Receiver batteries last?

Non-rechargeable battery receivers typically last 8-12 months with regular use, depending on the number of devices paired and usage frequency. 

Rechargeable battery receivers need 12-24 hours to fully charge and can last 6-12 months before requiring a replacement battery. 


### How do I troubleshoot connectivity issues?

If devices don't pair with the USB receiver, ensure the receiver is properly seated in a USB port and try using a different port. Additionally, make sure all devices are within the specified range and not obstructed by metal objects. 

For persistent issues, reset the receiver by removing it from the port, waiting 10 seconds, and then trying again. If problems continue, consider updating your computer's drivers or performing a system restore.
