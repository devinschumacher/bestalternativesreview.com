### How should baby dolls be cared for?

Care instructions vary by doll, but most soft baby dolls require spot cleaning or machine washing in a mesh bag. Check the manufacturer's guidelines for specific care requirements. 

Regularly inspect the doll for loose parts, wear, or damage. Replace or repair any damaged toys to ensure continued safety and enjoyment for your child. 


### What safety features should I look for in a baby doll?

Check that all parts of the doll, including hair, clothing, and accessories, are securely attached and free from small parts that could pose a choking hazard. 

Look for dolls that are washable, have rounded edges, and avoid those with sharp accessories like pacifiers with metal parts.
