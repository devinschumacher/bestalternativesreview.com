Martex towels are known for their quality and durability, making them a popular choice for both residential and commercial use. ### Material Composition

Martex offers towels in various materials, including cotton, microfiber, and blends. Cotton towels are suitable for absorbent, breathable options, while microfiber provides exceptional dryness and quick absorption. Blended fabrics often combine the best properties of both materials, offering durability and absorption capabilities.

### Towel Weight and Thickness

Towel weight is measured in grams per square meter (gsm), with higher weights generally indicating thicker, more plush towels. For everyday use, towels in the 400-600 gsm range offer a good balance of softness and absorption. Commercial or guest towels may need higher weights (800 gsm and above) for durability and increased drying power. 


### Color and Pattern Options

Martex provides a wide range of color and pattern options, from solid colors to elaborate patterns. Consider the towel's intended use and its placement in your home or establishment when selecting these features. Solid colors typically offer more versatility across different decors and maintenance requirements.

### Intended Use

Understanding the towel's primary use is crucial when selecting Martex products. Bath towels require different specifications than hand towels or beach towels. For instance, bath towels should focus on thickness and absorbency, while hand towels need lightweight construction and quick-drying properties.
