## FAQ


### What is home defense spray?

Home defense spray is a non-lethal, chemical-based deterrent used for personal protection against potential threats in the home. When activated, it creates a cloud of irritating particles that temporarily incapacitates attackers, allowing for safe retreat or further response. 

These sprays typically contain capsaicin or other irritants that trigger the body's natural defense mechanisms, causing immediate discomfort and prompting rapid withdrawal. They are available in various forms, including aerosols, canisters, and specialized containers, each designed for specific deployment scenarios. 


### How effective is home defense spray?

Home defense spray is highly effective for immediate personal protection, with most users reporting successful deterrent effects within seconds of activation. The precise duration of effectiveness varies by product but typically lasts between 30 seconds to several minutes, providing time for safe retreat or additional response measures. 

While the primary function is to deter attackers, some sprays also include secondary benefits like tear gas or pepper content to further disrupt an attacker's ability to continue their threat. 


### What are the key ingredients in home defense spray?

The primary ingredient is capsaicin, derived from chili peppers, which creates the irritant effect. Some premium products may include additional components like hydroquinone, phenol, or other irritants for enhanced effectiveness. It's important to note that the concentration and combination of ingredients can significantly affect product performance. 

These sprays typically contain between 1% to 2% capsaicin by volume, though specialized military-grade versions may contain higher concentrations for professional use. Always check product specifications for the most accurate ingredient details. 


### Are home defense sprays legal to own and use?

Home defense sprays are legal to own and use in most jurisdictions, though specific regulations vary by location. It's important to check local laws to ensure compliance with ownership and carry restrictions. 

Most areas allow the purchase and possession of home defense sprays for personal protection, but some may have restrictions on carrying or displaying them in public. Always familiarize yourself with local regulations to avoid legal complications. 


### How should I store home defense spray?

Home defense spray should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Most products recommend temperatures between 60-80°F (15-27°C) and a humidity level below 75%. Proper storage helps maintain product effectiveness and ensures safe handling. 

Store the spray upright to prevent leakage and in its original container to preserve packaging integrity. Some products may require specific storage conditions, so always refer to individual product instructions for guidance. 


### How should I use home defense spray?

When using home defense spray, stand between 3-5 feet from the threat and aim directly at their face. Activate the spray and move away quickly, maintaining distance while the irritant takes effect. Avoid getting the spray in your eyes or on your skin. 

Always use short bursts to prevent unnecessary exposure and maintain control over the situation. If the threat persists, continue to apply short bursts of spray while maintaining a safe distance.
