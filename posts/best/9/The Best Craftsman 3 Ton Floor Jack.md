## Buyer's Guide

A 3-ton floor jack is a powerful tool for lifting heavy vehicles and equipment, making it an essential piece of automotive and industrial equipment. Understanding its features and specifications will help you choose the right jack for your needs. 


### Technical Specifications

The jack's primary rating is its lifting capacity, which determines how much weight it can safely support. Additionally, consider the jack's stroke length (how far it can lift) and its load-bearing capacity (how much weight it can hold once lifted). 


### Design and Build Quality

Materials like forged steel provide greater strength and durability but increase weight. Check if the jack has a tubular design for added stability or features like a telescoping shaft that can extend the jack's reach. 


### Safety Features

Built-in safety mechanisms like automatic locking, hydraulic pressure relief valves, and warning systems are crucial. Look for features that prevent accidental lowering while in use, such as safety pins or secondary locking mechanisms. 


### Operational Considerations

Assess the jack's operation method (manual, hydraulic, or electric), as well as its activation force. Consider the working height range and whether it includes a base plate for stability. Also, check if it comes with essential accessories like stand-off blocks or mounting plates.

### How do I properly use a Craftsman 3 Ton Floor Jack?

Use wheel chocks to stabilize the vehicle and secure the jack with the built-in safety lock before working under the vehicle.
