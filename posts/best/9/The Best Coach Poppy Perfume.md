## Buyer's Guide

Coach's Poppy fragrance combines fruity and woody notes to create a versatile scent that works well for both daytime and evening wear. When choosing a Poppy perfume, consider your personal preference for floral or fruity accords, as well as the intensity level that suits your lifestyle. 


### Top Notes

Poppy perfumes typically start with fresh, uplifting top notes that last about 5-10 minutes. Common top notes in these fragrances include pink pepper, bergamot, and grapefruit, which provide immediate character to the scent. 


### Heart Notes

The middle or heart notes form the core of the fragrance and last around 1-2 hours. In Poppy perfumes, these notes might include juicy peach, cherry, or apricot, along with hints of rose or jasmine. The heart notes give the scent its signature character and should align with your personal taste. 


### Base Notes

Base notes develop slowly and can last anywhere from 3-6 hours, providing depth and longevity to the fragrance. For Poppy perfumes, base notes might include warm vanilla, woody cedar, or light patchouli. These elements help the scent project and create a lasting impression. 


### Factors Influencing Scent Perception

The way a Poppy perfume smells can vary based on your skin type, body temperature, and the amount of product applied. Some people may notice more of the fruity elements, while others might pick up more on the woody undertones. Testing the fragrance on your skin before purchasing is the best way to determine if it's a good match for you.
