### What is a port glass?

A port glass is a specialized wine glass designed specifically for enjoying port wine. This type of glass features a smaller bowl than a standard wine glass, which helps to preserve the port's flavors and concentration. 

The glass typically has a wider opening at the top than a traditional wine glass, which allows the aromas to develop properly as the port warms in the glass. The shape is also designed to keep the port warm while drinking, enhancing its taste and complexity. 


### Why choose a port glass?

A port glass helps to showcase the unique characteristics of port wine by preserving its flavors and concentration. The specialized design enhances the drinking experience by allowing proper aeration and warming of the wine as it's enjoyed. 

Using a port glass can also help prevent the wine from oxidizing too quickly, which is particularly important for this type of fortified wine. The proper glassware highlights port's rich flavors and smooth finish.
