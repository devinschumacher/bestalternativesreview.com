Mild soap is specifically formulated to be gentler on the skin, making it suitable for daily use and ideal for people with sensitive skin. It often contains moisturizing ingredients and has a lower concentration of harsh chemicals compared to regular soap. 

Mild soaps usually have a pH closer to skin's natural pH of 5.5, which helps maintain skin's protective barrier. They're less likely to cause irritation, redness, or dry skin that can occur with harsher soaps. 


### What ingredients should I look for in mild soap?

Ingredients like glycerin, shea butter, and aloe vera are common in mild soaps, as they help moisturize and soothe the skin. Look for products that list natural or organic ingredients and avoid those with synthetic fragrances or dyes, which can be irritating. 

Avoid harsh chemicals like sodium lauryl sulfate (SLS) and triclosan. These can strip the skin of its natural oils and potentially cause irritation. Instead, choose mild soaps that are ph balanced and gentle enough for daily use.

Yes, mild soaps are designed for regular use and effectively remove dirt, oil, and bacteria without stripping the skin of its natural moisture. Their gentler formulation makes them suitable for daily cleansing, even on sensitive areas like the face and private parts.

### Can I use mild soap every day?
