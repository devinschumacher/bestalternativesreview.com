## Buyer's Guide

A 5x5 picture frame is a popular choice for displaying smaller photos and memorabilia. Understanding the key features and specifications will help you select the right frame for your collection. 


### Frame Material

Common materials include wood, metal, and plastic. Wood offers natural grain patterns, while metal adds industrial appeal. Plastic frames are lightweight and available in various colors. 

![5x5-Picture-Frame-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/5x5-Picture-Frame-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)
