Hitachi offers both corded and cordless drill models. Corded drills provide consistent power without the need for recharging but require access to an electrical outlet. Cordless drills offer greater freedom of movement and convenience, with options for various battery sizes and voltages. Consider your typical usage and job site conditions to determine which type best suits your needs.
