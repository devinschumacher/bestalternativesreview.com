### Heating Elements: Ceramic vs. Tourmaline

Ceramic plates distribute heat more evenly, reducing the risk of hot spots that can damage hair. Tourmaline coating releases negative ions that help neutralize positively charged hair molecules, resulting in softer, more damage-resistant styled hair. For professional results, look for straighteners with both ceramic plates and tourmaline coating.
