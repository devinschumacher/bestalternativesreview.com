A blood vial necklace is a jewelry piece that incorporates a sealed glass vial containing blood, typically red glass or crystal. The vial is often suspended in the center or bottom of the necklace, creating a distinctive centerpiece.

### How does a blood vial necklace function?

Most blood vial necklaces include a small cap that seals the glass vial, preventing blood leakage. The sealed vial remains contained within the necklace's structure, maintaining its integrity over time. 

Some necklaces may feature additional mechanisms to prevent the blood from coming into direct contact with the skin or clothing, ensuring the piece remains practical and wearable. 


### What materials are used in blood vial necklaces?

Standard components include glass vials in red or crystal, sealed with caps made of metal or plastic. The necklace itself may be crafted from various materials such as silver, gold, or blackened metal, depending on the designer's preference.

### How should blood vial necklaces be cared for?
