## FAQ


### What is a dog soccer ball?

A dog soccer ball is an exercise toy designed specifically for dogs, featuring durable construction to withstand biting and chewing. These balls typically include features like squeaking mechanisms and varying textures to engage dogs' senses and encourage play. 

Manufacturers often create these balls using puncture-resistant materials like Nitrile or TPU (Thermoplastic Polyurethane), making them ideal for dogs who like to bite or chew on their toys. 


### How do I choose the right dog soccer ball?

Consider your dog's size and energy level when selecting a soccer ball. Smaller breeds may prefer softer balls, while active dogs might need firmer options that resist flattening. 

Check the ball's inflation requirements to ensure it holds its shape during play. Some balls come with multiple sizes for deflation, allowing them to be used safely with dogs of different ages and sizes. 


### What are the benefits of playing with a dog soccer ball?

These balls help improve dogs' coordination, balance, and reaction time through running and quick turns. The physical activity also strengthens their cardiovascular health. 

The interactive component – such as chasing the ball – mimics natural hunting behaviors, which can reduce destructive chewing and increase mental stimulation. 


### How durable are dog soccer balls?

High-quality dog soccer balls can last for months with regular use, thanks to their puncture-resistant materials like Nitrile or TPU. The outer layers typically protect the internal mechanisms from damage even when subjected to bites and tugs. 

However, balls with softer surfaces may wear down more quickly when subjected to repeated bites from very aggressive chewers. Regular inspection for worn spots or loose seams helps ensure the ball remains safe for continued play. 


### What sizes are available for dog soccer balls?

Most dog soccer balls come in a range of sizes from small (8-10 inches diameter) to extra-large (16-18 inches diameter). The appropriate size depends on the dog's breed and natural body size. 

Some manufacturers offer specific sizes for puppies and senior dogs, as well as sizes that can be adjusted for inflation based on the dog's activity level. 


### How do I clean a dog soccer ball?

Routine cleaning of dog soccer balls involves wiping the surface with a damp cloth and mild soap solution. For dirtier balls, you can place them in the washing machine with a mild pet-safe detergent, then air dry. 

Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach, as these can damage the ball's surface and potentially harm your dog if ingested during play. Regular cleaning helps maintain hygiene and extends the ball's lifespan.
