### What is a sheer bralette?

A sheer bralette is a delicate, lingerie-style bra made from translucent or see-through fabric. The lightweight design often features thin straps and no underwire, making it ideal for everyday wear under casual outfits. 

These bralettes combine the support of traditional bras with the airy feel of sheer fabrics, perfect for adding a touch of sophistication to your wardrobe without compromising on comfort. 


### What materials are used to make sheer bralettes?

Sheer bralettes are typically constructed from fine mesh, chiffon, or lace fabric. These materials offer a soft, breathable quality while maintaining shape and support. 

The construction often includes elastic edges and built-in cups to ensure both lift and proper positioning while maintaining a seamless appearance.

### How should I clean my sheer bralette?

Machine wash your sheer bralette using a gentle cycle and cold water to preserve the fabric's texture and color. 

Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents that can damage the delicate material. Instead, opt for a lingerie-specific detergent to keep your bralette looking its best.
