### How do I properly set up a North Face tent?

Select a flat, level site away from hazards like rocks and tree roots. Pitch the tent according to the included instructions, usually starting with the footprint (floor), then the center pole and outer walls, securing all guy lines to anchors in the ground. 

Always check the weather forecast before setting up and stake out the tent before fully erecting it. This helps prevent damage from wind and ensures proper setup instructions are followed. 


### What features should I look for in a North Face tent?

### How do I maintain and clean my North Face tent?

Regularly clean the tent fabric using mild soap and water, avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage the coatings. Store the tent in a dry place when not in use to prevent mold and mildew growth. 

Check the poles for bends or corrosion annually and store them with the tent. Replace any damaged guy lines or stakes promptly to maintain proper functionality. 


### What is the weight capacity of North Face tents?

The weight capacity varies by model but typically ranges from 200 to 400 pounds. It includes the weight of the tent itself, gear, and all users. Always account for additional weight when determining the maximum safe load.
