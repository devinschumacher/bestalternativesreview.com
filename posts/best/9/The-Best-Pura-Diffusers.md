### What is a Pura diffuser?

A Pura diffuser is an electronic device that disperses essential oils into the air through various methods, including ultrasonic waves, cold air diffusion, and nebulization. These diffusers allow users to enjoy the therapeutic benefits of essential oils while creating a pleasant ambient scent. 

Developed by the Swedish company Pura, these diffusers offer a range of features including adjustable mist levels, timer functions, and compatibility with essential oils and water. They are designed for home and personal use, providing a natural way to enhance air quality and promote relaxation. 


### What types of Pura diffusers are available?

Pura offers several diffusers, including the Pura Classic, Pura Mini, and Pura Pebble. The Classic model features adjustable mist levels and a built-in timer, while the Mini and Pebble models offer portability with similar functionality in smaller packages. 

Each model includes essential oil diffusing capabilities, with the Classic and Mini featuring water tanks for nebulization, while the Pebble uses ultrasonic technology for oil and water diffusion. 

![Pura-Diffusers-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Pura-Diffusers-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do Pura diffusers work?

Most Pura diffusers utilize ultrasonic technology to create microscopic mist particles that disperse essential oils and water into the air. This method does not heat the oils, preserving their therapeutic properties. 

Some models, like the Classic and Mini, can also diffuse essential oils without water through nebulization. The Pebble model uses ultrasonic technology to diffuse both essential oils and water together. 


### What maintenance is required for Pura diffusers?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the diffuser tank and any included accessories weekly to prevent mineral buildup. This is typically done by filling the tank with water, running the diffuser for 3-5 minutes, and then emptying and drying it thoroughly. 

Essential oil cartridges should be replaced every 2-3 months based on usage and room size. The air filter in the Classic model should be replaced every 6-12 months depending on usage and environmental factors.

### Can Pura diffusers be used with any essential oils?

Most Pura diffusers are compatible with essential oils sold in their proprietary packaging or other reputable brands that match their specifications. These oils typically contain between 15-30% essential oil concentration. 

Users should avoid using concentrated carrier oils or those containing artificial fragrances. Always perform a skin patch test before regular use to ensure no adverse reactions occur. 


### How long do Pura diffusers last?

With regular use and proper maintenance, a Pura diffuser can last 3-5 years. The lifespan varies based on usage frequency, cleaning routines, and environmental factors such as humidity and temperature.
