### What makes 16x16 frames unique?

16x16 frames stand out in the marketplace due to their precise sizing and versatile applications. These frames eliminate the need for mitering and trimming, offering a clean installation process while providing ample space for artwork or mirrors. 

The standardized 16x16 inch dimensions make these frames ideal for matching sets, photo albums, or as spacers between larger frames, creating balanced gallery walls. 


### How do these frames compare to standard picture frames?

Standard picture frames often require mitering at the corners and may need additional pieces for larger artwork. In contrast, 16x16 frames install flush with the edges, reducing material waste and simplifying the framing process. 

While similar in basic function, 16x16 frames offer specific advantages in terms of size consistency and installation efficiency, making them particularly useful for repetitive framing projects or precise layout applications.
