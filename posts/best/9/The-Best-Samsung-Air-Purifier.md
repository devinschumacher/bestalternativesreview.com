### CADR Ratings

Superior air purification requires adequate Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR). Samsung models typically offer CADR ratings for particles and smoke. Higher CADR values correlate with better cleaning performance. Consider a model with CADR values that exceed the square footage of your room for optimal results. 


### Sensor Capabilities

Modern Samsung air purifiers feature advanced sensor technology. Particulate sensors monitor air quality and adjust filtration settings automatically. Some models also include formaldehyde sensors, which detect and display levels of this common indoor pollutant, allowing for targeted purification.
