## FAQ


### What exactly is a half wig?

A half wig, also known as a piece wig or extension wig, is a smaller version of a full wig that covers only the top of the head or specific areas, such as the crown or sides. It attaches directly to existing hair, providing volume, length, or color without the bulk of a full wig. 

These wigs attach using clips, combs, or adhesives and can be removed easily, making them ideal for special occasions or when natural hair needs a quick styling boost. 


### How much hair does a half wig add?

Most half wigs add between 2-4 inches of length to the existing hair, though some can add more depending on their volume. The actual length increase varies based on the wig's density and texture. 

These wigs typically don't add as much volume as full wigs but provide a natural-looking boost that can dramatically change the appearance of shorter hair. 


### What are the main advantages of a half wig?

Half wigs offer several advantages: - Easy application and removal

- Quick transformation of hair length and style

- Less bulk compared to full wigs

- Cost-effective alternative to permanent hair changes

These wigs are particularly useful for special occasions, trying out new styles, or covering thinning hair areas without major commitment. 


### Who should consider a half wig?

Half wigs are suitable for: - Women with short hair who want added length or volume

- Those seeking a natural-looking hair extension

- People with fine hair who prefer less bulk

- Individuals needing temporary styling solutions

These wigs are ideal for anyone who wants to enhance their hairstyle without a long-term commitment. 


### What maintenance is required for half wigs?

Most half wigs require basic maintenance: - Daily brushing and styling with appropriate products

- Regular cleaning with specialized wig shampoo

- Storage in a protective wig pouch when not in use

These wigs usually need cleaning every 1-2 weeks, depending on usage and environmental conditions. 


### Can half wigs be customized?

Half wigs can be customized in several ways: - Adjusted to match natural hair color and texture

- Styled with heat tools for various looks

- Cut to specific lengths and shapes as needed

While some customization is possible, professional trimming and color correction may be required for the best results.
