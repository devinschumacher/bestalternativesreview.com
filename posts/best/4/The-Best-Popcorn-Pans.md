### What's the Best Size for a Popcorn Pan?

The ideal popcorn pan size depends on your cooking needs. For personal use, 1-2 liter pans are suitable. Families or frequent users might prefer 3-4 liter options, while occasional users could get by with 1 liter.

Hand wash your popcorn pan with mild dish soap and a soft sponge. Rinse thoroughly and dry immediately to prevent water spots and maintain non-stick coating.
