## Buyer's Guide

When selecting an inflatable pool, the Intex 15 x 48 pool offers an ideal balance of size and portability. This guide examines key features and considerations to help you make an informed choice. 


### Size and Shape

Measure your space before selecting a pool to ensure adequate clearance around the inflatable edges. The 15 x 48 configuration provides approximately 704 square feet of surface area, suitable for small groups but requiring careful placement consideration. 


### Material and Durability

The pool's vinyl material is crucial for longevity and water retention. Higher-quality PVC options resist punctures and UV damage better than cheaper alternatives. Check for reinforced seams and double-walled construction in premium models. 


### Ground Coverage

Prepare the ground where you'll place the pool by removing debris and leveling the surface. Grass may work for temporary setups, but concrete or smooth surfaces prevent damage to the pool's base. Avoid sharp objects that could puncture the vinyl. 


### Setup and Storage

Consider the ease of setup when measuring your storage space. The pool typically requires around 15-20 minutes to inflate fully using water pressure. Opt for models with reinforced handles and stable bases for simple setup and transport.
