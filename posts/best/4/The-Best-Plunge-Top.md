### What is a Plunge Top?

A plunge top is a button-style closure that extends below the neckline, creating a deep V-shape at the front. It's commonly found on dresses, blouses, and bodysuits, adding both style and practicality by allowing for easy on-and-off wear. 

This design feature not only enhances the visual appeal of the garment but also provides better coverage than a standard button-down front, making it suitable for various occasions from casual outings to formal events. 


### How Do You Properly Wear a Plunge Top?

Start by aligning the front edge of the garment with your body's natural contour. The deep V-neck should sit slightly below your breasts to create the desired visual effect while ensuring modesty. 

Ensure the bottom hem is even on both sides and tuck in any excess fabric. For optimal comfort and appearance, consider wearing a well-fitted bra that matches the silhouette of your top. 

![Plunge-Top-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Plunge-Top-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What is the Best Way to Care for a Plunge Top?

Most plunge tops can be machine washed on a gentle cycle using mild detergent. Avoid bleach and harsh detergents that may damage the fabric or elastic components. 

Air drying is recommended to maintain the shape and integrity of the garment. If you must use a dryer, select the lowest heat setting and place a cloth between the garment and the dryer's heated elements. 


### Can You Wear a Plunge Top with Pajamas?

While plunge tops are appropriate for many casual occasions, they may not be the most practical choice for everyday loungewear. Consider opting for a more relaxed, everyday top for your pajama set. 

If you want to incorporate a plunge top into your nightwear rotation, choose a softer, more relaxed style with a higher neckline for better coverage and comfort during sleep.

### What Should You Wear Under a Plunge Top?

A well-fitted bra with good support is essential under a plunge top. Look for styles with extended straps and a deep plunge front to match the garment's design. 

For added modesty, consider wearing a matching bralette or slip that extends down to the bottom hem of your top. This ensures a smooth silhouette and prevents any visibility of your bra's underwiring. 


### How Do You Style a Plunge Top?

Pair a simple plunge top with tailored pants or skirts for a sleek, professional look suitable for work or formal events. Opt for solid colors or subtle patterns for the bottom half to draw attention to your top. 

Create a casual yet stylish outfit by tucking the top into high-waisted jeans or a pencil skirt. Accessorize with statement jewelry and a structured handbag to complete the look.
