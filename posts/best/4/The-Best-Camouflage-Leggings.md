### How do you maintain the quality of camouflage leggings?

Machine wash your camouflage leggings using cold water and mild detergent to prevent color bleeding and fabric damage. Avoid bleach and fabric softeners, which can compromise the integrity of the camouflage pattern and fabric. 

Air drying in a mesh bag is best to prevent shrinkage and maintain the shape of the leggings. If you must use a dryer, opt for the lowest heat setting and remove them promptly to prevent excess static cling and fabric damage.
