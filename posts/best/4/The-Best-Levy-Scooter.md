### What are the key components of a Levy Scooter?

A Levy Scooter usually consists of a motorized board, battery pack, handlebars, wheels, and control system. The motor is typically powered by a lithium-ion battery, and the control system allows the rider to adjust the level of assistance.

### How fast can a Levy Scooter go?
