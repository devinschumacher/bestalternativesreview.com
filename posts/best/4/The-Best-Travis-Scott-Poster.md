## Buyer's Guide

Wall art featuring Travis Scott's iconic designs has become increasingly popular among fans. When selecting a poster, several key factors can help you find the perfect piece. 


### Image Quality and Resolution

Look for posters with high resolution and clear printing. The image should appear sharp, especially in areas with details like text or logos. Premium posters often use higher-quality paper and printers, resulting in better visual clarity. 


### Size and Frame Options

Consider the available frame styles and sizes to match your space and personal preference. Common poster sizes range from standard (11x14 inches) to large formats suitable for living rooms or bedrooms. Frame options vary from simple matting to elaborate shadow boxes, allowing you to customize your display. 


### Material Durability

Print quality and material durability affect the poster's longevity. Look for photo paper with glossy or matte finishes, depending on your preference. For high-traffic areas or outdoor installations, consider posters with waterproof or UV-resistant properties to prevent fading and damage. 


### Printed vs. Unframed Options

Both printed and unframed poster variants offer advantages. Printed posters come ready to hang with professional mounting, while unframed options provide more flexibility for custom framing. Consider your installation needs and the additional cost of framing when making your choice.
