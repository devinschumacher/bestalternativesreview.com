### What is cortisol and why should I care about its levels?

Cortisol is a hormone produced by the adrenal glands in response to stress. While essential for normal bodily functions, chronically elevated cortisol levels can contribute to various health issues including increased abdominal fat, weakened immune system, and disrupted sleep patterns. 

Reducing cortisol through natural means like proper nutrition and supplementation can help support overall health and well-being. 


### Which key ingredients are effective in reducing cortisol levels?

Several well-studied ingredients can help reduce cortisol. These include Rhodiola rosea, Ashwagandha, and Magnolia bark extract. **Rhodiola rosea** helps regulate the HPA axis, **Ashwagandha** possesses adaptogenic properties, and **Magnolia bark extract** inhibits cortisol production. 

These ingredients work by modulating stress response mechanisms, promoting relaxation, and supporting hormonal balance.
