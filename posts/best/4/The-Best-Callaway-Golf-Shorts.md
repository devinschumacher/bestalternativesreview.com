When selecting golf shorts, it's important to consider several key factors that impact both performance and comfort during your round. Callaway offers a variety of golf shorts, each designed with specific features to enhance your game and keep you comfortable throughout your swing.

High-quality stretch fabrics provide flexibility while keeping you cool and dry through moisture-wicking technology.
