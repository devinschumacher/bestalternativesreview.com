### How should I maintain and store a Milwaukee utility knife?

Regularly check the blade for sharpness and replace it when dull. Use a sharpening stone or professional service for proper blade sharpening. When not in use, store the knife in its original sheath to protect the blade and maintain its balance. 

Keep the knife in a dry place, away from extreme temperatures and corrosive substances. Regularly inspect the tool for any signs of damage before each use.

### Are Milwaukee utility knives suitable for heavy-duty cutting tasks?

Milwaukee utility knives excel in both light and heavy-duty cutting tasks due to their robust construction. They effectively slice through materials like wood, plastic, and metal, thanks to their precision-engineered blades and durable handles.
