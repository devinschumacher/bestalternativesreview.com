## Buyer's Guide

Transitioning from the driving range to the golf course, finding the right hat can enhance both your performance and your style. New Era golf hats blend comfort with functionality, offering several features to consider before making your selection. 


### MATERIALS AND CONSTRUCTION

High-quality New Era golf caps are typically made from moisture-wicking fabrics that keep you dry during your round. Look for reinforced stitching in the bill and crown for durability, and consider the ventilation options like mesh panels to maintain airflow.

### BILL SHAPE AND SIZE

The bill's shape can affect how the hat sits on your head and how much shade it provides. Opt for a straight bill for consistent shade coverage across your face, or a curved bill for a more distinctive look. The size should allow for some adjustment to prevent movement during your swing. 


### ADJUSTABILITY AND FIT

A properly fitting cap should sit level on your head with minimal wobble. Most New Era golf hats feature adjustable back closures, but the fit should still allow for easy sizing adjustments before and during your round. Consider how the hat feels during simulated swing motions to ensure it remains secure.

### Can any New Era golf hat be worn backward?

Most New Era golf hats can be worn both ways, with backward and casual styles becoming increasingly popular. The fit may change slightly when worn backward, so consider this when selecting your size. 

Some hats may be specifically designed for forward wear only, usually indicated in the product description. These typically feature a structured crown that doesn't hold its shape well when reversed. 


### How do I clean my New Era golf hat?

Machine wash your hat with a mild detergent in a mesh bag to protect the shape. Turn it inside out and wash it separately to avoid color bleeding. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners. 

Hand washing is also effective - use cool water and mild soap. Gently massage the hat and avoid twisting or stretching. Rinse thoroughly and air dry flat, reshape as needed before drying.
