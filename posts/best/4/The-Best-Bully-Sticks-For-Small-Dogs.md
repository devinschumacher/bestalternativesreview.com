### What are bully sticks, and why are they suitable for small dogs?

Bully sticks are durable chew treats made from cow penises, offering a natural chew alternative to rawhide. They're ideal for small dogs because they provide a long-lasting chewing session without the risk of choking associated with smaller rawhide pieces. 

The structure of bully sticks makes them resistant to tearing, allowing small dogs to enjoy a satisfying chew while preventing digestive issues that can arise from rapid consumption of smaller chews. 


### Are bully sticks safe for all small dogs?

Bully sticks are generally safe for small dogs when given in appropriate sizes. However, dogs with certain health conditions like kidney disease or gastrointestinal issues may need to avoid them. 

Always check the product size recommendations and choose a chew that's slightly smaller than your dog's mouth to prevent choking hazards.

### How long do bully sticks typically last?

The durability of a bully stick depends on your dog's chewing speed and the specific product. On average, small to medium-sized bullies last 2-3 hours, while larger ones can last up to 6 hours. 

Freezing the bully stick before giving it to your dog can enhance its durability and make it last longer. 


### Can bully sticks help with dental health?

The act of chewing on a bully stick can help remove plaque and tartar, contributing to better dental health. The abrasive surface helps scrub away bacteria and food particles from your dog's teeth.
