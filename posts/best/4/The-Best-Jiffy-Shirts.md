## FAQ


### What is a Jiffy Shirt?

A Jiffy Shirt is a lightweight, quick-drying t-shirt made from moisture-wicking fabric. It's designed for active wear and outdoor activities where breathable, comfortable clothing is essential. 

Typically featuring moisture-absorbing properties, ventilation panels, and moisture-repellent finishes, these shirts help keep you dry and comfortable during exertion. 


### What are the key features of Jiffy Shirts?

Most Jiffy Shirts incorporate moisture-wicking technology to pull sweat away from the skin, keeping the wearer dry. They often include mesh panels for ventilation and are made from lightweight fabric for added comfort. 

Popular features also include stretchy materials for mobility, UV protection (usually rated at UPF 30 or higher), and colorblocking designs for visual appeal. 


### What are Jiffy Shirts made from?

These shirts are typically constructed from polyester or a blend of polyester and spandex. The fabric is engineered to be lightweight yet durable, with moisture-wicking properties that help prevent sweat buildup. 

Some premium models incorporate moisture-repellent finishes or use specialized fabrics like CoolMax, which is designed to enhance breathability and wicking performance. 


### How do Jiffy Shirts compare to regular t-shirts?

Jiffy Shirts offer superior moisture management compared to standard cotton t-shirts, which can retain moisture and cause discomfort during physical activity. They also typically provide better ventilation through their mesh paneling and stretchier construction. 

While regular t-shirts are more breathable when dry, Jiffy Shirts maintain their moisture-wicking properties even when sweaty, making them more practical for active wear and outdoor activities. 


### What sizes are available?

Jiffy Shirts come in a range of standard sizes, typically including S, M, L, XL, and occasionally XXL. Many manufacturers offer slim fits, regular fits, and performance fits to accommodate different body types and preferences. 

For the most accurate sizing information, always check the specific brand's size chart. Some manufacturers may offer different fits or sizing standards, so it's important to verify the details for your preferred brand. 


### How should Jiffy Shirts be cared for?

These shirts can typically be machine washed using a mild detergent. It's best to wash them separately from other clothing to prevent snagging and maintain the integrity of the moisture-wicking properties. 

Air drying or using a low heat setting in the dryer helps maintain the fabric's elasticity and moisture-wicking capabilities. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the moisture-wicking properties.
