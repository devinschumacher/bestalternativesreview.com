### How should bell bottom leggings fit?

The ideal fit is slightly tailored through the hips and thighs with a slight flare at the cuffs. They should not be too loose, which can affect your form during exercise, nor too tight, which can cause discomfort. 

Most brands recommend measuring your hips and comparing to their size chart. A snug waistband that stays in place during movement is key, but the flared legs should allow for about 1-2 inches of room at the ankle. 


### Are bell bottom leggings suitable for different activities?

While the flare can be practical for yoga and Pilates, activities requiring precise ankle control may be more comfortable with straight-leg options. They're generally suitable for casual workouts, stretching, and low-impact exercise. 

The elastic waist and stretchy material make them adaptable for various body types, though deep flares might restrict movement in more intense activities. 


### How do you wash and care for bell bottom leggings?

Machine wash your leggings using a gentle detergent in cold or lukewarm water to maintain color and elasticity. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals that can damage the fabric and reduce the lifespan of your workout clothes. 

Avoid drying in direct sunlight or using a dryer, as this can cause shrinkage and damage to the elastic. Instead, air dry your leggings indoors to keep them in top condition.
