### What age ranges are appropriate for princess toys?

Most princess toys are designed for toddlers and young children, typically ages 3-7. These toys often feature simple mechanisms and larger pieces suitable for small hands. However, some brands offer collections specifically for infants (0-3 years) with chunky pieces and fewer moving parts. 

It's important to check the age recommendations on each toy and ensure the product matches your child's developmental stage and safety requirements.

Princess toys traditionally target girls due to their themes and designs, but many modern options now appeal to both genders. Consider toys that encourage imagination, nurturing, and social skills, as these developmental benefits are universal.

### How should I clean and maintain princess toys?

Most princess toys require regular surface cleaning with mild soap and water, following manufacturer's instructions. Plush toys need spot cleaning for delicate fabrics, while electronic toys should be wiped externally with a slightly damp cloth. 

Check the toy's care requirements before purchase, and store in a dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent damage and maintain longevity.
