### Maintenance Requirements

Regular maintenance is crucial for optimal performance and longevity. This includes daily cleaning of the drip tray and grounds container, weekly descaling, and monthly cleaning of the brew group and filter screen. The machines automatically monitor descaling needs through built-in sensors, but users should still perform manual checks to ensure proper maintenance scheduling.
