## Buyer's Guide

Looking for the perfect poster to capture your love for Akira? With so many options available, finding the right one can be overwhelming. This guide will help you understand what makes a great Akira poster and how to choose the best one for your collection. 


### Artwork Quality and Details

Invest in a poster that captures the rich visual style of Akira. Look for high-quality printing with sharp details and accurate colors. Premium posters often feature better paper stock and smoother printing, making them stand out on your wall. 


### Print Size and Frame Compatibility

Measure your intended frame or wall space before purchasing. Common poster sizes include standard A3 (11.7" x 16.5") and A2 (16.5" x 23.4"). Consider whether you want the artwork to fill the frame or if you prefer some border around the edges. 


### Print Finish and Protection

Durability is key, especially for frequently viewed posters. Matte finish prints resist fingerprints and show less glare, while glossy prints offer vibrant colors but require more careful handling. For long-term display, look for waterproof or UV protection options to prevent fading. 


### Artist and Edition Information

If the poster includes original artwork, check the edition number to verify authenticity. Limited edition prints typically command higher value. For popular series like Akira, genuine prints often come with a certificate of authenticity.
