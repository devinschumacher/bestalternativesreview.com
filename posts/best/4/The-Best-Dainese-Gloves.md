Proper fit is crucial for both protection and comfort. Measure your hand using a cloth tape measure around the fullest part of the palm (excluding the thumb) and from the wrist to just below the knuckles. Gloves should have a snug fit without restricting blood flow or movement. Consider models with adjustable wrist straps or elastic panels for better lockdown. 


### Sizing and Hand Shape

Dainese gloves typically run true to size, but consider your hand shape when selecting dimensions. If you have a narrower hand, choose a size smaller than your measured palm circumference. Wider palms generally require larger sizes. Some models offer multiple width options (A, B, C) to accommodate different hand shapes and personal preferences.
