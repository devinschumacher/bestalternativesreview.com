## Buyer's Guide

Nike golf pullovers combine warmth with moisture management, making them a popular choice for cool-weather golfing. Understanding key features will help you find a pullover that enhances your performance and comfort on the course. 


### Materials and Construction

Nine-layer fabrics offer superior insulation without adding bulk. Look for advanced moisture-wicking properties that draw sweat away from your skin to keep you dry during your round. The construction should be tailored for golf, with strategic stitching to prevent snags during swing. 


### Insulation and Temperature Regulation

Three-ounce thermal fabric provides warmth without overheating, while zippered panels allow adjustment based on changing conditions. Consider models with internal gussets for enhanced mobility during your swing, maintaining your golf posture. 


### Fit and Tailoring

Golf-specific pullovers are engineered for performance, with slimmer fits that prevent bunching under your clubs. The hem should hit just above your hips for optimal swing freedom. Choose between cropped or full-length options based on personal preference and golf club style. 


### Additional Features

Look for ribbed cuffs and hem to prevent cold air from entering, with thumbholes in the cuffs for extra warmth. Many pullovers include secure pockets for your phone or golf tokens, with reflective materials for visibility during early morning or evening rounds.
