## FAQ

![Glass-Chair-Mat-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Glass-Chair-Mat-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What is a glass chair mat?

A glass chair mat is a protective barrier placed under office chairs, made from a thick sheet of tempered glass that prevents damage to floors from chair legs. Its primary functions are to protect both wooden and tile floors from scratches and dents caused by chair legs. 

The glass material also prevents furniture damage, as it creates an impenetrable surface that stops chairs from sinking into the floor. This protective feature is particularly useful in commercial settings where multiple chairs are used daily. 


### What size should I choose?

The standard glass chair mat size is 36 inches by 48 inches, which covers most standard office chairs. This size works for both single and double chair legs as long as they don't extend beyond 12 inches. 

Most mats measure 1/4 inch thick, which provides adequate protection for most chair legs. Mats thicker than 1/4 inch may be more difficult to clean and move across the floor. 

![Glass-Chair-Mat-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Glass-Chair-Mat-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I install it?

Installation is straightforward - simply place the glass mat over your existing floor covering, ensuring it covers the area where the chair legs will rest. No adhesives are required, and it can be moved easily whenever needed. 

For best results, the mat should be level with no more than a 1/8-inch difference in height at any point. This prevents the chair from wobbling on uneven surfaces. 


### What are the benefits?

In addition to floor protection, glass chair mats reduce noise from chair movements and prevent damage from spills by creating a barrier between the chair and floor. 

The non-slip surface also increases safety by preventing chairs from sliding across the floor, making it particularly useful in environments with multiple chairs in use. 

![Glass-Chair-Mat-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Glass-Chair-Mat-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are the drawbacks?

The primary drawback is the potential for breakage if the mat is dropped or impacted with a heavy object. While unlikely under normal use, this risk exists with tempered glass products. 

Installation requires careful positioning to avoid gaps under the chair legs, and the surface may show chair imprint marks after regular use, particularly with heavier chairs. 


### How long does it last?

With regular use, a glass chair mat typically lasts between 5-10 years, depending on the frequency of chair movement and environmental factors like foot traffic and maintenance. 

Most manufacturers offer warranties ranging from 2-5 years, which can be extended by proper installation and regular cleaning to prevent scratches that can reduce the mat's lifespan.
