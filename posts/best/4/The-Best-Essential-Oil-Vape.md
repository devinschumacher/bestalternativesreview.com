### What types of essential oils work best in vapes?

The most common essential oils used in vapes are those with natural, earthy, or woody scents, as these tend to hold up well during the heating process. Popular choices include lavender, eucalyptus, tea tree, frankincense, and cedarwood. 

Avoid using very volatile oils or those with exceptionally high boiling points, as these may cause the heating element to overheat. It's best to use essential oils specifically formulated for aromatherapy and vaporization.

Always start with a small amount of oil (usually 1-2 drops) and heat it for a few seconds to ensure it's properly vaporized. Never overheat the device, as this can damage the heating element and potentially release harmful compounds. 

Allow the vapor to cool slightly before inhaling, and avoid taking deep breaths directly from the heating chamber. It's also important to keep the device clean and free from debris to prevent clogging and maintain proper temperature regulation.
