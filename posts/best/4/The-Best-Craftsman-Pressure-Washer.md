## Buyer's Guide

A craftsman pressure washer combines durability and performance for professional cleaning results. Understanding key features ensures you select the right model for your needs. 


### Power and Pressure

Rated in PSI (pounds per square inch), pressure determines the cleaning force. For tough jobs, 2,000-3,000 PSI is effective. GPM (gallons per minute) measures water flow. Higher GPM supports simultaneous cleaning and rinsing. 


### Motor and Construction

Electric models offer convenience, while gas-powered units provide greater mobility and power. Look for commercial-grade components like stainless steel parts and reinforced hoses for lasting performance. 


### Attachments and Accessories

Variety in nozzles allows targeting specific cleaning tasks. Fan tips distribute water for large surface coverage, while needle tips create powerful streams for detailed work. 


### Portability and Storage

Rubber handles and lightweight designs improve maneuverability. Telescoping handles enhance reach. When not in use, consider models with built-in storage for accessory kits.
