## FAQ


### What is gold tissue paper?

Gold tissue paper combines delicate tissue paper with a thin layer of metallic foil, giving it a sparkling golden appearance. It's often used for decorative purposes, packaging, and crafting projects that require a touch of luxury. 

Unlike ordinary gold leaf, this paper version is more manageable and less prone to damage during application. The thin layer of foil provides a consistent, professional-looking finish without the complexity of working with real leaf. 


### How is gold tissue paper made?

Manufacturers typically start with high-quality tissue paper, often made from cotton or other cellulose materials. They then apply a thin layer of gold-colored metallic coating using techniques that create a smooth, durable finish. 

The production process carefully controls the thickness of the coating to ensure the paper remains lightweight and flexible while retaining its golden appearance and texture. 


### What are the typical dimensions and weight of gold tissue paper?

Standard gold tissue paper sheets measure approximately 30 inches by 13.5 feet (30" x 162") in most brands. They typically weigh between 60 to 100 grams per square meter (gsm). 

The thin weight allows for multiple layers or overlays without becoming too heavy, while the standard size provides ample coverage for most craft and packaging needs. 


### How should gold tissue paper be stored?

Store gold tissue paper in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Humidity can affect its integrity, while exposure to light may cause the metallic coating to degrade over time. 

When not in use, roll the paper tightly and store it in an airtight container or pouch to protect it from dust and environmental factors. 


### What are the best ways to use gold tissue paper?

Common applications include gift wrapping, cardmaking, scrapbooking, and crafting projects where a golden highlight is desired. Artists also use it for detailed paper sculptures and other three-dimensional projects. 

For best results, apply it using specialized products designed for tissue paper adhesion. This helps prevent the foil layer from peeling or coming off during application. 


### What factors should be considered when choosing gold tissue paper?

The most important factors are thickness and durability of the foil layer, consistency of color, and ease of application. Consider the paper's weight and texture for different projects, as some may be more suitable for intricate work than others. 

The size and packaging dimensions often affect how much you need to purchase, so consider your specific project requirements when selecting a product.
