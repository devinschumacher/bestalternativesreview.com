### How should baby boy overalls fit?

Baby boy overalls should fit snugly but allow for easy movement. The legs should not be too tight, as this can restrict leg development, but should not hang loosely either. The waistband should sit comfortably on the hips without being too tight around the abdomen. 

Most overalls have adjustable features like elastic waistbands, ribbed cuffs, and expandable legs to allow for proper sizing. It's important to measure your baby for the correct length and inseam before purchasing to ensure a safe, comfortable fit. 


### What sizes are available in baby boy overalls?

Baby boy overalls typically come in sizes ranging from newborn (0-3 months) to toddler (12-18 months) with increments in between. Some brands offer larger sizes up to 3 years old for extended wear. 

Be sure to check the specific size chart for each brand, as sizing standards can vary between manufacturers. Some brands may offer smaller increments (1-2 months) for newborns, while others combine multiple months into one size category.

### How do I care for baby boy overalls?

Baby boy overalls are usually machine washable in cold or warm water using mild detergent. Delicate designs may require hand washing or gentle cycle settings to prevent snagging. 

Air drying is recommended to maintain the shape and integrity of the overall, though some machines may have gentle drying cycles suitable for these garments. Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach, which can damage the material and affect the fit. 


### What should I consider when selecting baby boy overalls?
