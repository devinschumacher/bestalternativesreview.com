### How do I properly care for a Ginger Lace Front Wig?

Handle the wig by the edges rather than the hair to prevent tangling. Gently brush with a wide-tooth comb and wipe the lace front with a soft cloth after each use. 

When storing, place the wig in a mesh bag to maintain ventilation and prevent tangling. Avoid direct heat styling tools to preserve the hair quality. 


### Does the lace front require special grooming?

Regularly clean the lace front with a damp cloth to remove any oil or product residue. Apply a gentle solution of water and vinegar to soften stiff lace fibers. 

Store the lace front flat when not in use to prevent warping and maintain its shape. Always reattach the lace to the wig cap before storage.
