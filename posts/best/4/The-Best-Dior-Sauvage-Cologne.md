### What are the key notes in Dior Sauvage Cologne?

At the heart of Dior Sauvage Cologne is a distinctive blend of cardamom and lemon. The top notes kick off with fresh citrus, followed by the robust warmth of cardamom. This middle note pairs perfectly with the aromatic drydown of musk and wood, creating a sophisticated balance of modern spiciness and classical masculine scent profiles. 

The scent opens with a sharp, zesty freshness that quickly evolves into a rich, enveloping heart. The base note combination of musk and wood provides lasting depth, making Dior Sauvage suitable for both daytime and evening wear. 


### How should I wear Dior Sauvage Cologne?

As a cologne, Dior Sauvage is designed for lighter application compared to traditional colognes or eau de toilettes. A light spritz on pulse points like the wrist or behind the ears allows the cardamom and lemon notes to shine through while the musk and wood build character over time. 

This fragrance pairs exceptionally well with casual to business-casual attire and works for both spring and autumn weather. It's versatile enough to wear alone or layer under a more intense fragrance.

### What is the recommended application method?

Unlike heavier perfumes that require a generous dose, colognes like Dior Sauvage demand precision and lightness. Apply a single spritz to pulse points - wrists, neck, and behind the ears - where body heat will naturally diffuse the scent. Avoid spraying directly on clothing to prevent alcohol from leaving telltale marks. 

For optimal results, allow a minute or two for the fragrance to settle before dressing. This gives the cardamom and lemon notes time to fully express themselves before the musk and wood notes begin their development. 


### How long does Dior Sauvage Cologne last?

As a cologne, Dior Sauvage has a mid-range longevity profile. Expect the top and middle notes of cardamom and lemon to last around 2-3 hours, after which the base notes of musk and wood will carry the scent for another 4-6 hours. 

This midday-to-evening longevity makes Dior Sauvage ideal for most daily wear scenarios. However, application in colder temperatures or humid environments may affect both longevity and projection.

### Which skin types are suitable for Dior Sauvage Cologne?

Being an alcohol-based cologne, Dior Sauvage is generally well-suited for most skin types, including sensitive skin. The fragrance's composition contains natural ingredients like cardamom, making it less likely to cause irritation than many synthetic colognes. 

That said, individuals with extremely sensitive skin or known fragrance allergies should test the product on a small area first to ensure compatibility before broader use. 


### Can Dior Sauvage Cologne be worn year-round?

While the base notes of musk and wood give the fragrance a somewhat autumnal feel, Dior Sauvage's primary character comes from its fresh cardamom and lemon top notes. These elements make it particularly refreshing for spring and early summer. 

However, the scent's longevity means it performs well into autumn when worn alone, though it may feel too light in cold winter temperatures. Overall, Dior Sauvage works best as a spring through late autumn fragrance.
