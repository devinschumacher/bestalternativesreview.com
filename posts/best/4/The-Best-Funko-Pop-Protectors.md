### What is a Funko Pop Protector?

A Funko Pop Protector is a protective display case specifically designed for containing Funko Pop figures. These custom cases keep your collectibles clean and secure while displaying them with premium presentation. 

The protector case includes three main components: a top display section, a bottom base section, and a protective back panel. This three-part design allows for easy removal of the figure while maintaining dust protection. 


### What materials are used in the construction of Funko Pop Protectors?

The top and bottom sections of the protector case are typically made from impact-resistant plastic, chosen for its durability and ability to prevent scratches and damage to the display surface. 

The back panel and interior surfaces are covered with acid-free, pH-neutral material to protect the artwork and reduce the risk of environmental damage. The case edges feature an anti-glare coating to minimize reflection.

### How do I properly display a Funko Pop in its protector?

With the bottom base section empty, carefully place the Funko Pop figure into the top display section. Align it with the guide notches and gently press down to secure it in place. 

Slide the top section with the figure intact into the bottom base section. The back panel clicks securely into place once both sections are aligned. This locked position prevents any movement during display. 


### Can I display multiple Funko Pop Protectors together?

Yes, you can display multiple protector cases side by side. The clear design allows you to view all displayed figures simultaneously without interference. 

For optimal display, ensure there is adequate space between each case. The standard gap should be about 1 inch (2.54 cm) to prevent visual obstructions and maintain a clean appearance.

### How do I maintain the appearance of a Funko Pop Protector?

Regular maintenance involves wiping the exterior surfaces with a soft, lint-free cloth using mild soap and water solution. Avoid harsh chemicals that could damage the plastic or backing materials. 

When cleaning the interior, use a microfiber cloth to dust and remove any loose particles. For deeper cleaning, you can remove the top section and use a soft-bristled brush to clean the display surface. 


### What is the recommended size for a Funko Pop Protector?

The standard protector case size is designed to accommodate all official Funko Pop figures, which typically measure 4 inches tall (10.16 cm). These cases include up to an inch of additional space at the base for display stand compatibility.
