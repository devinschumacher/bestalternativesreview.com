### Key Features to Consider

Look for pillowcases with a high thread count for increased breathability and comfort. The material should be soft against your skin and preferably hypoallergenic if you have sensitivities. Some cases offer various sizes to match different pillow types, so measure your pillow before purchasing. 


### Benefits of Using a Blissy Pillowcase

These cases protect your pillow from dust, dirt, and daily wear, extending its lifespan. They also make it easy to change your pillow's appearance by simply switching out the cover rather than washing the entire pillow. 


### Installation and Care

Most Blissy pillowcases have simple installation - simply slide your pillow out of its cover, place the new case over it, and zip it closed. For cleaning, most cases are machine washable. Proper care instructions typically allow for regular rotation of your pillowcases to maintain freshness and prevent odor buildup. 


### Selecting the Right Fit

Ensure the pillowcase fits your pillow size (standard, king, or specialty) and shape (cylinder, pillowtop, etc. ). The case length should cover both the top and sides of your pillow. Consider how deeply you sleep and whether you need additional moisture management features during summer months.
