## Buyer's Guide

The Taurus TX22 has earned its reputation through its blend of reliability and affordability, making it a popular choice among concealed carry enthusiasts. However, finding the right holster is crucial for both comfort and legal compliance. This guide helps you choose a holster that matches your carry preferences and firearm. 


### Carry Position

Your carry position significantly affects holster selection. Whether you prefer concealment under clothing or a more external carry method, the holster design must match. High ride holster options provide better concealment but require longer pocket depth, while low ride options offer quicker draws at the cost of visibility. 

![Taurus-Tx22-Holster-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Taurus-Tx22-Holster-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Safety Features

Look for holsters with positive retention mechanisms that prevent accidental discharge while allowing quick releases when needed. Detachable magazine pouches or dual retention loops can add security, though they may reduce quick draw capabilities. Consider if the holster has a thumb break or other secure release option that meets your comfort and safety standards. 


### Multifunctionality

Some holsters offer additional storage solutions, such as card holders or key fobs. While these can be convenient, ensure they don't compromise the holster's primary function of securely attaching your firearm. The holster should accommodate potential additions without affecting its reliability or your carry experience. 

![Taurus-Tx22-Holster-3](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Taurus-Tx22-Holster-3/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Material Quality

Vegetable-tanned leather holsters provide lasting durability and natural break-in characteristics. Molded plastic or Kydex options offer excellent retention and protection but may not conform to the firearm as well. Higher-quality holsters often use premium materials that resist wear and maintain their shape over time, though they typically come with a higher price point.
