### How does the DTX technology work?

DTX technology in the Yamaha P71 works by selectively enhancing the transient response of audio signals. This process involves analyzing the incoming audio data and applying specific algorithms to restore sharp attacks and precise timing in musical notes. The result is a sound reproduction that maintains the original recording's dynamic range and articulation. 

This technology works particularly well with live performances and acoustic instruments, where maintaining the natural attack of notes is crucial. The processing occurs within the system's digital signal processor (DSP), requiring no additional setup or configuration from the user.
