### How to Melt Wax Beads

Melting wax beads depends on the wax type and the approach taken. Paraffin wax beads can be melted using a double boiler or a microwave. To melt wax beads using a double boiler, fill a pot with water, heat it, and place a heatproof bowl with the wax beads inside. Stir the wax until it reaches the desired temperature and melts completely.

To melt wax beads using a microwave, place the wax beads in a microwave-safe container with a little water. Heat the container for 15-20 seconds, then check the wax to ensure it has melted. Repeat until the wax has melted completely. Be cautious not to overheat the wax, as it can catch fire if left unattended.
