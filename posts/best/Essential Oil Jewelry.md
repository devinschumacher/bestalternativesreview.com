When purchasing Essential Oil Jewelry, consider the materials used in its production. ### Care and Maintenance

To ensure the longevity of your Essential Oil Jewelry, proper care and maintenance are essential. Keep the jewelry clean by wiping it down with a soft cloth or rinsing it with warm water. Avoid soaking the jewelry in water, as this can damage the vial or compartment sealing and lead to leaking. Store the jewelry in a dry, cool place when not in use, and consider storing extra essential oils separately to prevent leaking.
