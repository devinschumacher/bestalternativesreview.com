### How do I care for my studded combat boots?

Proper care and maintenance of your studded combat boots are essential for preserving their appearance and extending their lifespan. Generally, it is recommended to clean the boots regularly, removing dirt and debris with a soft, dry cloth or brush. If the boots become wet, it is crucial to immediately hang them to dry, avoiding exposure to direct heat or sunlight. 

Leather boots should also be conditioned periodically to maintain their flexibility and prevent cracking. Inspect the studs regularly and tighten them if necessary to ensure their secure attachment. Store your combat boots in a cool, dry place when not in use, using a protective material like shoe trees or newspapers to help maintain their shape.
