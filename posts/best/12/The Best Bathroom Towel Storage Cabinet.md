### How do I measure for a bathroom towel storage cabinet?

Taking accurate measurements is crucial for ensuring a cabinet fits properly in your space. Measure the width, height, and depth of the intended mounting location. Leave enough clearance for door hinges and installation hardware.

### How do I maintain a bathroom towel storage cabinet?

Regular maintenance helps extend the life of your cabinet and prevent mold growth. Wipe down the exterior with a mild soap solution and dry thoroughly after each cleaning. For interiors, use a damp cloth to remove dust and moisture, then make sure the cabinet is completely dry before closing.
