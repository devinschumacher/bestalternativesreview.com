## Buyer's Guide

Choosing the right protein powder can significantly enhance your fitness regimen, but with so many options on the market, making an informed decision can be overwhelming. Our guide walks you through the key factors to consider when selecting a Truefit Protein Powder, helping you find the perfect product for your goals and preferences. 


### Protein Quality and Source

Not all proteins are created equal. Truefit offers various sources, including whey, casein, and plant-based options like pea and brown rice protein. Whey is quickly absorbed for post-workout recovery, while casein provides sustained release. Plant-based proteins are suitable for vegetarians and those with dairy sensitivities, offering complete amino acid profiles. 


### Protein Content and Serving Size

Check the label for protein content per serving and total volume of powder. Quality proteins typically range from 20-30 grams per scoop, with larger containers offering better value. Consider your daily protein needs based on your fitness goals and body weight. 


### Additional Nutrients and Ingredients

Many Truefit varieties include added nutrients like BCAAs, L-glutamine, and digestive enzymes. Some formulas contain added flavors, sweeteners, or thickeners. Opt for products with minimal additives and consider whether you need specialized nutrients based on your fitness level and dietary requirements. 


### Storage and Shelf Life

Proper storage can affect protein powder quality and shelf life. Look for packaging that provides oxygen barrier protection and moisture resistance. Most Truefit products maintain optimal quality for 1-2 years from the production date when stored correctly. Keep powders in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.

### Are Truefit protein powders effective for weight loss?

While Truefit protein powders can support weight loss through increased satiety and improved muscle-to-fat ratio, they should be part of a comprehensive plan including balanced nutrition and regular exercise. The specific effectiveness varies based on individual metabolism and lifestyle factors.

### How long does Truefit protein powder last once opened?
