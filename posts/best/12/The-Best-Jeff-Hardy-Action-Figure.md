Figures typically feature durable plastic construction with some offering PVC or rubber materials for specific elements like masks or gloves. Premium versions may include detailed sculpting and paint applications, while budget-friendly options prioritize basic design and affordability.

Premium releases often include multiple head choices, alternate poses, or display stands. Packaging quality varies, with some collectors preferring sealed boxes or blister packs for preservation.
