The Terrex Swift R3s run true to size. For a more secure fit during hiking, we recommend choosing your normal shoe size rather than going half a size down as some Gore Tex boots suggest.

### Are these shoes suitable for all weather conditions?

While the waterproof Gore Tex construction keeps your feet dry in light rain and moisture, these shoes aren't designed for extended exposure to heavy rainfall or floodwaters.

### How durable are these shoes?

The synthetic leather and mesh upper has shown good resistance to scuffs and scratches during regular use. However, the Gore Tex membrane can tear if the material is punctured, so avoid sharp objects in your hiking boots.

### What's the moisture management like inside the shoes?
