### How should Tommy Hilfiger hats be cared for?

To maintain the quality and appearance of a Tommy Hilfiger hat, wash it by hand or machine wash it on a gentle cycle with mild detergent. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the fabrics. 

Air drying is recommended to prevent shrinkage and maintain the shape. Never use a dryer, as high heat can damage the materials. If needed, reshape the hat while it's still damp and allow it to dry naturally. 


### What are the sizing differences between baseball caps and beanies?

Baseball caps typically measure around 7 inches in size, while beanies usually run between 5 5/8 to 6 inches in length. The crown of a baseball cap is generally higher and more structured, while beanies are more fitted and sit lower on the head. 

Baseball caps usually have an adjustable strap in the back, allowing for a more customized fit. Beanies often come with elastic bands or drawstrings to adjust the snugness around the ears and head. 


### Are Tommy Hilfiger hats weather-resistant?

Some Tommy Hilfiger hats feature water-resistant treatments to protect against light rain and moisture. These treatments are most common in outdoor and casual styles like snapbacks and bucket hats.

### How do I properly store a Tommy Hilfiger hat?

When storing a Tommy Hilfiger hat, avoid hanging it by the brim, as this can cause distortion over time. Instead, fold the hat or place it in a breathable hat box to maintain its shape.
