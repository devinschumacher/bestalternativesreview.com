### How do heatless curlers work?

Heatless curlers work by encapsulating small sections of hair between foam or silicone cores. The natural heat from your scalp and the moisture in your hair create steam that helps the curlers retain their shape. Unlike hot tools that rely on direct heat application, these curlers utilize your hair's natural warmth and humidity to achieve curls. 

The process typically involves dividing your hair into small sections, applying styling products if desired, and securing each section with a curler. The curlers can be left in for several hours to allow your hair to absorb moisture and set the curls naturally.
