### What is a wedge set?

A wedge set consists of adjustable wedges that can be inserted under the toes or ball of the foot to prevent overpronation, a common running and walking gait issue. These small, triangular devices help align the foot and improve biomechanics. 

Wedge sets are made from materials like rubber, plastic, or gel, and can be easily adjusted for different angles of correction. They are typically used in athletic footwear and orthopedic shoes to enhance comfort and performance. 


### How do wedge sets work?

Wedge sets correct excessive foot motion by applying pressure to specific areas of the foot. The wedge shape helps lift and realign the arch, reducing strain on the foot and lower leg muscles. 

The adjustable nature of these devices allows users to fine-tune their fit. Proper wedge positioning can improve running efficiency, reduce injury risk, and enhance overall foot health. 

![Wedge-Set-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Wedge-Set-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Who should use wedge sets?

Wedge sets are beneficial for individuals who overpronate (feet roll inward excessively), experience foot pain, or have conditions like plantar fasciitis or flat feet. They are particularly effective for runners, athletes, and anyone who spends long hours on their feet. 

These devices are not recommended for people with specific foot conditions that require custom orthotic inserts. It's advisable to consult with a podiatrist before using wedge sets. 


### How do I measure for wedge sets?

The correct wedge set size depends on your specific foot shape and arch height. You can determine your needs by comparing your foot to a flat surface, measuring the arch height, and checking for overpronation in your current footwear. 

Many wedge sets come in different angles (1/8", 3/16", 1/4") and heights (1/4", 1/2"). A common starting point is the 1/8" wedge, which provides mild correction. If you have moderate overpronation, you may need 3/16" or 1/4" wedges.

### How long do wedge sets last?

Wedge sets typically last 6-12 months with regular use, depending on the quality of the material and how frequently they are used. They may need replacement more often if you notice significant wear or if your foot conditions change. 

Proper care involves cleaning the wedges regularly with soap and water to prevent bacteria buildup. Store them flat when not in use to maintain their shape. 


### Can I use wedge sets with orthopedic shoes?

Yes, wedge sets can be used in orthopedic shoes to provide additional support and correction. However, since orthotic shoes often have specialized insoles, you may need to adjust the positioning of the wedges slightly. 

Always follow the manufacturer's instructions for your specific shoe model. In some cases, you may need to remove the original insole to accommodate the wedge set properly.
