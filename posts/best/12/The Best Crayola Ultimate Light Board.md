### How does the glow-in-the-dark feature work?

The glow-in-the-dark paint absorbs light when exposed to regular room or natural light. After the light source disappears, the paint glows for several minutes, creating a lasting visual effect. 

The included light board intensifies the glow, revealing vibrant, illuminated designs that can be enjoyed for longer periods. 


### Are the sheets and paint reusable?

Yes, the sheets are entirely reusable. After each session, simply wipe them clean with a damp cloth to remove any remaining paint.

### How long do the glow effects last?

The glow typically lasts between 5 to 10 minutes after exposure to a light source, though this can vary based on the brightness of the light and environmental factors. 

Once the glow fades, simply expose the sheet to light again to reactivate it, creating a fresh canvas for each use. 


### What age group is this product suitable for?

### How should I care for the light board?
