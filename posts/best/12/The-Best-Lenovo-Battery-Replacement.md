### What factors affect battery performance and longevity?

Battery performance decreases over time due to chemical degradation and cumulative usage. Most Lithium-ion batteries see a decline of 20-30% in capacity after one year of daily use. 

External factors impact battery life: heat, depth of discharge, and storage conditions. Optimal conditions include temperatures between 20-25°C, a 50% charge level, and storage in a cool, dry place. 


### How do I properly maintain my Lenovo battery?

Charge your battery to 80-90% before storing it. Avoid fully discharging or overcharging the battery. Charge the battery once every 4-6 weeks if not in regular use. 

Avoid extreme temperatures, especially during charging. Use the original charger and consider investing in a quality battery from a reputable retailer.
