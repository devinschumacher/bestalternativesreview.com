## Buyer's Guide

Vera Bradley's popular blanket collection combines the brand's signature design elements with quality textiles for a cozy addition to any bedroom or living space. Understanding the key features and considerations can help you select the perfect blanket that matches your style and needs. 


### Design Elements

Vera Bradley blankets feature the brand's distinctive graphic patterns, often incorporating floral, geometric, and abstract designs. These patterns are printed on high-quality cotton or cotton-blend fabrics, providing durability while maintaining a soft handfeel. The color palettes typically include a mix of bold and neutral tones. 


### Fabric Quality

Most Vera Bradley blankets are made from 100% cotton or cotton blends, which ensures breathability and quick drying. The fabric quality ranges from everyday wear to premium options with added features like moisture-wicking. Higher-end blankets may feature special finishes for enhanced softness or texture. 


### Size and Dimensions

Vera Bradley blankets generally come in standard double (60 x 80 inches), king (76 x 80 inches), or queen (90 x 90 inches) sizes. Consider the intended use - double blankets work well for everyday use, while queen or king sizes are ideal for larger beds or layering. The thickness option varies from lightweight throw blankets to plush blankets suitable for cooler evenings. 


### Maintenance Requirements

Proper care instructions vary by material and design details, so review the specific care requirements before purchase. Standard cotton blankets typically need gentle machine washing with similar colors, while specialty finishes may require hand washing or air drying. Velcro closures or decorative details may affect care methods, so follow the manufacturer's recommendations for best results.
