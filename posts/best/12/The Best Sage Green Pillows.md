## Buyer's Guide

Sage green pillows combine natural earthy tones with the comfort of quality bedding. When selecting sage green pillows, consider these key factors to find a pillow that matches your preference and supports your sleep posture. 


### Material Quality

Look for pillows filled with high-quality materials like down, down alternative, or natural fibers. High-quality materials ensure longevity and provide optimal comfort. Consider the durability of feathers vs. synthetic alternatives, and the hypoallergenic properties of natural materials. 


### Ergonomic Design

Choose a pillow that matches your sleep position - from plush comfort for side sleepers to firmer options for back sleepers. Consider the pillow's ability to maintain its shape and provide proper neck support, especially important for preventing discomfort during the night. 


### Size and Fit

Ensure the pillow's dimensions match your mattress and box spring. Consider the pillow's length and width to determine if it's appropriate for your sleep needs and bed size. Proper size allows for natural head and neck alignment while sleeping. 


### Care Requirements

Understand the care instructions for your pillow type. Down and down alternative pillows require regular cleaning every 6-12 months. Natural fiber pillows may need less frequent cleaning. Consider how easy it is to maintain the pillow's cleanliness and condition over time.
