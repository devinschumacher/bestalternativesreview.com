## FAQ


### What is a stackable tray?

A stackable tray is a kitchen or storage accessory designed to consolidate and organize items in vertical space. These trays typically feature shallow, open compartments that can be nested together for easy storage and retrieval of contents. 

The primary benefits of stackable trays include maximizing cabinet space, improving organization through categorized storage, and maintaining cleanliness by minimizing visible clutter. 


### What materials are stackable trays typically made from?

Stackable trays are commonly made from durable materials such as plastic, stainless steel, and melamine. These materials are chosen for their resistance to scratches, warping, and bacterial growth. 

Plastic trays are lightweight and available in a wide range of colors, making them suitable for both functional and decorative purposes. Stainless steel options provide excellent durability and a professional appearance, while melamine trays offer a more affordable alternative with similar properties. 


### How should I clean stackable trays?

Most stackable trays are dishwasher-safe, making cleaning simple and convenient. For those that are not dishwasher-safe, hand washing with mild soap and warm water is recommended to maintain hygiene and extend the product's lifespan. 

Regular cleaning helps prevent the accumulation of food particles and bacteria, which can affect both the functionality and appearance of the trays. It's also advisable to inspect trays for any signs of wear or damage during each cleaning session. 


### How do stackable trays help with organization?

Stackable trays excel at organizing small kitchen items, such as utensils, baking supplies, and office accessories. The shallow compartments allow precise placement of items, while the nested design ensures everything stays in its designated spot. 

The categorization of contents within stackable trays makes them particularly useful for tasks like meal prep, where ingredients and tools need to be accessible yet contained. 


### What sizes are available for stackable trays?

Stackable trays come in various sizes to accommodate different storage needs. Standard sizes typically range from 2 to 6 inches in depth, with diameters between 6 and 12 inches for circular trays. 

Many manufacturers offer modular systems where trays can be customized to fit specific cabinet dimensions, allowing users to create tailored storage solutions. 


### How do I choose the right stackable trays for my kitchen?

When selecting stackable trays, consider the specific items you'll store and the available cabinet space. Think about whether you need specialized compartments for certain items, like measuring cups or magnetic utensils. 

Think about color coordination with your kitchen decor and choose BPA-free materials for safety and longevity. Reading reviews and measuring your cabinets before purchasing can help ensure a perfect fit and optimal organization solution.
