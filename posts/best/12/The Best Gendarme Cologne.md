## FAQ


### What is Gendarme Cologne?

Gendarme Cologne is a distinctive men's fragrance that combines top notes of bergamot and lemon with heart notes of lavender and rosemary. The base notes feature cedarwood and vetiver, creating a lasting, woody aroma that's both fresh and earthy. 

The scent has been compared to other popular colognes like L'Eau d'Issey but with a more pronounced herbal edge. It's suitable for daytime wear and has a moderate longevity of 6-8 hours. 


### What's the recommended application method?

Apply Gendarme Cologne directly to pulse points such as the wrists, neck, and behind the ears. The intensity is best when applied in the morning or early afternoon. 

Allow the fragrance to absorb fully before layering with any heavier scents or cologne splashes. This ensures the top notes have time to develop before the middle and base notes take over. 


### Can I mix it with other colognes?

Mixing Gendarme with another cologne can create unexpected results, as the herbal character might clash with the sweetness or spiciness of other fragrances. It's generally best to use it as your primary scent unless you're layering specifically with a woody or herbal fragrance. 

If you do choose to mix, test a small amount on your skin first to ensure compatibility before applying to your whole body. 


### How should I store my Gendarme?

Store your Gendarme in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. A dark, cool closet or drawer is ideal. Exposure to light and heat can degrade the fragrance's quality and accelerate its evaporation. 

Avoid storing opened bottles near strong chemicals or scented products that might affect its integrity. The original packaging helps preserve the fragrance's quality, so keep it sealed when not in use. 


### Is Gendarme suitable for daily wear?

Yes, Gendarme's fresh, herbal scent makes it appropriate for most daily activities. It performs well in both casual and slightly more formal settings, though it may be too strong for very formal occasions like weddings or black-tie events. 

Its moderate longevity of 6-8 hours ensures you'll need to reapply only once during the day, making it practical for both work and leisure.
