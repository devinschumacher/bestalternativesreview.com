## Buyer's Guide

IQ air purifiers represent a sophisticated category of air cleaning devices designed to improve indoor air quality by removing pollutants, allergens, and other airborne particles. Understanding their key features and how they operate will help you select a unit that meets your specific needs and environmental conditions. 


### Filter Technology

IQ air purifiers employ advanced filtration systems, typically featuring multi-stage filtration processes. The most effective models utilize HEPA filters to capture 99.97% of particles as small as 0.1 microns, including dust, pollen, and tobacco smoke. Some advanced units incorporate additional technologies such as activated carbon filters for odors, UV-C light for bacteria and virus elimination, and photocatalytic oxidation for further purification. 


### Airflow and Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR)

Efficient air purification requires adequate airflow throughout the room. CADR ratings measure a purifier's ability to remove specific pollutants (smoke, dust, and pollen) and ensure consistent performance across different particle sizes. Consider a unit with a CADR rating appropriate for your room size to guarantee thorough air circulation and reliable filtration.
