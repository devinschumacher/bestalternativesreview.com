Standard Sportcraft tables measure 8 feet x 4 feet, requiring a minimum playing surface of 8 feet 6 inches by 4 feet 6 inches, plus additional space for movement and setup.

### What maintenance is needed for Sportcraft tables?

Regular maintenance includes monthly inspections of screws and cushions, weekly cleaning of the playing surface and rails, and bi-annual checking of the felt for wear. The manufacturer recommends professional inspection and felt replacement every 8-10 years. 

Spot clean spills immediately, avoid harsh chemicals, and store the table in a dry area when not in use to prevent damage from humidity or temperature changes.
