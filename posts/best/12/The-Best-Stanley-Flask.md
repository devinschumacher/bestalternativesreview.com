## FAQ


### What makes a Stanley Flask different from other insulated containers?

Stanley Flasks feature their patented Vacuum Insulation Technology, which creates a double-wall vacuum-sealed structure that keeps beverages hot for up to 24 hours or cold for up to 12 hours. This innovative design significantly outperforms traditional insulated containers in maintaining temperature. 

The Stainless Steel construction of Stanley Flasks adds durability, with the vacuum-sealed walls preventing thermal transfer through contact with the exterior. 


### How do I properly clean and maintain my Stanley Flask?

Stanley recommends washing their flasks with warm water and mild dish soap using a soft sponge or brush to remove any residue. The interiors should be rinsed thoroughly to prevent soap buildup. 

For stubborn stains, you can use a mixture of baking soda and water to create a paste, applying it to the affected areas and letting it sit before washing as usual. Proper maintenance helps preserve the vacuum insulation and maintains hygiene. 


### Can Stanley Flasks be used with hot liquids immediately after filling?

While Stanley Flasks are designed for both hot and cold beverages, it's recommended to let the contents cool down to room temperature before sealing the lid tightly. Sudden temperature changes can affect the vacuum seal's effectiveness. 

Avoid filling the flask completely to the top when using hot liquids, as the expanding steam needs space to escape and prevent pressure build-up. 


### What sizes are available for Stanley Flasks?

Stanley offers a range of flask sizes, from 12-ounce mini flasks to 33-ounce standard bottles. Their sizing options cater to different needs, from quick sips to full-day hydration. 

Each size maintains the same vacuum insulation properties, so the thermal performance remains consistent across their selection. 


### Are Stanley Flasks BPA-free?

Stanley has transitioned all their products to BPA-free materials, including their flasks. Their current designs use either stainless steel or food-grade polypropylene for components in contact with beverages. 

BPA-free construction ensures safety and peace of mind, particularly when using the flask with hot or cold liquids. 


### How should I transport my Stanley Flask?

When carrying a Stanley Flask, especially when full, it's recommended to use protective cases or holders to prevent damage from impacts or pressure. These accessories can also help maintain the vacuum seal integrity during transport. 

Avoid placing heavy objects on top of the flask and ensure it's properly secured in any bag or backpack to prevent accidental drops or compression.
