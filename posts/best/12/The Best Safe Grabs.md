### Grab Design and Construction

Grabs come in two main designs: open-faced and closed-grip. Open-faced grabs are suitable for loose materials and bulk loads, while closed-grip models excel with precise placement of smaller objects. Safe grabs use various mechanisms to secure loads, including mechanical levers, hydraulic systems, and motorized controls.
