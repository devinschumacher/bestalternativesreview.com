Developmentally appropriate outdoor toys for 1-year-olds emphasize sensory stimulation and basic motor skills. Look for toys that provide tactile experiences, such as soft fabrics or textured surfaces, and encourage cause-and-effect play through simple mechanisms like buttons or levers.
