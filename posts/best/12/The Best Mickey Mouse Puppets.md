### What materials are used to make these puppets?

Mickey Mouse puppets are typically constructed from durable fabric with a blend of polyester and cotton. The inner structure often includes a wire frame for hand puppets to maintain their shape during performance. 

For costumes, elastic and flexible materials are used to ensure the puppet fits comfortably on a performer's hand while allowing for natural finger movement. 


### Are the puppets washable?

Yes, most puppets are machine washable using a gentle cycle with mild detergent. It's best to avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, which can damage the fabric and wire frame. 

Hand puppets should be hung to dry, while costumes can be laid flat. Do not use a dryer, as high heat can shrink the elastic materials.

### Can the puppets be customized?

Many puppet makers offer customization options such as adding names, changing colors, or personalizing costumes with special designs. This allows customers to create unique puppets that match specific themes or events. 

For hand puppets, features like interchangeable hands or facial expressions can be customized to give each puppet distinct personality traits. 


### What sizes are available for the puppets?

Mickey Mouse puppets come in various sizes to accommodate different performances and age groups. Standard hand puppets range from small ( suitable for children under 5) to medium (for children 5-8) and large (for children 8 and up). 

Costume puppets are available in adult sizes, with options for different body types and heights to ensure proper fit and comfort during performance.

### How long do the puppets last?

With proper care, Mickey Mouse puppets can last several years, making them a worthwhile investment for frequent performers or collectors. The durability depends on usage frequency, care practices, and storage conditions. 

Regular cleaning, proper drying, and periodic inspection of the wire frame and stitching can extend the puppet's lifespan and maintain its performance quality.
