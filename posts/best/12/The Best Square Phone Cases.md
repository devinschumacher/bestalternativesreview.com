## Buyer's Guide

Square phone cases offer a distinctive way to protect your device while adding a personal touch through their geometric design. These cases come in various materials ranging from soft protective bumpers to rigid polycarbonate shells, each offering different levels of impact absorption and durability. 


### Protection Features

When selecting a square phone case, consider its primary protective elements. Transparent cases provide basic shock absorption through their cushioning material, while colored or patterned cases often contain additional protective layers. Hardshell cases made from polycarbonate or glass-filled nylon offer the highest impact resistance and drop protection.

### Size Compatibility

Square cases can vary significantly in their fit across different phone models. It's crucial to measure your device and compare these dimensions with those provided by the case manufacturer. Keep in mind that squares may not fit snugly like traditional cases, especially around the corners, which can affect overall protection. 


### Back-Side Design

The back of square cases often features open slots for camera lenses and ports, which is practical for quick access but requires careful selection to maintain functionality. Consider cases with built-in protection extensions for these critical areas to prevent scratches and damage when the phone is placed on surfaces. 

![Square-Phone-Cases-3](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Square-Phone-Cases-3/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Cleaning and Maintenance

Square cases can collect more dust and debris than traditional cases due to their open design and increased surface area. Look for cases made from easily cleanable materials, such as matte-finished plastics or rubberized coatings. Cases with reversible designs or built-in wipeable covers can simplify maintenance while keeping your phone visible through the case.

Square cases generally offer good protection against drops and scratches thanks to their protective materials. Most include TPU layers that absorb shock and provide some flexibility during falls.

Yes, most square cases allow for easy charging. The case should not interfere with your phone's charging port, and you should be able to charge your phone just as you would without the case.
