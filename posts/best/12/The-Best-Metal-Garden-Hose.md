## FAQ


### What makes metal garden hose different from traditional hoses?

Metal garden hoses offer superior durability and flexibility compared to traditional rubber hoses. The metal construction prevents kinking and twisting, ensuring steady water flow. Additionally, metal hoses maintain their shape and retain water pressure more effectively. 

Unlike rubber hoses that can crack in cold weather, metal hoses remain flexible and functional in temperatures as low as -40° F (-40° C). This makes them ideal for year-round garden maintenance. 


### How long do metal garden hoses typically last?

Metal garden hoses can last 10-20 years with regular maintenance, significantly longer than the 3-5 years typical for rubber hoses. Their durability stems from the robust metal construction and protective coatings. 

Proper storage and protection from the elements can extend their lifespan, but occasional exposure to harsh conditions may still cause minor surface wear over time. 


### What maintenance does a metal garden hose require?

Regular cleaning with soap and water removes mineral buildup and prevents rust formation. Check for sharp bends or signs of wear, and repair any damage promptly to maintain optimal performance. 

In winter, allow the hose to drain completely and coil it loosely for storage. This prevents water from freezing within the hose, which could cause expensive damage. 


### How much water pressure can metal garden hoses handle?

Most metal garden hoses can withstand pressures up to 120 psi (pounds per square inch), making them suitable for high-pressure sprayers and powerful water jets. Always check the specific product specifications for maximum pressure ratings. 

While metal hoses handle pressure well, extreme fluctuations in pressure can damage the connecting fittings. It's essential to maintain steady water flow whenever possible. 


### Can metal garden hoses be used with chemical garden treatments?

The metal construction of these hoses makes them resistant to most common garden chemicals. However, always check the manufacturer's recommendations for compatibility with specific products. 

Some harsh chemicals, particularly concentrated solutions, can still damage metal hoses over time. Limit exposure and rinse the hose thoroughly after use with any chemical treatments. 


### What are the temperature limits for using metal garden hoses?

Metal garden hoses can safely operate between -40° F (-40° C) and 212° F (100° C), making them suitable for both freezing and hot weather conditions. They maintain performance throughout these temperature extremes. 

Extended exposure to extreme temperatures can cause minor changes in hose flexibility. Keep the hose in a shaded area during hot weather and protect it from direct sunlight to maintain optimal performance.
