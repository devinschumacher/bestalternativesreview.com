### Storage and Handling

Proper storage prevents oxidation and contamination, preserving the oil's effectiveness. Store benzoin oil in a dark glass bottle to protect it from light, which can degrade essential oils. Keep it in a cool place, ideally between 15-24°C (60-75°F), to maintain its properties. 

Handle benzoin oil with care, as its strong properties can damage plastics and rubber. Use a dropper for accurate measuring and transfer the oil to appropriate containers for storage. Always label your oils clearly, noting the name and date of purchase or bottling.

### How Should Benzoin Essential Oil Be Used?

Benzoin oil can be used in several ways: as a natural skin care ingredient, in homemade skincare products, or in aromatherapy. It's often combined with carrier oils for safe topical application.
