### What are the dimensions of a 22 x 28 frame?

A 22 x 28 frame measures 22 inches by 28 inches, with standard frame thickness typically ranging between 1.5 and 2 inches. The total framed size, including matting and backing, will be slightly larger than the artwork itself. 

Exact measurements can vary slightly depending on the frame style and backing materials, but the primary dimensions remain consistent. 


### What types of artwork fit in a 22 x 28 frame?

Standard 22 x 28 frames accommodate artwork with the same dimensions, such as photographs, prints, or paintings measuring up to 22 inches in width and 28 inches in length. 

There's some versatility for artwork with a bit more depth, as long as it doesn't exceed the frame's interior dimensions. 

![22-X-28-Frame-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/22-X-28-Frame-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I measure my artwork for a 22 x 28 frame?

Measure the artwork's width and height, including any matting or mounting materials. Ensure the combined measurements do not exceed 22 inches in width and 28 inches in length. 

Consider measuring multiple times to double-check your results, as precise measurements are crucial for proper framing. 


### What are my options for mounting in a 22 x 28 frame?

You can mount your artwork directly in the frame, use a mat board to create a border around the piece, or combine both methods for added depth. 

Popular choices include acid-free mat boards, foam core, and conservation-grade mounting materials to protect your artwork. 

![22-X-28-Frame-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/22-X-28-Frame-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I properly install my artwork in a 22 x 28 frame?

Insert the artwork into the frame either by removing the back panel or from the sides, depending on the frame style. Apply pressure to center the piece within the frame. 

Use appropriate mounting materials to secure the artwork to the backing, ensuring it's centered and properly aligned. 


### What maintenance does a 22 x 28 frame require?

Regularly dust the frame's exterior using a soft, dry cloth. For the interior, clean the backing and matting with a slightly damp cloth as needed. 

Recheck the artwork's positioning and tighten any mounting hardware if the frame has adjustable features.
