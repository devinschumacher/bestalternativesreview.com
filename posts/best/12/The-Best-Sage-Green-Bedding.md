### What care instructions should I follow for sage green bedding?

Most sage green bedding requires standard care, including washing in cold water and gentle cycles. Always check the care label for specific instructions from the manufacturer. 

Avoid using harsh bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the color and fabric quality. Instead, consider using color-safe bleach and natural fabric softeners. 


### Can sage green bedding be mixed with other colors?

Sage green pairs well with neutral colors like white, cream, and gray, as well as warmer tones like rust and mustard yellow. It also works with cooler colors like blue and purple. 

Avoid mixing sage green with complementary colors that are directly opposite on the color wheel, such as orange or magenta, as these combinations can be visually overwhelming.
