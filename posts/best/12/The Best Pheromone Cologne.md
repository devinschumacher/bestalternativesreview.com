### What exactly is pheromone cologne?

Pheromone cologne is a type of fragrance that mimics natural chemical signals used by animals to attract mates or signal emotions. These fragrances contain blend of chemicals designed to trigger subconscious responses in people, potentially making the wearer more attractive. 

Most pheromone colognes combine genuine pheromones with traditional fragrance components like essential oils and synthetic fragrances. The effectiveness varies widely, with some containing only tiny amounts of pheromones. 


### How do pheromone colognes work?

While there's limited scientific evidence, many believe pheromone colognes work through 'scent detection. ' Our noses detect pheromones and send signals to the brain, triggering primal responses like increased confidence or attraction. 

Some ingredients may also influence behavior indirectly. For example, musky scents traditionally signal masculinity, while floral notes can evoke feelings of romance and femininity.

### Are pheromone colognes safe for daily use?

Most pheromone colognes are safe for daily use when applied properly. Ingredients vary, so patch test new products and consult with a dermatologist if you have sensitive skin. 

Some people may experience mild irritation, especially from synthetic or heavy scents. If you notice redness, itching, or other reactions, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional. 


### How long do pheromone colognes last?

Most pheromone colognes last 4-6 hours with regular application. Natural degradation, exposure to light, and individual chemistry affect longevity. 

For best results, apply after showering when pores are open. Reapply throughout the day as needed, especially after sweating or washing hands.

### What's the difference between genuine and synthetic pheromones?

Genuine pheromones are extracted from human sweat or other sources, while synthetic pheromones are created in a laboratory. Synthetic versions are more common due to cost and consistency.
