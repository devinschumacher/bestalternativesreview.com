## Buyer's Guide

Oversized beach towels offer generous size and extra absorbency for sunny days at the beach or by the pool. These versatile towels combine comfort with practicality, perfect for drying off or lounging in style. 


### Size and Dimensions

Oversized beach towels typically measure between 60-75 inches in length and 45-55 inches in width, compared to standard beach towels which are usually 50x70 inches. The extra length and width provide more coverage and comfort, especially for drying off completely after swimming. 


### Materials and Construction

Premium cotton or cotton blends form the base of quality oversized beach towels. Look for thicker, 1000-1200gsm towels with a plush weave that retain their shape and thickness after multiple washes. Some towels feature microfiber topcoats for extra absorbency, while others combine cotton with mesh backing for quicker drying. 


### Additional Features

Quality oversized beach towels often include built-in pockets, straps, or mesh storage bags to keep your belongings organized while you relax. Ribbed edges prevent fraying and improve grip. Towels with UV protection woven into the fabric safeguard against sun damage while providing vibrant colors that resist fading. 


### Care and Maintenance

The best oversized beach towels remain soft and absorbent with proper care. Machine wash them separately in cold water with a mild detergent to prevent color bleeding. Air drying is preferable to minimize wear from dryer heat. Fold rather than roll to store, and consider using a separate towel for heavy use like sports or mud baths to maintain their quality.

### How should I care for an oversized beach towel?

Oversized beach towels can be machine washed using a gentle cycle with a mild detergent. It's recommended to wash them separately from other textiles to prevent excess lint transfer. For optimal care, air dry the towel by hanging it, as machine drying may affect its size due to the extra material. 

Store the towel flat or folded neatly, avoiding heavy weights that could cause damage. If you need to roll it, place it between two towels to prevent damage to the material's edges. 


### What sizes are available for oversized beach towels?

Oversized beach towels typically come in dimensions ranging from 50" by 27" to 60" by 30". Most manufacturers offer multiple sizes within this range, allowing you to choose based on your specific needs. The longer and wider sizes provide more drying space but are slightly heavier and bulkier.
