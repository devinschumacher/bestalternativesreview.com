### How do I choose the right size for my waterproof duffel bag?

### Do all waterproof duffel bags float?

While some manufacturers advertise their bags as water-resistant floats, not all waterproof duffel bags are designed to float. Those specifically engineered for this purpose typically have additional buoyancy features and are marked accordingly. 

If floating capability is important, look for bags that meet industry standards for water displacement or those certified by organizations like the US Coast Guard. 


### What maintenance requirements do waterproof duffel bags have?

Regular cleaning is essential to maintain your bag's water resistance. Use mild soap and water, avoiding harsh chemicals that can damage the waterproof coating. Allow the bag to air dry completely before use. 

Check the zippers and hardware for wear and replace any damaged components. Store the bag in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent mildew and maintain its structural integrity.

### Can I wash waterproof duffel bags with dry cleaning products?

Most waterproof duffel bags should be washed with mild soap and water. The use of dry cleaning products can damage the waterproof coating and compromise the bag's water-resistant properties. 

Follow the manufacturer's care instructions, which may specify certain cleaning methods or detergents. Improper cleaning can reduce the bag's effectiveness and shorten its lifespan. 


### How long do waterproof duffel bags typically last?

With proper care, a high-quality waterproof duffel bag can last 3-5 years. Factors affecting durability include regular maintenance, load weight, and exposure to environmental elements.
