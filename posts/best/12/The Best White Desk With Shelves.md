### What makes a good white desk with shelves?

A quality white desk with shelves combines style, functionality, and durability. Look for a sturdy frame with adjustable shelving options and ample counter space. The desk should feature clean lines, smooth finish, and UV-resistant coating to maintain its white appearance over time. 

Choose materials like MDF with PVC coating or solid wood for lasting quality. The shelves should be securely attached with hidden brackets to prevent wobbling and ensure even weight distribution. 


### How much storage space do most white desk with shelves offer?

Standard white desks with shelves provide 2-3 open shelves ranging from 12 to 18 inches deep. Some larger models may include additional storage options like closed cabinets or drawers. 

Counter space typically measures between 24 and 48 inches long, with standard height ranging from 29 to 30 inches. These dimensions accommodate most office equipment and personal items.

### How should I measure for a white desk with shelves?
