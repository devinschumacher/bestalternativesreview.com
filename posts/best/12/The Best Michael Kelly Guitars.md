### Tone and Electronics

The most basic models come with single-coil pickups, while mid-range guitars feature humbuckers or a combination of both. Look for at least two humbucking pickups in higher-end models for enhanced tonal versatility. Pay attention to the pickup configuration and switching options to match your preferred playing style. 


### Bridge and Tuning Hardware

Most Michael Kelly guitars use a string-through-body bridge for improved sustain and reduced buzz. The tuning machines are usually basic and reliable, though higher-priced models may feature more premium hardware like locking tuners. The bridge design can significantly affect playability and sustain, so consider this when comparing similar models.
