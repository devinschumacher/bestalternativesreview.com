### What are Gatorade Pods?

Introduced in 2019, Gatorade Pods are convenient, single-serving pouches that contain the electrolyte drink mix. These pouches dissolve quickly in water, making it easy to get an electrolyte boost on-the-go. 

Each pod provides approximately 8 ounces of Gatorade and comes in several flavors, including original, citrus, and berry. The pods are designed for quick consumption and easy storage. 


### How do Gatorade Pods Work?

To use a Gatorade Pod, simply drop one into a bottle of water and let it dissolve completely. The water-to-powder ratio is pre-measured, so there's no need for additional mixing or measurements. 

The pods dissolve in cold or lukewarm water and can be consumed immediately. For warmer weather or intense activity, you can also mix them with ice water to maintain the correct temperature.
