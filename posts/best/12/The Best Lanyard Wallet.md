## FAQ


### What is a lanyard wallet?

A lanyard wallet is a compact, specialized wallet designed to attach to a lanyard or neck strap. It typically features multiple card slots and a small compartment for cash, making it ideal for carrying essential items while keeping your hands free. 

These wallets are commonly used in environments where frequent handwashing is recommended, such as healthcare facilities, laboratories, and food service industries. They also appeal to individuals who need a secure way to carry cards and small items while maintaining hygiene standards. 


### How do lanyard wallets differ from regular wallets?

Lanyard wallets prioritize functionality and hygiene over traditional storage capacity. While regular wallets might offer more extensive organization with multiple compartments, lanyard wallets streamline essential features into a single, secure unit. 

The primary difference lies in their design: regular wallets are held in your pocket, while lanyard wallets are worn around your neck or wrist, providing constant, clean access to your cards and cash. 


### What materials are lanyard wallets typically made from?

Most lanyard wallets are crafted from durable, hygienic materials such as medical-grade PVC, silicone, or TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane). These materials resist bacteria growth and maintain their shape and integrity over time. 

Some premium options incorporate biodegradable materials like plant-based TPU or natural rubber lanyards, offering both environmental benefits and enhanced grip stability. 


### How should I maintain my lanyard wallet?

Regular cleaning is crucial for maintaining both the functionality and hygiene of your lanyard wallet. Most manufacturers recommend wiping the wallet with an appropriate disinfectant solution daily and allowing it to air dry completely before use. 

Store your wallet in a clean, dry place when not in use, and consider using a dedicated wallet cleaner or sanitizing wipe for regular maintenance. 


### Can I use any type of lanyard with my wallet?

While many lanyard wallets come with interchangeable lanyards, it's important to choose a compatible length and material. Common lanyard options include adjustable nylon, silicone, and medical-grade PVC. Always ensure the lanyard matches the wallet's specifications for optimal fit and function. 

If you're replacing a lanyard, test its compatibility with the wallet's hardware before installation. The lanyard should fit securely into the wallet's attachment points without excessive slack or tightness. 


### What types of items can fit in a lanyard wallet?

A standard lanyard wallet can typically accommodate between 6-10 business cards, a single cash note, and a small identification card. Some larger wallets may include additional features like key clips or a separate coin compartment. 

Essential items like driver's licenses, medical ID cards, and employee badges fit well in these wallets. For card-heavy users, consider opting for a wallet with additional card slots or a slim profile to maximize storage capacity.
