Wire shelving parts are typically made from cold-rolled steel, which provides strength and durability while maintaining a lightweight profile. The wire mesh design allows for air circulation and quick drying of stored items.

Wire shelving parts offer advantages in flexibility and weight capacity compared to solid shelving. They can support up to 150 pounds per square foot and can be customized to fit specific space requirements.
