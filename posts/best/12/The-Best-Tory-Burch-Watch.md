## FAQ


### Which Tory Burch watch models are available?

Tory Burch offers several watch models, including the Lindy, Maitland, and Elizabeth series. Each collection brings its own style and features, so there's a model suitable for every taste and occasion. 

Some popular features across their collection include automatic and quartz movements, sleek stainless steel cases, and elegant leather straps. They also incorporate signature Tory Burch elements like curved lugs and distinctive hour markers. 


### What are the key differences between the automatic and quartz models?

Automatic (mechanical) watches require movement to maintain their accuracy. They're powered by the wearer's wrist movement and feature a visible mechanical movement through the case back. These watches typically have a manual winding option as a backup. 

Quartz watches, on the other hand, use a small battery to power a precise electronic movement regulated by a quartz crystal. They offer superior timekeeping accuracy and lower maintenance compared to automatic models. 


### How does Tory Burch ensure their watches are water-resistant?

While no watch is completely waterproof, Tory Burch watches undergo rigorous testing to meet specific water-resistance standards. The Maitland collection, for instance, is water-resistant up to 30 meters (98 feet), suitable for splashes and rain. 

Water resistance is achieved through sealed cases and gaskets at joints. However, it's important to note that wearing a watch while showering, swimming, or diving can still damage the mechanism and case over time. 


### Are the leather straps hypoallergenic?

Most of Tory Burch's leather straps are made from high-quality, hypoallergenic leather that's tested for sensitivity. However, individuals with severe allergies might still experience reactions. 

Tory Burch offers alternative strap options in rubber and metallic finishes for those with sensitivities. Regular maintenance and proper cleaning can further minimize allergic reactions. 


### What's the difference between U. S. and Global models?

U. S. models typically feature distinct designs and dial layouts compared to Global versions. For example, the U. S. Lindy watch has a vertical date window, while the Global version displays it horizontally. 

U. S. models often include additional functions or complications that may not be present in Global versions. These differences allow Tory Burch to cater to regional preferences while maintaining a consistent collection. 


### How long does the battery last in quartz models?

Quartz batteries typically last between 1.5 to 2 years, depending on usage and battery quality. Modern Tory Burch quartz watches incorporate power-saving technologies to extend battery life. 

When the battery reaches its final stages, the second hand may move erratically before stopping. Regular battery changes ensure your watch maintains its accuracy and reliability.
