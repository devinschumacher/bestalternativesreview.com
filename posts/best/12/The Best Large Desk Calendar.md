### What's the difference between a wall-mounted and desk-mounted calendar?

Wall-mounted calendars are designed for display on a vertical surface and typically have fewer features than desk models. They're ideal for shared spaces where everyone can see the calendar at once. Desk-mounted calendars offer more functionality, with larger fonts, better visibility, and often include additional features like weather updates or motivational quotes. 

Wall calendars are usually less expensive and take up less desk space, making them suitable for offices or smaller workspaces. Desk calendars provide convenient access to the calendar throughout the day and are perfect for personal use or small team environments. 


### How do I choose the right size for my large desk calendar?

The ideal size depends on your desk space and how frequently you'll refer to it. A calendar measuring between 18" to 24" wide is generally practical, offering enough space for monthly views while remaining manageable on most desks. Consider whether you need extra features like weather updates or moon phases, as these can extend the calendar's physical dimensions. 

Think about your primary use case. If you need daily reminders and frequent updates, a larger calendar with more features might be necessary. For basic reference, a mid-sized calendar (18-24") often proves sufficient.
