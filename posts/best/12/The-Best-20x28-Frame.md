A 20x28 frame combines generous size with versatile applications, suitable for both primary and supplementary display roles. Size considerations, material durability, and framing options are crucial factors in selecting the right frame.

### Material Durability

Given the frame's substantial size, material quality significantly impacts longevity. Choose between wood, metal, and high-density plastic options, each with distinct advantages. Premium wood frames offer excellent visual appeal, while metal provides exceptional durability and scratch resistance. High-density plastic frames balance style and functionality at an accessible price point.

### Frame Style Options

The 20x28 format benefits from a wide array of styling choices to complement various decor preferences. Traditional molding styles, such as carved or sleek profiles, provide classic elegance. Contemporary designs featuring minimalist lines or artistic embellishments offer modern alternatives. The frame's profile should be at least 2 inches deep to maintain visual impact while properly housing and protecting the artwork. 


### Mounting and Hardware

![20x28-Frame-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/20x28-Frame-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)

Securing a 20x28 frame requires robust mounting hardware to prevent sagging or damage from weight. Use at least four mounting points distributed evenly across the frame's back, opting for corner brackets or wire hangers to distribute weight effectively. Consider UV protective backing for photo prints and add shatter-resistant glass or acrylic to protect both the artwork and frame. 


## FAQ

### What is a 20x28 frame?

A 20x28 frame is a standard frame size, measuring 20 inches in width and 28 inches in height. This versatile size works well for both portraits and landscapes and fits most standard wall spaces. 

The frame's dimensions give it a generous 8x14 image area, making it suitable for capturing detailed subjects or filling larger wall spaces effectively. 


### What types of materials are 20x28 frames made from?

![20x28-Frame-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/20x28-Frame-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)

Common materials include wood (poplar or pine), metal (aluminum or steel), and composite materials. Wood frames offer a classic look with natural variances, while metal frames provide durability and modernity. 

The frame material affects its weight, durability, and style appeal. For indoor display, wood and metal are popular choices that complement various decors. 


### How do I choose between frame finishes?

Available finishes include natural wood, painted (in various colors), and metallic (chrome, bronze, etc. ). The choice depends on your wall color and existing decor style. 

Natural wood frames complement traditional interiors, while painted or metallic frames work well in contemporary or minimalist spaces. 


### Should I get a frame with or without glass?

Frames with glass protect the artwork from dust and UV light, but require regular cleaning. They are suitable for most indoor display situations. 

Unframed art can be less expensive and maintenance-free, but should be stored in a dust-free environment. 


### What considerations are there for gilding or painting a frame?

Hand-gilded or painted frames add a custom touch but require professional application for durability. The process involves applying gold leaf or paint in multiple precise steps. 

DIY options are available for basic painting, but consider your skill level and the potential damage from mistakes.
