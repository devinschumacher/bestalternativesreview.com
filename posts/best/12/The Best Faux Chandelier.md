While both real crystal and faux chandeliers share similar designs and mounting requirements, they differ in material composition and durability. Faux chandeliers provide an affordable and maintenance-friendly alternative. Their synthetic materials resist chipping, cracking, and fading, making them ideal for busy household environments. These chandeliers also eliminate the need for expensive crystal cleaning and maintenance.

### Are faux chandeliers easy to install?

Most faux chandeliers can be installed with standard household tools and basic electrical knowledge. The installation process typically involves mounting the fixture to the ceiling joist using included hardware and attaching the electrical components per local wiring standards.
