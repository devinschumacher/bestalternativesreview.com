## FAQ


### What makes an oversized pillow different from a regular pillow?

Oversized pillows combine plush comfort with generous proportions, typically measuring between 20-26 inches in length and 14-20 inches in width. They offer extra cushioning and surface area for various uses, from lounging on the sofa to providing an additional layer of support during travel. 

These pillows feature extra-sturdy construction with reinforced stitching and denser filling, usually ranging from 3 to 7 pounds, to maintain their shape while supporting more weight than standard pillows. 


### What are the main benefits of oversized pillows?

Oversized pillows excel as supportive sleep solutions, particularly for side sleepers who need more surface area and cushioning. Their ample dimensions make them suitable for various sleeping positions and body types, providing consistent support throughout the night. 

When used on the couch, these pillows create inviting nooks for reading, relaxing, or serving as extra seating. Their generous size also allows for creative styling options, such as stacking them for additional comfort or using them as decorative elements. 


### Which fillings are commonly used in oversized pillows?

The most common fillings for oversized pillows include down, down alternative blends, and high-density foam. Down pillows provide exceptional softness and loft, while down alternative blends offer a humane and hypoallergenic option. High-density foam versions maintain shape and support without the weight of traditional down pillows. 

Less common fillings include buckwheat hulls for natural support, and feathers for a balance of softness and structure. The choice of filling affects the pillow's weight, durability, and ability to maintain its shape over time. 


### How should I care for an oversized pillow?

Oversized pillows generally require the same care as regular pillows - spot clean stains immediately with a mild detergent and water, and air dry or machine wash according to the care label instructions. Avoid using bleach or harsh chemicals, as they can damage the filling and reduce the pillow's lifespan. 

When storing or transporting an oversized pillow, ensure it's completely dry and compressed to avoid flattening. For down-filled pillows, consider purchasing a breathable pillow protector to extend their lifespan and maintain hygiene. 


### What are the key considerations when choosing an oversized pillow?

When selecting an oversized pillow, consider the intended use (sleeping or lounging), fill material (down, down alternative, foam), and the room's available space. Think about the pillow's support level, hypoallergenic properties, and whether it matches your existing bedding or furniture aesthetic. 

Test the pillow's weight and comfort before purchasing, as oversized pillows can vary significantly in how they feel when you're lying on them. Consider factors like machine washability, durability, and the pillow's ability to maintain its shape over time. 


### How do I clean an oversized pillow?

For most oversized pillows, spot cleaning is sufficient for daily maintenance. In case of spills or accidents, gently blot the stain with a clean cloth and mild detergent, then air dry completely before using the pillow again. For occasional deep cleaning, follow these steps: 
1. Secure any detachable covers or pillows with zippers. 2. Gently beat the pillow outdoors to remove loose dust and debris. 3. Machine wash in a mesh bag using mild detergent and cold water. 4. Air dry completely before use, as heat can damage the filling. 

Down and down alternative pillows may need to be hand washed or dry cleaned, so check the care label before attempting machine washing. Always ensure the pillow is completely dry before use to prevent mold or mildew growth.
