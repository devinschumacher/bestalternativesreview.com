### How do I clean and maintain a Bosch microwave?

Regular cleaning of the exterior can be done with mild soap and water using a damp cloth. The interior should be cleaned with a solution of water and baking soda or vinegar, followed by a quick rinse with water and a dry cloth. 

The cavity should be wiped down after each use to prevent food residue buildup. The turntable and rack can be removed for deeper cleaning. Avoid using abrasive cleaners, metal utensils, or harsh chemicals that could damage the appliance. 


### What's the average lifespan of a Bosch microwave?

With proper care, a Bosch microwave typically lasts between 10 to 15 years. The microwave oven technology itself is designed to be reliable, and Bosch builds their appliances to maintain performance over time. 

Common components that might require replacement include the heating element (around 10 years), magnetron (8-12 years), and seals (6-7 years). Regular maintenance, such as keeping the door seals clean and replacing them as needed, can extend the appliance's lifespan.

### Are Bosch microwaves energy efficient?

Bosch microwaves are engineered to be energy efficient, with models featuring Inverter Technology that adjusts power output during cooking to save energy without compromising performance. 

The average monthly energy cost for a Bosch microwave falls between $5 to $12, depending on usage patterns and local electricity rates. Their energy-efficient designs help reduce both utility costs and environmental impact. 


### Can I install a Bosch microwave in my existing kitchen?

Bosch microwaves are designed to fit standard kitchen cabinets, with most models measuring 16 to 24 inches wide and 11 to 12 inches deep. The height typically ranges from 26 to 29 inches, including the control panel.
