## Buyer's Guide

Juicy Couture perfume is known for its blend of sweet and spicy notes with a modern twist. When choosing a Juicy Couture fragrance, consider the following factors to find your perfect match. 


### Top Notes

The top notes are the first scents you detect and typically last anywhere from 2 to 5 minutes. Common top notes in Juicy Couture perfumes include cinnamon, cardamom, and other warm spices. These zesty beginnings set the stage for the aroma that follows. 


### Middle Notes

These are the heart of the fragrance and usually last between 1 to 3 hours. Middle notes include floral scents like rose, gardenia, and jasmine, as well as spicy elements like nutmeg and clove. They provide the core aroma that defines the perfume's character. 


### Base Notes

The base notes develop about 30 minutes after application and can last from several hours to days. Common base notes in Juicy Couture fragrances include vanilla, amber, and musk. These deeper scents give the perfume its lasting quality and overall character. 


### Size and Strength

Perfume comes in various strengths: Eau de Toilette, Eau de Cologne, Eau Fraîche, and Extrait. Eau de Toilette offers a good balance of longevity and intensity, making it the most popular choice. Consider whether you prefer a strong, immediate impact or a more subtle, nuanced scent.
