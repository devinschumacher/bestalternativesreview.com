## FAQ


### What is an Avocado Blanket made of?

Avocado blankets feature a premium, soft texture that combines natural elements with modern comfort. The core material is often a blend of organic cotton and bamboo fibers, known for their moisture-wicking properties and natural temperature regulation. 

The outer layer may include natural elements like pima cotton or modal, chosen for their fine, silky texture that mimics the softness of an avocado fruit's skin. 


### How do Avocado Blankets maintain their shape?

These blankets use high-quality cotton voile for the interior, which helps prevent the filling from bunching or shifting shape. The cotton voile also provides natural ventilation, keeping the blanket's shape while allowing it to breathe. 

The outer layer's slightly stretchy nature and the careful placement of the filling work together to maintain an even, pillow-like shape over time. 


### What makes them hypoallergenic?

The organic cotton and bamboo blend used in avocado blankets forms a natural barrier against allergens, reducing the likelihood of irritation for sensitive skin. 

The production process includes rigorous washing and treatment to remove any potential irritants, ensuring that these blankets are suitable for people with allergies or sensitivities. 


### How do they stay so soft?

The blending of organic cotton and bamboo creates a natural, protective layer that prevents the filling from direct contact with the skin, maintaining the soft texture over time. 

The filling itself may include natural elements that resist compression, helping to preserve the blanket's softness through regular use. 


### How should they be cared for?

The blanket can be machine washed and dried using gentle cycles to maintain its shape and softness. Fabric softener is optional but may be used to enhance the existing softness. 

Avoid using harsh detergents or fabric softeners that could damage the natural fibers or affect the blanket's texture. 


### How do they compare to other plush blankets?

Like other premium plush blankets, avocado blankets offer superior comfort through their carefully crafted blend of natural materials and thoughtful design. 

The key distinction lies in the specific combination of organic cotton and bamboo, which provides additional benefits like natural moisture regulation and hypoallergenic properties.
