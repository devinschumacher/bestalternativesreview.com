## Buyer's Guide

White wedge heels combine elegance with comfort, making them a popular choice for both casual and formal occasions. These versatile shoes elevate any outfit while keeping your feet happy during long days on your feet. 


### Heel Height and Stability

The heel's height significantly affects both comfort and style. Taller wedges (4-6 inches) offer more height and slimming effects but may be less stable. Shorter heels (1-3 inches) provide better balance and ease of movement, perfect for daily wear or less formal events.

### Width and Fit

Most white wedge heels come in standard widths, but custom options are available. Ensure the toes have enough room to move freely without squishing, and the heel fits snugly without slipping. A half-size down is often recommended for optimal support. 


### Material and Quality

Premium materials like leather or suede offer durability and a professional look. These materials require proper care but prevent scuffs and fading. Synthetic options are more affordable but may scratch more easily. Check for reinforced stitching and sturdy soles that can handle regular wear.

### Shoe Care and Maintenance

Regular cleaning and proper storage extend the life of white wedge heels. Use a soft cloth to remove dust, and avoid harsh chemicals that can damage the material. Store in clean, dry conditions to prevent scuffs and odors. Consider investing in a shoe horn for easier wearing and better posture.
