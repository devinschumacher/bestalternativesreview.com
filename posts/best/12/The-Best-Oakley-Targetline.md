## Buyer's Guide

The Oakley Targetline series blends style with functionality, making them a popular choice for active wear enthusiasts. Understanding their key features will help you select the right pair that matches your lifestyle and performance expectations. 


### Frame Construction and Materials

Oakley Targetline sunglasses utilize lightweight materials like polycarbonate and TIVX for durability and impact resistance. The frame design prioritizes stability during intense activities, with adjustable nose pads and temple arms that prevent slippage. 


### Lens Options and Technology

The lenses employ Trivex technology for optical clarity and shatter resistance. Oakley's Prizm lens treatments enhance color perception and visibility in various lighting conditions, though specific performance varies by lens color and coating. 


### Fit and Comfort

Frame sizes range from 46 to 62mm, with both single and interchangeable lens options. The adjustable nose pads and temple arms accommodate different facial shapes, while the slightly curved frame design optimizes field of view during movement. 


### Additional Features

Mirrored coatings and scratch-resistant treatments are standard across the line. Some models include integrated foam shields, while the RF-90 line incorporates extended ear flaps for improved side protection. The Targetline also offers interchangeable lenses and nose pads for customization.
