## Buyer's Guide

Calphalon air fryers combine the convenience of an airy fry with the health benefits of reduced oil usage. Understanding their key features will help you choose the right model for your needs. 


### Sizes and Portion Control

Calphalon air fryers come in various sizes, from compact countertop models to larger, stove-top options. Consider your typical meal portions and storage requirements when selecting a capacity. The cooking basket's size impacts how many items you can cook at once, so match it to your meal planning habits. 


### Cooking Performance

These appliances use convection technology with a powerful fan to circulate hot air, achieving similar results to deep frying with minimal oil. Look for features like adjustable temperature control and a precise thermostat to ensure even cooking. Some models offer multiple cooking functions beyond frying, giving you more cooking flexibility.
