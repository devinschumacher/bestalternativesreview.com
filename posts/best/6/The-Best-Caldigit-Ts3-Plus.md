## FAQ


### What is the Caldigit Ts3 Plus?

The Caldigit Ts3 Plus is a USB-C external hard drive enclosure designed for both Mac and Windows systems. It supports USB 3.2 Gen 2 connectivity, delivering speeds up to 1,050 MB/s for reliable data transfer. 

The device comes in capacities of 500GB, 1TB, 2TB, and 4TB, with a durable aluminum construction that protects your data in various environments. 


### What operating systems are compatible with the Ts3 Plus?

Designed for modern systems, the Ts3 Plus works seamlessly with macOS Catalina and later versions, as well as Windows 10. It's a great solution for users who need reliable cross-platform storage. 

For Mac users, the device appears as a standard external drive, while Windows systems recognize it via USB 3.2 Gen 2 technology. 


### What makes the Ts3 Plus different from other USB-C drives?

Unlike many USB-C drives that require specific cable types, the Ts3 Plus uses standard USB-C cables, making it compatible with most modern devices and ports. This flexibility is crucial for users who frequently switch between different computers or notebooks. 

The Ts3 Plus also features improved thermal management compared to its predecessor, keeping the drive cool during intense data transfers and extending its lifespan. 


### How do I format the Ts3 Plus after installation?

After connecting the Ts3 Plus to your computer, you can format it using the built-in disk utility of your operating system. On macOS, go to Disk Utility and select the drive, choosing the appropriate file system (APFS or Mac OS Extended). 

On Windows, right-click the drive in File Explorer, select Format, and choose NTFS or exFAT for optimal compatibility with both Windows and Mac systems. 


### What should I consider when purchasing a Ts3 Plus?

When selecting a Ts3 Plus, consider your specific storage needs and choose the capacity that best matches your data requirements. All models support both Mac and Windows, so select a port that matches your computer's USB-C specifications. 

While the Ts3 Plus is suitable for most users, consider the model's durability features and compatibility with your existing equipment before making a purchase. 


### Can the Ts3 Plus be used as a time machine backup?

The Ts3 Plus can be used as a backup destination for Mac systems, including Time Machine backups. To set up Time Machine on a Mac, connect the Ts3 Plus and select it as the backup hard drive in the Time Machine preferences. 

For Windows users, the Ts3 Plus functions as a standard external drive and can be used with various data management tools, but it's not specifically optimized for Windows backup solutions.
