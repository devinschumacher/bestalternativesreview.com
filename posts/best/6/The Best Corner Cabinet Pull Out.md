### Are pull-out corner cabinets right for my kitchen?

Cornert cabinets are ideal if you have limited counter space and need extra storage. They maximize vertical space and provide easy access to items in hard-to-reach corners. 

Consider your kitchen size and layout before installing. They're particularly useful in small to medium kitchens where wall space is valuable. 


### How do pull-out cabinets save space?

Pull-out cabinets save space by extending into the cooking area when opened, allowing you to access and reach items stored in the corner that would otherwise be inaccessible. 

The rolling mechanism keeps the cabinet at a consistent height while extending, preventing it from hitting countertops or appliances when fully opened.

### What types of items are best stored in pull-out cabinets?

Safely store heavy or awkward items like pots, pans, baking sheets, and rarely used kitchen tools where you won't risk dropping them while reaching into the corner. 

Think of it as a second pantry - keep ingredients like canned goods, boxes, and dry goods organized and accessible in these spaces. 


### How do I measure for a pull-out cabinet?

Measure the cabinet's interior corners, noting the depth, height, and width where items need to fit. Add an extra inch for the sliding mechanism. 

Ensure your cabinet depth plus the slide extension doesn't exceed your available kitchen cabinet depth to prevent hitting walls or appliances.
