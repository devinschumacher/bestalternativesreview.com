### How should a black balaclava fit?

A properly fitting balaclava should sit snugly without causing pressure points. The face mesh should allow clear vision with minimal fogging, while the hood should provide good coverage for cold ears.

Machine wash balaclavas in a mesh bag using mild detergent to prevent snagging. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, which can damage the synthetic fibers. 

Rinse thoroughly and air dry flat to maintain the shape and integrity of the material. Do not use a dryer, as heat can cause shrinkage and damage the elastic components.
