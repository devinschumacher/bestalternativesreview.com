### How should I care for Hello Kitty earrings?

Regular cleaning with a soft cloth prevents scratching and extends the life of your earrings. Store them separately to avoid tangling and protect the surface finish. 

Avoid harsh chemicals or abrasive materials that could damage the surface. For metal pieces, wipe with a mild soap solution and dry thoroughly before storage. 


### What's the difference between plastic and metal Hello Kitty earrings?

Plastic earrings typically cost less and come in brighter colors. They're suitable for occasional wear and less likely to cause irritation. 

Metal earrings, especially those with gold tones, offer more durability and a higher polish finish. They're ideal for daily wear but may cause slight skin irritation for some.
