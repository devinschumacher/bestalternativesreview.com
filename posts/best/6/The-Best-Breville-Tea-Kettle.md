### What materials are used in Breville tea kettles?

Breville tea kettles are primarily made from stainless steel and food-grade materials. The exterior construction typically features stainless steel or BPA-free plastic for durability and ease of cleaning. The interior contains 304-grade stainless steel, known for its resistance to corrosion and safe use with water.

### How much water can a Breville tea kettle hold?

Breville tea kettles generally come in standard sizes suitable for both single and family use. The largest models can hold up to 1.7 liters, while smaller options range from 1 to 1.2 liters. This capacity allows you to brew multiple cups of tea without refilling frequently. 

The kettles are designed with an optimal water level indicator. Most models have marks indicating 250ml, 500ml, 750ml, and full capacity, helping you measure the right amount of water for your brewing needs.

### How long does a Breville tea kettle take to boil water?

Breville tea kettles heat water efficiently due to their rapid-boil technology. The typical 1.2-liter model brings water to a boil in under 3 minutes, while larger 1.7-liter models take approximately 4 minutes. The heating time depends on the initial water temperature and household electrical supply voltage. 

Once the water reaches the selected temperature, the kettle maintains it with minimal power consumption. This feature helps prevent overcooking and saves energy compared to traditional kettles that continuously reheat the water. 


### How should I maintain and clean a Breville tea kettle?

Regular cleaning helps maintain the performance and longevity of your Breville tea kettle. After each use, empty the kettle and allow it to cool before cleaning. Use warm, soapy water and a sponge to remove any residue or limescale. For tough stains, a mixture of baking soda and water can be effective. 

To prevent mineral buildup, especially in hard water areas, run a vinegar solution through the kettle every two weeks. Start by filling the kettle with white vinegar, let it sit for several hours, then flush with water and dry thoroughly before use.
