### What are Aqua Socks?

Aqua Socks are specialized swimming socks designed to prevent sand, chlorine, and other pool chemicals from sticking to your feet and legs. They're made from a blend of spandex and chlorine-resistant materials that allow for freedom of movement while providing excellent foot coverage. 

These socks typically feature a seamless toe design and reinforced heel and toe areas to prevent bunching and wear during extended use. The thin, sheer material allows you to use your feet naturally and comfortably while swimming laps or floating in the pool. 


### What sizes are available?

Aqua Socks come in a range of sizes to fit feet from children to adults. Most brands offer sizes from Youth 1-3 up to Adult Large-XXL, ensuring there's a comfortable fit for every user. 

It's recommended to measure your foot length and compare it with the size chart provided by the manufacturer. While many styles come in a single unisex size, some options provide separate sizing charts for men and women based on average foot measurements.

### How long do they last?

With regular use, Aqua Socks typically last between 3-6 months before showing signs of wear. Factors affecting their longevity include usage frequency, water quality, and how they're cared for after each use. 

Signs that it's time to replace your socks include thinning material, increased transparency, or visible holes in the toe or heel areas. Proper care through washing with mild detergent and air drying can significantly extend their lifespan. 


### Are they suitable for all water conditions?

Aqua Socks are designed for use in pools, spas, and other chlorinated environments, offering good protection against sand and chemicals in these settings. However, they may not provide the same level of protection in saltwater, particularly when exposed to high salinity levels for extended periods. 

For saltwater use, consider looking for socks specifically marketed as saltwater resistant or choosing a thicker, more durable option. Regular pools and spas typically have no issues with standard Aqua Socks.

### Can I use them for aquatic exercises?

Yes, Aqua Socks are ideal for aquatic exercises like kickboarding, water walking, and pool aerobics. Their lightweight design and material flexibility allow for natural foot movements during these activities while still providing essential protection.

### How should I wash and care for them?

Machine wash your Aqua Socks in a mesh bag using a mild detergent to prevent tangling. Turn them inside out before washing to protect the outside surface. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the material. 

Air dry the socks completely before wearing them again to prevent bacteria growth and maintain their shape. Consider using clips or hooks to keep them off the floor during drying to prevent tangling and damage.
