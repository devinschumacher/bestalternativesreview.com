## Buyer's Guide

A portable upholstery cleaner is an essential tool for maintaining clean and comfortable furniture. ### Power Source and Operating Requirements

Portable upholstery cleaners come in three primary power sources: corded, cordless (rechargeable battery), and manual. Corded models provide consistent power but are limited by cable length. Cordless cleaners offer greater mobility with longer run times on a single charge, typically ranging from 15-30 minutes. Manual cleaners require physical effort and suction power, making them suitable for small areas or limited access.

### Attachment Varieties and Cleaning Capabilities

Attachments are crucial for the versatility of a portable upholstery cleaner. Common attachments include crevice tools for tight spaces, dusting brushes for delicate surfaces, and upholstery attachments for cleaning fabric and leather. ### Water and Filtration Systems

Water management systems vary between models. Bucket systems allow added water for deeper cleaning, while direct flow systems attach the water container directly to the machine. Filtration systems remove soil and particles from the cleaning solution, protecting both the machine and the environment. Look for high-quality filters that trap small particles and maintain consistent pressure.

### What is a portable upholstery cleaner?

A portable upholstery cleaner is a compact machine designed to clean furniture and upholstery on-site. Unlike bulky home vacuum cleaners, these specialized tools are lightweight and can operate anywhere, from cars to outdoor seating. 

Portability is achieved through rechargeable batteries, reduced water capacity, and simplified designs that allow easy transport to different cleaning locations. 


### What types of dirt does a portable upholstery cleaner remove?

These cleaners effectively remove everyday dirt, dust, and minor spills from fabric and leather upholstery. They excel at addressing pet hair, allergens, and surface stains found in homes and vehicles. 

For tougher cleaning jobs, consider models with hot water extraction capabilities or additional cleaning attachments that can reach into crevices and upholstery seams.
