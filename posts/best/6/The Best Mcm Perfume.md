### Size and Packaging

MCM offers perfumes in multiple size options, from travel-friendly 1.7 oz (50 ml) miniatures to full-sized 8.4 oz (250 ml) bottles. Their distinctive packaging incorporates the brand's signature elements, such as the iconic double-M logo and luxurious gold finishes. The design choices align with MCM's preference for modern sophistication and clean lines. 


### Application and Storage

Proper application technique and storage can significantly impact how a fragrance performs. For MCM perfumes, apply in the pulse points (like wrists, neck, and behind ears) where body heat intensifies the scent. Store your fragrance in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources, which can degrade the quality of the perfume over time.
