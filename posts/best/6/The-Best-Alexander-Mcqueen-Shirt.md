### Can you wear Alexander McQueen shirts with business attire?

Many Alexander McQueen shirts, particularly dress shirts, work well with traditional business attire. The brand's modern twists on classic shirt designs can add a sophisticated edge to formalwear. 

However, very avant-garde pieces might not be suitable for conservative business environments. Opt for more understated designs when dressing for work or formal occasions. 


### What is the sizing system for Alexander McQueen shirts?

Alexander McQueen sizes typically run true to size, with their shirts offering a contemporary fit that sits close to the body. The brand suggests measuring your chest and comparing it to their sizing chart for the best fit. 

Consider ordering a size down if you prefer a sleeker silhouette, or stay true to your regular size for a modern fitting shirt.

### Who should I choose between Alexander McQueen and Dior Homme for a tailored shirt?

While both brands offer quality shirts, Alexander McQueen's modern designs and innovation lean towards the younger, more fashion-forward market. Dior Homme, on the other hand, offers a more refined and traditional approach to tailored shirts. 

Both brands excel in craftsmanship, so the choice largely depends on your personal style preferences and how you plan to wear the shirts. 


### How should I care for my Alexander McQueen shirts?

Alexander McQueen recommends dry cleaning their shirts to preserve the quality of the fabrics and the integrity of any special treatments like water-resistant coatings. Regular dry cleaning helps maintain the shirt's shape and prevents color fading. 

For at-home care, use a mild detergent and cool water for spot cleaning. Avoid ironing over any special treatments or intricate details, and store the shirts in a climate-controlled environment to prevent damage.
