### How do I choose the right size bait for crappie?

### What color baits work best for crappie?

Crappie seem to respond well to natural earthy tones like green, chartreuse, and blue when fishing in clear water. In stained water, bright colors like chartreuse, white, and blue often produce better results. 

Experiment with different colors to find what works best in your local conditions, as fish may develop preferences based on local forage and water visibility. 


### How should I rig live minnows for crappie?

Use a small live bait hook (size 6-8) and place the minnow through the top lip, leaving the tail intact. This rig keeps the minnow active and easy to cast. 

For best results, fish the live minnow near structure or along ledges where crappie are likely to be holding. 


### What techniques improve crappie bite rates?

Slow, deliberate presentations work best - troll plastic worms near cover or fish minnows with an umbrella rig just above the bottom. Crappie often take food slowly and methodically. 

Use light line (6-10-pound test) and sensitive rods to feel the subtle strikes, and be prepared to move your rig frequently to different areas of likely fish-holding structure.
