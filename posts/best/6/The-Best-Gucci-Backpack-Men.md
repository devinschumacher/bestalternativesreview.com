### How much weight can a Gucci backpack handle?

The backpacks are designed to carry a normal work or school load, with capacity ranging from 15-25 liters. The specific weight limit varies by model, with most comfortable carrying up to 15 pounds.

The backpacks come in standard sizes, with dimensions typically ranging from 13-15 inches in length, 11-12 inches in width, and 5-6 inches in height.

### How do the backpacks protect electronic devices?

Most models feature dedicated laptop sleeves that protect 15-inch laptops with padding and mesh ventilation. The sleeves also include pockets for accessories like USB drives and cables. 

Additional pockets and organizer panels help keep smaller items like phones, wallets, and notebooks secure during transport. 


### What about the backpack's comfort features?

Key comfort features include padded back panels and shoulder straps, adjustable straps to fit various body types, and breathable mesh panels for ventilation. 

Some premium models incorporate ergonomic designs with multiple compartments and padded waist straps to distribute weight evenly. 


### How should I maintain my Gucci backpack?

Regular care involves cleaning the backpack with mild soap and water, allowing it to air dry completely before use. Leather pieces should be treated with conditioner to maintain their appearance. 

Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasives, and store the backpack in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent damage and preserve its condition.
