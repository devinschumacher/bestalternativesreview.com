### Refill Compatibility

### Portability and Storage

Lighter refills often include travel caps or protective sleeves. Opt for packaging that securely protects the refills during transport and storage to prevent damage or leakage. 


### Environmental Impact

## FAQ


### What types of butane lighter refills are available?

Butane lighter refills come in two primary types: standard butane cans and mini butane canisters. Standard cans are suitable for traditional lighters and have a larger capacity, while mini canisters are designed for compact lighters and portable devices.
