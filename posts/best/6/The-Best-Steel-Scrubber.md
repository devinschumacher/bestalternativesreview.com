## FAQ


### What is a steel scrubber?

A steel scrubber is a powerful cleaning tool featuring a high-velocity water jet system that utilizes steel balls to remove stubborn dirt and grime from surfaces. The combination of water pressure and steel media delivers exceptional cleaning results even in heavy-duty industrial applications. 

The process begins with water entering the scrubber's chamber, where it mixes with steel balls and is pressurized to high velocity. The resulting jet stream impacts the surface at massive force, effectively breaking up and dislodging even the most persistent debris. 


### How does a steel scrubber work?

The scrubber system combines water and steel balls in a pressurized chamber. Water enters the chamber, picks up steel balls, and is rapidly accelerated to high velocity. The pressurized water stream exits through a nozzle, delivering an intense cleaning impact. 

The impact of the steel ball-laden water stream removes dirt and grime by forcefully agitating the surface. This mechanical cleaning action effectively breaks apart tough residues, leaving surfaces clean and polished. 


### What surfaces can be cleaned with a steel scrubber?

Steel scrubbers excel at cleaning concrete, brick, granite, and other harsh surfaces where traditional cleaning methods fail. The combination of water pressure and steel media proves particularly effective on areas with stubborn grease, oil, and baked-on grime. 

Special attachments and lower pressure settings allow these versatile machines to safely clean delicate surfaces like delicate masonry or sensitive equipment without causing damage. 


### What maintenance is required for a steel scrubber?

Regular maintenance includes checking pressures, lubricating moving parts, inspecting the water tank, and cleaning debris from the chamber and hoses. Steel ball replacement is necessary when they become worn or damaged during use. 

The water filter should be checked and cleaned weekly, and the interior of the scrubber must be thoroughly cleaned after each use to prevent mineral buildup and maintain optimal performance. 


### What are the benefits of using a steel scrubber?

Steel scrubbers offer superior cleaning results for tough jobs, with improved efficiency and reduced labor compared to manual cleaning methods. The consistent force of the steel ball-laden water stream ensures thorough cleaning across large areas. 

These machines require less water and cleaning chemicals than traditional methods, making them more cost-effective while providing environmentally friendly cleaning solutions. 


### What safety precautions should be taken when using a steel scrubber?

Always wear appropriate personal protective equipment, including safety goggles, gloves, and steel-toed boots when operating a steel scrubber. Ensure proper drainage before operation and keep bystanders at a safe distance. 

Check the water supply pressure and the condition of the steel balls before each use. Never point the nozzle at people or delicate surfaces, and maintain a secure footing while operating the machine.
