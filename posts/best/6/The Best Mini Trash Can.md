### How do I properly dispose of mini trash can contents?

Always empty mini trash cans into appropriate waste receptacles, following local recycling and garbage guidelines. Avoid mixing recyclables with regular trash, and empty pet waste into designated disposal units. 

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the can with soap and water, allowing it to dry completely before reuse. This simple maintenance routine extends the life of your trash can and keeps your living space clean.

### Can mini trash cans be used outdoors?

Many mini trash cans are designed for both indoor and outdoor use, featuring weather-resistant materials and sealed lids to prevent leaks. However, some models may not be suitable for extreme conditions or heavy use. 

When using outdoor mini trash cans, place them on a stable surface and protect them from direct sunlight, rain, or exposure to harsh chemicals. Always check the manufacturer's recommendations for specific outdoor use guidelines. 


### How should I clean a mini trash can?

Regular cleaning involves emptying the contents, scraping out any debris, and washing the can with soap and water. For tough stains, a mixture of baking soda and vinegar can help break down residue. 

After cleaning, thoroughly dry the trash can before replacing the lid. This prevents moisture accumulation, which can lead to mold growth and odors.
