## FAQ


### Which birthday shirts are best for boys?

Birthday shirts combine fun designs with practicality, making them ideal gifts or casual wear options. Look for wrinkle-resistant fabrics, comfortable fits, and moisture-wicking materials that keep kids cool during indoor or outdoor celebrations. 

Popular designs range from sports themes and animal prints to pop culture references and educational motifs. Consider the recipient's interests to find a shirt that'll become a favorite. 


### What size options are available for birthday shirts?

Birthday shirts come in standard children's sizes from 2T to 16 years, measured by chest circumference rather than waist size. Most brands offer S, M, L, and XL for older children, though some may offer extra sizes for larger frames. 

For accurate sizing, check the brand's size chart before purchasing. Measure the child's chest at their natural waist level to ensure a proper fit. 


### How should I wash birthday shirts?

Most birthday shirts can be machine washed in cold or warm water on a gentle cycle, using mild detergent. Avoid harsh bleach or fabric softeners, which can damage print quality. 

Air drying is recommended to preserve the shape and color of the shirt. If you must use a dryer, use low heat and remove the shirt while it's still slightly damp to prevent shrinkage. 


### What makes a birthday shirt durable?

Durable birthday shirts feature high-quality printing methods like heat transfer or sublimation, which bond the design permanently to the fabric. The shirt itself should use strong seam construction and reinforced stitching at stress points. 

Knit fabrics like cotton-poly blends often provide the best durability, as they resist stretching and maintain their shape well over time. Avoid excessively tight designs that could stretch the fabric. 


### Can I return birthday shirts if they don't fit?

Most retailers offer returns or exchanges for unworn birthday shirts within 30-90 days of purchase, provided the shirt remains in its original condition. Check the retailer's policy for specific details on returns and restocking fees. 

If you're ordering online, consider purchasing multiple sizes to ensure you get the right fit. Many brands offer free returns or exchanges if you need to exchange for a different size. 


### What safety standards apply to birthday shirts?

Birthday shirts are regulated by ASTM F1852-18, which sets standards for children's sleepwear. This includes requirements for flame resistance, fabric content, and label information. 

Look for safety certifications on the packaging or label. For non-sleepwear shirts, ensure they meet ASTM F963-17 standards for toy safety, especially if the shirt includes small accessories or embellishments.
