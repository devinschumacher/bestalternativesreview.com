## Buyer's Guide

The Urbini stroller line offers a range of comfortable, practical options for parents. Understanding key features will help you select the right stroller for your needs. 


### Frame and Construction

Urbini strollers use durable materials like aluminum or steel for their frames. The construction should feel sturdy and well-connected. Look for reinforced joints and a solid base design. 


### Reclining Features

Adjustable-reclining seats are standard, offering comfort for both babies and toddlers. The recline should reach a fully flat lay-flat position for naps. Check if the stroller's seat and footrest can be adjusted independently. 


### Folding and Storage

Easy-to-fold strollers with single-hand operation are convenient for urban environments. Consider how the stroller folds - some models tuck into a compact shape, while others fold outward. Check storage options like cup holders, built-in storage bins, or a convenient push seat basket. 


### Wheels and Maneuverability

Look for strollers with either 4 or 5 wheels, with 5-wheel models providing better stability. Tires should be pneumatic (air-filled) for smoother rides, with diameter measuring 16 inches or larger. The stroller's weight distribution affects its maneuverability - 3-in-1 or convertible models with a foot forward configuration can be more nimble.
