## FAQ


### What is foam cannon soap?

Soap designed for use with foam cannons creates dense, long-lasting foam that helps remove dirt and grime from vehicles and other surfaces. It's concentrated, meaning less product goes further, and often contains additives for enhanced cleaning power. 

When used with a foam cannon, this type of soap produces a thick foam that clings to surfaces longer than traditional liquid soaps, allowing more effective cleaning with fewer applications. 


### What makes foam cannon soap different from regular car wash soap?

Standard car wash soap is formulated for direct application and rinsing, while foam cannon soap is specifically engineered to create a thick foam when mixed with water. This foam stays on surfaces longer, allowing more thorough cleaning with less product. 

Concentrated formula reduces waste and expenses compared to regular soaps, making foam cannon soap a cost-effective choice for professional car washes and serious detailers. 


### How should foam cannon soap be diluted?

The recommended dilution ratio is typically between 1:40 and 1:80, depending on the product. Always check the manufacturer's instructions for the specific ratio recommended for your selected soap. 

Start with the lower ratio and adjust as needed based on the soil level and water pressure. Too concentrated a solution can damage vehicle finishes, while too diluted a solution won't produce sufficient foam. 


### What type of foam cannon should I use with foam cannon soap?

Retail foam cannons are designed for use with household detergents and may not produce the optimal foam with specialized cleaning products. Professional foam cannons, often referred to as "car wash" or "pressure wash" foam cannons, are specifically engineered for use with dedicated car wash soaps. 

Professional-grade foam cannons are built to handle concentrated cleaning solutions and produce more consistent, higher-quality foam, essential for thorough vehicle cleaning. 


### Can foam cannon soap be used for anything other than cars?

While specifically formulated for vehicle cleaning, foam cannon soap can effectively clean many other surfaces including boats, RVs, and concrete. It's particularly effective on surfaces that require a lot of foam for cleaning. 

Always perform a test on an inconspicuous area before using any cleaning product on valuable or delicate surfaces to ensure compatibility. 


### How long does foam cannon soap last once mixed with water?

Mixed foam cannon soap typically remains effective for about 24 hours when stored in a closed container. The solution may begin to break down after this time, resulting in reduced foam quality and potential chemical degradation. 

For best results, limit the use of mixed soap to 8-12 hours. If not using the full batch within this timeframe, consider using a portion for immediate use and saving the remainder.
