### How should I maintain Dewalt power tools for optimal performance?

To maintain Dewalt power tools, always use the correct torque setting for your application and avoid over-tightening, which can damage both the tool and the material. Regularly check and tighten all screws, especially in the chuck and battery connections. 

After each use, clean the tool thoroughly, particularly around the motor and battery connections. Store tools in a dry place and keep them charged to extend battery life. For cordless tools, store them with the battery fully charged when not in use.
