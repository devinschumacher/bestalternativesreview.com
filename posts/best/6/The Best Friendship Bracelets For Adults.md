Standard nylon friendship bracelets can last anywhere from 3 to 6 months with regular wear. Factors affecting longevity include the quality of materials used and how often the bracelet is worn.

### What are the health risks of wearing friendship bracelets?

The primary health concern with friendship bracelets is allergic reactions to materials like dyes or metals in decorative elements. Most allergic reactions are minor, causing redness or itching. 

Occasionally, some people may experience more severe reactions to certain materials. If you notice unusual symptoms, it's advisable to stop wearing the bracelet and consult a healthcare provider. 


### Can friendship bracelets be machine washed?

For standard nylon bracelets, it's generally safe to machine wash them in a mesh bag on a gentle cycle with cold water. This helps prevent tangling and protects the shape of the bracelet. 

Bracelets with embellishments like metal charms or beading should be hand washed to avoid damage to the decorative elements.
