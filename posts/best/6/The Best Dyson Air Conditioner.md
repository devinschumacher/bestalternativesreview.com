## FAQ


### What makes a Dyson air conditioner different from traditional models?

Unlike conventional air conditioners that use compressors and refrigerants, Dyson's Air Multiplier technology creates cool air by accelerating existing moisture in the air, passing it through a high-speed digital motor, and amplifying it through the bladeless design. This approach uses up to 70% less energy than traditional ACs, making it more efficient and quiet. 

The absence of visible blades in Dyson's air conditioners also eliminates the risk of injury from moving parts and reduces the likelihood of debris accumulation, requiring fewer maintenance checks than traditional models.
