### What is a Lutron dimmer switch?

A Lutron dimmer switch is an electronic device that allows you to control the brightness of connected lighting fixtures. Unlike traditional switches that turn lights on or off at full intensity, a dimmer provides continuous control over how bright or dim your lights appear. 

These switches work by modifying the amount of electricity reaching the light fixture, effectively controlling the brightness through a process called pulse-width modulation. This allows for smooth dimming without the flickering common in older incandescent dimming systems.
