## Buyer's Guide

G8 LED bulbs represent a practical upgrade over traditional halogen bulbs in ceiling fixtures. These energy-efficient alternatives offer several advantages, including longer lifespan and reduced power consumption. Understanding their specifications and applications will help you select the right bulb for your lighting needs. 


### Brightness and Lumens

When evaluating G8 LED bulbs, the most crucial specification is lumens, which measure light output. Compare the lumen rating to your fixture's requirements or the brightness of your existing bulb. Higher lumens result in brighter light, but keep in mind that your space's size and ceiling height affect how much brightness you actually need.
