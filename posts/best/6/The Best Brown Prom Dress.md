### How should I care for my brown prom dress?

Most brown prom dresses require dry cleaning to maintain their quality and appearance. Check the dress's care label for specific instructions and take it to a professional cleaner the same day you wear it.

Brown prom dresses pair beautifully with metallic jewelry, such as gold or silver pieces, adding a touch of elegance to the natural earthy tones. Consider statement earrings, a sparkling clutch, and a soft wristwatch to complete your look.
