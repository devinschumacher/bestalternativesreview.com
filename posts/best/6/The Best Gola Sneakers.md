The brand's commitment to comfort through detailed cushioning sets it apart from some more minimalist competitors, though prices tend to be slightly higher than budget-friendly options. 


### What materials are used in Gola sneakers?

Popular materials include premium leather for uppers in their flagship styles, while newer models incorporate synthetic alternatives for better durability and moisture resistance. The brand also increasingly uses recycled materials in their environmental initiatives. 

Footbeds typically feature EVA foam for medium cushioning, while performance models incorporate memory foam and gel pads for enhanced support. 


### Are Gola sneakers comfortable for everyday wear?

Most Gola sneakers offer good all-day wearability, with standard width fittings and medium arch support. The brand's signature cushioning provides adequate shock absorption for daily commuting or casual activities.

### How should I care for my Gola sneakers?

Standard care instructions include cleaning with mild soap and water, air drying, and occasional conditioning with leather care products for suede or leather uppers. The brand advises against using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners. 

For best results, Gola recommends storing shoes in a cool, dry place and rotating between pairs to extend their lifespan. Regular maintenance can help prevent odor and maintain the shoes' appearance. 


### What sizes are available in Gola sneakers?
