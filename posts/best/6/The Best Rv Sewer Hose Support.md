### What is an RV sewer hose support?

RV sewer hose supports are specialized clamps or hangers designed to secure the hose at its connections, preventing kinks and maintaining proper flow. They typically attach to vehicle walls or floors near the sewer outlet. 

These supports help prevent the hose from sagging or twisting, which can cause blockages and backup issues while the RV is in motion or stationary. 


### What types of materials are these supports made from?

Most RV sewer hose supports are made from durable plastic or metal. High-quality models often feature stainless steel or aluminum construction to prevent corrosion and maintain hygiene standards. 

Some supports incorporate locking mechanisms or adjustable clamps to secure hoses of different diameters, while others have built-in anti-kink features.

### How do I install an RV sewer hose support?

Installation typically requires drilling two small holes in the vehicle's wall or floor near the sewer outlet. The support mounts to these holes, creating a stable base for the hose clamp.

Most RV sewer hoses will work with standard supports, but specialized adapters may be needed for different hose sizes or shapes. It's important to select a support that matches your specific hose dimensions.
