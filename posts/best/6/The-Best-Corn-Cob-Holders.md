### What is a corn cob holder?

A corn cob holder is a kitchen gadget designed to keep entire ears of corn fresh and crisp while boiling. The holder typically features an adjustable wire cage that secures the corn in place, allowing water to circulate around each ear. 

Some models include extra features like moisture-retaining liners or temperature-resistant handles, while others are made from stainless steel for lasting durability. 


### How do I use a corn cob holder?

Place the corn cob holder in boiling water, making sure the ears of corn are fully submerged. Adjust the holder to accommodate the size of the ears and the pot's dimensions. 

Boil the corn for 5-7 minutes or until tender. Use a utensil to carefully remove the corn from the holder and serve immediately.

### What are the benefits of using a corn cob holder?

The holder prevents corn ears from floating to the surface during cooking, ensuring even heating. It also keeps the corn secure while cooking, making it easier to handle and serve.

### How do I clean a corn cob holder?

After each use, rinse the holder thoroughly with water and dry it completely. The wire mesh can be wiped with a sponge or cloth using mild detergent if needed.
