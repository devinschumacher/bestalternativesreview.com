The best Marilyn Monroe wigs combine realistic hair texture with manageable styling requirements. Look for wigs made from high-quality synthetic hair that closely resembles natural hair in both texture and shine. The base should be secure and lightweight, with a comfortable fit that prevents sliding or irritation. 


### Hair Length and Style Options

Marilyn Monroe wigs typically come in various lengths, from shoulder-length to full waist-curl styles. Consider what length works best with your face shape and daily routine. The style should be relatively maintenance-free, with simple styling requirements like brushes or light sprays rather than complicated styling tools. 


### Color and Highlights

The natural blonde color of Marilyn Monroe's hair was a mix of soft highlights and subtle highlights. When choosing a wig, opt for a shade that matches this natural look rather than overly processed blonde. The highlights should be distributed evenly throughout the hair for the most authentic appearance. 


### Wearing and Maintenance

These wigs require regular maintenance to keep them looking their best. This includes weekly cleaning with a mild shampoo suitable for synthetic hair, proper storage to prevent tangling, and regular inspection for any loose or damaged strands. With proper care, a quality Marilyn Monroe wig can last for years.
