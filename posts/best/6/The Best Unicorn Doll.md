### What features should I look for in a unicorn doll?

Search for dolls with secure attachments for accessories like wands or wings to prevent small parts from becoming choking hazards. The stitching should be firmly sewn, and eyes should be firmly attached. 

For realistic play, consider dolls with moveable limbs, articulated joints, or poseable features like swiveling head or bendable legs. The materials should be either hypoallergenic fabrics or safe plastic. 


### How should I care for a unicorn doll?

To keep your unicorn doll looking its best, spot clean with mild soap and water when needed. Use a damp cloth to remove dust and grime, and avoid abrasive cleaners that could damage the surface. 

Store your doll in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to prevent fading. Consider purchasing a storage bag or pillow case to protect the doll during transport or when not in use.
