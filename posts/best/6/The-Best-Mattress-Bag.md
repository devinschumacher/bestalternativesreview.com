### What is a mattress bag?

A mattress bag is a protective cover specifically designed to encase a mattress, providing comprehensive protection against stains, wear, and allergens. These specially engineered covers feature inner layers that prevent liquid penetration and outer fabrics that resist tear and puncture. 

The bags typically include elasticized corners and a secure zipper closure system to ensure an snug fit around the mattress. Many versions also incorporate adjustable straps or tension cords to maintain the cover's position during use. 


### Why invest in a mattress bag?

These protective covers offer several advantages, including enhanced durability by preventing direct contact between the mattress and external factors that could cause damage or degradation.
