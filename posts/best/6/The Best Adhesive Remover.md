### What is adhesive remover?

Adhesive remover is a specialized product designed to dissolve and remove adhesives, including superglue, double-sided tape, and sticker residue. These formulas are typically methylene chloride or acetone-based and are effective on various surfaces. 

Most adhesive removers are designed for use on non-porous surfaces like glass, metal, and plastic. However, they can damage certain materials like paper, wood, and painted surfaces, so it's crucial to test the product on a small area first. 


### What surfaces can adhesive remover be used on?

Adhesive remover works best on non-porous surfaces such as glass, metal, ceramic, and painted surfaces. These products effectively dissolve most adhesives without requiring harsh scrubbing. 

While some formulas can damage materials like paper, wood, and certain types of plastic, always refer to the manufacturer's guidelines before use to ensure compatibility with your surface material. 

![Adhesive-Remover-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Adhesive-Remover-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do I remove superglue with adhesive remover?

To remove superglue, apply a small amount of adhesive remover to a cloth or cotton swab and gently rub it onto the glued surface. Let it sit for a few minutes to dissolve the glue before carefully peeling off or scraping away the excess. 

If the superglue has set deeply, you may need to soak the affected area with the remover before attempting removal. Always wear gloves and work in a well-ventilated area to avoid inhaling the fumes. 


### Can I use adhesive remover to remove stickers?

Adhesive remover is effective at removing sticker residue by softening the remaining adhesive. Apply a small amount of remover to the sticky area, wait a few minutes, then carefully peel off the residue with a credit card or spackle knife. 

For stubborn residue, you may need to reapply the remover or use a slightly larger application. Always test a small, inconspicuous area first to ensure the product doesn't damage the surface.

### How do I store adhesive remover properly?

Store adhesive remover in its original container in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. The optimum temperature range is typically between 60-80°F (15-27°C). 

Keep the container tightly sealed when not in use to maintain its effectiveness and prevent evaporation of the active ingredients. Avoid storing it near flammable materials or incompatible substances. 


### Are adhesive removers safe to use?

Adhesive removers contain chemicals that are potent and may be harmful if used improperly. Always read the product label carefully and follow all safety instructions. 

Work in a well-ventilated area, wear protective gloves to prevent skin contact, and avoid inhaling fumes. Keep the product out of reach of children and pets, and dispose of it properly according to local regulations.
