## FAQ


### What makes Nerf Rival guns different from other Nerf products?

Nerf Rival guns feature advanced Bluetooth connectivity, allowing players to join battles in real-time. This connection enables features like targeted fire, team play, and interactive gaming. The technology also supports customization, enabling players to adjust weapon settings through a dedicated app. 

Rival guns utilize specialized ammunition, such as PowerPellets and Hybrid Bolts, which offer unique performance characteristics compared to standard Nerf darts. 


### How does the Bluetooth system work between Rival guns?

The Bluetooth system allows up to 16 Rival guns to connect in a single battle, enabling players to team up or compete against each other. Battles can range from local multiplayer to connected play across different locations, as long as each player's gun has a clear line of sight to a wireless access point. 

Players can toggle between Team and Free-for-All modes in real-time through the app, changing the game dynamics in mid-battle. 


### What type of customization options are available?

Through the Rival app, players can adjust various weapon settings, including fire rate, accuracy, and shot power. The app also allows users to customize their in-game appearance, including weapon skins and character avatars. 

Customization extends to the physical guns as well, with interchangeable parts available for some models. This includes different barrel profiles, grips, and sights that affect performance and personalization. 


### What is the app compatibility and requirements?

The Rival app works with both iOS and Android devices. It requires a stable internet connection to maintain Bluetooth communication between guns, and players need to be within 100 feet of their weapon for proper functionality. 

Gun-to-device pairing is done via the app, which also handles firmware updates and compatibility checks. The app can run in the background, allowing players to keep their phone in their pocket during battles.
