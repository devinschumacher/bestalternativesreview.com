Hypoallergenic dog shampoos are specially formulated to minimize skin irritations that can affect pets with sensitive or allergy-prone skin. Key characteristics of hypoallergenic dog shampoos include the absence of harsh chemicals, artificial fragrances, and potential allergens like soy and wheat. They often contain soothing ingredients like oatmeal, aloe vera, and chamomile, which help to calm irritated skin and reduce inflammation. 


### How do I choose the right hypoallergenic shampoo for my dog?

The best hypoallergenic shampoo for your dog depends on their specific skin condition and needs. Consider factors such as whether they have dry skin, frequent itchiness, or known allergies to certain ingredients.

### How often should I use hypoallergenic dog shampoo?

The frequency of use can vary based on your dog's specific skin condition and the product's instructions. For dogs with severe allergies or skin conditions, your veterinarian may recommend daily or every-other-day applications initially.
