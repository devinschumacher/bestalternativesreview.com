## Buyer's Guide

French cleats, also known as paddle wall mounts, offer an elegant way to install shelves and mirrors. They provide strong support while maintaining clean lines. Understanding their features and correct installation requirements ensures you make an informed choice. 


### Key Features

French cleats consist of a rail screwed into the wall and a matching bracket that clips onto the back of the shelf or mirror. Common finishes include polished chrome, brushed nickel, and oil-rubbed bronze. The rail configuration can be single or double, with double cleats offering increased stability. 

![French-Cleats-2](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/French-Cleats-2/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### Installation Requirements
