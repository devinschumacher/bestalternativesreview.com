### How do low block heels compare to stilettos?

While stilettos offer much taller heights, typically 4 inches or more, low block heels provide stability and comfort through their wider, boxy design. Stilettos concentrate weight on a small heel point, while block heels distribute pressure more evenly across the foot. 

This difference makes low block heels more practical for extended wear and suitable for those with lower heel experience or longer walks. They're often considered a safer option for daily commuting or standing work environments.
