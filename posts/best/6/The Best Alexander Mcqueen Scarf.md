### How should I care for my Alexander McQueen scarf?

Hand wash the scarf in cold water using a mild detergent, avoiding harsh chemicals that could damage the silk fibers. Gently squeeze out excess water and lay flat to dry on a clean towel. 

Avoid using the dryer, as the heat can shrink the fabric and cause damage. If needed, spot clean any stains with a damp cloth and mild soap before air drying.
