Invest in posters with vibrant colors and crisp details. Check for professional printing techniques like matte or gloss finishes, depending on your preferred look and room lighting. 


### Size Options

Standard poster sizes range from A3 (11.7" x 16.5") to larger options suitable for feature walls. Consider the room's dimensions and distance from the wall to determine the most impactful size. 


### Framing Requirements

Quality frames protect the poster while enhancing its appearance. Opt for acid-free mounting materials and UV protection to prevent yellowing and fading over time. 


### Installation and Maintenance

Use bubble wrap or protective plastic during transit. For indoor installations, consider the poster's backing method - some adhere directly while others use mounting clips or tapes.
