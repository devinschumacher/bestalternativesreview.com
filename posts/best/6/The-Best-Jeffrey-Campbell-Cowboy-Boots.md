### Material Quality

Look for durable materials like full-grain leather for premium boots. The quality of stitching and hardware indicates longevity. Premium boots often feature hand-stitching and nickel hardware.

Steel shanks provide arch support and prevent bending, crucial for work boots. The heel design affects stability: lower heels are more comfortable for casual wear, while taller heels offer better arch support.
