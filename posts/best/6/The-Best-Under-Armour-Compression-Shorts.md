### How long do compression shorts last?

Under Armour compression shorts, like most athletic compression garments, typically last 6-12 months with regular use. Factors affecting longevity include frequency of use, washing frequency, and quality of washing/drying methods. 

The compression effect can gradually weaken over time, but the shorts remain functional for general athletic wear. For optimal performance and durability, follow the care instructions provided with your specific model. 


### What maintenance do compression shorts require?

Machine wash these shorts using cold water and a mild detergent to preserve the compression integrity and fabric integrity. AvoidUsing hot water, which can damage the moisture-wicking properties and compression level. 

Avoid using fabric softeners or bleach, as they can deteriorate the compression material and reduce the moisture-wicking performance. Hang dry or use a low-heat setting in the dryer to maintain the compression properties.
