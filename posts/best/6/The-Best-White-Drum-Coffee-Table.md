## Buyer's Guide

A white drum coffee table pairs modern simplicity with functional appeal, making it a popular choice for contemporary living rooms. When selecting this versatile piece, consider several key factors to find one that matches your space and style preferences. 


### Size and Proportion

The table's dimensions should complement your room's scale. Calculate the length-to-width ratio of your seating area and aim for a coffee table that's between one-third to one-half the length of your sofa. Consider the room's dimensions - in smaller spaces, opt for a slightly shorter table to maintain visual openness.

### Construction Quality

### What makes a drum coffee table unique?

Drum coffee tables feature circular or oval bases that resemble a drum shape, with legs extending from the sides. This design combines visual interest with practicality, providing a unique centerpiece for any living room while offering ample storage space within the base. 

The drum shape adds a sculptural element to traditional coffee tables, making them suitable for both contemporary and eclectic interiors. They're available in various sizes to accommodate different space requirements. 


### What materials are commonly used?

White drum coffee tables often feature solid wood construction for durability and natural grain patterns. Some modern designs incorporate engineered wood or MDF with painted or stained finishes to create clean, minimalist aesthetics. 

Storage compartments typically use matching materials, sometimes with contrast colors or textures for added visual interest. Metal legs provide stability and can complement both modern and traditional styles.

### How much storage space can I expect?

The storage capacity varies by model, but typical drum coffee tables offer 15-30 cubic feet of storage space, with shelves ranging from 1 to 3 levels. Some models include built-in organizational features like adjustable shelves and concealed compartments.
