## FAQ


### What is a Rainbow Pillow?

A Rainbow Pillow is a decorative throw pillow featuring a gradient of rainbow colors that subtly transition from one shade to the next. The pillows are available in various sizes and materials, making them suitable for both indoor and outdoor use. 

The vibrant colors and soft texture make these pillows an eye-catching addition to couches, beds, or outdoor furniture, perfect for adding a pop of color to any space. 


### What makes Rainbow Pillows unique?

Unlike a standard solid color pillow, Rainbow Pillows display a gradual blend of colors that create a natural transition effect. This unique design element adds visual interest without being too loud or overwhelming. 

The pillows often feature high-quality materials that can withstand regular use and occasional exposure to sunlight, making them a durable addition to your home decor. 


### How do Rainbow Pillows coordinate with other decor?

The subtle color transitions in Rainbow Pillows make them versatile enough to complement most existing color schemes. They work particularly well with neutral backgrounds, while their vibrant colors can help balance out more subdued rooms. 

These pillows are also compatible with various design styles, from modern minimalism to eclectic boho interiors, thanks to their non-specific color palette that blends well with different aesthetic approaches. 


### Are Rainbow Pillows suitable for outdoor use?

Most Rainbow Pillows are designed to be weather-resistant, featuring durable materials that can handle occasional outdoor exposure. They're often machine washable, making them easy to clean after being used in outdoor settings. 

However, it's advisable to keep them covered during heavy rain or prolonged exposure to direct sunlight to maintain their color quality and texture. 


### How should Rainbow Pillows be cared for?

Most Rainbow Pillows can be cared for with regular washing in a washing machine using the appropriate settings for the material. Always check the care label for specific instructions regarding temperature settings and drying methods. 

The pillows should be rotated regularly to maintain even wear and tear, and any spills should be addressed promptly to prevent staining. If needed, they can be air-dried to prevent shrinkage. 


### Are Rainbow Pillows available in different sizes?

Rainbow Pillows come in various sizes to suit different needs and spaces. Common sizes include 18 inches, 20 inches, and 24 inches in diameter, though specialty sizes may be available upon request. 

These pillows work well in pairs or sets of three to create a cohesive look on your furniture, with larger sizes suitable for statement pieces and smaller sizes ideal for accent pillows.
