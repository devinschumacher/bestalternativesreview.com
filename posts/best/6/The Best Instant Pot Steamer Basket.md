## Buyer's Guide

Instant Pot steamers offer a quick, healthy way to cook vegetables, fish, and other foods by retaining their nutrients and natural flavors. Understanding the key features and considerations will help you choose the right steamer basket for your Instant Pot model. 


### Steamer Basket Design and Compatibility

Modern Instant Pot steamers feature slotted or mesh designs that allow steam to circulate freely. Pay attention to the basket's shape and size compatibility with your specific Instant Pot model. Common sizes include 1-quart and 2-quart baskets, suitable for single or multiple servings.

### Material and Durability

The steamer basket should be made from durable, corrosion-resistant material like stainless steel or specialized steaming material. Avoid plastic baskets that can warp or degrade when exposed to steam. Look for baskets with BPA-free, dishwasher-safe construction for easy cleaning and longevity. 


### Handle and Grip Safety Features

A secure, comfortable handle is crucial for lifting the hot basket. Opt for baskets with locked, securely attached handles that prevent wobble and provide a secure grip. Consider baskets with steam-dampening features to prevent steaming-hot handles, ensuring safer handling during cooking and serving.

### Cleanability and Maintenance

Choose a steamer basket that's dishwasher-safe for convenient cleaning. Alternatively, look for models with easy-to-disassemble parts for thorough washing. Some baskets feature removable steam traps or drip mechanisms that collect excess water, making cleaning more efficient and reducing steam spillage.

### What is an Instant Pot steamer basket?

The Instant Pot steamer basket is a specialized cooking accessory designed to sit inside your Instant Pot electric pressure cooker. It allows you to steam food while cooking other dishes in the main cooking chamber, or use the cooker as a dedicated steamer. 

The basket features multiple tiers that can hold different foods simultaneously, and comes with a steam-proof lid that prevents spills and maintains steam pressure. 


### What materials is it made of?

Most Instant Pot steamer baskets are made from stainless steel, which ensures durability and easy cleaning. The basket may also include a steam-proof lid made from heat-resistant plastic or silicone. 

The inner baskets often feature wire mesh construction, allowing steam to circulate while keeping liquids contained.

### What can I steam with it?

The steamer basket is versatile enough for a wide range of foods, including vegetables, fish, poultry, and even small portions of meat. You can steam everything from broccoli and rice to dumplings and steamed buns. 

Be sure to use compatible steaming bags or secure wrapping for delicate items to prevent leaks and ensure proper cooking. 


### How do I clean it?

Cleaning the steamer basket is simple - remove any food residue, wash the baskets with warm, soapy water, and rinse thoroughly. For tough residue, you can use a non-abrasive cleaner and soft brush. 

After cleaning, make sure the basket is completely dry before storing to prevent rusting and odors.
