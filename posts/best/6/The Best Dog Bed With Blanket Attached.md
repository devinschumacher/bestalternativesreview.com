### Size and Comfort Features

Start by measuring your dog from nose to tail and adding about 10 inches for the length. The bed should be at least 3 inches wider and longer than your dog's body to ensure sufficient space. Look for beds with multiple firmness options, including memory foam and zoned foam designs that provide targeted pressure relief. The attached blanket should be easily removable and machine washable for regular cleaning.
