### What is Cricut Joy?

Cricut Joy is a versatile crafting machine that combines cutting, engraving, and weeding capabilities in one compact device. It's designed for both beginners and experienced crafters who want to create detailed projects like portraits, graphics, and intricate designs. 

The machine features a user-friendly mobile app with thousands of pre-designed projects, making it accessible to users of all skill levels. It's compatible with various materials, including vinyl, paper, and cardstock, allowing for diverse crafting projects. 


### How does Cricut Joy compare to other Cricut machines?

Compared to the Cricut Explore series, Joy offers simplified controls with fewer programmable functions but includes built-in design features. It's slightly more portable and requires less maintenance than higher-end models like the Cricut Maker. 

While the base features are similar to budget-friendly options like the Cricut Play, Joy includes more advanced design capabilities through its dedicated app and better material handling.
