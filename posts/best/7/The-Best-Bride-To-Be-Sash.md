### What is a Bride to Be sash?

A Bride to Be sash is a formal piece worn by brides-to-be during important occasions such as engagement parties, wedding showers, and engagement photoshoots. It often features the couple's names, wedding date, or a romantic phrase. 

The sash typically measures 4-6 inches in width and is worn around the shoulders or across the chest, secured with adjustable velcro straps. It comes in various designs, from elegant lace and satin to fun and playful options, allowing couples to choose a style that matches their personality and the event's theme. 


### What should I wear with a Bride to Be sash?

Most Bride to Be sashes coordinate well with cocktail dresses, chic blouses, or elegant separates. Consider wearing solid-colored tops or dresses that allow the sash to be the focal point of your outfit. 

Add complementary accessories like a delicate necklace, matching earrings, or simple bracelets to complete your look. Avoid wearing heavy jewelry or competing patterns that might distract from the sash's design.

### How do I choose the right size?

Bride to Be sashes are available in standard sizes to fit most adult women (S-M, M-L, L-XL). Measure your bust or shoulder width to determine your size, and ensure the sash has adjustable straps for a perfect fit. 

If you fall between sizes or have an unusual body shape, consider ordering a larger size and using the adjustable straps to create the proper fit. The sash should hang smoothly across your shoulders without bunching or sagging. 


### How should I care for my Bride to Be sash?

The care requirements vary by material. Most sashes made from satin, velvet, or cotton can be hand washed in cool water using a gentle detergent. Gently reshape the sash while it's wet and lay it flat to dry. 

Avoid machine washing or drying, as this can damage the fabric and affect the sash's shape. Store the sash loosely rolled in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight to maintain its condition.
