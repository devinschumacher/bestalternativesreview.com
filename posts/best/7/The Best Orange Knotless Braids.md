### What exactly are Orange Knotless Braids?

Orange Knotless Braids are a type of hair extension that provides a seamless, natural-looking enhancement without the bulkiness of traditional braids. These extensions are made from high-quality synthetic hair and are designed to be attached to your natural hair using a special, knotless technique that eliminates visible knots and tangles. 

The braids are typically orange in color, but the synthetic hair can be dyed to match any hair color. Installation usually requires professional application to maintain the knotless look and ensure proper adhesion to your natural hair. 


### How does the knotless technique work?

The knotless technique used in Orange Knotless Braids involves creating small sections of hair and attaching synthetic braids in a way that eliminates traditional knots. This is achieved by using specialized tools that slide under the braid and natural hair, creating a smooth transition between the two. 

During installation, the stylist carefully wraps the synthetic braid around your natural hair, then uses a tool to push the free end under the braid and natural hair, effectively hiding the connection point. This method creates a seamless, continuous strand of hair that feels as though it's part of your natural hair.

### How long do Orange Knotless Braids last?

Orange Knotless Braids typically last between 4-6 weeks with proper care. The lifespan can vary based on how frequently you wash your hair, the quality of the product, and how well you maintain them. 

Regular maintenance includes gentle cleaning with a knotless braid-specific shampoo and conditioner, as well as regular inspection for any signs of wear or looseness. If properly cared for, these braids can maintain their appearance and quality for several weeks before needing to be reapplied. 


### What's the difference between knotless and traditional braids?

Knotless braids are a specific technique that eliminates the visible knots typically found in traditional braiding methods. This results in a more natural, seamless look that's less likely to snag or tangle. 

Traditional braids involve creating knots between sections of hair, which can be more noticeable and prone to tangling. The knotless technique requires more precise application and specialized tools, making it generally more expensive but offering a more natural appearance.

### Are Orange Knotless Braids suitable for all hair types?

Orange Knotless Braids can work with most hair types, including fine, medium, and thick hair. However, extremely curly or coarser hair may need additional consideration for proper application and maintenance. 

The braids are designed to attach to both straight and curly hair types, though results can vary. For very curly hair, a stylist may recommend extra steps to ensure proper adhesion and reduce the risk of braids coming loose. 


### Can Orange Knotless Braids be removed at home?

While some knotless braids can be removed at home, professional removal is generally recommended for the best results. Professional removal ensures that your natural hair is properly cleaned and any necessary regrowth is addressed. 

At-home removal can be attempted using specialized tools, but it's important to follow proper techniques to avoid damaging your natural hair. If you decide to remove the braids yourself, thoroughly clean the area and check for any signs of hair loss or irritation before regrowing new hair.
