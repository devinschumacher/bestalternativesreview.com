### How does a Konus Scope work?

The lens utilizes a complex optical formula that incorporates multiple concave mirror elements. These mirrors reflect light in a radial pattern, creating a series of distorted reflections that converge at the focal point of the lens. The result is a photographic image that appears to radiate outward from a central point, with symmetrical patterns emerging from any reflective surface the lens encounters. 

Users can achieve different effects by adjusting the lens's position relative to the reflective surface and the camera. The distance between the lens and the reflective object, as well as the angle of incidence, significantly influence the resulting image, offering vast possibilities for creative experimentation.
