### How do I measure my index finger correctly?

To measure accurately, wrap a piece of string or ribbon around your finger at the base, where your wedding ring typically sits. Use a ruler to measure the string's length, ensuring it's loose enough to slide on but not too snug. 

Index fingers are usually the smallest, so measure in millimeters to get precise sizing. Consider getting two sizes if you have wider joints, as your finger may be slightly different sizes.
