### What's the typical setup process like?

Setting up the Launchpad irons requires basic measurement tools like a protractor and square. Players or their instructors typically start by measuring their current lie angle, then adjust the club to match. The face angle adjustment is done by sliding a small wedge between the hosel and clubface to the desired position. 

Once the base setup is complete, players can make minor tweaks based on their specific swing characteristics. The process takes about 15-20 minutes per club and should be done with the intended golf ball and grip in place.
