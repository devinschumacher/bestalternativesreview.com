### What are turkey tendons and why are they suitable for dogs?

Turkey tendons are strong, connective tissues from a turkey's legs. They make excellent chew treats for dogs due to their natural toughness, which keeps dogs engaged for longer periods. The texture and shape of turkey tendons provide dental benefits by helping to remove plaque and tartar while chewing. 

These treats are also safe for dogs of all sizes and activity levels, making them a practical addition to your pet's snack rotation. The natural ingredients mean they're free from artificial preservatives, flavors, and colors. 


### What are the nutritional benefits of turkey tendon snacks?

Turkey tendons offer several nutritional advantages. They are rich in protein, providing essential amino acids that support muscle development and overall health. The connective tissue structure also contains glucosamine and chondroitin, which may help maintain joint health and reduce inflammation. 

These treats are low in fat and calories, making them suitable for regular consumption as part of a balanced diet. The natural composition provides vitamins and minerals beneficial for canine health without the additives found in some commercial treats. 


### How should I introduce turkey tendons to my dog?

When introducing turkey tendons, start with shorter chews (1-2 inches) to prevent choking hazards, especially for smaller dogs or those new to this type of treat. Supervise your dog initially to ensure they use the chew safely and appropriately. 

Monitor your dog's reaction to the new treat. If they show any signs of discomfort or digestive upset, discontinue use and consult your veterinarian. If your dog enjoys the treat, gradually increase the length of chews as they become more comfortable with the texture and size. 


### How long do turkey tendon snacks last?

When stored properly in a cool, dry place, fresh turkey tendons can last up to 3 weeks unopened. Once opened, it's important to refrigerate them and consume within 7 days for optimal freshness and safety. 

Freeze-dried turkey tendons typically have a longer shelf life, lasting up to 6 months unopened and 2-3 weeks once opened, when stored in the refrigerator. Always check for signs of spoilage before offering them to your dog, such as unusual odor, changes in texture, or mold growth. 


### Are turkey tendons suitable for all dogs?

Turkey tendons are generally safe for most dogs, but it's important to consider individual factors. Dogs with dental issues should be cautious, and very small breeds may need shorter chews to prevent swallowing problems. Dogs with liver disease should avoid high-protein treats like turkey tendons. 

Before introducing turkey tendons, particularly if your dog has health concerns or dietary restrictions, it's advisable to consult with your veterinarian to ensure these treats are appropriate for your pet's specific needs.
