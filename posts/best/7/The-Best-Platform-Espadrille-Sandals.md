## Buyer's Guide

Espadrille sandals blend casual comfort with elegant style, making them a popular choice for both men and women. These platform versions add extra height while maintaining their traditional open-toe design. When selecting platform espadrille sandals, consider several key factors to find a pair that matches your lifestyle and preferences. 


### Heel Height and Stability

Platform height ranges from low (1-2 inches) to high (3-4 inches). While higher platforms offer more vertical lift, they may affect stability. Most people are comfortable with 2-3 inch heels. If you're new to elevated footwear, start with lower platforms and gradually adjust.

### Material Quality and Construction

The upper materials can include canvas, linen, or synthetic options, with natural materials often providing better breathability. The espadrille sole combines a canvas or mesh base with a jute rope midsole. Higher quality constructions use thicker jute and more durable stitching. Consider how the materials feel against your skin and how they'll withstand regular wear. 


### Footbed Design and Padding

Espadrilles typically have minimal footbeds, which can be uncomfortable for some wearers. Look for models with extra padding under the arch or a slightly raised heel to improve comfort. If you have wide feet or high arches, check the width options available and consider trying on shoes before purchasing.

### Color and Versatility

### What are platform espadrille sandals?

Platform espadrille sandals combine the casual elegance of traditional espadrilles with elevated height, typically ranging from 2 to 4 inches. These sandals feature a flexible canvas or textile upper with natural jute rope soles, giving them their signature look and comfort. 

Key characteristics include breathable canvas uppers, sturdy jute soles, and adjustable ankle straps for support. The platform design adds height while maintaining the lightness of espadrilles, making them suitable for both casual and slightly dressed-up occasions. 


### What materials are used in platform espadrille sandals?

These sandals primarily feature natural materials to maintain their laid-back coastal aesthetic. The uppers are constructed from breathable canvas or lightweight cotton, providing air circulation and reducing moisture buildup. 

The outsoles are made from durable jute rope, which is both flexible and supportive. Some designs incorporate leather or suede elements for texture and durability, while others incorporate synthetic materials for increased water resistance.

### How should I care for my platform espadrille sandals?

To maintain the quality and appearance of your platform espadrille sandals, regular care is essential. Clean them using a soft brush or cloth to remove dirt and dust, and allow them to air dry completely before wearing again. 

For deeper cleaning, use a mild soap solution on the canvas or textile parts, avoiding the jute soles, which can be damaged by excessive moisture. Store your espadrilles in a cool, dry place when not in use to prevent damage from humidity and heat. 


### Can women with wide feet wear platform espadrille sandals?

Many platform espadrille brands offer wider fits to accommodate various foot shapes. It's important to measure your foot and compare it to the manufacturer's sizing chart before purchasing. 

Wider options often include additional room in the toe box and across the arch, enhancing comfort for feet of all shapes and sizes. Consider trying on both narrow and wide styles to determine which fits you best.

Platform espadrille sandals work well for both casual and slightly more formal occasions. They're ideal for outdoor events, beach weddings, and casual date nights, providing a touch of elevated comfort to your outfit.
