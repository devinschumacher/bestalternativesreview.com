## FAQ


### What makes Ralph Lauren's lamps unique?

The design aesthetic ranges from contemporary to classic, with both modern interpretations and traditional chandeliers available. Their signature collection includes floor lamps with distinctive cylindrical legs and table lamps featuring sleek, curved forms. 


### How do I choose the right size lamp?

Measure the room's height and length, then add these dimensions together. This total should match the lamp's height and width combined. For floor lamps, allow 3-4 inches of clearance from walls. 

Consider the scale of your furniture, with floor lamps working best in spacious rooms and table lamps suitable for side tables and nightstands. The lamp should complement the room's overall dimensions without overwhelming the space. 


### What types of light bulbs are compatible?

Most Ralph Lauren lamps accept standard A19 or A21 bulbs, though specific models may have specialized requirements. Always check the lamp's specifications to ensure compatibility with your desired bulb type. 

For decorative purposes, they recommend using LED bulbs in warm tones (2700-3000K) to maintain the lamps' intended color temperature. LED bulbs also offer longer lifespans and energy efficiency compared to traditional incandescent options. 


### Can I modify the lamp's finish?

The manufacturer advises against DIY modification of the lamp's finish to maintain its warranty and preserve the product's value. Any alterations should be performed by a qualified professional or through an authorized Ralph Lauren retailer. 

For those seeking different finishes, consider professional refinishing services that specialize in matching original colors. This approach allows for consistent results while maintaining the lamp's integrity. 


### How do I care for the lamp?

Clean dust regularly using a soft cloth, avoiding harsh chemicals that could damage the finish. For wood surfaces, use a mild wood polish appropriate for the lamp's specific material type. 

When handling the lamp, support it from both bottom and top to prevent damage. If the lamp has decorative elements like glass shades or crystal prisms, exercise extra care to avoid scratches or breakage. 


### What's the difference between modern and traditional styles?

Modern Ralph Lauren lamps feature clean lines, streamlined shapes, and minimal ornamentation. These designs often incorporate materials like metal, glass, and sleek wood finishes, with a focus on contemporary aesthetics. 

Traditional lamps, on the other hand, embrace rich wood grains, ornate detailing, and classic shapes inspired by historical designs. These pieces often feature hand-turned legs, intricate base designs, and deeper, more saturated finishes.
