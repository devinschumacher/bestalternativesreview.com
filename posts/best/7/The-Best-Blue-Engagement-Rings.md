### What should I consider when choosing a blue gemstone's cut?

Blue stones benefit from deep cuts that maximize their color brilliance. The ideal cut depends on the specific stone and setting. Blue sapphires and tanzanites excel in three-stone or stepped cuts, while topaz and aquamarine benefit from oval or pear shapes. 

Consider the stone's natural color depth - if the stone has strong color saturation, a deeper cut can enhance its brilliance. If color is more subtle, a medium-depth cut balances brilliance and color visibility.
