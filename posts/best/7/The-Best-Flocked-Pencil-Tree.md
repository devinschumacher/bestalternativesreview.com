## FAQ


### What is a flocked pencil tree?

A flocked pencil tree is a small Christmas tree made from pencil-shaped branches, with white flocking applied to create a snowy appearance. These trees typically stand between 1 and 2 feet tall and are ideal for desks, shelves, or small spaces. 

Unlike traditional trees, they don't require a stand or decorations, making them easy to maintain. The compact size and modern design make them suitable for both casual and formal spaces. 


### What sizes are available?

Most flocked pencil trees range from 12 to 24 inches tall, with some larger options available up to 30 inches. The most common size is approximately 18 inches, offering a good balance of height and portability. 

Size matters based on intended placement: smaller trees work well on desks or dressers, while larger ones can serve as a centerpiece for a small console table. 


### What materials are used?

The branches are made from flexible plastic or PVC, giving them a natural pencil appearance while ensuring durability. The white flocking is applied to create a realistic snowy effect, which can be easily brushed off if needed. 

These trees come in two primary color options: white and silver. Silver trees offer a modern twist with their metallic sheen. 


### Are they suitable for all occasions?

These trees work well year-round, making them suitable for various occasions. They can serve as Christmas tree alternatives, holiday decorations, or simply bring a touch of greenery to your space. 

Despite being called "pencil trees, " they can enhance any room's aesthetic, including offices, bedrooms, or living rooms. 


### How should they be maintained?

Regular maintenance involves dusting the branches with a soft cloth or brush. The flocking can be gently brushed off if it becomes dislodged, and the tree can be stored in its original package when not in use. 

For the best results, keep the tree away from direct heat sources and ensure it is adequately supported, as the plastic branches can become slightly flexible. 


### Are they safe for pets and children?

These trees pose no safety hazards due to their sturdy plastic construction and small size. The flocking is non-toxic and can be easily removed if a pet or child plays with it. 

While they're generally safe, always supervise children and pets to prevent accidental damage or ingestion of small flock particles.
