## FAQ


### What notes are included in the Viktor Rolf fragrance?

Viktor Rolf's signature scents feature a distinctive blend of violet leaf, ambrette seed, and ozonic top notes. The heart notes include violet, ambrette, and pepper, while the base notes introduce patchouli and musk. 

Each fragrance maintains Rolf's signature approach to modern perfumery, combining unconventional notes in innovative ways. 


### How has Viktor Rolf influenced modern perfumery?

Viktor Rolf revolutionized perfumery in 1979 with the release of Women, known for its groundbreaking violet leaf top note. This scent established Rolf as a pioneer in creating unconventional and modern fragrances. 

His subsequent creations, including Body Double and Power, continued to push the boundaries of traditional scent composition, influencing thousands of perfumes that followed. 


### What makes Viktor Rolf fragrances unique?

Each Viktor Rolf fragrance features a distinctive combination of notes that challenge conventional scent categories. The company's approach focuses on innovation rather than traditional gender-based fragrance formulas. 

Rolf's modernist philosophy and use of unconventional ingredients continue to set his fragrances apart in the competitive perfume market. 


### How should Viktor Rolf perfumes be stored?

Like all perfumes, Viktor Rolf fragrances should be stored in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat sources. This helps preserve the longevity and integrity of the scent. 

Air travel should be approached with caution; keep the bottle sealed and store it in a protective case to prevent damage during transit. 


### What is the typical shelf life of Viktor Rolf perfume?

Viktor Rolf perfumes generally maintain their quality for 3-5 years when stored properly. However, the integrity of the scent may gradually diminish over time. 

Opening the perfume bottle accelerates this process, so it's important to use the product regularly to prevent premature degradation of the fragrance. 


### Are Viktor Rolf perfumes suitable for all skin types?

Viktor Rolf fragrances contain complex ingredient combinations that may not suit everyone's skin type. However, the company's formulas are designed to be long-lasting and memorable. 

If you have sensitive skin or a known fragrance allergy, it's advisable to perform a patch test before committing to a full-size bottle.
