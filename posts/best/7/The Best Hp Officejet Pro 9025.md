### Network Connectivity

### Power Consumption and Maintenance

When comparing models, note the 9025's power consumption of 16.5 watts in standby mode and 66.0 watts during printing. Regular maintenance includes replacing the toner cartridge every 3,000 to 4,000 pages and cleaning the printhead to maintain print quality.

### What are the recommended ink cartridges?

For best performance, use original HP cartridges in black (CN045A) and color (CN046A). These are compatible with the printer's standard and high yield options.
