The pop socket's design helps prevent screen fatigue by allowing the phone to be held away from the body while still being securely in view. This versatile accessory can be flipped up to shield the screen from direct sunlight or accidental bumps. 


### What types of phone cases have pop sockets?

Pop socket accessories are compatible with both phone cases and smartphones without cases. They attach directly to the phone's back or the case's back panel, supporting various case types including clear, protective, and slim designs. 

The pop socket's universal design allows it to work with most phone models, from compact smartphones to larger devices. However, the case or phone must have a flat back surface for proper attachment. 


### How do I install a pop socket phone case?

Installation is straightforward. First, position the pop socket's clip on the back of your phone or case. Press firmly to lock it in place. The spring mechanism should extend automatically, securing the clip's legs perpendicular to the phone surface. 

For optimal stability, align the clip's legs with your dominant hand when holding the phone. This positioning helps prevent accidental pops or clicks during use. The socket can be easily adjusted by pressing it back into the phone to change the clip's angle. 


### How strong is a pop socket's grip?

A pop socket's grip strength varies based on the material and design of your phone or case. When properly attached, the socket holds the phone securely in place, allowing for stable one-handed use and screen support. 

The spring mechanism provides consistent pressure, preventing the phone from slipping. However, the grip can be affected by phone weight, case material, and surface texture. For best results, attach the socket to a flat, unblemished surface on your phone or case back.
