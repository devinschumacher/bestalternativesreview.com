### How should I maintain and care for my Michael Kors travel bag?

Proper maintenance extends the life of your Michael Kors travel bag. Clean the exterior with a damp cloth and mild soap, avoiding harsh chemicals that may damage the surface. For water-resistant canvas bags, periodically treat the surface with a water-repellent solution. 

Store the bag in a cool, dry place when not in use, and protect it from direct sunlight. For water-resistant models, keep the zippers closed and store the bag with some air circulation to prevent moisture buildup.
