### What are Celsius Packets?

Calpet's Celsius Packets are specialized hydration salts designed to help maintain proper electrolyte balance during physical activity. These packets replace essential minerals like sodium, potassium, and magnesium lost through sweat. 

The formula helps prevent hyponatremia, or electrolyte imbalance, which can occur during prolonged exercise or in hot conditions. Each packet is mixed with water to create a sports drink that aids in optimal performance and recovery. 


### How should I use Celsius Packets?

Follow the recommended serving size of one packet per 20 ounces of water (approximately 590 ml). Mix thoroughly before consumption. The packets should be dissolved completely to avoid gritty mouthfeel during consumption. 

Use during workouts or competitions lasting more than one hour, especially in hot conditions. Replenish every 20-30 minutes of intense exercise to maintain proper electrolyte levels.

### What if I miss a dose?

If you miss a scheduled dose, consume the packet as soon as possible during your exercise session. Do not double up the next time, as excess electrolytes can be harmful. 

In hot conditions or during prolonged activity (over 90 minutes), consider maintaining a consistent electrolyte intake rather than attempting to "catch up" on missed packets. 


### Are Celsius Packets suitable for everyone?

While generally safe, some individuals may be sensitive to high sodium content. If you have cardiovascular issues or high blood pressure, consult a healthcare provider before use. 

Avoid use if you experience swelling, confusion, or nausea, as these can be symptoms of hyponatremia. Always follow the "better safe than sorry" approach when managing your hydration and electrolyte intake.

### What are the benefits of Celsius Packets?

Proper electrolyte balance leads to better performance by ensuring optimal fluid absorption and muscle function during exercise. This can enhance endurance and reduce the risk of cramping or fatigue. 

The product helps prevent common issues like "exercise-associated collapse" and nausea that can occur when electrolyte levels become imbalanced during activity. 


### Are there any side effects?

The most common side effects are associated with incorrect usage, such as flavor issues from undissolved packets or stomach upset from excessive intake. Most people experience no issues when following the recommended guidelines. 

Be aware of the potential for gastrointestinal discomfort with rapid consumption or excessively diluted solution. The primary concern is hyponatremia risk, which can be managed by following the product instructions carefully.
