### What are Freestyle Lite Test Strips?

Freestyle Lite Test Strips are diagnostic test devices designed to measure blood glucose levels in individuals with diabetes. They work in conjunction with the Abbott Freestyle Lite Blood Glucose Monitoring System to provide accurate and reliable test results. 

These test strips are small, single-use cartridges that contain the necessary reagents to react with glucose in a blood sample, producing a measurable response that is then converted into a glucose concentration reading. 


### How accurate are the Freestyle Lite Test Strips?

The Freestyle Lite Test Strips have been clinically validated for accuracy in measuring glucose levels across a wide range of concentrations, from 40 mg/dL to 600 mg/dL. The system is designed to provide consistent results within the specified range. 

While minor variations in readings can occur due to factors such as blood volume and technique, the Abbott Freestyle Lite system maintains a proven track record of reliable performance when used as directed.

### Are Freestyle Lite Test Strips suitable for all diabetes types?

### What is the recommended storage temperature for Freestyle Lite Test Strips?

The Freestyle Lite Test Strips should be stored between 36°F and 86°F (2°C to 30°C) to maintain their optimal performance. They should not be frozen or exposed to extreme heat or humidity. 

Once the test strips have been removed from their protective packaging, they should be used promptly to prevent degradation of the reagents.
