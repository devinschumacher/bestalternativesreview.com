## FAQ


### What are lint roller refills?

Lint roller refills are replacement adhesive sheets that attach to the handle of a lint roller, allowing users to remove excess hair, pet fur, and debris from surfaces. These refills extend the lifespan of lint rollers and reduce waste compared to disposable alternatives. 

Each refill typically consists of a thin, adhesive sheet that can be applied to the roller's surface using a built-in applicator. The adhesive helps capture hair and debris, making it easy to remove unwanted particles from clothing, furniture, and pet grooming. 


### How do lint roller refills work?

The roller consists of two main components: the handle and the adhesive sheet. The handle houses the applicator mechanism, while the adhesive sheet is designed to collect hair and debris. When pressed against a surface, the rotating motion of the roller picks up loose particles. 

To use a refill, peel back the protective backing and press it firmly onto the roller's surface. The adhesive will activate and secure the sheet in place. The roller can then be rotated to remove hair and debris, which will be trapped between the adhesive sheet and the roller surface. 


### Which surfaces work best with lint roller refills?

Lint roller refills effectively remove hair and debris from various surfaces, including clothing, upholstery, car interiors, and pet grooming brushes. The adhesive strength helps capture fine particles that regular cleaning methods might miss. 

For best results, use the refills on soft, porous surfaces like fabrics and carpets. They work less effectively on hard, non-porous surfaces like glass or metal, where particles may not adhere as well. 


### How long do lint roller refills last?

Lint roller refills typically last between 1-3 months with regular use, depending on the frequency of use and the amount of hair or debris collected. Factors like environmental conditions (humidity, temperature) and usage intensity (number of surfaces cleaned) can impact their longevity. 

Users often find that a single refill can clean multiple surfaces before needing replacement. To determine if a refill needs replacing, check for visible hair buildup or a decrease in cleaning effectiveness. 


### Can lint roller refills be reused?

Most lint roller refills cannot be reused once the adhesive has picked up hair and debris. The primary purpose of the adhesive is to trap particles, which deactivates its effectiveness for future use. 

Some advanced refills feature multiple layers that can be peeled off and reused. However, these are less common and typically require the inner layers to be completely exposed and clean before reuse. 


### What's the best way to dispose of lint roller refills?

Lint roller refills should be disposed of properly to prevent environmental waste. Rinse off any loose particles before disposal to minimize contamination. 

When disposing, use regular trash bins or recycling facilities that accept office paper. Avoid composting or outdoor burning, as the adhesive might be harmful to plants and wildlife.
