### What is a solar wall light?

Solar wall lights are outdoor lighting fixtures that harness solar energy to charge rechargeable batteries during the day. This stored energy powers LED lights at night, providing illumination without the need for electrical wiring. 

These fixtures are installed on walls or attached to wall-mounted brackets, making them ideal for landscape lighting, pathway illumination, and decorative accents. 


### How do solar wall lights work?

Solar wall lights contain solar panels that convert sunlight into electrical energy. This energy charges internal batteries, which store the power for use after sunset. At night, LED lights powered by the stored energy light up automatically when it gets dark. 

Some models include motion sensors to further conserve energy by only illuminating when someone or something passes by in the illuminated area.

### What features should I look for in solar wall lights?

Consider the lighting range, which typically varies from 15 to 100 feet. Look for lights with at least 300 lumens for adequate brightness. IP ratings indicate water resistance - an IP65 rating is suitable for occasional outdoor use, while IP67 or IP68 are better for wet conditions. 

Check the battery capacity in watt-hours and the runtime in hours of continuous illumination. Some models offer adjustable brightness levels, remote control options, or built-in movement sensors. 


### How long do solar wall lights last?

Performance varies by brand and manufacturer, but most solar wall lights last between 3-5 years with regular use. The solar panels typically maintain 70-80% efficiency after 10 years of exposure to sunlight. 

Rechargeable batteries usually last 3-5 years before requiring replacement. Environmental factors, such as shading from trees or buildings, can significantly impact system efficiency and lifespan.

### What maintenance do solar wall lights require?

Regular maintenance includes checking for loose connections, replacing batteries when necessary, and cleaning the solar panels to maintain optimal energy conversion. The panels should be free of dirt, debris, and objects that cast shadows. 

Inspect the lights and mounting hardware for signs of damage or corrosion, especially in coastal or humid environments. Secure all connections to prevent electrical issues and ensure the lights remain firmly attached to the wall. 


### How much do solar wall lights cost?

Solar wall light kits typically range from $20 to $100 per fixture, depending on brand, features, and quantity purchased. Installation costs vary based on labor, materials, and local regulations. 

Higher-priced models often offer better energy efficiency, longer runtime, and additional features like motion sensing or remote control. Consider the cost per year of operation compared to electricity or traditional battery-powered lighting to determine overall value.
