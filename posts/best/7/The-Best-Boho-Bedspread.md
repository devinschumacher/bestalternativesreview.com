## FAQ

### What is a boho bedspread?

A boho bedspread combines earthy tones, natural elements, and intricate patterns to create a warm, inviting centerpiece for your bedding. It often features bold prints like florals, geometrics, and tribal designs, along with textures such as fringe, embroidery, and crochet. 

These spreads typically blend multiple colors and materials to create visual interest, making them a versatile addition to any boho-chic bedroom. 


### What makes boho bedspreads unique?

Boho bedspreads stand out with their handcrafted quality and cultural influences. Many feature traditional patterns from around the world, while others incorporate modern designs with a bohemian twist. The natural materials and layered textures give each spread a distinct, handmade look. 

Their generous size (usually king or queen) allows for creative layering and makes them ideal for adding warmth and texture to your bedding. 

![Boho-Bedspread-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Boho-Bedspread-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How should I choose the right boho bedspread?

Selecting a boho bedspread involves considering the room's existing color scheme and personal style. Earthy tones complement neutral walls and furniture, while bold prints can serve as a statement piece in a more modern bedroom. 

Size and scale are also important. Ensure the spread matches your bed dimensions and doesn't overpower the room. A good rule of thumb is to choose a spread that extends 12-18 inches beyond each side of your mattress. 


### What care instructions should I follow?

Most boho bedspreads require similar care to traditional bedding. Machine wash them using gentle settings and mild detergent. Avoid bleach and harsh chemicals that could damage the fibers. 

Air drying is best to maintain the spread's shape and texture. If you need to use a dryer, use low heat and remove the spread promptly to prevent wrinkling. 

![Boho-Bedspread-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Boho-Bedspread-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### How do boho bedspreads affect room temperature?

Boho bedspreads typically add warmth to a bedroom due to their thickness and natural materials. They trap air close to your body, making them excellent for cooler months or drafty rooms. 

However, they can make a room feel stuffy if used in conjunction with other heavy bedding. Opt for lighter fabrics like cotton or linen for warmer weather and reserve thick boho spreads for cooler seasons. 


### Can boho bedspreads be used year-round?

While boho bedspreads excel in cooler months, they can be adapted for summer with proper layering. Use them as a topper over a lightweight sheet or pair with a breathable cotton comforter. 

For year-round use, consider seasonal rotation. Start with a thinner boho spread in spring, switch to a warmer option in fall, and remove it completely in summer for a lighter bedding setup.
