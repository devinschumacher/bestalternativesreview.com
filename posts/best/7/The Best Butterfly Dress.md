The dress material significantly influences its look and feel. Chiffon offers a romantic, ethereal appearance while being breathable and suitable for warm weather. Key decorative features include the bodice design, skirt silhouette, and any added trimmings. Common bodice styles range from simple sheaths to more structured corset designs. Butterfly dresses often require careful sizing to achieve the desired look. The waist measurement is crucial for creating the proper fit and gathering.
