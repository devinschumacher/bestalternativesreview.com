## FAQ


### What is an air hockey table?

Think of it as a high-tech version of the classic arcade game, but with a table surface and faster-paced action. The air hockey table uses powerful fans to create an air cushion that allows the pucks to glide smoothly, making for intense, fast-moving gameplay. 

These tables typically feature a regulation-size playing surface with raised borders that hold the air pucks in place during play. The playing field usually includes grooved channels and scoring slots to add to the game's strategy and challenge. 


### What's the difference between a regular air hockey table and a pro model?

Regular air hockey tables offer basic functionality with standard play elements, while pro models incorporate advanced features like improved puck guidance systems, faster response times, and more durable construction materials. 

Pro models often include additional gaming modes, enhanced graphics displays, and better maintenance systems to ensure consistent performance during intense matches. 


### How much space do I need for an air hockey table?

Most air hockey tables require a minimum playing surface of 5 feet by 3 feet, plus additional space for clearance around the edges. Ensure you have at least 6 feet of clearance on all sides for safety and proper gameplay. 

Consider the full dimensions of the table and its weight when planning your installation area, especially if you're setting it up in a common living space like a family room or gaming area. 


### What maintenance do air hockey tables need?

Regular maintenance includes cleaning the playing surface and fans, checking the air flow system, and testing all game features to ensure proper functionality. This helps prevent gameplay disruptions and extends the table's lifespan. 

Deep cleaning every few months should include removing the playing surface, inspecting for damage, and applying protective coatings as needed. The table's components, including the puck guidance channels and scoring mechanisms, should also be regularly checked and cleaned. 


### Are air hockey tables difficult to maintain?

With regular care, air hockey tables are generally straightforward to maintain. Basic cleaning and inspection routines can be performed in a few minutes daily, while deeper cleaning sessions every few months handle more extensive maintenance needs. 

Most maintenance requirements focus on keeping the playing surface clean and the air flow system functioning properly. Following the manufacturer's recommended maintenance schedule ensures your table remains in top condition. 


### Can air hockey tables be played by beginners?

While air hockey requires some skill to master, most tables are designed with adjustable difficulty settings that make it accessible for beginners. Many models offer practice modes and training features to help new players develop their technique. 

With regular practice and attention to basic strategies, beginners can enjoy competitive matches while developing their skills. Most air hockey tables balance accessibility with enough challenge to keep experienced players engaged.
