### What's the difference between indoor and outdoor units?

Indoor unit processes air and contains the blower motor, heat exchanger, and filter. Outdoor unit houses compressor, motor, and condenser coil, responsible for refrigerant processing. 

While both use refrigerant and electricity, the outdoor unit is exposed to the elements and requires more maintenance. The indoor unit is primarily filter maintenance.

### How do I choose between split-system and window unit?

Split-systems offer better room coverage, quieter operation, and modern aesthetics, making them suitable for both new construction and retrofitting. 

Window units are cheaper upfront and install more quickly, ideal for temporary cooling needs or rentals. Consider space constraints, room size, and desired installation speed. 


### How long do Midea air conditioners last?

With regular maintenance, indoor components typically last 10-15 years. Outdoor units, exposed to the elements, usually last 8-12 years. 

Signs of aging include reduced cooling efficiency, frequent repairs, and unusual noises. Regular check-ups and prompt maintenance extend lifespan.
