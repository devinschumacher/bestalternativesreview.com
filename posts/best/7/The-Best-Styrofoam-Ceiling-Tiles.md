## Buyer's Guide

Styrofoam ceiling tiles offer a cost-effective way to enhance room insulation, acoustics, and appearance. These lightweight tiles provide a simple DIY solution for ceiling renovation projects. 


### Insulation and Sound Absorption

Styrofoam ceiling tiles excel in insulation, offering an R-value of approximately 3.5 per inch. This quality helps maintain consistent room temperatures while reducing energy costs. The closed-cell foam structure also effectively absorbs airborne sound, making it suitable for both residential and commercial applications.
