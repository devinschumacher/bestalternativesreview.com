### How do I determine my ideal stability level?

Observe your natural foot strike pattern: runners who land with the heel hitting first and then pronating (rolling inward) moderately are likely to benefit from stability shoes. 

Contact a local running store for a gait analysis, which can help determine the appropriate stability level and specific model needed for your running style. 


### Are Brooks stability shoes suitable for all foot types?

These shoes work well for high arches due to their midfoot support, making them versatile for different arch shapes. However, runners with very high arches may need additional arch support.
