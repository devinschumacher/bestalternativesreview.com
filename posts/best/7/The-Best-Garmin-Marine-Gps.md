### What are the mounting options for Garmin marine GPS units?

Garmin marine GPS units typically require either a bracket mount or a through-hull mount. ### Which Garmin marine GPS units support integrated sonar capabilities?

Garmin marine GPS units that support integrated sonar include the M90, M90R, M100, and M100R. These models feature built-in CHIRP sonar technology for improved underwater imaging and fish finding capabilities. 

The sonar feature requires compatible Garmin fish finder transducers, which can be attached to the unit's dedicated plug. These transducers transmit sound waves that bounce off underwater objects, sending back data for display on the unit's screen.
