### Understanding the Brand

Inis offers a range of fragrances, each with its own character and composition. Familiarizing yourself with the brand's approach to scent development can help guide your selection process. 


### Identifying Your Scent Preference

Scent families (like floral, woody, or oriental) can influence your choice. Consider what notes attract you, whether it's sweet florals, earthy woods, or spicy blends. 


### Understanding Scent Characteristics

Important elements include top notes (the initial burst of fragrance), middle notes (the heart of the scent), and base notes (long-lasting depth). These layers create the overall character of a perfume. 


### Evaluating Sillage and Projection

Sillage refers to how far a scent travels, while projection indicates how strongly it's perceived. Consider your needs for daily wear versus special occasions when selecting your fragrance.
