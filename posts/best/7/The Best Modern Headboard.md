### How do modern headboards differ from traditional designs?

Modern headboards emphasize clean lines, simple forms, and a lack of ornate detailing. They often feature geometric shapes, minimal carvings, or a smooth, unadorned surface. 

Traditional headboards incorporate rich textures, carved embellishments, and intricate woodwork. They may also include elements like upholstered panels, nailhead trim, or ornamental motifs. 


### What care requirements do modern headboard materials have?

Materials like solid wood and engineered wood may require regular dusting and occasional polishing to maintain their finish. Consider protective coatings or sealer recommendations from the manufacturer. 

Metals need periodic cleaning to remove dust and prevent corrosion. Leather and vinyl headboards typically require simple cleaning with appropriate products and regular maintenance to preserve their appearance.
