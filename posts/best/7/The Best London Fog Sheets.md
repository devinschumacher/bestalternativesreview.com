### What are London Fog Sheets?

London Fog Sheets are premium window films designed to replicate the classic London fog effect. These transparent films create a hazy, slightly blurry appearance when applied to glass windows, giving your home an authentic urban look. 

The sheets come in various shades, from soft smudges to deep, dramatic grays, allowing you to customize the intensity of the fog effect to match your preferences. 


### How do London Fog Sheets work?

These window films use specialized pigmentation that selectively filters light passing through glass. This filtration creates a slight diffraction effect, making the glass appear hazy while maintaining transparency. 

The films also offer UV protection, helping to block harmful sun rays and reduce glare while giving your windows their distinctive fogged appearance. 

![London-Fog-Sheets-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/London-Fog-Sheets-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What surfaces can I apply London Fog Sheets to?

The films are compatible with both single-pane and insulated glass windows, including those in doors and skylights. They work best on clear glass surfaces with minimal existing coatings or treatments. 

For best results, ensure your windows are clean and free of obstructions before application. The films may not adhere properly to surfaces with significant existing treatments or coatings. 


### How do I install London Fog Sheets?

Installation typically requires a DIY approach, with the films available in roll form for precise cutting and application. Professional installation is recommended for windows larger than 4x4 feet or those requiring specialized handling. 

The process involves measuring, cutting, and carefully positioning the film on the glass surface, often using specialized tools to ensure proper adhesion and reduce bubbles.

### How long do London Fog Sheets last?

With proper installation and maintenance, London Fog Sheets can last 5-7 years, depending on sun exposure and cleaning frequency. The films are resistant to fading from UV light but may show slight color degradation over time. 

Regular cleaning with appropriate window cleaners and avoidance of harsh chemicals can help maintain the film's appearance and extend its lifespan. 


### Are London Fog Sheets easy to clean?

These films are generally easy to maintain, requiring only routine cleaning with standard window cleaners and a squeegee. The hazy appearance remains consistent even after regular cleaning. 

Avoid abrasive cleaners or harsh scrubbing, as these can damage the film's surface texture and affect its appearance. Proper maintenance helps preserve the film's clarity and fogged effect.
