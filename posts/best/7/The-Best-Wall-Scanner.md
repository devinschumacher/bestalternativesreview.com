### How does a wall scanner work?

Wall scanners capture images using high-quality cameras and specialized software. Many models feature motorized arms that extend and move across the wall surface, ensuring consistent coverage and minimizing human error. 

The captured images are then processed in software that can stitch them together into a complete map of the wall surface. This software often provides tools for measurement, annotation, and further image manipulation. 


### What surfaces can a wall scanner image?

Modern wall scanners can image a wide variety of surfaces, including drywall, brick, concrete, and plaster. Some models are specifically designed for curved surfaces or intricate architectural details.
