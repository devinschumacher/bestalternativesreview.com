Look for bags constructed from high-quality materials like nylon or canvas with reinforced stitching at stress points. ### Size and Spaciousness

Consider the dimensions that work best for your travel style. Standard sizes include 20", 24", and 28" in checked luggage, with slim carry-ons for cabin travel. Check airline size restrictions and ensure the bag fits under seat counterparts or in overhead bins. 

Internal organization can significantly impact a bag's usefulness. Look for multiple compartments, dedicated laptop sleeves, and zippered pockets for easy access to travel documents and electronics. 


### Security Features

Many modern Tommy Hilfiger luggage pieces include TSA-approved lock options for secure checked baggage. Consider bags with concealed locks that don't compromise the bag's appearance or convenience. 

Tag management systems can prevent misrouting. Look for cases with integrated baggage tags or built-in loops for attaching additional tags. 


### Warranty and Customer Support

A quality luggage warranty indicates the manufacturer's confidence in their product. While specific terms vary, most reputable luggage brands offer at least a one-year warranty on workmanship and materials. 

Customer support availability can affect your long-term satisfaction with the product. Consider brands that offer repair services or direct support channels for addressing warranty claims or service needs.
