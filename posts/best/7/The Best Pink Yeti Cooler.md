## FAQ


### What is a Yeti cooler, and why is it pink?

A Yeti cooler is a premium insulated container designed for keeping beverages and food fresh for extended periods. The pink color, while distinctive, has no impact on the cooler's performance - it's simply a choice of aesthetics that stands out in any crowd. 

The interior is made from medical-grade HDPE plastic that's resistant to cracks and chips, while the exterior features a durable polyethylene blend that protects against scratches and dings. All models come standard with the Yeti Tundra thermal coating for optimal insulation. 


### How does the Yeti Tundra thermal coating work?

The Tundra coating forms a microscopic layer of air between the inner shell and outer layer of the cooler. This trapped air creates a natural barrier that slows down heat transfer, keeping contents cold for up to 40% longer than similar coolers without this technology. 

The coating also prevents condensation from forming on the outside of the cooler, keeping it dry to the touch in any weather conditions. This feature alone makes the Tundra coating invaluable during outdoor activities like camping or tailgating. 


### What sizes are available, and how do I choose the right one?

Yeti offers a variety of sizes, from the small 15-quart cooler up to their massive 80-quart model. Consider how many people you'll be serving and what types of food and drinks you'll be storing. The most popular sizes among consumers are the 20-quart and 45-quart models, which balance portability with ample storage. 

Think about the weight you're willing to carry, as larger models can get quite heavy. Also, consider the specific features each size offers - some models have built-in drink carriers or ice retention innovations that might be worth the extra weight in certain situations. 


### Can I put hot items in the Yeti cooler?

While Yeti coolers excel at keeping cold items fresh, they're not designed for hot food or beverages. The Tundra thermal coating is optimized for cold retention and doesn't provide adequate insulation for maintaining heat. Attempting to use it as a warming container risks damaging the cooler and compromising its temperature control capabilities. 

For reheating or serving hot foods, consider getting a separate Yeti cooker or using a microwave-safe container outside the cooler. This approach ensures your food stays safely at the correct serving temperature while keeping your cold items fresh. 


### How long does a Yeti cooler last, and what's the warranty?

With proper care, a Yeti cooler can last 10+ years, though most warranties only cover the first 5 years. The company backs their products with a limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects and failures in materials or workmanship. Premium models may offer extended warranties for an additional fee. 

To maintain your warranty coverage, keep the original receipts and follow the manufacturer's care instructions. Properly storing your cooler, cleaning it regularly, and using protective covers when not in use will significantly extend its lifespan and keep your warranty valid. 


### How do I clean and maintain my Yeti cooler?

Clean your Yeti cooler after each use by hand with warm water and mild dish soap. For stubborn stains, you can use a 50:50 mixture of water and white vinegar. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners, as they can damage the Tundra coating and interfere with future sealing capabilities. 

When not in use, store the cooler in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Use the included protective covers when traveling to prevent scratches and maintain the cooler's appearance. Regular maintenance not only keeps your cooler looking great but ensures it continues to perform at its best for years to come.
