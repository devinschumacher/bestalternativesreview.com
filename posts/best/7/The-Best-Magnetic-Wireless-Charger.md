### What is a magnetic wireless charger?

A magnetic wireless charger is a device that uses magnetic induction to transfer power between the charger and a compatible smartphone or device. Unlike traditional wireless chargers that require precise alignment, magnetic chargers maintain a constant connection without needing to be placed exactly over a specific spot. 

The technology works by creating an electromagnetic field that the device's built-in receiver picks up, converting it back into usable power. This method ensures that even if your phone moves slightly while charging, you won't lose power transfer efficiency. 


### Which devices are compatible with magnetic wireless chargers?

Most smartphones with wireless charging capabilities can be used with magnetic chargers, including models from Samsung, Apple, and Motorola. However, compatibility depends on the specific model and year of your device. 

Many magnetic chargers include multiple connection points on their pads or stands to accommodate different device sizes, from small smartphones to tablets.
