### What is a battery operated tea light?

These lights are popular for creating a soft, welcoming glow in home decor, outdoor gatherings, and event settings. They're particularly useful in environments where open flames are prohibited or inconvenient. 


### What are the main advantages of battery operated tea lights?

Battery operated tea lights offer several advantages over traditional candles. They produce no heat, making them completely safe to use in any environment, including near flammable materials, fabrics, or electronic devices. The lights are also resistant to wind and weather, allowing them to stay lit even in outdoor settings. 

Maintaining these lights is simple - just replace the batteries when they lose their charge, and they're ready to use again. The lack of open flame reduces the risk of burns and fire hazards, making them a practical choice for homes with children or pets.

### How long do battery operated tea lights typically last?

Most battery operated tea lights have an LED bulb that lasts between 6-12 hours on a single charge, depending on the specific model and quality of the LED. The batteries themselves usually provide about 100-200 hours of usage before needing replacement. 

When not in use, it's recommended to remove the batteries to conserve power. This simple step can extend the life of the batteries and prevent leakage that might affect the metal contacts. 


### What types of batteries are compatible with battery operated tea lights?

These lights typically use standard AAA, AA, or CR2 batteries, which are widely available and easy to find. Some models may use AAAA batteries, so it's important to check the specific requirements before purchasing. 

Always use genuine replacement batteries or high-quality equivalent brands to ensure consistent performance and longevity of your tea light. Using damaged or incompatible batteries can reduce light output and potentially damage the LED bulb.

### Can battery operated tea lights be used outdoors?

Many battery operated tea lights are specifically designed for outdoor use and can withstand exposure to wind, rain, and sunlight. These outdoor-safe models feature waterproof or weather-resistant enclosures to protect the LED and battery components.

### How should battery operated tea lights be stored?

To extend the life of your tea lights and maintain optimal performance, store them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight when not in use. Keep them in their original packaging or in a sealed container to protect the batteries from moisture.
