### Are pink tool boxes safe for outdoor storage?

Outdoor storage requires weather-resistant boxes with protective coatings against rust and UV damage. Opt for models specifically marked for outdoor use, which often feature reinforced corners and water-tight seals. 

To extend the box's lifespan, store it on a level surface away from direct sunlight and extreme temperature changes. Consider additional protection with outdoor-rated covers or storage sheds.

### How can I keep my pink tool box organized?

Organize tools by category using partitions, dividers, or compartmentalized containers. Label each section clearly to maintain order during storage and retrieval. 

Regular maintenance keeps your tools and box in good condition. Clean and dry tools before storage, and check the box for damage or wear regularly to address issues promptly.

Standard sizes range from 12 to 30 inches in length, with heights between 8 and 20 inches and widths from 6 to 12 inches. These measurements accommodate common hand tools and basic household equipment.

### How much does a pink tool box weigh?
