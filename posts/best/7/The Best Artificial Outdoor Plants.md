### What are artificial outdoor plants made from?

### How do artificial outdoor plants compare to real plants?

While real plants offer natural beauty and air purification, artificial plants require no maintenance, water, or soil. However, they don't produce oxygen or support local wildlife, making them ideal for settings where natural plants struggle. 

Artificial plants consume 75% less water than live plants and never need pruning, making them a practical choice for busy homeowners or those with limited gardening skills.

### What maintenance is needed for artificial outdoor plants?

These plants require minimal care beyond occasional dusting with a soft brush or cloth. Periodic inspections for loose parts or faded areas help maintain their appearance. 

During harsh winter conditions, the framework should be checked for snow buildup and debris that might damage the structure. Occasional shampooing with mild soap and water removes heavy soiling. 


### How long do artificial outdoor plants last?

With proper care, artificial outdoor plants typically maintain their appearance for 8-10 years. High-quality brands promise durability through weather cycling and resist fading from UV exposure. 

After this period, some components may show signs of wear, but the plants can often be refreshed through cleaning and minor repairs. Replacing occasional damaged pieces maintains their aesthetic appeal.
