## Buyer's Guide


### Materials and Construction

The quality of materials significantly affects a sandal's durability and comfort. Leather uppers provide lasting style while breathable mesh keeps feet cool. Quality straps and stitching ensure the sandal remains secure and intact through regular wear.

### Fit and Comfort Features

Proper fit prevents slipping and ensures all-day wearability. Look for adjustable straps and wide ankle openings. The heel height impacts stability, with moderate heights (2-3 inches) offering a balance of style and comfort. Consider any built-in arch support or cushioning that enhances foot comfort during wear.
