### Spill Prevention

Air release valves or pressure relief mechanisms help prevent leaks by allowing trapped air to escape as you fill or empty the bottle. This maintains a consistent seal and reduces the risk of spills during movement. 


### Threaded vs. Click Lock

Standard threaded lids require you to screw them tightly by hand, while click locks engage with a distinct click when properly sealed. Consider which method offers better security for your needs - click locks are often more convenient but may not seal as tightly as manual threading.
