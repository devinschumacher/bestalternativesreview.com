212 Cologne is a popular fragrance that combines modern appeal with recognizable scent notes. Understanding the key aspects of this fragrance will help you make an informed decision when purchasing it.

212 Cologne opens with fresh, zesty top notes that include Mandarin Orange and Bergamot. These initial scents create an energetic, uplifting start to the fragrance. 


### Heart Notes

The heart notes contain a blend of Bergamot, Neroli, and Cardamon. This layer adds depth and complexity, with hints of spiciness and citrus sweetness. 


### Base Notes

The base notes of 212 Cologne incorporate Vanilla, Tonka Bean, and Musk, providing warmth and longevity to the fragrance. These elements work together to create a sensual, modern scent profile. 


### Application and Longevity

For best results, apply 212 Cologne to pulse points like the wrists, neck, and behind the ears. Reapply every 3-4 hours for lasting fragrance. The scent typically lasts 6-8 hours on most skin types.
