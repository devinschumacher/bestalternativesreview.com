### What is The Pink Stuff Cleaner, and how does it work?

The Pink Stuff Cleaner is a specialized cleaning solution designed to break down and remove particularly stubborn stains from clothing and fabrics. Unlike traditional cleaning products, it works through a process called encapsulation, where it surrounds and encapsulates dirt and stains, making them easier to wash away. 

The cleaner is effective against a wide range of stains, including oil, grease, and red wine. It's particularly useful for items that haven't been treated immediately or thoroughly enough with regular detergents. 


### What types of fabrics and materials is The Pink Stuff Cleaner suitable for?

The Pink Stuff Cleaner is generally safe for most fabrics, including cotton, polyester, and blends. However, it's important to test the product on a small, hidden area of the fabric first to ensure it doesn't cause any color bleeding or damage. 

Avoid using the cleaner on silk, wool, or other delicate fabrics unless you're certain it's suitable. For these materials, it's recommended to use a specialized cleaner or consult with a professional cleaner.

### How should I use The Pink Stuff Cleaner?

Saturate the stained area with The Pink Stuff Cleaner, applying enough to cover the stain completely. Allow it to sit on the stain for at least 5 minutes before normal washing. This gives the cleaner's encapsulating properties time to work effectively. 

Wash the item in a regular wash cycle using your usual detergent. The encapsulated particles will break down during washing, making the stain easier to remove. 


### How many times can I use The Pink Stuff Cleaner before it needs replacing?

The Pink Stuff Cleaner typically has a shelf life of 12-18 months when stored properly. Each bottle usually contains enough solution for multiple applications depending on the size and severity of the stains you're treating. 

If you notice the solution's effectiveness diminishing or the solution gets contaminated, it's time to replace the bottle.

### Does The Pink Stuff Cleaner bleach colors or damage fabrics?

The Pink Stuff Cleaner is not designed to bleach colors or damage fabrics. It works by encapsulating stains rather than chemically breaking them down, which makes it safer for most fabrics. 

However, if you notice color bleeding or changes after using the cleaner, it's important to test the product on a small, hidden area first or consult with a professional cleaner. 


### What should I do if The Pink Stuff Cleaner doesn't remove the stain?
