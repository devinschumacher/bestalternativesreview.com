## FAQ


### What is Valentino Uomo Intense?

Valentino Uomo Intense is a powerfully masculine fragrance that captures the intensity and energy of the modern man. Released in 2017, it's the second iteration of the Uomo line, designed to reflect the wearer's strength andconfidence. 

The fragrance combines top notes of lemon, bergamot, and black pepper with a heart of juniper, cumin, and nutmeg. The base features patchouli, tonka bean, and vetiver, creating a warm, sensual foundation that lingers throughout the day. 


### Who is Valentino Uomo Intense suitable for?

Designed for the contemporary man who exudes confidence and leadership, Valentino Uomo Intense suits professionals, entrepreneurs, and individuals who want to make a strong impression. Its bold, sophisticated scent works well for both daytime and evening wear. 

The fragrance's intensity might be particularly appealing to men who prefer powerful, aromatic scents over lighter, more subtle options. While universally appealing, its strongest appeal may lie with those who appreciate bold, assertive fragrances. 


### How can I test the fragrance before purchasing?

Valentino typically offers at least 30-day return policies on fragrances, allowing you to test the scent at home before committing to a purchase. Many customers also turn to online sample kits or travel-sized versions for an extended preview. 

When testing, apply the fragrance to pulse points like the wrist and behind the ears, as these areas hold the scent longer and give a more authentic impression of how the fragrance will perform throughout the day. 


### How long does Valentino Uomo Intense last?

Valentino Uomo Intense has excellent staying power, with its top notes fading in 2-3 hours and its heart notes lasting 4-6 hours. The base notes continue to develop and project for up to 12 hours, making it a strong all-day fragrance. 

The intensity may vary based on skin type and environmental factors. Some wearers find it lasts longer than others, especially in cooler temperatures or when applied more sparingly. 


### What is the best way to apply Valentino Uomo Intense?

Apply Valentino Uomo Intense to pulse points such as the wrists, neck, and behind the ears. These areas have higher concentrations of blood flow, which helps the fragrance diffuse more slowly and release its notes gradually. 

Consider applying a small amount to your wrist and then gently rubbing your hands together to warm the fragrance, as this can enhance its projection and longevity. 


### Is Valentino Uomo Intense suitable for everyday wear?

Valentino Uomo Intense's bold character makes it more suitable for occasional wear rather than daily use. Its intensity might be overwhelming in more formal settings or for personal use at home. 

Pair it with a classic suit or darker wardrobe pieces for a strong, sophisticated presence in professional environments. For everyday wear, consider layering with a lighter scent or using it sparingly in key areas.
