## Buyer's Guide

A white and gold coffee table brings a touch of elegance and warmth to any living room, combining classic elements with modern appeal. When selecting the perfect piece, consider several key factors to ensure it meets both your aesthetic preferences and practical needs. 


### Visual Impact and Proportion

The size of your coffee table should complement the scale of your room and other furniture.
