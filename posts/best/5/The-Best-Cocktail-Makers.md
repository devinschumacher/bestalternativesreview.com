## FAQ

![cocktail-makers-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/cocktail-makers-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What is the most accurate way to measure spirits for cocktails?

Most cocktail recipes require precise measurements of spirits. The most accurate method is using a jigger, which typically comes in 1.5-ounce (45ml) and double-ounce (90ml) sizes for standard cocktail measurements. 

Alternatively, digital scales provide precise measurements for ingredients, useful when mixing drinks with specific nutritional requirements or adjusting recipes for large-scale events. 


### How long after mixing can cocktails be served?

Most mixed cocktails can be served immediately after mixing, especially those that don't contain fresh ingredients that may oxidize or lose flavor. For drinks with fresh ingredients, serve within 15-30 minutes to maintain peak flavor. 

The exception is tropical and fruity cocktails with fresh fruit pulp, which should be served immediately after mixing to prevent oxidation of the fruit components. 

![cocktail-makers-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/cocktail-makers-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What is the difference between a shaker and a blender?

A shaker is used to mix spirits with liquids and is ideal for creating classic cocktails with a chilled and well-blended finish. It consists of two parts - a cylindrical container and a tight-fitting lid with a built-in strainer. 

A blender, on the other hand, combines all ingredients into a puree, which is typically served in a glass with ice for immediate drinking. It's commonly used for tropical cocktails, daiquiris, and other blended drinks. 


### Can shaken cocktails be blended for a smoother finish?

While a shaken cocktail's base is already well-mixed, blending can help further break down ice particles and create a smoother consistency, especially for drinks with fruit purees or complex ingredients. 

However, blending can dilute the spirits more than shaking alone, so use this technique sparingly and adjust your ice quantities accordingly. 

![cocktail-makers-6](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/cocktail-makers-6/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What are the key components of an electric cocktail maker?

Electric cocktail makers typically feature a built-in blender base with interchangeable components for different drink sizes, adjustable ice crush settings, and specialized mixing paddles for various drink types. 

Some models also include built-in shakers, blending cones, and temperature control features for precise drink chilling. 


### How should cocktail makers be cleaned to maintain hygiene?

Regular cleaning includes running each component through your dishwasher's sanitize cycle. Always disassemble parts completely to reach all crevices. 

For deeper cleaning, soak parts in a solution of baking soda and water, then wash thoroughly with mild dish soap and a soft brush. Rinse completely before reassembly.
