### What are cluster eyelash extensions?

Cluster eyelash extensions are a type of eyelash enhancement where multiple individual extensions are applied to one natural lash. This technique creates fuller, more voluminous lashes without the need for multiple single extensions per lid. 

The extensions typically measure between 1.5-2.5 inches in length and are applied to the natural lash for added length and thickness. The clustering technique allows for various degrees of fullness, from light volume to dramatic looks. 


### How long do cluster extensions last?

Clustering the extensions can slightly affect their longevity compared to single lash extensions. On average, cluster extensions last between 3-4 weeks with proper care. Regular touch-ups every 2-3 weeks are recommended to maintain the desired fullness and appearance. 

Factors that can affect longevity include frequency of touch-ups, lash health, and adherence to aftercare instructions. Maintaining good hygiene and avoiding excessive touching or tugging helps extend the life of the extensions.

### Are cluster extensions safe for all lash types?

Cluster extensions are suitable for most lash types, including thicker or longer natural lashes. The technique works best on healthy lashes with sufficient natural volume. Lashes that are too fine or sparse may not hold extensions as well. 

Individuals with sensitive eyes or a history of eyelash or eye problems should consult with a licensed lash technician before proceeding with the service. A professional assessment can determine if cluster extensions are appropriate for your specific lash condition. 


### How long does it take to apply cluster extensions?

The application process for cluster eyelash extensions typically takes between 1.5-2.5 hours, depending on the desired fullness and the technician's speed. This time includes cleaning and shaping the natural lashes before application. 

The extended application time allows for precise placement of multiple extensions per natural lash, ensuring even fullness and natural-looking results. Rushing the process can compromise the quality and longevity of the extensions.

### What maintenance is required for cluster extensions?

Maintaining cluster eyelash extensions requires regular professional touch-ups every 2-3 weeks to maintain the desired fullness and shape. This involves cleaning and re-attaching any loose extensions. 

Between touch-ups, daily maintenance includes removing makeup carefully around the lash lines, avoiding excessive eye makeup removal products, and not curling or tugging at the lashes. Regular lash grooming at home helps maintain the appearance and integrity of the extensions.
