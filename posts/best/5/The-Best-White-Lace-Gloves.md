### What care instructions should I follow for white lace gloves?

White lace gloves typically require special care to maintain their appearance and lace integrity. Hand washing in cold water with mild detergent is recommended, and let them air dry flat without forcing the shape. 

Avoid using bleach or harsh detergents, and resist the temptation to use a dryer, as heat and tumbling can damage the lace and affect the gloves' shape. Store them properly to prevent tangling and maintain their form.
