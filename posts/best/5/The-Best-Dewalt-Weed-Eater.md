A straight-shaft Dewalt weed eater has a vertical handle that runs from the motor to the head of the tool. This design is ideal for cutting high weeds and overgrowth, as it allows direct pressure on the handle for stability and control. 

Curved-shaft models feature a handle that angles from the motor to the head, making them more comfortable for users with reduced arm reach. The curved design also helps prevent operator fatigue during prolonged use, particularly when working in tight spaces.
