## FAQ


### What is a Scrub Daddy soap dispenser?

A Scrub Daddy soap dispenser is an innovative cleaning solution that combines a high-quality foam technology with a reusable, washable scrubber pad. The dispenser creates a rich, moisturizing foam that cuts through dirt and grime effectively while reducing the amount of soap needed. 

These dispensers come in various sizes and styles to suit different needs, from travel-friendly options to large-capacity models for home use. The scrubbers are made from natural materials and can be machine washed for repeated use. 


### How does it work?

The Scrub Daddy soap dispenser uses a unique foam technology that mixes air with the soap to create a rich lather. Unlike traditional soap dispensers, you don't need to shake or pump the dispenser before use – simply wet the scrubber pad and squeeze the dispenser to release the foam. 

The scrubber pad can then be applied directly to surfaces or used to wash your hands or dishes. The foam adheres well to the scrubber, ensuring efficient cleaning with minimal soap usage. 


### What materials are used to make the scrubbers?

The scrubbers are made from a blend of natural materials including cellulose (plant-based material) and other biodegradable components. These materials are designed to be tough and durable while being safe for household use. 

Each scrubber has tiny bristles that help remove dirt and grime while the foam technology ensures thorough cleaning with less soap. The scrubbers are also gentle on hands, making them ideal for everyday use in the kitchen and bathroom. 


### How do I clean and maintain the scrubber?

After each use, simply rinse the scrubber under running water and store it in the dispenser. For deeper cleaning, remove the scrubber from the dispenser, place it in the sink, and scrub it with a little soap and water. Rinse thoroughly and allow it to air dry completely before reinserting it into the dispenser. 

Regular maintenance includes checking for any signs of wear or damage. Replace the scrubber when it shows signs of fraying, thinning, or loss of effectiveness to ensure optimal cleaning performance. 


### Can I use it with liquid or bar soap?

The Scrub Daddy soap dispenser is compatible with both liquid and bar soap. For liquid soap, fill the dispenser as directed, making sure to leave enough space for the foam to form. For bar soap, cut the bar into small pieces and place them in the top of the dispenser. 

Most users find that using a combination of liquid soap and the built-in foaming agent produces the best results. However, you can use either type of soap effectively with your Scrub Daddy dispenser. 


### How long does it last?

With proper care, a Scrub Daddy soap dispenser can last for years. The dispenser itself is made from durable plastic that resists cracking and warping. The reusable scrubber pad typically lasts 3-6 months with regular use, depending on how often you wash your hands or dishes. 

The extended lifespan of the system comes from reduced soap consumption and the ability to wash the scrubber multiple times. This makes the dispenser a cost-effective alternative to single-use cleaning solutions.
