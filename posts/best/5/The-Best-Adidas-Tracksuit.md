The fit of your tracksuit should balance comfort with proper movement. A standard fit offers standard sizing with room for layering, while a modern fit is more streamlined and form-fitting. ### How do you care for an Adidas tracksuit?

Machine wash the tracksuit using a mild detergent in cold or lukewarm water. Turn it inside out to protect printed graphics and avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as these can damage the materials. 

Air dry the tracksuit flat away from direct heat sources like radiators or sunlight. For faster drying, shake out the garment before hanging it up. Avoid using a dryer, as high heat can compromise the elasticity of the fabric.
