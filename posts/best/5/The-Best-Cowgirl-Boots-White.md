### What makes cowgirl boots white?

White cowgirl boots typically feature bleached leather or synthetic materials that maintain their color through proper care. Some boots use natural white leather, while others incorporate white panels or stitching for visual interest. 

Manufacturers may achieve white color through dyeing processes, but genuine white leather maintains its appearance better over time with regular maintenance. Synthetic white materials often require less maintenance and are more resistant to fading. 


### How do I care for white cowgirl boots?

White cowgirl boots require regular cleaning to maintain their appearance. Use a soft-bristled brush and mild soap to remove dirt and grime. For tougher stains, apply a white-specific cleaning solution and gently scrub. 

After cleaning, allow the boots to air dry naturally. Avoid exposing them to direct sunlight or heat sources, as this can cause discoloration. Store them in a cool, dry place to prevent fading and maintain their white color.
