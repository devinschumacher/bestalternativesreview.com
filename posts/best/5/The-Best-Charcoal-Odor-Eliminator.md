## FAQ


### What is a charcoal odor eliminator?

A charcoal odor eliminator is a device that uses activated charcoal to absorb and neutralize unpleasant odors. The activated charcoal has a high surface area that allows it to trap particles and gases responsible for odors. 

These products come in various forms, including charcoal odor absorbers, odor eliminators, and air fresheners. They are commonly used in vehicles, homes, and offices to reduce unwanted smells. 


### How effective are charcoal odor eliminators?

Charcoal odor eliminators are effective for most common household and vehicle odors, including pet smells, cigarette smoke, and moldy odors. However, they may not eliminate strong or persistent odors completely. 

The effectiveness varies based on factors like the product's size, the type of activated charcoal used, and the size of the area it's treating. Regular replacement or movement of the activated charcoal product ensures continuous odor control. 


### What types of odor molecules do charcoal odor eliminators remove?

Activated charcoal primarily removes volatile organic compounds (VOCs), which are often responsible for unpleasant odors. It can also trap odorous particles like dust, pollen, and tobacco residue. 

The charcoal's iodine adsorption capacity indicates its odor-removing ability, with higher capacities indicating better performance. However, it's less effective against inorganic compounds and some specific odors like cooking smells or chemical odors. 


### How long do charcoal odor eliminators last?

A typical activated charcoal odor eliminator lasts between 3-6 months with regular use, depending on the product's size and the environment's humidity and temperature. Once the charcoal loses its effectiveness, it will no longer adsorb odors. 

Product placement in well-ventilated areas and proper maintenance, such as regular replacement or resetting (for self-resetting models), can extend their operational life. 


### Can charcoal odor eliminators be reused?

Most activated charcoal odor eliminators require replacement after 3-6 months. However, some products feature self-resetting mechanisms that allow the charcoal to regain its adsorption capacity after a period of activation. 

For models that can be reset, this process typically involves removing the charcoal from the device, allowing it to dry completely, and then replacing it. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for proper resetting procedures. 


### Are charcoal odor eliminators safe for pets and humans?

Activated charcoal odor eliminators are generally safe for pets and humans when used as directed. The charcoal particles are large enough to prevent inhalation or ingestion risks. 

However, keep the products out of reach of pets and children, and place them in appropriate locations to avoid accidental contact. Some individuals may experience skin irritation if exposed to large amounts of charcoal dust, so use in well-ventilated areas.
