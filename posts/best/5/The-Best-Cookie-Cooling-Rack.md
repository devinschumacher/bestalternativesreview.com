## FAQ


### What is a cookie cooling rack?

A cookie cooling rack is a kitchen tool designed to help cookies cool safely and maintain their shape. Unlike baking sheets, which retain heat and can cause cookies to spread, cooling racks allow air circulation underneath the cookies. 

These racks typically feature wire mesh or sturdy wire frames with multiple tiers. The wire construction prevents heat transfer between cookies and the surface, while the elevated design promotes even cooling. 


### Why use a cooling rack instead of just letting cookies cool on the baking sheet?

When cookies cool directly on the baking sheet, residual heat from the oven causes them to continue baking and potentially spread. This can result in soggy centers and undesirable shapes. 

A cooling rack allows cookies to cool completely and maintain their intended shape and texture, preventing them from becoming soggy or misshapen. 


### How do I properly store cookie cooling racks?

Clean racks should be stored in a dry place, ideally in their original packaging or a dedicated storage container. This protects the wire structure and prevents the racks from tangling with other baking utensils. 

If space is limited, wire racks can be stored with the wire elements compressed together. Ensure they're completely dry before stacking to prevent rust. 


### What materials are cooling racks typically made from?

The most common materials for cookie cooling racks are wire and metal. Wire racks are lightweight and allow for excellent airflow, making them ideal for cooling freshly baked goods. 

Some premium racks use stainless steel for durability, while others incorporate BPA-free plastic for oven-safe storage containers. Aluminum is also used, though it may not be as durable as other materials. 


### How should I care for my cookie cooling rack?

Cooling racks should be hand washed with mild soap and water after each use. Avoid using harsh chemicals or abrasive cleaners that can damage the wire structure. 

Racks should be air-dried completely before storing to prevent rust and prolong their lifespan. If using metal racks, consider using a rust inhibitor spray occasionally for additional protection. 


### Can I use a cookie cooling rack in an oven?

Most wire cooling racks are oven-safe and can be used directly in the oven to keep cookies warm while cooling. However, always check the manufacturer's instructions for specific temperature limits. 

Some premium racks are designed with heat-resistant polymer bases for added stability in the oven. For safety, ensure the rack is completely cool before touching or cleaning.
