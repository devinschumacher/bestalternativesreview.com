### How much Diva Laundry Detergent should I use?

Start with the recommended dose of 1-2 tablespoons per load, depending on the soil level of your laundry and water hardness. This amount provides excellent cleaning power without overloading the washing machine. 

If you're using heavily soiled clothes or a very dirty load, you can increase to 3 tablespoons. For lightly soiled loads or small loads, the 1-tablespoon amount is sufficient. Always check the lid of your detergent container for specific instructions. 


### Can I use Diva Laundry Detergent in high-efficiency (HE) washers?

Yes, Diva Laundry Detergent is formulated to work effectively in both standard and high-efficiency washers. For HE machines, measure the detergent directly into the dispenser before adding clothes, as these machines require pre-measured doses. 

If you're transitioning from a standard washer to an HE, you may need to adjust your detergent amount based on the manufacturer's recommendations for your specific washer model.

### Does Diva Laundry Detergent leave residue in clothes?

Because of its natural formula with no harsh chemicals, Diva Laundry Detergent rarely leaves residue in clothes. The plant-based cleaners rinse out easily, and the absence of artificial fragrances means there are no lingering chemicals that can affect fabric quality. 

If you notice any residue after washing, it's likely due to hard water or excessive detergent use. Reducing the amount of detergent or using a water softener can help prevent residue buildup. 


### Can I use Diva Laundry Detergent with fabric softeners?

Yes, you can use Diva Laundry Detergent with fabric softeners if you prefer the additional moisture-adding properties of softeners.
