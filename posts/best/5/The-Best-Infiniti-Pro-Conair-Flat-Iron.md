### Ceramic vs. Ionic Technology

Infiniti Pro Conair flat irons utilize either ceramic or ionic technology. Ceramic plates distribute heat evenly, reducing frizz and promoting hydration. Ionic technology releases negative ions that neutralize positive hair charges, minimizing frizz further. Consider your hair type and styling goals to determine which feature is most beneficial. 


### Temperature Settings and Heating Options

The temperature range on an Infiniti Pro Conair flat iron affects styling versatility. Professional models often offer multiple temperature settings to accommodate different hair types and styles. Some versions include quick-heat options that reach optimal temperature faster, ideal for busy mornings. 


### Plate Width and Separation

Plate width determines the amount of hair that can be styled at once. Standard plates measure between 1-1.5 inches, suitable for most hair types. Separation distance affects the flat iron's glide and coverage. Closer separation is ideal for fine hair to prevent tangling, while wider separation works well for thicker hair. 


### Safety Features and Ergonomics

Professional-grade flat irons like the Infiniti Pro Conair incorporate safety features such as automatic shut-off and cool-tip mechanisms. Ergonomic handles improve grip and reduce hand fatigue during extended use. Consider these elements to ensure the flat iron remains a practical tool for regular styling sessions.
