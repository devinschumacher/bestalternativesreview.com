### What types of dinosaur birthday decorations are available?

The most common dinosaur decorations include life-size models, inflatable toys, and plush stuffed animals. Many stores also offer themed banners, tablecloths, and napkins featuring popular dinosaurs like T-Rex, Stegosaurus, and Triceratops. 

Decorations can range from simple prints and posters to elaborate room setups with multiple pieces. Consider your space and budget when selecting the right combination of decorations. 


### How should I display dinosaur decorations?

Decorate using a mix of hanging elements and standing pieces to create visual interest. Hang banners and paper decorations on walls, while larger items like statues and inflatable toys can be placed on floors or tables. 

For indoor events, use balloons and streamers to add color and fun. Outdoor events can incorporate additional elements like cardboard cutouts and photo backdrops for guests to interact with.

### What are the size options for dinosaur decorations?

Dinosaur decorations come in various sizes to suit different spaces. Smaller options include 12-24 inch models and inflatable toys, ideal for small rooms or portable displays. 

Larger pieces range from 48 inches up to life-size models, perfect for bigger spaces like living rooms or outdoor patios. Consider the room dimensions when selecting your decorations. 


### How do I care for inflatable dinosaur decorations?

Proper care for inflatable dinosaurs includes regular deflation between uses to prevent wear. Store them in a cool, dry place when not in use. 

Before each use, check for any damage or leaks. Inflate them gradually to ensure even air distribution and avoid sudden pressure changes that could damage the material.

### What materials are dinosaur decorations typically made from?

Most dinosaur decorations are made from PVC, which is durable and flexible. For inflatable toys, materials often include TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) for extra resilience. 

Plush stuffed animals and small models typically use polyester or similar materials. Check the specific product description for material details before purchasing. 


### How long do dinosaur decorations last?

Properly cared-for PVC and TPU decorations can last several years with regular use. Inflatable toys typically maintain their shape and integrity for 1-2 years with proper maintenance. 

Plush stuffed animals and smaller models usually last 3-5 years before showing signs of wear. Consider these timelines when planning for future events or multiple uses.
