Consider how easily the design elements can be maintained to factor into your purchase decision.

### How do purple leaf umbrellas compare to regular umbrellas?

### What maintenance is required for purple leaf umbrellas?

To keep your purple leaf umbrella looking its best, wipe down the frame and leaves with a damp cloth after each use. This simple cleaning removes dirt and debris while maintaining the color. 

For more thorough cleaning or tougher stains, use a mild soap solution, avoiding harsh chemicals that might damage the treated leaves. Store the umbrella indoors when not in use to protect it from extreme weather conditions.
