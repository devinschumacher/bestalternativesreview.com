### What are the key care requirements for a navy blue velvet dress?

Velvet requires gentle handling to maintain its shape and texture. The dress should be dry cleaned or hand washed in cool water using a mild detergent, then laid flat to dry. Storing it properly is crucial - consider a padded hanger with natural fibers to prevent crushing and regular vacuuming of the surface to remove dust. 

Avoid storing in humid areas or direct sunlight, which can damage the fabric's integrity. For the best results, have it professionally cleaned annually, especially before important events. 


### Can a navy blue velvet dress be worn for different occasions?

Despite its rich appearance, a navy blue velvet dress is surprisingly versatile for most formal events. It works beautifully for weddings as both a bridesmaid dress or a stylish alternative to traditional evening wear. It pairs wonderfully with both white and colored gowns for a cohesive look. 

For more casual events, consider pairing the dress with a simple pearl necklace and nude heels. Its depth of color and texture make it adaptable to various settings, from formal dinners to cocktail parties.
