### How do I measure for correct arch support?

Use a small piece of paper or a specialized arch measurement tool to place on the floor. Stand on the paper with both feet and observe where your weight is distributed. If you see distinct arches with minimal contact, you have high arches that may need less support. If you see a full footprint with only a small arch support area, you may need enhanced arch support. 

For accurate measurement, stand on a wet surface and create a footprint, then measure the distance between your heel and the ball of your foot. This measurement can help determine the arch height, but professional footwear analysis is recommended for precise arch support needs.
