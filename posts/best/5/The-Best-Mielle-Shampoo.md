Mielle shampoos contain natural ingredients like aloe vera, jojoba oil, and plant extracts. Key ingredients often include vitamins A, C, and E, along with antioxidants to nourish the scalp and hair. ### Usage

Most Mielle shampoos require working up a generous lather, massaging it into the scalp, and rinsing thoroughly. Curly hair types may benefit from using a wide-tooth comb or your fingers to detangle wet hair after washing. Avoid over-washing, which can strip natural oils, and be mindful of your hair's response to different products. 


### Scalp Considerations

Mielle products are designed for all scalp types, including sensitive scalps. Look for formulas that address specific scalp concerns like dandruff, irritation, or inflammation. If you have an existing condition, consider consulting with a dermatologist to determine the right product for your needs.
