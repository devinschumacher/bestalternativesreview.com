### What makes a high-quality cheetah blanket?

High-quality cheetah blankets combine durable materials with attention to detail. Look for blankets made from premium cotton blends that resist fading and pilling, with reinforced stitching around the edges to prevent unraveling. 

The best cheetah blankets also feature carefully placed faux fur edging, which adds warmth while maintaining the pattern's integrity. Quality control is important, so check for any loose threads or uneven stitching before purchasing. 


### How should I clean a cheetah blanket?

Cheetah blankets typically require regular spot cleaning rather than washing. Use a mild detergent and cool water to clean stubborn stains, allowing the blanket to air dry completely to prevent shrinkage. 

For routine cleaning, dust and loose dirt can be removed with a vacuum brush attachment. If the blanket needs deeper cleaning, machine wash it on a gentle cycle with like colors, using a small amount of mild detergent.

### Can I wear a cheetah blanket as a sleepshirt?

While cheetah blankets are cozy, they may not provide enough cover for overnight sleep. Most are designed to be worn with additional bedding, so consider the temperature of your room before using it as a sleepshirt. 

Men's sizes usually offer more coverage than women's, often featuring longer lengths and larger armholes. Check the measurements before purchasing to ensure proper fit for your body type. 


### What size cheetah blanket should I buy?

Standard cheetah blankets range from 52" x 66" to 72" x 84", with women's sizes typically smaller than men's. For a suitable sleepshirt, look for a blanket approximately 60" to 72" long, depending on your preference for coverage. 

Consider the blanket's weight and material when choosing a size. Lighter blankets may be more appropriate for warm rooms, while heavier options are ideal for cooler temperatures or as an additional layer.

### How do I style a cheetah blanket?

Cheetah blankets work well in both casual and dressy settings. As a sleepshirt, tuck it into pajama bottoms for a cozy look. Over regular clothing, pair it with loose pants and a simple top for a casual daytime outfit. 

Add texture to your living space by positioning a cheetah blanket on a couch or bed. Fold it neatly when not in use to maintain its appearance, or drape it casually for an informal look. 


### Are cheetah blankets machine washable?

The care requirements for cheetah blankets vary depending on their construction. Most can be machine washed on a gentle cycle with mild detergent and cold water to prevent fading and shrinkage. 

Check the care label for specific instructions, as some may require hand washing or dry cleaning. Always test for colorfastness before washing, especially with patterned blankets where color bleeding could occur.
