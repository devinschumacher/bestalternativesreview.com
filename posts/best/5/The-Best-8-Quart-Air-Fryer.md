Additional features to consider include digital displays, pre-programmed cooking settings, and easy-to-read controls. Some models also offer functions like air frying, baking, roasting, and dehydrating, expanding their culinary capabilities. 


### How long does an 8-quart air fryer take to heat up?

Most 8-quart air fryers reach their cooking temperature in about 3-5 minutes, depending on the specific model and current room temperature. Preheating time may vary slightly based on these factors.
