Our guide covers everything from moisture-wicking fabrics to reinforced stitching, helping you choose uniforms that stay fresh and look great game after game. 


### Material Quality

Made from breathable, moisture-wicking fabrics, the latest Giants uniforms draw sweat away from your body to keep you dry. High-quality materials also prevent pilling and maintain their shape wash after wash. 


### Stitching and Construction

Look for uniforms with reinforced stitching around key stress points like seams and hems. Professionally-grade thread and double-stitched seams ensure your uniform lasts through multiple washes and wears. 


### Bleach and Care

Skip the bleach entirely when cleaning your uniform. Instead, use mild detergent and cold water to maintain the quality of the materials. For best results, air dry your uniform rather than using a dryer. 


### Size and Fit

Measure your torso and hips to find the right size, as uniforms can run small. Order based on your natural measurements rather than loose-fitting clothes you might typically wear.
