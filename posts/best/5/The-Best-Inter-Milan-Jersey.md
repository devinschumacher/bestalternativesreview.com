## Buyer's Guide

Choosing the right Inter Milan jersey is more than just selecting a favorite color or design. This comprehensive guide will help you understand the key features, materials, and sizing options to consider when purchasing an official Inter Milan jersey. 


### Official vs. Authentic Jerseys

While both official and authentic jerseys carry the Inter Milan brand, official jerseys are produced directly by the club, while authentic jerseys come from licensed manufacturers. Official jerseys often include unique features like microtags for authenticity verification. 


### Match vs. Training Jersey

Match jerseys typically feature premium materials for moisture-wicking and breathability, while training jerseys prioritize flexibility and quick-drying properties. Consider your intended use - match, training, or casual wear - to select the appropriate jersey type. 


### Sizing and Fit

Inter Milan offers multiple fit options including Regular, Half-Round, and Slim Fit. Regular fit matches traditional sizing, Half-Round provides a slightly more relaxed fit, and Slim Fit offers a tailored look. Always check the specific measurements for each fit type to ensure the best fit. 


### Materials and Construction

High-quality Inter Milan jerseys feature moisture-wicking fabrics to keep you dry, while premium models incorporate stretchy four-way stretch for enhanced mobility. Consider the weight and thickness of the material based on your playing position and climate conditions. 


## FAQ


### What colors are available for Inter Milan jerseys?

Inter Milan primarily offers the squad version in classic blue and white. The official club shop also stocks alternate kits in green and black, plus various match-worn jerseys in different color schemes throughout the seasons. 

Note that color options may vary based on the specific jersey type (home, away, third, etc. ) and the print run. Always check the product description for the exact color range of the particular jersey model. 


### Who designs Inter Milan's jerseys?

Since 1998, Italian fashion brand Puma has been the official uniform supplier for Inter Milan, designing their home, away, and alternate kits. Puma collaborates with the club's marketing partners to refresh the design annually while maintaining the team's visual identity. 

For match-worn jerseys, Puma works closely with the club's technical team to ensure the kits meet international football standards and Inter's specific requirements. 


### What materials are used in Inter Milan jerseys?

Modern Inter Milan jerseys typically feature a polyester blend fabric that provides moisture-wicking properties and rapid drying. The shorts and socks may use similar synthetic materials or a higher-performance polypropylene construction. 

For training versions or outdoor alternatives, you might find additional fabrics like mesh panels for increased ventilation or technical fabrics with Dri-FIT technology to enhance performance during exercise. 


### How should I care for my Inter Milan jersey?

Machine wash your jersey in cool water using mild detergent to preserve the fabric's integrity and prevent color fading. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, which can damage the material or alter the colors. 

Air dry the jersey instead of using a tumble dryer, as heat can shrink the polyester blend and affect the jerseys' fit. Store the jersey flat or hang it using the shoulders to maintain its shape and prevent stretching. 


### Are Inter Milan jerseys gender-specific?

Inter Milan produces gender-specific jerseys for males, females, and youth sizes, with slight design variations to accommodate different body shapes and sizes. The technical specifications and materials used remain consistent across genders. 

Men's jerseys typically feature a slim fit, while women's versions offer a more relaxed silhouette. Youth jerseys are designed specifically for growing children and may include sizing charts that differ from adult models.
