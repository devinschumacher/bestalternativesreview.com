### What are high-calorie snacks?

High-calorie snacks are foods that pack a significant amount of energy (calories) into each serving. These snacks typically contain a combination of fats, sugars, or a high concentration of other nutrients that provide quick energy. 

Examples include nuts, seeds, cheese, chocolate, and dried fruits. While these snacks provide quick energy and essential nutrients, they should be consumed in moderation as part of a balanced diet. 


### Are high-calorie snacks unhealthy?

Not necessarily. While high-calorie snacks contain more energy-dense ingredients, they can still be nutritious. Many contain healthy fats, protein, or fiber that support overall health when consumed as part of a balanced diet. 

However, these snacks should be enjoyed in moderation due to their high calorie content. Regular consumption of too many high-calorie snacks can lead to excess calorie intake, potentially resulting in weight gain or other health issues.
