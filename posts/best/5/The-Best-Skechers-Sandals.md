### Upholstery Materials

The sandal's upholstery material significantly impacts comfort and durability. Consider the specific climate conditions where you'll wear the sandals to determine the most suitable material. 


### Strap Design and Fit

Strap configurations affect both comfort and style. Multiple cross-straps provide better foot coverage and stability, while minimalist designs offer more flexibility. Ensure the straps fit snugly without restrictions but can adjust to accommodate various foot shapes and sizes. 


### Heel Construction

Heeled sandals must balance style with practicality. Rigid heel cups offer better stability, but flexible designs allow for natural foot movement. Consider whether you'll primarily wear these for formal occasions or casual outings when selecting the heel height and construction type.
