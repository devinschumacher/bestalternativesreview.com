Elf shoes typically come in fabric or mesh construction, offering lightweight comfort. Quality materials prevent snags and maintain shape. Check for sturdy stitching and reinforced stress points like the heel and toe.

These shoes often run small, so measuring your foot accurately is crucial. Allow extra room for socks if you plan to wear them. The arch support should match your foot's natural curve, with no tight spots.
