### What should I consider when choosing the right size?

Measure around your natural waist and compare the results to the pants' sizing chart, as cargo pants often run slightly larger than standard jeans. Consider your intended use - looser fits are suitable for casual wear, while tighter options may be more practical for work or athletic activities. 

The rise (tightness around the waist) varies between styles, with regular rise providing a less fitted look and low rise offering more room at the waist and hips. Choose based on your personal preference and specific needs. 


### How should I care for these pants?

Cargo pants typically require machine washing in cold or lukewarm water with a mild detergent. Avoid using bleach, as it can damage the fabric and fade colors. For deeper cleaning, you can use a wool dryer ball to prevent shrinkage and maintain shape. 

Air drying is recommended to preserve the pants' elasticity and prevent shrinkage. If you must use a dryer, use low heat and remove the pants promptly while still slightly damp to avoid wrinkling.
