### How do I determine the right size refrigerator for my kitchen?

Measure your available kitchen space, including doorways and clearance requirements, to ensure the refrigerator can be installed easily. Common refrigerator sizes range from 24 to 36 inches wide, with depths between 27 and 36 inches. 

Consider how many people will use the refrigerator and their dining habits. A general guideline is 18-21 cubic feet per person, though this can vary based on meal sizes and meal preparation needs. 


### What maintenance is required for a Beko refrigerator?

Regular maintenance includes checking door seals for proper closure, cleaning condenser coils every 6-12 months, and ensuring adequate ventilation around the unit. The evaporator tray should be drained of accumulated water every 1-2 months. 

Set the refrigerator temperature between 37-40°F (3-4°C) and the freezer at 0°F (-18°C) using the digital control panel. Allow new fridge models 24-48 hours to reach optimal temperature after installation. 


### Are Beko refrigerators reliable?

Beko refrigerators carry warranties of 1-2 years on parts and labor, which indicates a level of manufacturer confidence in their products. Independent reviews often compare the brand favorably to its competitors in terms of durability and performance.

### How do I choose between top-freezer and bottom-freezer Beko models?

If you prioritize freezer space and live in a warm climate, a top-freezer model may be more energy-efficient as ice forms naturally on top. These tend to have more spacious freezer compartments but slower freezing capabilities. 

Bottom-freezer models typically offer a more practical layout with refrigerator space at a comfortable height. They often perform better with French doors and multi-compartment designs. Consider your family's needs and how you use the refrigerator when making your choice.
