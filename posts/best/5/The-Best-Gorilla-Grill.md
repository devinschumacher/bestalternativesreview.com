## FAQ


### What is a Gorilla Grill?

A Gorilla Grill is a portable, fuel-efficient outdoor grill that features an innovative design to cook food quickly and evenly. Unlike traditional grills that lose heat through open vents, Gorilla Grills maintain consistent temperatures by trapping heat within their insulated chambers. 

These grills are made from weather-resistant materials and come in various sizes, from compact models for camping to larger options for backyard gatherings. They typically run on charcoal or propane, though some newer models incorporate electric heating elements for convenience. 


### How does the cooking process work?

Unlike conventional grills that cook foods directly over an open flame, Gorilla Grills utilize a special cooking chamber that distributes heat evenly. This design eliminates the need for precise placement of food over the fire, making it easier to cook multiple items simultaneously. 

The cooking chamber keeps heat trapped inside, similar to how a conventional oven works. This allows for faster cooking times and a more consistent temperature throughout the cooking process, regardless of changes in ambient air temperature. 


### What can you cook on a Gorilla Grill?

A typical Gorilla Grill can accommodate various cooking surfaces, including grilling grates, baking sheets, and even cast-iron skillets. This versatility makes it suitable for grilling meats, vegetables, and baked goods, as well as cooking tasks like boiling water for hot drinks or cooking pasta. 

Many users also report success with cooking unconventional items like bread, pizza, and even small cuts of meat like sausages or chicken breasts. 


### Are Gorilla Grills easy to clean?

Yes, Gorilla Grills are designed with cleaning in mind. The cooking grates and interior surfaces are typically made from stainless steel or other easy-to-clean materials. After each use, you can wipe down the grates and the interior of the cooking chamber with mild soap and water.

### How long do Gorilla Grills last?
