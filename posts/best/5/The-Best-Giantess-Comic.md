## FAQ


### What are Giantess comics?

These epic fantasy series combine Norse mythology with original storytelling, featuring powerful female characters and immersive world-building. 

Each comic stands alone while contributing to the larger mythos, making them accessible to both newcomers and fans of the series. 


### Who are the main characters?

Our story follows a young woman named Freya who discovers she's one of the Giantesses - powerful descendants of Norse gods. She must learn to control her abilities and protect her people. 

The series also introduces other key characters including her mentor, a wise elder Giantess; and a mysterious warrior with his own secrets. 


### What's the setting like?

The comics are set in a version of Earth where Norse mythology has woven itself into the fabric of reality. Giant mountains, enchanted forests, and hidden realms coexist with modern technology. 

The artwork brings this richly imagined world to life through vibrant colors and detailed illustrations of fantastical creatures and landscapes. 


### Is it suitable for all ages?

While the story themes and artwork are designed to appeal to a wide audience, some issues contain mature themes like bloodshed and political intrigue. 

Younger readers might enjoy the standalone nature of each issue and the introduction to Norse mythology, while older readers will appreciate the complexity of the storytelling. 


### How are the issues structured?

Each comic features a main story arc centered around Freya's journey, with smaller tales of other Giantesses woven throughout. 

The issues conclude with cliffhangers that build anticipation for the next installment while also providing satisfying conclusions to subplots. 


### What format do the comics come in?

These standalone issues are available in several formats: print editions with high-quality paper and cover art, digital versions for tablets and computers, and audiobook versions for on-the-go listening. 

Each format captures the story's essence, with digital and audiobook versions offering advantages for busy readers or those who prefer different ways of consuming content.
