## FAQ

![Small-Paper-Shredder-4](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Small-Paper-Shredder-4/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What's the difference between cross-cut and straight-cut shredders?

Cross-cut shredders chop paper into small, confetti-like pieces, typically 0.1 x 1.5 inches. This makes it extremely difficult to reconstruct documents, offering higher security. Straight-cut shredders create long, thin strips, which are easier to collect but less secure against document recovery. 

For home use, cross-cut is generally recommended for sensitive documents like financial records. Straight-cut is suitable for everyday paper shredding tasks like office printers and personal documents that don't require military-grade security. 


### How much paper can a small shredder handle at once?

Most small paper shredders are designed for personal use and can handle 5-10 sheets of letter-sized paper at once. This capacity is adequate for daily office use but may not be suitable for businesses with high volume document disposal needs. 

Attempting to shred more paper than the shredder's capacity can damage the machine and compromise its performance. Always refer to your shredder's user manual for specific paper handling guidelines. 

![Small-Paper-Shredder-5](https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Small-Paper-Shredder-5/h=540,fit=pad,background=black)


### What materials can a small paper shredder safely process?

Small shredders are optimized for paper documents. They can handle letter and legal-size paper, as well as cardstock up to 65 lb weight. Many models can also process printer paper, carbon paper, and some glossy photo paper, though results may vary. 

Avoid feeding the shredder with materials beyond its specifications, such as binders, staples, spiral-bound documents, or non-paper items like CDs or USB drives, as these can damage the shredder. 


### How often should I empty the paper shredder's waste bag?

The frequency for emptying the waste bag depends on usage. On average, a standard 7-gallon bag fills within 1-2 weeks of normal use. Monitor the bag's fill level regularly and empty it when about 1/3 full to prevent overloading. 

Overfilling can impact shredding performance and increase the risk of paper jams. Regularly checking and emptying the bag ensures optimal operation and extends the shredder's lifespan.

### How long do small paper shredders last?

Small paper shredders typically last between 3 and 7 years with regular use, depending on usage frequency and maintenance. Light daily use (1-2 times per week) generally yields a longer lifespan, while frequent heavy use (daily or multiple times per day) may shorten it. 

To maximize your shredder's lifespan, follow the manufacturer's maintenance guidelines, avoid overloading, and replace parts like the cutting mechanism when signs of wear appear. 


### What is the noise level of small paper shredders?

Most small paper shredders operate quietly, typically between 65-75 decibels. This noise level is similar to a normal conversation and is suitable for home or office environments. 

Hospitals, libraries, and workspaces where absolute quiet is essential may want to consider models specifically designed for reduced noise operation. These typically cost more but offer quieter performance.
