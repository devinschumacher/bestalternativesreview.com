## Buyer's Guide

Cigarette trousers, also known as pencil trousers, are designed to create a slimming effect by hugging the legs while allowing ease of movement. ### Critical Fit Elements

Cigarette trousers rely on precise measurements to achieve their distinctive shape. The legs should taper smoothly from the waistband to just above the ankle, creating an hourglass-like silhouette. Critical measurements include the waist, inseam, and hem width. Consider a slightly roomier rise if you ride horses or engage in activities that require more movement.

### Waistband and Closure

The waistband of cigarette trousers typically sits higher than traditional pants, often at the natural waistline to prevent rolling down. Elastic waistbands offer comfort and flexibility but may not hold their shape during extended wear. For a more formal look, choose pants with buttoned or zipper closures that match your belt style. 


### Maintenance and Durability

Wool and wool blends are popular for their durability and ability to hold their shape, though some find them cumbersome in warmer weather. Rayon and artificial blends offer better breathability and lower maintenance requirements. Consider pants with reinforced stitching at stress points and quality zippers to ensure longevity and proper fit retention.

### What are cigarette trousers?

Cigarette trousers, also known as flare trousers, have a slightly wider leg opening that tapers towards the hem, creating a slimming effect similar to the shape of a cigarette. This design works particularly well with long boots or heels. 

These pants originated in the 19th century and experienced a resurgence in popularity during the 1980s, though their distinctive silhouette has remained a fashion staple for women's trousers. 


### What cuts are available for cigarette trousers?

Standard cigarette trousers feature a straight rise with a slightly flared leg that tapers to the ankle. Variations include high-waisted styles that create additional length in the leg, and relaxed fits that offer more ease in the overall silhouette. 

For those who prefer a more contemporary look, slim cigarette pants maintain the flared leg shape but with a more tailored, modern cut.

### How do I care for cigarette trousers?

Cigarette trousers should be treated like any other pair of dress pants - spot clean stains immediately with a damp cloth and mild detergent, and air dry. If needed, professional dry cleaning is recommended to maintain the fabric's integrity. 

Avoid using harsh chemicals or bleach, which can damage the fabric. Regular ironing can help maintain the pants' shape and eliminate any wrinkles that may develop. 


### What fabrics are suitable for cigarette trousers?

The most common fabrics for cigarette trousers include cotton, polyester, and rayon blends, which offer a balance of durability and comfort. These materials also work well for casual wear and can be found in both solid and print patterns. 

For a more formal look, opt for trousers made from wool or wool blends, which are suitable for both dressy occasions and cooler weather.

### What kind of shoes work with cigarette trousers?

Cigarette trousers pair particularly well with ankle boots, which help accentuate the flared leg shape. They also complement heeled ankle boots or pumps for a more formal look. 

Flat footwear like Mary Janes or loafers work in casual settings, while chunky boots or combat boots provide a more informal pairing. 


### How should I size myself for cigarette trousers?

Measure your waist using a flexible tape measure and compare it to the pants' measurements. Most cigarette trousers run true to size, so selecting your standard waist measurement should suffice. 

If you tend to wear your pants on the loose side, consider ordering one size up. The slightly wider leg opening can accommodate slightly larger hips and thighs.
