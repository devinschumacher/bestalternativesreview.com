Lucas luggage products come in various sizes, from compact carry-on options to larger suitcases. Consider the number of days you'll be traveling and any specific size requirements your airline might have for carry-on or checked luggage.

### Material and Construction

The durability of your luggage depends on its material and construction quality. Lucas offers options in hard-shell, soft-sided, and hybrid designs. Hard-shell cases provide the most protection but can be heavier, while soft-sided cases are more flexible and typically lighter. 


### Features and Functionality

Important features include wheels, locks, and interior organization. Lucas luggage often includes thoughtful design elements like multiple-compartment interiors, USB ports, and rolling designs for smoother movement. Consider whether you need specific features based on your travel style and needs.

### Care and Maintenance

### What size options are available for Lucas suitcases?

Lucas offers several standard suitcase sizes: 20-inch, 24-inch, and 28-inch. These sizes meet most domestic and international airline requirements for carry-on and checked baggage. 

Business travelers may also find the 22-inch and 26-inch sizes suitable for overnight stays or weekend trips. Each model includes multiple interior and exterior pockets for organizing clothing and accessories. 


### How much does Lucas luggage weigh?

Hard-sided suitcases typically weigh between 6 and 8 pounds, while soft-sided options range from 4 to 6 pounds. The weight varies slightly by model and configuration. 

Travel backpacks usually weigh between 2 and 4 pounds, with the heaviest models including built-in hydration systems and additional storage features.
