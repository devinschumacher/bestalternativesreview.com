### Burn Time

### Container Material

Candles come in various container materials, including glass and plastic. Glass provides a classic look and allows you to see the wax melt, while plastic options are lighter and more portable. Consider the material that best matches your decor and usage needs. 


### Care and Maintenance

To maximize your candle's lifespan and performance, trim the wick to 1/4 inch before each use, let the wax pool reach the edges during the first burn, and store candles in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and drafts.
