## Buyer's Guide

A Keurig dual coffee maker allows you to brew multiple cups simultaneously, making it ideal for household use or office settings. Understanding its features and capabilities will help you select the right model for your needs. 


### Capacity and Brew Size

These machines typically offer 12-cup and 16-cup options. Consider how many people will be drinking coffee regularly to determine the appropriate size. The brew size setting lets you customize the amount of coffee produced for different preferences or occasions. 


### Water Reservoir and Maintenance
