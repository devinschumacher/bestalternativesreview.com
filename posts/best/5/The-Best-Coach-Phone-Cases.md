Coach phone cases combine premium leather craftsmanship with protection for your smartphone. These cases offer a stylish alternative to plastic or silicone options, though they come with trade-offs in terms of thickness and potential damage from falls.

Coach cases are typically made from genuine leather or high-quality imitation leather.
