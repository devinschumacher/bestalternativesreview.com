## FAQ


### What is a magnetic popsocket?

A magnetic popsocket is a portable grip accessory that combines a flexible popsocket with magnetic functionality. It helps users hold devices steady and apply controlled pressure for taking photos or videos. 

The magnetic feature allows it to attach to metal surfaces, making it useful for holding phones while streaming or gaming. The popsocket provides additional grip and positioning options. 


### How do you use a magnetic popsocket?

Attach the magnetic end to metal surfaces like fridge doors or vehicles for quick device mounting. Position your phone or tablet in the holder, then extend the popsocket legs for optimal grip. 

To release the phone, simply squeeze the popsocket legs together while moving it away from the attachment point. This releases the tension and allows for smooth removal. 


### What are the benefits of a magnetic popsocket?

The magnetic functionality enables versatile mounting options, ideal for both stationary and mobile use cases. The popsocket provides adjustable grip points for precise positioning. 

The combination of magnetic and popsocket features adds stability to handheld devices, reducing accidental drops and providing better control during use. 


### Are magnetic popsockets durable?

Most magnetic popsockets are made from sturdy plastic and metal components designed for everyday use. They can handle regular use on most devices. 

However, the magnetic strength varies between models, with some featuring stronger magnets that may affect certain electronic devices or credit cards. 


### What devices work with magnetic popsockets?

Most magnetic popsockets are compatible with smartphones and tablets up to 7 inches in size. Some larger models can accommodate 8-9 inch devices. 

The magnetic attachment works best with metal-backed devices. For glass-back phones, a small magnetic pad may be needed to ensure proper sticking. 


### How do you clean a magnetic popsocket?

Regular cleaning can prevent dirt buildup and maintain the magnetic strength. Wipe the magnetic end with a damp cloth, then use an anti-static wipe for the popsocket portion. 

Avoid exposing the magnetic popsocket to extreme temperatures or harsh chemicals, as this can damage the plastic and affect magnetic properties.
