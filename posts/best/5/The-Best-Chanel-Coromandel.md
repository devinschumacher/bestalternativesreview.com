### How should I store Chanel Coromandel products?

Proper storage significantly extends the life of fragrances and maintains their quality. Keep Coromandel products in their original containers, away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Ideal storage conditions are between 60-70 degrees Fahrenheit (15-21 degrees Celsius). 

Avoid storing fragrances near fruits or vegetables, as natural chemicals can affect scent stability. Always close the bottle tightly after use and store vertical to minimize evaporative loss and maintain consistent performance.
