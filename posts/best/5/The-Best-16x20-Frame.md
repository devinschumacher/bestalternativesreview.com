### How do I maintain and clean my 16x20 frame?

Regular maintenance of your 16x20 frame involves cleaning both the frame's surface and the protective glass or acrylic cover. Use a soft, lint-free cloth to wipe away dust and fingerprints from the frame's exterior. For the glass or cover, use a microfiber cloth with a mild glass cleaner, being careful not to apply too much pressure that could damage the surface. 

When cleaning the print's backing or mounting materials, use acid-free products specifically designed for photographic prints. Avoid touching the print's surface directly, as oils from your hands can damage the image quality. For deep cleaning or professional maintenance, consider taking your frame to a local frame shop for specialized care. 


### What should I consider when choosing between glass and acrylic for my 16x20 frame?

The primary decision between glass and acrylic in a 16x20 frame comes down to weight and protection. Glass is heavier and more prone to breakage but provides better UV protection and clarity. Acrylic, while lighter and more impact-resistant, scratches more easily and has a slight yellowing over time. Consider the display location: glass is ideal for indoor display, while acrylic performs better in high-traffic areas or outdoor installations. 

Cost is another factor. standard glass is typically less expensive, but thicker acrylic sheets and specialized mounting systems can affect the overall price. Weigh these factors against your specific needs for protection, display location, and maintenance requirements to choose the right material for your frame.
