### Size and Space

Measure your room and doorways before purchasing. Add 1-2 feet to each dimension for maneuverability. Think about how the couch will affect your space's flow and functionality. 


### Brand Reputation and Warranty

Research the manufacturer's reputation for durability and customer service. Look for warranties that cover structural issues and manufacturer defects. A solid warranty indicates confidence in the product's quality.
