Hecate is a powerful goddess in Greek mythology, associated with magic, moon worship, and crossroads. She is often depicted with three faces, representing her dominion over the past, present, and future. 

Her statues typically include symbols such as torches, animals (especially dogs), and snakes, which represent her wisdom, protective nature, and connection to the underworld.
