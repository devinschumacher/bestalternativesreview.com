Unlike traditional rose gold, Pandora Rose contains no nickel, making it suitable for those with nickel allergies. The alloy's durability is comparable to 14K gold, withstanding daily wear while maintaining its color integrity. 


### How do Pandora Rose gold rings compare to 14K gold?

In terms of color, Pandora Rose gold has a slightly more vivid pink tone than 14K rose gold, appearing more saturated. In terms of durability, both alloys perform similarly in everyday wear conditions, though 14K gold may retain its color slightly longer in certain environments. 

Pandora Rose gold offers the visual appeal of rose gold at a more accessible price point. It's worth noting that Pandora Rose jewelry is significantly lighter than 14K gold, making it more comfortable for extended wear.
