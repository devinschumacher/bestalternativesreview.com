### What is a Cambro Hot Box?

A Cambro Hot Box is a specialized food serving solution that keeps food warm and fresh. It consists of a double-walled insulation system with a drainage channel and an internal rack for food containers. 

The box's dual walls create an air gap that acts as a thermal barrier, maintaining food temperatures effectively. The drainage channel prevents condensation buildup, while the internal rack keeps food off the bottom for better air circulation. 


### What types of food items are suitable for Cambro Hot Boxes?

Cambro Hot Boxes excel for serving hot foods like soup, chili, and casserole dishes. They're ideal for events, restaurants, and catered meals where maintaining food temperature is crucial. 

The boxes can handle liquids and solid foods, but items with extremely high moisture content may still release some condensation. Foods containing acidic ingredients might slightly alter the plastic's taste profile.
