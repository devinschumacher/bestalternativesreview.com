### How do the active noise cancellation settings work?

Raycon earbuds use dual-microphones in the ear tips to detect ambient noise. The ANC (Active Noise Cancellation) feature then generates opposing sound waves to cancel out external noise, providing a quiet listening environment. You can adjust the intensity of ANC through the companion app. 

The system works best with consistent sounds like steady traffic noise or air conditioning. It's less effective against sudden, sharp noises or multiple sound sources. During calls, ANC switches to noise suppression mode to improve audio quality.
