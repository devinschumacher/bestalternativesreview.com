### How do I clean a fondue pot?

Cleaning a fondue pot depends on the pot's material. For stainless steel or cast iron pots, use a spatula to remove any solid remains and then soak the pot in warm soapy water. If there are stubborn stains, use a non-abrasive scrub pad or mildly abrasive cleaner, making sure not to scratch the surface. Rinse thoroughly and dry the pot on the stove with low heat before storing it. 

For ceramic pots, avoid using abrasive cleaners or metal scouring pads, as they can damage the surface. Instead, fill the pot with warm soapy water and allow it to soak for a few minutes. Apply a soft brush or sponge to remove any residue, working gently to avoid scratching the surface. Rinse and dry the pot with a clean towel before storing it.
