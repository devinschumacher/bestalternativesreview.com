### How do Magnetic Rowing Machines Function?

Magnetic rowing machines operate via a magnetic resistance system. When you row, the magnetic flywheel, connected to the rowing handle, moves through a magnetic field created by the resistance mechanism. This magnetic field generates resistance, the force you feel while rowing. The level of resistance can be altered by adjusting the strength of the magnetic field, with most machines offering multiple resistance levels to suit your fitness goals and preferences.

As you row, the flywheel rotates, driving air resistance through a fan. This air resistance creates the sensation of rowing on water. Each magnetic rowing machine functions differently; however, they all generally share this basic design.
