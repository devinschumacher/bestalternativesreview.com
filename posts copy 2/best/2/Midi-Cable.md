### MIDI vs. MIDI over USB: What's the Difference?

MIDI and MIDI over USB are both communication protocols used for transferring musical data between devices. The main difference between them lies in their physical connections and supported devices. MIDI cables are typically used for connecting electronic musical instruments, computers, and other devices with MIDI-compatible connectors.

MIDI over USB, on the other hand, uses a USB connector to establish communication between devices, extending its compatibility to include computers, smartphones, and tablets. The choice between MIDI and MIDI over USB depends on the specific devices and the requirements of the music production or performance scenario.
