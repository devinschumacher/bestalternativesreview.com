### What are Harley Quinn Funko Pops?

Harley Quinn Funko Pops are a collectible series from Funko, a popular toy brand. These fun characters are designed in the style of pop art, with bright colors and unique features. Each Harley Quinn Funko Pop represents the iconic DC Comics villain, sporting her signature pigtails, red and black outfit, and manic grin. 

The series may contain different versions of Harley Quinn, such as exclusive editions, variants, and collaborations with other brands or franchises. Collectors often strive to obtain the entire set, making them popular and sought after items in the marketplace for fans of the Batman universe and Funko Pop collectors. 


### What materials are Harley Quinn Funko Pops made of?

Harley Quinn Funko Pops are typically made from PVC (polyvinyl chloride) plastics. PVC is a common material used for producing collectible figurines due to its durability and flexibility, allowing for intricate and detailed designs without the risk of breakage. The paint used on these figurines is often applied using a process called "screen printing, " which involves pressing ink through a stencil onto the plastic surface. 

Funko Pops are also designed to be easy to handle and display, with a sturdy base that supports the figure and a hollow, non-detachable body with a single point of articulation – the neck. This enables collectors to rotate the head for different viewing angles and facial expressions, adding to their collectibility and overall appeal.
