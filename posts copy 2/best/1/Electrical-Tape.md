### What are the safety classes of electrical tapes?

The safety classes of electrical tapes are rated according to the level of protection they provide for the user. There are five safety classes: Type I, II, III, IV, and V. Each safety class represents an increasing level of protection, with Type V providing the highest level of safety. 

For instance, Type II tapes are suitable for general outdoor wiring and installations, while Type I and II tapes are suitable for low and high voltage installations, respectively. It is essential to choose the appropriate safety class for the project, as using a lower-rated tape could compromise the safety of the installation and the surrounding environment.
