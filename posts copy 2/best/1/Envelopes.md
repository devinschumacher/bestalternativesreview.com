Consider the material and finish that best aligns with your requirements and budget.

### What are the different types of envelopes?

There are several types of envelopes, including traditional white envelopes, colored envelopes, window envelopes, padded envelopes, and more. The type of envelope you choose will depend on the occasion, the formality of the letter, and the contents you are sending. For example, padded envelopes are ideal for sending documents or photos, while colored envelopes can add a personal touch to your correspondence. 

Window envelopes, also known as see-through envelopes, are a convenient option that allows the recipient to see the contents without having to open the envelope. They are especially useful for invitations and business correspondence. Traditional white envelopes are the most common and versatile type of envelope, suitable for a wide range of occasions. 


### How can I ensure my envelopes are secure when sending them by mail?
