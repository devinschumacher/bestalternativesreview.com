### What is Stacked Books Decor?

Stacked Books Decor refers to a decorative trend where books are arranged in a stack, either horizontally or vertically, to create an artistic and visually appealing display. This style is often used to add a touch of elegance, sophistication, and coziness to a room, especially when used as a bookend or accent piece. 

Stacked Books Decor is available in various styles, materials, and sizes, allowing for customization based on individual preferences and interior design needs.
