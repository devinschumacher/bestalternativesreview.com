### What is a Tan Sectional?

A Tan Sectional refers to a type of outdoor furniture that includes a combination of seating options and tanning surfaces. These sectionals are typically designed for sunbathing and relaxing outdoors, providing a durable and stylish addition to any backyard or pool area. They often come with various features such as reclining sections, adjustable positions, and protective UV-resistant materials. 

Tanning sectionals are available in different styles, materials, and sizes, catering to various preferences, taste, and budgets. Some popular options include aluminum, polymer, and wicker materials. Aluminum tan sectionals offer durability, while polymer and wicker ones provide a more natural, traditional look. 


### How do I take care of a Tan Sectional?

To maintain the appearance and functionality of your tan sectional, it's essential to follow a proper cleaning and maintenance routine. First, use a soft brush or a vacuum to remove loose dirt and debris from the surface. Next, use a mild soap or detergent solution to clean the entire sectional, paying special attention to any crevices or areas that may harbor dirt. It is crucial to avoid using abrasive materials, harsh chemicals, or high-pressure cleaning tools, as these may cause damage to the material or its finish. 

Additionally, store your tan sectional properly during inclement weather, such as rain or snow. Cover it with a waterproof, breathable cover to protect it from the elements and potential mildew growth. Regularly check for signs of wear and tear, such as fading, rust, or peeling, and address any issues promptly to prolong the life of your sectional.
