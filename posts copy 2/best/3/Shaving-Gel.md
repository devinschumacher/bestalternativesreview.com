### What are shaving gels composed of?

Shaving gels often include a blend of water, surfactants, and additional components such as scents, emulsifiers, and preservatives. Together, these ingredients create a velvety, effortlessly lathering texture. Certain shaving gels also incorporate conditioning agents, which can soften and protect the skin throughout and post-shaving.
